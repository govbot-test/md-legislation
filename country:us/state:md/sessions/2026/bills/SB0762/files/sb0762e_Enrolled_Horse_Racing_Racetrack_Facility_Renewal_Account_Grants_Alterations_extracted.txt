1 AN ACT concerning
2 Horse Racing – Racetrack Facility Renewal Account Grants – Alterations
3 FOR the purpose of authorizing, rather than requiring, a certain racing licensee to receive
4 a grant for a capital construction plan from the Racetrack Facility Renewal Account
5 without providing and expending a matching fund; requiring the racing licensee that
6 receives the grant without providing and expending a matching fund to conduct live
7 racing in the State for a certain number of years; requiring the State Racing
8 Commission to recapture certain grant funds under certain circumstances and in a
9 certain manner; altering the amount of the balance in the Racetrack Facility Renewal
10 Account that is required to be made available to certain racing licensees if certain
11 racetracks are closed and no longer used for live racing; and generally relating to
12 grants from the Racetrack Facility Renewal Account.

23 Code of Maryland be renumbered to be Section(s) 9–1A–01(w–2) and (w–3), respectively.
24 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That the Laws of Maryland read
25 as follows:
26 Article – State Government
27 9–1A–01.
28 (a) In this subtitle the following words have the meanings indicated.
29 (W–1) “RACING LICENSEE” MEANS THE HOLDER OF A LICENSE ISSUED BY THE
30 STATE RACING COMMISSION TO HOLD A RACE MEETING IN THE STATE UNDER
31 TITLE 11 OF THE BUSINESS REGULATION ARTICLE.
32 9–1A–09.
33 [(a) In this section, “racing licensee” means the holder of a license issued by the
34 State Racing Commission to hold a race meeting in the State under Title 11 of the Business
35 Regulation Article.]
SENATE BILL 762 3
1 [(b)] (A) As a condition of eligibility for funding under § 9–1A–29 of this subtitle,
2 a racing licensee shall:
3 (1) (i) 1. for Rosecroft Raceway, FOR ANY YEAR THAT FUNDING IS
4 PROVIDED, conduct a minimum of 60 annual live racing days unless otherwise agreed to
5 by the racing licensee and the organization that represents the majority of licensed
6 standardbred owners and trainers in the State or unless the racing licensee is prevented
7 by weather, acts of God, or other circumstances beyond the racing licensee’s control; and
8 [(ii)] 2. for Ocean Downs Racetrack, FOR ANY YEAR THAT
9 FUNDING IS PROVIDED, conduct a minimum of 40 annual live racing days unless
10 otherwise agreed to by the racing licensee and the organization that represents the majority
11 of licensed standardbred owners and trainers in the State or unless the racing licensee is
12 prevented by weather, acts of God, or other circumstances beyond the racing licensee’s
13 control; AND
14 (II) IF THE RACING LICENSEE RECEIVES FUNDING WITHOUT
15 PROVIDING AND EXPENDING A MATCHING FUND, AGREE TO CONDUCT LIVE RACING
16 DAYS IN THE STATE FOR AT LEAST 10 YEARS AFTER THE CONSTRUCTION PLAN
17 REQUIRED UNDER § 9–1A–29(E) OF THIS SUBTITLE IS COMPLETE;
18 (2) develop and submit to the State Racing Commission a multiyear plan
19 to improve the quality and marketing of horse racing at racetrack locations owned or
20 operated by the racing licensee in Maryland, which shall include:
21 (i) goals, indicators, and timelines for specific actions that will be
22 taken by the racing licensee to improve the quality and marketing of the horse racing
23 industry in Maryland; and
24 (ii) a master plan for capital improvements that reflects, at a
25 minimum:
26 1. commitments that have been made to the State Racing
27 Commission;
28 2. updates to any prior master plan specifically identifying
29 capital improvements and expenditures made on or after January 1, 2018; and
30 3. an ongoing investment in capital maintenance and
31 improvements in the horse racing facilities; AND
32 (3) for each year that funding is requested, spend at least the following
33 minimum amounts for capital maintenance and improvements, which may include
34 amounts provided as a matching fund [as required] IF THE RACING LICENSEE ELECTS
35 TO PROVIDE AND EXPEND A MATCHING FUND under § 9–1A–29(e)(2) of this subtitle:
4 SENATE BILL 762
1 (i) for Rosecroft Raceway, $300,000; and
2 (ii) for Ocean Downs Racetrack, $300,000.
3 [(c)] (B) As part of the capital maintenance and improvement items in the plan
4 submitted under subsection [(b)(2)] (A)(2) of this section, a racing licensee shall include
5 any improvements necessary to ensure that the condition of any part of the racetrack
6 facility where individuals reside is satisfactory for human habitation and meets minimum
7 housing and sanitation standards in the county where the facility is located.
8 [(d)] (C) The plans required under subsection [(b)] (A) of this section shall also
9 be provided to the Department of General Services and to the Legislative Policy Committee
10 of the General Assembly.
11 9–1A–28.
12 (h) (1) To obtain operating assistance under this section:
13 (i) a [holder of a racing license to race] RACING LICENSEE at Ocean
14 Downs Race Course or Rosecroft Raceway may apply to the Secretary of Labor for the
15 reimbursement of expenditures made by the racing licensee to conduct the annual live
16 racing schedule; and
17 (ii) a [holder of a racing license to race] RACING LICENSEE at
18 Rosecroft Raceway shall:
19 1. agree to rehire workers employed at the facility prior to
20 the end of live racing on June 27, 2008; and
21 2. recognize collective bargaining agreements that were in
22 place as of June 1, 2008.
23 9–1A–29.
24 (a) There is a Racetrack Facility Renewal Account under the authority of the
25 State Racing Commission.
26 (b) (6) (i) Subject to subparagraph (ii) of this paragraph, the State Racing
27 Commission may use the services of a certified public accountant to review an eligible
28 request for OR THE EXPENDITURES OF a grant under this section.
29 (ii) [The holder of a license to hold a race meeting in the State] A
30 RACING LICENSEE that has requested a grant under this section shall reimburse the State
31 Racing Commission for any expenditures for services under subparagraph (i) of this
32 paragraph.
SENATE BILL 762 5
1 (c) Except as provided in subsection (d)(1) of this section, funds from the Account
2 shall be used to provide a grant to the [holder of a license to hold a race meeting in the
3 State] RACING LICENSEE for racetrack facility capital construction and improvements.
4 (e) (1) In order to obtain a grant, a [holder of a license to hold a race meeting
5 in the State] RACING LICENSEE shall[:
6 (1)] submit a capital construction plan to be implemented COMPLETED
7 within a specified time frame to the State Racing Commission for approval[; and].
8 (2) SUBJECT TO § 9–1A–09(A)(1)(II) OF THIS SUBTITLE AND THE
9 APPROVAL OF THE GRANT FOR THE CAPITAL CONSTRUCTION PLAN BY THE STATE
10 RACING COMMISSION, THE RACING LICENSEE MAY provide and expend a matching
11 fund.
12 (f) (1) After a grant has been provided under this section, the State Racing
13 Commission shall:
14 [(1)] (I) in consultation with the Department of General Services,
15 monitor the implementation of the approved capital construction plan; and
16 (II) ON WRITTEN REQUEST FROM THE GRANTEE, IN
17 CONSULTATION WITH THE DEPARTMENT OF GENERAL SERVICES, CERTIFY IN
18 WRITING THAT THE GRANTEE HAS COMPLETED THE APPROVED CAPITAL
19 CONSTRUCTION PLAN; AND
20 [(2)] (II) (III) make provisions for recapture of grant money if the capital
21 construction plan is not implemented COMPLETED within the time frame approved by the
22 State Racing Commission.
23 (2) IF A RACING LICENSEE RECEIVES A GRANT FOR A CAPITAL
24 CONSTRUCTION PLAN WITHOUT EXPENDING A MATCHING FUND AND DOES NOT
25 COMPLETE THE CAPITAL CONSTRUCTION PLAN OR CONDUCT LIVE RACING DAYS IN
26 THE STATE FOR AT LEAST 10 YEARS AFTER THE CONSTRUCTION PLAN IS COMPLETE,
27 THE STATE RACING COMMISSION SHALL:
28 (I) IF THE RACING LICENSEE DOES NOT CONDUCT LIVE RACING
29 DAYS IN THE STATE FOR AT LEAST 10 YEARS AFTER THE CONSTRUCTION PLAN IS
30 COMPLETE, CALCULATE THE AMOUNT TO BE REPAID ON A PRO RATA BASIS; AND
31 BASIS AND RECAPTURE THE GRANT MONEY; OR
32 (II) RECAPTURE THE GRANT MONEY IN THE AMOUNT AS
33 CALCULATED UNDER ITEM (I) OF THIS PARAGRAPH.
6 SENATE BILL 762
1 (h) (1) The State Racing Commission shall adopt regulations to implement the
2 provisions of this section, including regulations to:
3 (i) address minimum criteria for the types of improvements to be
4 made by the [holder of a license] RACING LICENSEE; [and]
5 (ii) establish a formula to allocate funds under subsection (d)(2) of
6 this section between Rosecroft Raceway and Ocean Downs Race Course; AND
7 (III) ESTABLISH GUIDELINES FOR A RACING LICENSEE THAT
8 REQUESTS A GRANT FOR A CAPITAL CONSTRUCTION PLAN WITHOUT EXPENDING A
9 MATCHING FUND.
10 (2) (I) If Rosecroft Raceway is closed and no longer used for live racing,
11 75% OF the unencumbered fund balance, including accrued interest, of the amount
12 available to Rosecroft Raceway from the Racetrack Facility Renewal Account under
13 subsection (d)(1)(ii) of this section shall be made available to Ocean Downs Race Course.
14 (II) IF OCEAN DOWNS RACE COURSE IS CLOSED AND NO
15 LONGER USED FOR LIVE RACING, 75% OF THE UNENCUMBERED FUND BALANCE,
16 INCLUDING ACCRUED INTEREST, OF THE AMOUNT AVAILABLE TO OCEAN DOWNS
17 FROM THE RACETRACK FACILITY RENEWAL ACCOUNT UNDER SUBSECTION
18 (D)(1)(II) OF THIS SECTION SHALL BE MADE AVAILABLE TO ROSECROFT RACEWAY.
19 SECTION 3. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect July
20 1, 2026.
