CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Video Tolls – Class G (Trailer) Vehicles – Liability
3 FOR the purpose of establishing that the registered owner of a Class G (trailer) vehicle is
4 liable under certain circumstances to the Maryland Transportation Authority for
5 payment of a video toll assessed at the time of passage through a toll collection
6 facility, subject to certain standards and procedures for the transfer of liability; and
7 generally relating to liability for the payment of video tolls.
