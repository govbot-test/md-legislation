A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Washington Suburban Sanitary Commission – Personnel Management –
3 Disciplinary Actions
4 PG/MC 104–26
5 FOR the purpose of requiring the Washington Suburban Sanitary Commission to take
6 certain actions before suspending or removing an employee of the Commission;
7 providing that the Commission may take any disciplinary action not later than
8 within a certain number of days after the Commission acquires knowledge of the
9 misconduct for which the disciplinary action is imposed; authorizing an employee
10 who is suspended for disciplinary purposes to appeal to the Office of Administrative
11 Hearings; requiring the Office to dispose of a case or conduct a hearing and issue a
12 final decision in an appeal under this Act; and generally relating to the Washington
13 Suburban Sanitary Commission.
