A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Public High Schools – Student Transcripts – Traumatic Loss Notation
3 FOR the purpose of providing that a certain high school student who experiences a certain
4 traumatic loss may request the inclusion of a traumatic loss notation on the student’s
5 official transcript; requiring a local school system to print a certain traumatic loss
6 notation on a certain student’s official transcript under certain circumstances;
7 requiring the State Department of Education to develop standard language for a
8 traumatic loss notation; requiring high school counselors to provide certain
9 information to students and surviving parents under certain circumstances; and
10 generally relating to a traumatic loss notation on high school student transcripts.

12 Article – Education
13 Section 7–213

1 (3) “TRAUMATIC LOSS” MEANS THE DEATH OF A PARENT AS A RESULT
2 OF REPRODUCTIVE CANCER, INCLUDING BREAST CANCER, CERVICAL CANCER, AND
3 UTERINE CANCER.
4 (B) A STUDENT WHO EXPERIENCES A TRAUMATIC LOSS WHILE ENROLLED
5 IN HIGH SCHOOL MAY SUBMIT A WRITTEN REQUEST TO THE LOCAL SCHOOL SYSTEM
6 TO INCLUDE A TRAUMATIC LOSS NOTATION ON THE STUDENT’S OFFICIAL
7 TRANSCRIPT TO INDICATE AN IMPACT ON THE STUDENT’S ACADEMIC
8 PERFORMANCE, ACHIEVEMENT, OR ATTENDANCE.
9 (C) A STUDENT IS ELIGIBLE TO REQUEST A TRAUMATIC LOSS NOTATION ON
10 THE STUDENT’S OFFICIAL TRANSCRIPT IF THE STUDENT SUBMITS TO THE LOCAL
11 SCHOOL SYSTEM:
12 (1) A REQUEST TO INCLUDE A TRAUMATIC LOSS NOTATION ON THE
13 FORM PROVIDED BY THE LOCAL SCHOOL SYSTEM; AND
14 (2) (I) A COPY OF THE PARENT’S DEATH CERTIFICATE LISTING A
15 CAUSE OF DEATH THAT QUALIFIES AS A TRAUMATIC LOSS; OR
16 (II) A LETTER FROM A LICENSED HEALTH CARE PROVIDER
17 VERIFYING THAT THE PARENT’S CAUSE OF DEATH QUALIFIES AS A TRAUMATIC LOSS.
18 (D) (1) ON RECEIPT OF A REQUEST FROM A STUDENT ELIGIBLE UNDER
19 SUBSECTION (C) OF THIS SECTION, A LOCAL SCHOOL SYSTEM SHALL PRINT A
20 TRAUMATIC LOSS NOTATION ON THE STUDENT’S OFFICIAL TRANSCRIPT USING THE
21 STANDARD LANGUAGE DEVELOPED BY THE DEPARTMENT UNDER SUBSECTION (E)
22 OF THIS SECTION.
23 (2) A LOCAL SCHOOL SYSTEM SHALL REMOVE A TRAUMATIC LOSS
24 NOTATION FROM A STUDENT’S OFFICIAL TRANSCRIPT AT THE WRITTEN REQUEST OF
25 THE STUDENT.
26 (E) (1) THE DEPARTMENT SHALL DEVELOP STANDARD LANGUAGE FOR A
27 TRAUMATIC LOSS NOTATION PRINTED ON AN OFFICIAL TRANSCRIPT BY A LOCAL
28 SCHOOL SYSTEM UNDER THIS SECTION.
29 (2) TO MAINTAIN STUDENT PRIVACY, THE STANDARD LANGUAGE
30 DEVELOPED UNDER THIS SECTION SHALL REFER TO THE TRAUMATIC LOSS AS
31 EITHER:
32 (I) A DOCUMENTED COMPELLING PERSONAL CIRCUMSTANCE;

2 (3) A TRAUMATIC LOSS NOTATION:

12 (1) INFORM THE STUDENT AND ANY SURVIVING PARENT OF THE

15 (2) ADVISE THE STUDENT ON HOW THE TRAUMATIC LOSS NOTATION
16 MAY BE USED TO EXPLAIN FLUCTUATIONS IN ACADEMIC PERFORMANCE,
