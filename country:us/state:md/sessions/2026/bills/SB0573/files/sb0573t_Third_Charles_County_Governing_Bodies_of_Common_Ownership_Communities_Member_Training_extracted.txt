1 AN ACT concerning
2 Charles County – Governing Bodies of Common Ownership Communities –
3 Member Training
4 FOR the purpose of requiring each member of a board of directors or officer of a council of
5 unit owners of a condominium in Charles County and each member of a governing
6 body of a homeowners association in Charles County to successfully complete a
7 certain training curriculum in a certain manner and subject to certain requirements;
8 and generally relating to members of governing bodies of condominiums and
9 homeowners associations in Charles County.

2 Charles County – Governing Bodies of Common Ownership Communities –
3 Member Training
4 FOR the purpose of requiring each member of a board of directors or officer of a council of
5 unit owners of a condominium in Charles County and each member of a governing
6 body of a homeowners association in Charles County to successfully complete a
7 certain training curriculum in a certain manner and subject to certain requirements;
8 and generally relating to members of governing bodies of condominiums and
9 homeowners associations in Charles County.

6 11–109.
7 (a) The affairs of the condominium shall be governed by a council of unit owners
8 which, even if unincorporated, is constituted a legal entity for all purposes. The council of
9 unit owners shall be comprised of all unit owners.
10 (b) The bylaws may authorize or provide for the delegation of any power of the
11 council of unit owners to a board of directors, officers, managing agent, or other person for

13 (C) (1) THIS SUBSECTION APPLIES ONLY IN CHARLES COUNTY.

18 (I) A COMMON OWNERSHIP COMMISSION RECOGNIZED BY THE
19 STATE OR A LOCAL GOVERNMENT; OR

21 TRAINING CURRICULUM BY A RECOGNIZED COMMON OWNERSHIP COMMISSION.

23 COUNCIL OF UNIT OWNERS SHALL SUCCESSFULLY COMPLETE THE TRAINING
24 CURRICULUM REQUIRED UNDER THIS SUBSECTION WITHIN 90 DAYS AFTER THE
25 INDIVIDUAL:
26 (I) IS FIRST ELECTED OR APPOINTED TO THE OFFICE; OR
27 (II) FOR AN INDIVIDUAL WHO IS IN OFFICE ON OCTOBER 1,
28 2025, BEGINS A SUBSEQUENT TERM AS A MEMBER OF THE BOARD OF DIRECTORS OR
29 AN OFFICER OF THE COUNCIL OF UNIT OWNERS.
