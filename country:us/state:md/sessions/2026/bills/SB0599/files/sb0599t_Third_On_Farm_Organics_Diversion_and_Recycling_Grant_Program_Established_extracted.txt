CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 On–Farm Organics and Wasted Food Reduction and Diversion and Recycling
3 Grant
4 Programs Program – Established
5 FOR the purpose of establishing the On–Farm Organics Diversion and Recycling Grant
6 Program in the Department of Agriculture to award grants to eligible entities to
7 develop and implement on–farm organics recycling and compost use, wasted food
8 prevention, and food rescue; establishing the Wasted Food Reduction and Diversion
9 Grant Program and the County Wasted Food Reduction Block Grant Program in the
10 Department of the Environment to award grants and fund certain projects for
11 infrastructure, programs, and education that reduce, rescue, and divert wasted food
12 in the State and certain counties; and generally relating to wasted food reduction
13 and diversion on–farm organics recycling and compost use, wasted food prevention,
14 and food rescue in the State.
