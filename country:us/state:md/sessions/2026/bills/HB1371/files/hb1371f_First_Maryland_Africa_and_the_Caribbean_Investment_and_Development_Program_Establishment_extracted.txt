A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Maryland–Africa and the Caribbean Investment and Development Program –
3 Establishment
4 FOR the purpose of establishing the Maryland–Africa and the Caribbean Investment and
5 Development Program in the Department of Commerce to study and promote certain
6 trade relations; establishing an Advisory Board to conduct certain studies and make
7 recommendations to the Department; authorizing the Department to issue certain
8 funds to certain businesses from the Maryland African Diaspora Investment and
9 Development Fund for certain purposes; authorizing the Department to require
10 certain businesses to return certain funds under certain circumstances; and
11 generally relating to the Maryland–Africa and the Caribbean Investment and
12 Development Program and the Maryland African Diaspora Investment and
13 Development Fund.
14 BY adding to
15 Article – Economic Development
16 Section 5–2701 through 5–2707 to be under the new subtitle “Subtitle 27.
17 Maryland–Africa and the Caribbean Investment and Development Program”
18 Annotated Code of Maryland
