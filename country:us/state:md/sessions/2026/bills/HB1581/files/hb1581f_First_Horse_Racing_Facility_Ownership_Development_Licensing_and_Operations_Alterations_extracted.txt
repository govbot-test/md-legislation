A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Horse Racing – Facility Ownership, Development, Licensing, and Operations –
3 Alterations
4 FOR the purpose of altering the entities to which the State Racing Commission may issue
5 a license and award racing days for racing at mile tracks; altering the circumstances
6 under which the Preakness Stakes may be transferred to another track in the State;
7 altering certain requirements for the thoroughbred racetrack operator of and certain
8 long–term agreements related to the Pimlico racing facility site; providing that the
9 Maryland Stadium Authority is the successor of the Maryland Thoroughbred
10 Racetrack Operating Authority for the operation of a certain racing and community
11 development project; requiring the thoroughbred racetrack operator to prepare
12 certain financial statements and provide those statements to the Maryland Stadium
13 Authority in a certain manner; requiring certain income of the thoroughbred
14 racetrack operator to be distributed in a certain manner; altering certain
15 distributions from the Purse Dedication Account; and generally relating to horse
16 racing in the State.
