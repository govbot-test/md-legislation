WES MOORE, Governor Ch. 166
Chapter 166
(Senate Bill 772)
AN ACT concerning
Maryland Department of Health – Employment Training and Opportunity
Database
FOR the purpose of requiring the Maryland Department of Health to establish an
employment training and opportunity database for the purpose of assisting
individuals in the State in finding employment training and other opportunities that
may enable the individuals to become eligible and maintain eligibility for the
Maryland Medical Assistance Program and the Supplemental Nutrition Assistance
Program; requiring the Maryland Department of Health to assist individuals in
enrolling in programs listed in the database; requiring the Maryland Department of
Labor, the Secretary of Human Services, and the Department of Service and Civic
Innovation to establish a process for receiving recommendations for inclusion in the
database and to forward appropriate recommendations to the Maryland Department
of Health; and generally relating to an employment training and opportunity
database.

SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
Article – Business Regulation
2–111.
THE DEPARTMENT SHALL:
(1) ESTABLISH A PROCESS THROUGH WHICH A PERSON MAY
RECOMMEND A VOCATIONAL, WORKFORCE, TRAINING, OR JOB PLACEMENT
PROGRAM FOR INCLUSION IN THE DATABASE DEVELOPED AND MAINTAINED UNDER
§ 15–160 OF THE HEALTH – GENERAL ARTICLE; AND
(2) IF APPROPRIATE, FORWARD A RECOMMENDATION MADE UNDER
ITEM (1) OF THIS SECTION TO THE MARYLAND DEPARTMENT OF HEALTH FOR
INCLUSION IN THE DATABASE.
Article – Health – General
15–160.
(A) IN THIS SECTION, “DATABASE” MEANS THE EMPLOYMENT TRAINING
AND OPPORTUNITY DATABASE DEVELOPED AND MAINTAINED UNDER SUBSECTION
(B) OF THIS SECTION.

(B) (1) THE DEPARTMENT SHALL DEVELOP AND MAINTAIN AN
EMPLOYMENT TRAINING AND OPPORTUNITY DATABASE.
(2) THE PURPOSE OF THE DATABASE IS TO PROVIDE INDIVIDUALS IN
THE STATE WITH ASSISTANCE IN FINDING EMPLOYMENT TRAINING AND OTHER
OPPORTUNITIES THAT MAY ENABLE THE INDIVIDUALS TO BECOME ELIGIBLE AND
MAINTAIN ELIGIBILITY FOR:
(I) THE PROGRAM; AND
(II) THE SUPPLEMENTAL NUTRITION ASSISTANCE PROGRAM.
(3) IN DEVELOPING AND MAINTAINING THE DATABASE, THE
DEPARTMENT SHALL COORDINATE WITH THE FOLLOWING ENTITIES:
(I) THE MARYLAND DEPARTMENT OF LABOR;
(II) THE DEPARTMENT OF HUMAN SERVICES;
(III) THE DEPARTMENT OF SERVICE AND CIVIC INNOVATION;
(IV) THE MARYLAND CHAMBER OF COMMERCE;
(V) LABOR UNIONS;
(VI) TRADE ASSOCIATIONS;
(VII) EDUCATIONAL INSTITUTIONS;
(VIII) LOCAL WORKFORCE DEVELOPMENT BOARDS; AND
(IX) ANY OTHER ENTITY THAT THE DEPARTMENT DETERMINES
APPROPRIATE.
(C) THE DATABASE SHALL INCLUDE:
(1) VOCATIONAL, WORKFORCE TRAINING, AND JOB PLACEMENT
PROGRAMS AVAILABLE IN THE STATE;
(2) INFORMATION REGARDING HOW TO APPLY FOR A VOCATIONAL,
WORKFORCE TRAINING, OR JOB PLACEMENT PROGRAM INCLUDED IN THE
DATABASE UNDER ITEM (1) OF THIS SUBSECTION; AND

(3) A MECHANISM THAT ALLOWS AN INDIVIDUAL TO DIRECTLY
CONTACT THE VOCATIONAL, WORKFORCE TRAINING, OR JOB PLACEMENT PROGRAM
IN WHICH THE INDIVIDUAL IS INTERESTED.
(D) THE DEPARTMENT SHALL:
(1) MAKE THE DATABASE AVAILABLE TO THE PUBLIC ON A
DEDICATED PAGE ON THE DEPARTMENT’S WEBSITE;
(2) ASSIST INDIVIDUALS IN ENROLLING IN VOCATIONAL,
WORKFORCE TRAINING, AND JOB PLACEMENT PROGRAMS LISTED IN THE
DATABASE; AND
(3) UPDATE THE DATABASE AT LEAST EVERY 30 DAYS.
