CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Maryland Department of Health – Employment Training and Opportunity
3 Database
4 FOR the purpose of requiring the Maryland Department of Health to establish an
5 employment training and opportunity database for the purpose of assisting
6 individuals in the State in finding employment training and other opportunities that
7 may enable the individuals to become eligible and maintain eligibility for the
8 Maryland Medical Assistance Program and the Supplemental Nutrition Assistance
9 Program; requiring the Maryland Department of Health to assist individuals in
10 enrolling in programs listed in the database; requiring the Maryland Department of
11 Labor, the Secretary of Human Services, and the Department of Service and Civic
12 Innovation to establish a process for receiving recommendations for inclusion in the
13 database and to forward appropriate recommendations to the Maryland Department
14 of Health; and generally relating to an employment training and opportunity
15 database.
16 BY adding to
17 Article – Business Regulation
18 Section 2–111
19 Annotated Code of Maryland
20 (2024 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
21 BY adding to

7 WORKFORCE TRAINING, OR JOB PLACEMENT PROGRAM INCLUDED IN THE
8 DATABASE UNDER ITEM (1) OF THIS SUBSECTION; AND
9 (3) A MECHANISM THAT ALLOWS AN INDIVIDUAL TO DIRECTLY
10 CONTACT THE VOCATIONAL, WORKFORCE TRAINING, OR JOB PLACEMENT PROGRAM

15 (2) ASSIST INDIVIDUALS IN ENROLLING IN VOCATIONAL,
16 WORKFORCE TRAINING, AND JOB PLACEMENT PROGRAMS LISTED IN THE
17 DATABASE; AND
18 (3) UPDATE THE DATABASE AT LEAST EVERY 30 DAYS.
