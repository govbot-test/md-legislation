By: Senators Hettleman, Kramer, Love, Waldstreicher, Lewis Young, Zucker, and
Sydnor
Introduced and read first time: January 21, 2026
Assigned to: Education, Energy, and the Environment
A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 General Provisions – Commemorative Months – Muslim American Heritage and
3 Jewish American Heritage Months
4 FOR the purpose of requiring the Governor annually to proclaim certain months as Muslim
5 American Heritage and Jewish American Heritage months; requiring the
6 proclamations to urge certain organizations to observe the months with certain
7 programs and activities; and generally relating to Muslim American Heritage and
8 Jewish American Heritage months.
9 BY renumbering
10 Article – General Provisions
11 Section 7–501 through 7–503 and 7–504 through 7–507
12 to be Section 7–502 through 7–504 and 7–506 through 7–509, respectively
13 Annotated Code of Maryland
14 (2019 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
15 BY adding to
16 Article – General Provisions
17 Section 7–501 and 7–505
18 Annotated Code of Maryland
19 (2019 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
20 Preamble
21 WHEREAS, There are over 350,000 Muslim Marylanders who currently reside in
22 the State and, for generations, have contributed to enriching the artistic, business,
23 academic, and public service communities thereby significantly increasing the vibrancy of
24 Maryland; and
EXPLANATION: CAPITALS INDICATE MATTER ADDED TO EXISTING LAW.
[Brackets] indicate matter deleted from exist in g l aw . *sb0308*
2 SENATE BILL 308
1 WHEREAS, There are over 250,000 Jewish Marylanders who currently reside in the
2 State and, for generations, have contributed to enriching the artistic, business, academic,
3 and public service communities thereby significantly increasing the vibrancy of Maryland;
4 now, therefore,
5 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
6 That Section(s) 7–501 through 7–503 and 7–504 through 7–507 of Article – General
7 Provisions of the Annotated Code of Maryland be renumbered to be Section(s) 7–502
8 through 7–504 and 7–506 through 7–509, respectively.
9 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That the Laws of Maryland read
10 as follows:
11 Article – General Provisions
12 7–501.
13 (A) THE GOVERNOR ANNUALLY SHALL PROCLAIM THE MONTH OF JANUARY
14 AS MUSLIM AMERICAN HERITAGE MONTH IN RECOGNITION OF THE
15 CONTRIBUTIONS THAT MUSLIM AMERICANS HAVE MADE TO THE STATE.
16 (B) THE PROCLAMATION SHALL URGE EDUCATIONAL AND CULTURAL
17 ORGANIZATIONS TO OBSERVE MUSLIM AMERICAN HERITAGE MONTH PROPERLY
18 WITH APPROPRIATE PROGRAMS, CEREMONIES, AND ACTIVITIES.
19 7–505.
20 (A) THE GOVERNOR ANNUALLY SHALL PROCLAIM THE MONTH OF MAY AS
21 JEWISH AMERICAN HERITAGE MONTH IN RECOGNITION OF THE CONTRIBUTIONS
22 THAT JEWISH AMERICANS HAVE MADE TO THE STATE.
23 (B) THE PROCLAMATION SHALL URGE EDUCATIONAL AND CULTURAL
24 ORGANIZATIONS TO OBSERVE JEWISH AMERICAN HERITAGE MONTH PROPERLY
25 WITH APPROPRIATE PROGRAMS, CEREMONIES, AND ACTIVITIES.
26 SECTION 3. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
27 October 1, 2026.
