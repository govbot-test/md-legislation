CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Maryland Higher Education Commission – Practical Applications of Real Estate
3 Appraisal (PAREA) Grant Program – Established
4 FOR the purpose of establishing the Practical Applications of Real Estate Appraisal
5 (PAREA) Grant Program to provide grants to certain individuals with the goal of
6 becoming a real estate appraiser in certain communities; requiring the Maryland
7 Higher Education Commission to administer the Program; and generally relating to
8 the Practical Applications of Real Estate Appraisal (PAREA) Grant Program.
9 BY adding to
10 Article – Education
11 Section 18–4201 and 18–4202 to be under the new subtitle “Subtitle 42. PAREA
12 Grant Program”

14 (2022 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
15 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
16 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
17 Article – Education
18 SUBTITLE 42. PAREA GRANT PROGRAM.

2 HOUSE BILL 919
1 18–4201.
2 (A) IN THIS SUBTITLE THE FOLLOWING WORDS HAVE THE MEANINGS
3 INDICATED.
4 (B) “PAREA” MEANS THE PRACTICAL APPLICATIONS OF REAL ESTATE
5 APPRAISAL.
6 (C) “PROGRAM” MEANS THE PRACTICAL APPLICATIONS OF REAL ESTATE
7 APPRAISAL GRANT PROGRAM.
8 18–4202.
9 (A) THERE IS A PRACTICAL APPLICATIONS OF REAL ESTATE APPRAISAL
10 GRANT PROGRAM IN THE COMMISSION.
11 (B) THE PURPOSE OF THE PROGRAM IS TO:
12 (1) ASSIST IN CLOSING APPRAISAL GAPS THAT OCCUR IN
13 HISTORICALLY REDLINED NEIGHBORHOODS AND UNDERREPRESENTED
14 COMMUNITIES;
15 (2) AWARD GRANTS TO MINORITIES WHO RESIDE IN HISTORICALLY
16 REDLINED NEIGHBORHOODS AND UNDERREPRESENTED COMMUNITIES WITH THE
17 GOAL OF PURSUING PAREA; AND
18 (3) DIVERSIFY THE APPRAISAL FIELD.
19 (C) THE COMMISSION SHALL ADMINISTER THE PROGRAM.
20 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect July
21 1, 2026.
