2 SENATE BILL 847
1 GRANT, BY LAW, A PROPERTY TAX CREDIT AGAINST THE COUNTY OR MUNICIPAL
2 CORPORATION PROPERTY TAX IMPOSED ON REAL PROPERTY THAT IS:
3 (I) OWNED BY SALISBURY NEIGHBORHOOD HOUSING
4 SERVICES, INC., WITH THE INTENTION OF RELINQUISHING OWNERSHIP IN THE NEAR
5 FUTURE;
6 (II) USED FOR THE PURPOSES OF DEVELOPMENT,
7 REHABILITATION, AND TRANSFER TO A PRIVATE OWNER; AND
8 (III) NOT OCCUPIED BY ADMINISTRATIVE OR WAREHOUSE
9 BUILDINGS OWNED BY SALISBURY NEIGHBORHOOD HOUSING SERVICES, INC.
10 (2) IF THE GOVERNING BODY OF WICOMICO COUNTY OR THE
11 GOVERNING BODY OF A MUNICIPAL CORPORATION IN WICOMICO COUNTY GRANTS
12 A PROPERTY TAX CREDIT UNDER THIS SUBSECTION, SALISBURY NEIGHBORHOOD
13 HOUSING SERVICES, INC., SHALL SUBMIT AN ANNUAL WRITTEN REPORT TO THE
14 GOVERNING BODY THAT DOCUMENTS THE FOLLOWING:
15 (I) ALL REAL PROPERTY HOLDINGS OF SALISBURY
16 NEIGHBORHOOD HOUSING SERVICES, INC., IN THE JURISDICTION GRANTING THE
17 TAX CREDIT; AND
18 (II) ALL TRANSACTIONS INVOLVING REAL PROPERTY HOLDINGS
19 OF SALISBURY NEIGHBORHOOD HOUSING SERVICES, INC., IN THE JURISDICTION
20 GRANTING THE TAX CREDIT.
21 (3) THE GOVERNING BODY OF WICOMICO COUNTY OR THE
22 GOVERNING BODY OF A MUNICIPAL CORPORATION IN WICOMICO COUNTY MAY
23 PROVIDE, BY LAW, FOR:
24 (I) THE AMOUNT, TERMS, SCOPE, AND DURATION OF THE TAX
25 CREDIT UNDER THIS SUBSECTION; AND
26 (II) ANY OTHER PROVISION NECESSARY TO CARRY OUT THIS
27 SUBSECTION.
28 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect June
29 1, 2026, and shall be applicable to all taxable years beginning after June 30, 2026.
