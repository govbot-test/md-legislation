1 AN ACT concerning
2 Vehicle Laws – Required Security – Exemption of Farm Area Motor Vehicles
3 FOR the purpose of exempting farm area motor vehicles from the requirement for a motor
4 vehicle to carry required security; and generally relating to farm area motor vehicles.

6 Article – Transportation

9 (2020 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)

12 Section 17–102

14 (2020 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
15 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
16 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
17 Article – Transportation
18 13–935.
19 (a) (1) In this section the following words have the meanings indicated.
20 (2) “Farm area motor vehicle” means a motor vehicle owned by a farmer
21 and operated only on a farm or on a highway within a 25–mile radius of the farm.
