WES MOORE, Governor Ch. 724
Chapter 724
AN ACT concerning
Higher Education – Workforce Pell Grant Program – Implementation
FOR the purpose of implementing the federal Workforce Pell Grant Program in the State;
requiring the Governor, after consultation with certain entities, to determine
whether to approve certain short–term programs as eligible for Pell Grant funds
subject to certain requirements; requiring a certain registered apprenticeship
program to include certain components in the curriculum; prohibiting institutions of
postsecondary education from using certain funds subject to a certain exclusion;
prohibiting institutions of postsecondary education from operating short–term
programs in a certain manner; and generally relating to the Workforce Pell Grant
Program.
BY repealing and reenacting, without amendments,
Article – Education

SHORT–TERM PROGRAM MET ALL REQUIREMENTS OF THIS SECTION;
(II) DOCUMENTATION THAT THE SHORT–TERM PROGRAM HAS A
COMPLETION RATE OF AT LEAST 70% WITHIN 150% OF THE NORMAL TIME FOR

(III) DOCUMENTATION THAT THE SHORT–TERM PROGRAM HAS A
JOB PLACEMENT RATE OF AT LEAST 70% AFTER 180 DAYS OF COMPLETION FOR
EACH FEDERAL AID AWARD YEAR;
(IV) DOCUMENTATION THAT THE TUITION AND FEES FOR THE
SHORT–TERM PROGRAM ARE IN COMPLIANCE WITH THE REQUIREMENTS OF THE
PROGRAM; AND
(V) ANY INFORMATION THE GOVERNOR’S WORKFORCE
DEVELOPMENT BOARD OR THE COMMISSION REQUESTS TO VERIFY COMPLIANCE
WITH THIS SECTION AND THE PROGRAM.
(4) THE GOVERNOR’S WORKFORCE DEVELOPMENT BOARD, THE

GENERAL SUPERVISION OF THE FACULTY.
