A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Maryland Office of the Inspector General – Establishment
3 FOR the purpose of establishing the Maryland Office of the Inspector General; transferring
4 the Maryland Office of the Inspector General for Education and the Maryland Office
5 of the Inspector General for Health to be within the Maryland Office of the Inspector
6 General; providing for the appointment, qualifications, salary, and term of office of
7 the Inspector General of the State; providing for the employment of certain staff by
8 the Inspector General; establishing provisions related to the powers and duties of
9 the Inspector General; requiring certain units to cooperate fully with the Inspector
10 General in certain investigations, audits, and reviews; protecting certain records
11 prepared or obtained by the Inspector General from disclosure under the Public
12 Information Act; extending certain whistleblower protections to certain State
13 employees who make a complaint or provide information to the Inspector General or
14 staff of the Office; authorizing the Governor to transfer certain positions and funds
15 to the Office; providing certain protections for employees who are transferred to the
16 Office; and generally relating to the Maryland Office of the Inspector General.
