1 AN ACT concerning
2 Health – Dementia Services and Brain Health Program and Clinical Provider
3 Resource Toolkit
4 FOR the purpose of altering the duties of the Virginia I. Jones Alzheimer’s Disease and
5 Related Dementias Council; establishing the Dementia Services and Brain Health
6 Program in the Maryland Department of Health to lead the State’s public health
7 efforts relating to brain health and dementia; requiring the Director of Dementia
8 Services and Brain Health to administer the Program; requiring the Program, rather
9 than the Director, to perform certain duties and staff the Council; requiring the
10 Program to oversee implementation of a certain State Plan and support and promote
11 the delivery of dementia–capable care; requiring the Maryland Department of
12 Health, in partnership with the Department of Aging, the Council, and other entities,
13 to develop a clinical provider resource toolkit for dementia care for health care
14 providers; and generally relating to Alzheimer’s disease and related dementias and
15 dementia services.
