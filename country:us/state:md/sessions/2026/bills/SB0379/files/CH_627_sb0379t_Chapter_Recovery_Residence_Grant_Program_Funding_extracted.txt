Section 1:
WES MOORE, Governor Ch. 627
Chapter 627
AN ACT concerning
Recovery Residence Grant Program – Funding
FOR the purpose of extending through a certain fiscal year the requirement that a certain
appropriation be included in the annual budget bill for the Recovery Residence Grant
Program; and generally relating to funding for the Recovery Residence Grant
Program.
BY repealing and reenacting, without amendments,
Article – Health – General

Section 2:
Section 19–2505(a) and (b)
Annotated Code of Maryland
(2023 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
Article – Health – General

Section 3:
Section 19–2505(g)
Annotated Code of Maryland
(2023 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)

Section 4:
SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
Article – Health – General
19–2505.
(a) In this section, “Program” means the Recovery Residence Grant Program.
(b) (1) There is a Recovery Residence Grant Program in the Department.
(2) The purpose of the Program is to provide grants to recovery residences
in the State in accordance with this section.
(g) For each of fiscal years 2024 through [2027] 2030, the annual budget bill shall
include an appropriation of $500,000 for the Program.

Section 5:
SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect July
1, 2026.
Approved by the Governor, May 26, 2026.
