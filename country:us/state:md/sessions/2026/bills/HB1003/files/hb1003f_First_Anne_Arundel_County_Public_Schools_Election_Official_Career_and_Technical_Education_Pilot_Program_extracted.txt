HOUSE BILL 1003
F1 6lr1950
CF SB 408
By: Delegates D. Jones, Bagnall, Bartlett, Behler, Chang, Pruski, and Schmidt
Introduced and read first time: February 6, 2026
Assigned to: Ways and Means
A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Anne Arundel County Public Schools – Election Official Career and Technical
3 Education Pilot Program
4 FOR the purpose of requiring the Anne Arundel County Board of Education and the State
5 Board of Elections, in consultation with the State Department of Education, to
6 establish a pilot program in the county schools to prepare students for a career as an
7 election official; and generally relating to career and technical education in Anne
8 Arundel County Public Schools.
9 BY adding to
10 Article – Education
11 Section 21–211
12 Annotated Code of Maryland
13 (2022 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
14 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
15 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
16 Article – Education
17 21–211.
18 (A) THIS SECTION APPLIES ONLY IN ANNE ARUNDEL COUNTY.
19 (B) THE COUNTY BOARD AND THE STATE BOARD OF ELECTIONS, IN
20 CONSULTATION WITH THE DEPARTMENT, SHALL DEVELOP A CAREER AND
21 TECHNICAL EDUCATION PILOT PROGRAM IN THE COUNTY SCHOOLS THAT PREPARES
22 STUDENTS FOR A CAREER AS AN ELECTION OFFICIAL.
