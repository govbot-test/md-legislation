A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Agriculture – Controlled–Environment and Hydroponic Agriculture
3 FOR the purpose of authorizing grants to controlled–environment or hydroponic farmers
4 under the Urban Agriculture Water and Power Infrastructure Grant Fund; requiring
5 the Department of Agriculture to establish a certain one–stop portal as a centralized
6 source of certain information about the permitting and licensing of
7 controlled–environment or hydroponic agriculture in the State; establishing the
8 Maryland Controlled–Environment and Hydroponic Agriculture Program Fund as a
9 special, nonlapsing fund to provide grants for certain costs of establishing,
10 expanding, and maintaining a controlled–environment or hydroponic agriculture
11 facility for a certain number of pilot programs; establishing the
12 Controlled–Environment and Hydroponic Agriculture Workforce Training Initiative
13 under the Partnership for Workforce Quality Program to provide training assistance
14 to community colleges in the State for certain purposes; requiring certain legislative
15 bodies to adopt zoning regulations that provide flexibility to allow the incorporation
16 of controlled–environment and hydroponic agriculture in urban areas; establishing
17 certain overall percentage goals for procurement contracts for food from certified
18 local farms that use controlled–environment or hydroponic agriculture; making
19 permanent and increasing the General Fund appropriation to the Urban Agriculture
20 Water and Power Infrastructure Grant Fund that the Governor is required to include
21 in the annual budget bill; and generally relating to controlled–environment and
22 hydroponic agriculture.
