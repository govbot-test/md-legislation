A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Alcoholic Beverages – Class L Beer, Wine, and Liquor License – Established
3 FOR the purpose of establishing a Class L beer, wine, and liquor license; authorizing a local
4 licensing board to issue the license to a holder of a manufacturer’s license; specifying
5 that the license authorizes the license holder to sell or provide samples of certain
6 beer, wine, and liquor for on–premises consumption under certain circumstances;
7 and generally relating to alcoholic beverages licenses.
8 BY adding to
9 Article – Alcoholic Beverages and Cannabis
10 Section 2–220
11 Annotated Code of Maryland
12 (2024 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
13 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
14 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
15 Article – Alcoholic Beverages and Cannabis
16 2–220.
17 (A) THERE IS A CLASS L BEER, WINE, AND LIQUOR LICENSE.
18 (B) A LOCAL LICENSING BOARD MAY ISSUE THE LICENSE TO THE HOLDER
19 OF A MANUFACTURER’S LICENSE.
20 (C) THE LICENSE AUTHORIZES THE LICENSE HOLDER, FOR ON–PREMISES
21 CONSUMPTION, TO:
