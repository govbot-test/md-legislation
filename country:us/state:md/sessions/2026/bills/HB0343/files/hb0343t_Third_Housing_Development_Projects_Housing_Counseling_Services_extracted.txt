CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Housing Development Projects – Housing Counseling Services
3 FOR the purpose of requiring a recipient of certain funding from the Department of
4 Housing and Community Development to offer provide information about certain
5 housing counseling services to certain prospective residents; requiring the Governor
6 to appropriate funds in certain years for community development organizations to
7 partner with certain housing counseling agencies; and generally relating to housing
8 counseling services.
9 BY adding to
10 Article – Housing and Community Development
11 Section 2–304
12 Annotated Code of Maryland
13 (2019 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
14 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
15 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
16 Article – Housing and Community Development
17 2–304.
18 (A) (1) IN THIS SECTION THE FOLLOWING WORDS HAVE THE MEANINGS
19 INDICATED.
EXPLANATION: CAPITALS INDICATE MATTER ADDED TO EXISTING LAW.
[Brackets] indicate matter deleted from existing law.
Underlining indicates amendments to bill.
Strike out indicates matter stricken from the bill by amendment or deleted from the law by
amendment. *hb0343*
2 HOUSE BILL 343
1 (2) “APPROVED HOUSING COUNSELING AGENCY” MEANS AN
2 ORGANIZATION THAT IS APPROVED BY THE U.S. DEPARTMENT OF HOUSING AND
3 URBAN DEVELOPMENT TO PROVIDE HOUSING COUNSELING SERVICES DIRECTLY OR
4 THROUGH AFFILIATES OR BRANCHES.
5 (3) “COMMUNITY DEVELOPMENT ORGANIZATION” MEANS AN ENTITY
6 THAT MEETS THE QUALIFICATIONS OF § 6–203 OF THIS ARTICLE.
7 (4) “HOUSING COUNSELING” MEANS COUNSELOR–TO–CLIENT
8 ASSISTANCE THAT ADDRESSES UNIQUE FINANCIAL CIRCUMSTANCES OR HOUSING
9 ISSUES AND FOCUSES ON WAYS OF OVERCOMING SPECIFIC OBSTACLES TO
10 ACHIEVING A HOUSING GOAL.
11 (B) AS A CONDITION ON THE RECEIPT OF FUNDING FROM THE
12 DEPARTMENT FROM THE STRATEGIC DEMOLITION FUND OR THE BALTIMORE
13 VACANTS REINVESTMENT INITIATIVE FOR ANY PROJECT TO CONSTRUCT, ACQUIRE,
14 OR REHABILITATE RESIDENTIAL PROPERTY FOR USE AS HOUSING SINGLE–FAMILY
15 HOMEOWNERSHIP, A FUNDING RECIPIENT SHALL AGREE TO OFFER PROVIDE
16 INFORMATION ABOUT THE HOUSING COUNSELING SERVICES THAT ARE AVAILABLE
17 THROUGH THE U.S. DEPARTMENT OF HOUSING AND URBAN DEVELOPMENT TO
18 PROSPECTIVE RESIDENTS BEFORE THE EXECUTION OF A CONTRACT TO PURCHASE
19 THE PROPERTY.
20 (C) HOUSING COUNSELING SERVICES OFFERED UNDER SUBSECTION (B) OF
21 THIS SECTION SHALL BE PROVIDED BY HOUSING COUNSELORS CERTIFIED BY THE
22 U.S. DEPARTMENT OF HOUSING AND URBAN DEVELOPMENT AND EMPLOYED BY AN
23 APPROVED HOUSING COUNSELING AGENCY.
24 (D) IN FISCAL YEAR 2028 AND EACH FISCAL YEAR THEREAFTER, THE
25 GOVERNOR SHALL APPROPRIATE $200,000 $90,000 TO BE USED BY COMMUNITY
26 DEVELOPMENT ORGANIZATIONS TO PARTNER WITH APPROVED HOUSING
27 COUNSELING AGENCIES PROVIDING HOUSING COUNSELING SERVICES UNDER THIS
28 SECTION.
29 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect July
30 1, 2026.
