CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Consumer Protection – Retail Transactions for Essential Consumer Goods –
3 Cash Payments
4 FOR the purpose of prohibiting a merchant, in a certain transaction, from prohibiting a
5 person from making a cash payment to purchase a an essential consumer good or
6 service, requiring a person to purchase a an essential consumer good or service by
7 using a credit or debit card, or charging or collecting from a person a fee higher price
8 than for an equivalent cashless transaction for making a cash payment for the
9 purchase of a an essential consumer good or service, subject to certain exceptions;
10 establishing that a violation of this Act is an unfair, abusive, or deceptive trade
11 practice subject to certain enforcement and penalty provisions under the Maryland
12 Consumer Protection Act; and generally relating to retail cash transactions for
13 essential consumer goods.
