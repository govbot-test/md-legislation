A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Education – Minimum Wage for Education Support Professionals
3 FOR the purpose of establishing a certain minimum wage rate to be paid by county boards
4 of education to certain education support professionals beginning on or before a
5 certain date; and generally relating to a minimum wage for education support
6 professionals.
7 BY adding to
8 Article – Education
9 Section 6–306 to be under the amended subtitle “Subtitle 3. Salaries and Wages”
10 Annotated Code of Maryland
11 (2025 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
12 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
13 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
14 Article – Education
15 Subtitle 3. Salaries AND WAGES.
16 6–306.
17 (A) (1) IN THIS SECTION THE FOLLOWING WORDS HAVE THE MEANINGS
18 INDICATED.
19 (2) “EDUCATION SUPPORT PROFESSIONAL” MEANS A
20 NONCERTIFICATED PUBLIC SCHOOL EMPLOYEE DESIGNATED AS PART OF A
21 NONSUPERVISORY BARGAINING UNIT UNDER SUBTITLE 5 OF THIS TITLE.
