Assigned to: Health
A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Tissue Banks and Hospitals – Autologous and Directed Blood Donations
3 FOR the purpose of prohibiting tissue banks and hospitals from denying a patient a
4 physician–ordered autologous or directed blood donation under certain
5 circumstances; authorizing a patient or an individual authorized to make medical
6 decisions on a patient’s behalf to request an autologous or directed blood donation;
7 authorizing tissue banks and hospitals to decline a request for a physician–ordered
8 autologous or directed blood donation only under certain circumstances; and
9 generally relating to autologous and directed blood donations.
