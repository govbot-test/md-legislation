A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Maryland Aviation Administration and Local Governments – Transportation
3 Network Services – Prohibition on Taxes, Fees, and Charges
4 FOR the purpose of repealing certain provisions of law authorizing a county or municipal
5 corporation to impose certain assessments on transportation network services;
6 prohibiting a county or municipal corporation from imposing an assessment, a tax, a
7 fee, or a charge on transportation network services; repealing the Transportation
8 Network Assessment Fund; applying certain provisions of law to the administration
9 and enforcement of transportation network company impact fees; requiring the
10 Comptroller to enforce provisions of law regarding transportation network company
11 impact fees; prohibiting the Maryland Aviation Administration from fixing any
12 charges, rentals, or fees on the privilege of providing transportation network services
13 at an airport or a facility; prohibiting certain political subdivisions from fixing any
14 charges, rentals, or fees for the use of an airport or a facility to provide transportation
15 network services; and generally relating to taxes, fees, and other charges on
16 transportation network services.
17 BY repealing and reenacting, without amendments,
