A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Estates and Trusts – Guardianship of the Person of a Disabled Person –
3 Emergency and Temporary Guardianship Petitions
4 FOR the purpose of requiring an order for guardianship of the person of a disabled person
5 to allow for certain visitation; authorizing an interested person to file a certain
6 petition for guardianship of the person of a disabled person on the grounds that the
7 disabled adult’s current guardian is medically or physically neglecting the adult;
8 requiring a court to hear certain petitions within a certain amount of time;
9 authorizing an interested person to file a certain petition to appoint a temporary
10 guardian of a disabled person; and generally relating to petitions to appoint a
11 guardian of a disabled person.
12 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
13 Article – Estates and Trusts
14 Section 13–705, 13–708, and 13–709
15 Annotated Code of Maryland
16 (2022 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
