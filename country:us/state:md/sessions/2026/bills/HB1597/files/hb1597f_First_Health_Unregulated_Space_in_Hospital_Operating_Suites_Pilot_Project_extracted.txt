A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Health – Unregulated Space in Hospital Operating Suites – Pilot Project
3 FOR the purpose of establishing an unregulated space in hospital operating suites pilot
4 project; requiring the Health Services Cost Review Commission to administer the
5 pilot project; requiring the Commission to develop certain criteria and standards for
6 the pilot project; requiring certain ambulatory surgical centers and freestanding
7 surgical centers to provide the Commission with certain information; and generally
8 relating to the unregulated space in hospital operating suites pilot project.
9 BY adding to
10 Article – Health – General
11 Section 19–209
12 Annotated Code of Maryland
13 (2023 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
14 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
15 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
16 Article – Health – General
17 19–209.
18 (A) THERE IS AN UNREGULATED SPACE IN HOSPITAL OPERATING SUITES
19 PILOT PROJECT.
20 (B) THE PILOT PROJECT SHALL BE ADMINISTERED BY THE COMMISSION.
21 (C) (1) THE COMMISSION IS AUTHORIZED TO ALLOW UP TO FIVE
22 HOSPITALS TO PARTICIPATE IN THE PILOT PROJECT.
