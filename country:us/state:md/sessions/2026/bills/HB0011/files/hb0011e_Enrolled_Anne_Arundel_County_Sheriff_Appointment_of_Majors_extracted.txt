2 HOUSE BILL 11
1 Section 2–315(c)
2 Annotated Code of Maryland
3 (2020 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
4 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
5 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
6 Article – Courts and Judicial Proceedings
7 2–315.
8 (a) This section applies only in Anne Arundel County.
9 (c) (1) The Sheriff shall appoint deputies at a salary as provided by the County
10 Council of Anne Arundel County.
11 (2) The Sheriff may appoint a chief deputy who shall serve at the pleasure
12 of the Sheriff.
13 (3) THE SHERIFF SHALL MAY APPOINT THREE MAJORS WHO SHALL
14 SERVE AT THE PLEASURE OF THE SHERIFF AT A SALARY AS PROVIDED BY THE
15 COUNTY COUNCIL OF ANNE ARUNDEL COUNTY.
16 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
17 October 1, 2026.
