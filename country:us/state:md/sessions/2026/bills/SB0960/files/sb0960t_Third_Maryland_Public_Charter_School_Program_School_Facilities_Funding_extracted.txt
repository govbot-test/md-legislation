CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Maryland Public Charter School Program – School Facilities – Funding
3 FOR the purpose of requiring the Interagency Commission on School Construction to
4 distribute certain funds to public charter schools for the maintenance and operation
5 of capital improvements to public charter school facilities, debt service, or rent or
6 lease payments; requiring certain public charter schools to report to the Interagency
7 Commission on School Construction on the status of certain projects; and generally
8 relating to the funding of public charter school facilities in the Maryland Public
9 Charter School Program.
10 BY adding to
11 Article – Education
12 Section 9–109(c)
13 Annotated Code of Maryland
14 (2022 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
15 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,

2 SENATE BILL 960
1 (C) (1) IN THIS SUBSECTION, “PUBLIC CHARTER SCHOOL FACILITIES”
2 MEANS PREMISES THAT ARE:
3 (I) OWNED OR LEASED BY A PUBLIC CHARTER SCHOOL; OR
4 (II) OCCUPIED AND UTILIZED BY A PUBLIC CHARTER SCHOOL
5 TO PROVIDE A PROGRAM OF PRIMARY OR SECONDARY EDUCATION TO ITS
6 STUDENTS.
7 (2) (I) THE INTERAGENCY COMMISSION ON SCHOOL
8 CONSTRUCTION SHALL DISTRIBUTE FUNDS TO PUBLIC CHARTER SCHOOLS FOR THE
9 MAINTENANCE AND OPERATION OF IN ACCORDANCE WITH THIS SUBSECTION FOR
10 CAPITAL IMPROVEMENTS TO PUBLIC CHARTER SCHOOL FACILITIES, DEBT SERVICE,
11 OR RENT OR LEASE PAYMENTS.
12 (II) FUNDS DISBURSED UNDER SUBPARAGRAPH (I) OF THIS
13 PARAGRAPH SHALL BE PAID DIRECTLY TO THE PUBLIC CHARTER SCHOOL AND MAY
14 NOT BE PAID TO A LOCAL GOVERNING BODY OR COUNTY BOARD.
15 (3) IF A PUBLIC CHARTER SCHOOL IS LOCATED IN A BUILDING OWNED
16 BY THE LOCAL SCHOOL SYSTEM, THE INTERAGENCY COMMISSION ON SCHOOL
17 CONSTRUCTION MAY NOT DISTRIBUTE FUNDS TO THE PUBLIC CHARTER SCHOOL
18 FOR CAPITAL IMPROVEMENTS TO THE SCHOOL FACILITY, DEBT SERVICE, OR RENT
19 OR LEASE PAYMENTS UNDER THIS SUBSECTION IN FISCAL YEARS DURING WHICH
20 THE SCHOOL FACILITY IS UNDERGOING A PROJECT THAT IS RECEIVING STATE
21 FUNDING THROUGH THE CAPITAL IMPROVEMENT PROGRAM.
22 (4) IF THE TOTAL AMOUNT OF THE CAPITAL IMPROVEMENT
23 PROGRAM IN A FISCAL YEAR IS LESS THAN THE TOTAL AMOUNT IN THE PRIOR
24 FISCAL YEAR, THE AMOUNT OF FUNDS DISTRIBUTED TO PUBLIC CHARTER SCHOOLS
25 UNDER THIS SUBSECTION FOR THE FISCAL YEAR SHALL BE PROPORTIONATELY
26 REDUCED.
27 (5) THE INTERAGENCY COMMISSION ON SCHOOL CONSTRUCTION
28 MAY NOT USE FUNDS DISTRIBUTED TO A PUBLIC CHARTER SCHOOL UNDER THIS
29 SUBSECTION AS JUSTIFICATION TO REDUCE THE AMOUNT PROVIDED TO A LOCAL
30 SCHOOL SYSTEM THROUGH THE CAPITAL IMPROVEMENT PROGRAM.
31 (6) A PUBLIC CHARTER SCHOOL THAT RECEIVES FUNDS UNDER THIS
32 SUBSECTION DURING A FISCAL YEAR SHALL REPORT TO THE INTERAGENCY
33 COMMISSION ON SCHOOL CONSTRUCTION ON THE STATUS OF ANY PROJECT FOR
34 CAPITAL IMPROVEMENTS TO PUBLIC CHARTER SCHOOL FACILITIES, DEBT SERVICE,
35 OR RENT OR LEASE PAYMENTS BEFORE THE PUBLIC CHARTER SCHOOL MAY
36 RECEIVE FUNDS FOR THE FOLLOWING FISCAL YEAR.
