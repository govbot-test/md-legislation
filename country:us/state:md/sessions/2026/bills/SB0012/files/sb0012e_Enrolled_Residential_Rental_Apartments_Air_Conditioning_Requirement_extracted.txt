3 (A) (1) THIS SECTION APPLIES ONLY TO RESIDENTIAL RENTAL UNITS IN
4 APARTMENT BUILDINGS WITH FOUR 10 OR MORE INDIVIDUAL DWELLING UNITS.
5 (2) THIS SECTION DOES NOT APPLY TO A RESIDENTIAL RENTAL UNIT
6 THAT IS:
7 (I) LOCATED LOCATED ON PROPERTY LISTED ON THE
8 NATIONAL REGISTER OF HISTORIC PLACES;
9 (II) PART OF A HOUSING AUTHORITY OF BALTIMORE CITY
10 DEVELOPMENT BUILT BETWEEN 1940 AND 1950, INCLUSIVE; OR
11 (III) A ROWHOUSE PUBLIC HOUSING UNIT ACQUIRED AND
12 REHABILITATED BY THE HOUSING AUTHORITY OF BALTIMORE CITY BETWEEN 1960
13 AND 1997, INCLUSIVE.
14 (B) A LANDLORD SHALL PROVIDE AIR–CONDITIONING IN EACH
15 RESIDENTIAL UNIT IN ACCORDANCE WITH SUBSECTION (C) OF THIS SECTION AS
16 FOLLOWS:
17 (1) BEGINNING JUNE 1, 2026, FOR NEWLY CONSTRUCTED
18 RESIDENTIAL RENTAL UNITS; AND
19 (2) BEGINNING JUNE 1, 2026, FOR RESIDENTIAL RENTAL UNITS
20 WHERE, AT ANY TIME ON OR AFTER JANUARY 1, 2025:
21 (I) THE LANDLORD EQUIPPED THE UNIT WITH
22 AIR–CONDITIONING OR PROVIDED AIR–CONDITIONING; OR
23 (II) A LEASE REQUIRED THE LANDLORD TO PROVIDE
24 AIR–CONDITIONING FOR THE UNIT; AND
25 (3) BEGINNING OCTOBER 1, 2026, FOR RESIDENTIAL RENTAL UNITS
26 THAT UNDERGO RENOVATION THAT INCLUDES THE REPLACEMENT OR SUBSTANTIAL
27 UPGRADE OF ELECTRICAL SYSTEMS OR HEATING SYSTEMS.
28 (C) EXCEPT AS OTHERWISE NECESSARY FOR PURPOSES OF REASONABLE
29 MAINTENANCE AND REPAIR, EACH YEAR FROM JUNE 1 TO SEPTEMBER 30, BOTH
30 INCLUSIVE, A LANDLORD SHALL PROVIDE AIR–CONDITIONING IN EACH
31 RESIDENTIAL UNIT AS FOLLOWS:

SENATE BILL 12 3
1 (1) FOR AN AIR–CONDITIONING SYSTEM THAT IS NOT UNDER THE
2 CONTROL OF THE TENANT, THE LANDLORD SHALL MAINTAIN THE TEMPERATURE IN
3 THE UNIT AT NOT GREATER THAN 80 DEGREES FAHRENHEIT AT 3 FEET ABOVE THE
4 FLOOR LEVEL IN EACH HABITABLE SPACE IN THE UNIT; AND
5 (2) FOR AN AIR–CONDITIONING SYSTEM THAT IS UNDER THE
6 CONTROL OF THE TENANT, THE LANDLORD SHALL ENSURE THAT THE
7 AIR–CONDITIONING SYSTEM IS IN GOOD WORKING ORDER AND IS CAPABLE OF
8 MAINTAINING THE TEMPERATURE IN THE UNIT AT NOT GREATER THAN 80 DEGREES
9 FAHRENHEIT AT 3 FEET ABOVE THE FLOOR LEVEL IN EACH HABITABLE SPACE IN
10 THE UNIT.
11 (D) THIS SECTION SHALL PREEMPT AND SUPERSEDE A LOCAL LAW,
12 ORDINANCE, POLICY, OR REGULATION ENACTED BY A POLITICAL SUBDIVISION OR
13 AGENCY THAT IMPOSES AIR–CONDITIONING REQUIREMENTS INCONSISTENT WITH
14 THIS SECTION.
15 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall be construed to
16 apply only prospectively and may not be applied or interpreted to have any effect on or
17 application to any building construction or renovation for which the building permit is
18 issued before the effective date of this Act.
19 SECTION 3. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect June
20 1, 2026.
