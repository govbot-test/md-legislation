1 AN ACT concerning
2 Aquaculture – Placement of Shellfish, Bags, Nets, and Structures on Submerged
3 Aquatic Vegetation – Extension
4 FOR the purpose of extending the authorization of a leaseholder of certain aquaculture
5 leases, with prior written approval from the Department of Natural Resources, to
6 place shellfish, bags, nets, and structures on submerged aquatic vegetation, subject
7 to certain requirements; and generally relating to aquaculture leases and submerged
8 aquatic vegetation.
9 BY repealing and reenacting, without amendments,
10 Article – Natural Resources
11 Section 4–11A–10(c) and (c–1)
12 Annotated Code of Maryland
13 (2023 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
14 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
15 Chapter 238 of the Acts of the General Assembly of 2019, as amended by Chapter
16 113 of the Acts of the General Assembly of 2024
17 Section 3
18 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
19 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
20 Article – Natural Resources

2 HOUSE BILL 784
1 4–11A–10.
2 (c) A leaseholder may not:
3 (1) Place shellfish, bags, nets, or structures on submerged aquatic
4 vegetation without prior written approval from the Department;
5 (2) Plant or harvest shellfish within 500 yards of any stationary blind or
6 blind site that is occupied and being used for hunting migratory waterfowl;
7 (3) Sublease a lease;
8 (4) Transfer a lease without the approval of the Department;
9 (5) Harvest shellfish outside the hours established by the Department in
10 regulation; or
11 (6) Place unlawfully harvested oysters on a lease.
12 (c–1) In approving the placement of shellfish, bags, nets, or structures on
13 submerged aquatic vegetation under subsection (c)(1) of this section, the Department:
14 (1) May not authorize harvesting by dredge in areas where submerged
15 aquatic vegetation is present;
16 (2) Shall authorize for water column leases the placement of shellfish,
17 bags, nets, or structures in at least 10% of the area where submerged aquatic vegetation is
18 present; and
19 (3) Shall authorize harvest by diving in areas on any submerged land lease
20 where submerged aquatic vegetation is present.
21 Chapter 238 of the Acts of 2019, as amended by Chapter 113 of the Acts of 2024
22 SECTION 3. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect June
23 1, 2019. It shall remain effective for a period of [8] 11 years and 1 month and, at the end
24 of June 30, [2027] 2030, this Act, with no further action required by the General Assembly,
25 shall be abrogated and of no further force and effect.
26 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect June
27 1, 2026.
