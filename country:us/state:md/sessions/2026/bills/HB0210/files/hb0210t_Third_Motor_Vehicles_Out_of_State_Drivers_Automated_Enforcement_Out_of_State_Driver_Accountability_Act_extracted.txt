1 AN ACT concerning
2 Motor Vehicles – Out–of–State Drivers – Automated Enforcement
3 (Out–of–State Driver Accountability Act)
4 FOR the purpose of requiring each county and municipal automated traffic enforcement
5 program to submit to the Vision Zero coordinator a quarterly an annual report
6 containing certain information regarding each citation issued under the program
7 during the quarter to a motor vehicle registered in another state; and generally
8 relating to automated enforcement of the Maryland Vehicle Law against
9 out–of–state drivers.
