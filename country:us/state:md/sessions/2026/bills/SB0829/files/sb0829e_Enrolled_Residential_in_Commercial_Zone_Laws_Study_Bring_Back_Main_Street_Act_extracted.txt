6 FOR the purpose of requiring certain counties to allow multifamily developments and
7 mixed–use developments as a permitted use on certain parcels or lots; prohibiting

9 permitted developments; authorizing certain counties to require a permitted
10 development to have a certain percentage of available floor space dedicated to retail
11 uses and to include on–site parking; prohibiting a restriction on use from imposing
12 or acting to impose certain limitations on the development of a multifamily
13 development or mixed–use development; and generally relating to multifamily
