A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Land Use – Multifamily Developments and Mixed–Use Developments –
3 Authorization
4 (Bring Back Main Street Act)
5 FOR the purpose of requiring certain counties to allow multifamily developments and
6 mixed–use developments as a permitted use on certain parcels or lots; prohibiting
7 certain counties from imposing certain restrictions, requirements, or limitations on
8 permitted developments; authorizing certain counties to require a permitted
9 development to have a certain percentage of available floor space dedicated to retail
10 uses and to include on–site parking; prohibiting a restriction on use from imposing
11 or acting to impose certain limitations on the development of a multifamily
12 development or mixed–use development; and generally relating to multifamily
13 developments and mixed–use developments.
14 BY repealing and reenacting, without amendments,
15 Article – Land Use
16 Section 1–101(a) and (b) and 7–501(a), (b), (c), (d), and (i)
17 Annotated Code of Maryland
18 (2012 Volume and 2025 Supplement)
19 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
20 Article – Land Use
21 Section 1–401 and 10–103
22 Annotated Code of Maryland
23 (2012 Volume and 2025 Supplement)
24 BY adding to
25 Article – Land Use
26 Section 4–104(e)
27 Annotated Code of Maryland
28 (2012 Volume and 2025 Supplement)
