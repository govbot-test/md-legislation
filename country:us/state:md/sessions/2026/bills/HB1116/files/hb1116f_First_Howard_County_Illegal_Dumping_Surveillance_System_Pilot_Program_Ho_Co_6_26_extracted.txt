A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Howard County – Illegal Dumping – Surveillance System Pilot Program
3 Ho. Co. 6–26
4 FOR the purpose of authorizing a designated Howard County department to place certain
5 surveillance systems at certain dumping sites in Howard County; requiring the
6 District Court, in consultation with the designated Howard County department, to
7 prescribe a certain citation form; requiring the designated Howard County
8 department to mail a certain citation or warning to the owner of a motor vehicle
9 recorded by a certain surveillance system within a certain time period; providing
10 that a person who receives a certain citation in Howard County may pay a certain
11 civil penalty or elect to stand trial in the District Court; establishing certain defenses
12 to a certain violation in Howard County; prohibiting the Motor Vehicle
13 Administration from registering or transferring the registration of any vehicle
14 captured by a certain surveillance system in Howard County under certain
15 circumstances; and generally relating to the use of surveillance systems at dumping
16 sites in Howard County.
