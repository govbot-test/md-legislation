A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Health – Opioid Overdose Reversal Drugs – Possession, Storage, and
3 Administration Past Expiration Date
4 FOR the purpose of altering certain provisions of law relating to the possession, storage,
5 and administration of an opioid overdose reversal drug to allow for the possession,
6 storage, or administration of an expired opioid overdose reversal drug within a
7 certain amount of time after the expiration date; and generally relating to opioid
8 overdose reversal drugs.
