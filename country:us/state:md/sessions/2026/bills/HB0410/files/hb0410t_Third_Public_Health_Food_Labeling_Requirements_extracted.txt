CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Public Health – Food and Milk Product Labeling – Requirements
3 FOR the purpose of establishing requirements and prohibitions regarding food and milk
4 product labeling; requiring the Maryland Department of Health, in conjunction with
5 the University of Maryland Extension and food donation organizations, to create a
6 marketing program and educational materials for food labels on or before a certain
7 date and to post the information on the Department’s website; and generally relating
8 to food and milk product labeling requirements.
