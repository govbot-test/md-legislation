A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Economic Development – Rural Readiness Program and Rural Maryland
3 Capacity Building Fund – Establishment
4 FOR the purpose of establishing the Rural Readiness Program administered by the Rural
5 Maryland Council to provide assistance to rural communities to enhance their
6 capacity for economic development; requiring priority to be given when making
7 certain grants to entities that have completed the Rural Readiness Program;
8 establishing the Rural Maryland Capacity Building Fund as a special, nonlapsing
9 fund to be administered by the Council to provide grants to support certain planning
10 and capacity building initiatives; and generally relating to rural communities.

22 (4) RURAL COMMUNITIES EXPERIENCE CHALLENGES IN SOCIAL
23 COHESION AND NETWORKS THAT MAKE IT HARD TO MOBILIZE RESOURCES AND
24 COLLECTIVELY ADDRESS ISSUES;

29 WILL MANIFEST AS DISPARITIES IN HEALTH CARE ACCESS, EDUCATIONAL
30 ATTAINMENT, ECONOMIC OPPORTUNITIES, AND INFRASTRUCTURE DEVELOPMENT.
