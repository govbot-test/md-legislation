CHAPTER ____
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Public Schools – Individuals With Disabilities – Accessibility and Emergency
3 Planning
4 FOR the purpose of requiring each local school system to identify, in its regular safety
5 evaluation, issues of accessibility for individuals with disabilities; requiring local
6 school systems to include in a certain annual report instances in which a public
7 school facility became inaccessible for a student with a disability in a manner that
8 could impede evacuation or an emergency response; requiring a certain annual
9 report to be confidential and limiting disclosure of the report to certain individuals;
10 and generally relating to accessibility and emergency planning for individuals with
11 disabilities.

3 Article – Education
4 7–1510.
5 (a) On or before June 15, 2019, and regularly thereafter, each local school system
6 shall conduct a safety evaluation of each public school under the local school system’s
7 jurisdiction to:
8 (1) Identify and, if necessary, develop solutions for physical safety
9 concerns, including issues with building security AND ASSOCIATED ISSUES OF
10 ACCESSIBILITY FOR INDIVIDUALS WITH DISABILITIES; and
11 (2) Identify and evaluate any patterns of safety concerns on school property
12 or at school–sponsored events.
13 (b) In performing the safety evaluations, each designated safety coordinator shall:
14 (1) Consult with the Center for guidance;
15 (2) Coordinate with the Interagency Commission on School Construction’s
16 facility assessment process, established under § 5–310 of this article, in identifying issues
17 with public school facilities that could impact school safety; and
18 (3) Submit a summary of the completed safety evaluations to the Center.
19 (d) On or before August 1, 2020, and regularly thereafter, each local school system
20 shall update the school emergency plan for each public school in the school system’s
21 jurisdiction to:
22 (1) Include detailed plans for the manner in which each public school will
23 address:
24 (i) Behavioral threats;
25 (ii) Emergency events; and
26 (iii) Accommodations for students with disabilities in emergency
27 events;
28 (2) Conform with the Emergency Planning Guidelines updated under
29 subsection (c) of this section; and
30 (3) Incorporate any changes required under subsection (f) of this section.
