1 AN ACT concerning
2 Education – Purple Star Schools Program and Purple Star Colleges Program
3 FOR the purpose of altering the definition of “military–connected student” under the Purple
4 Star Schools Program to conform with certain terms defined under State law;
5 establishing the Purple Star Colleges Program to recognize certain colleges that
6 provide strong services and support for military–connected students; and generally
7 relating to the establishment of the Purple Star Colleges Program.

2 11–2001.
3 (A) IN THIS SUBTITLE THE FOLLOWING WORDS HAVE THE MEANINGS
4 INDICATED.
5 (B) “COLLEGE” MEANS:
6 (1) AN INSTITUTION OF HIGHER EDUCATION; OR

8 (C) “MILITARY–CONNECTED STUDENT” MEANS A STUDENT WHO IS:

10 (2) A VETERAN; OR
11 (3) A SPOUSE OR DEPENDENT OF AN INDIVIDUAL DESCRIBED IN ITEM

13 (D) “PROGRAM” MEANS THE PURPLE STAR COLLEGES PROGRAM.
14 11–2002.
15 (A) THERE IS A PURPLE STAR COLLEGES PROGRAM.
16 (B) THE PURPOSE OF THE PROGRAM IS TO RECOGNIZE COLLEGES THAT
17 PROVIDE STRONG SERVICES AND SUPPORT FOR MILITARY–CONNECTED STUDENTS.
18 (C) THE COMMISSION SHALL ADMINISTER THE PROGRAM.
19 11–2003.
20 (A) SUBJECT TO SUBSECTION (B) OF THIS SECTION, THE COMMISSION
21 SHALL DESIGNATE A COLLEGE AS A PURPLE STAR COLLEGE IF THE COLLEGE
22 APPLIES AND MEETS THE CRITERIA ADOPTED BY THE COMMISSION FOR A PURPLE
23 STAR COLLEGE.
24 (B) AT A MINIMUM, THE COMMISSION’S CRITERIA FOR A PURPLE STAR
25 COLLEGE SHALL REQUIRE A COLLEGE TO:

4 SENATE BILL 709
1 (1) DESIGNATE A STAFF MEMBER AS A UNIFORMED SERVICES
2 LIAISON, WHOSE DUTIES SHALL INCLUDE:
3 (I) IDENTIFYING MILITARY–CONNECTED STUDENTS WHO ARE
4 NEWLY ENROLLED AT THE COLLEGE;
5 (II) SERVING AS THE POINT OF CONTACT BETWEEN THE
6 COLLEGE AND NEWLY ENROLLED MILITARY–CONNECTED STUDENTS;

9 (IV) ASSISTING IN COORDINATING COLLEGE PROGRAMS FOR

13 (2) ESTABLISH ON THE COLLEGE’S WEBSITE AN EASILY ACCESSIBLE

18 (II) ACADEMIC PLANNING, COURSE SEQUENCES, AND

26 ISSUES RELATED TO MILITARY–CONNECTED STUDENTS TRANSFERRING TO NEW
