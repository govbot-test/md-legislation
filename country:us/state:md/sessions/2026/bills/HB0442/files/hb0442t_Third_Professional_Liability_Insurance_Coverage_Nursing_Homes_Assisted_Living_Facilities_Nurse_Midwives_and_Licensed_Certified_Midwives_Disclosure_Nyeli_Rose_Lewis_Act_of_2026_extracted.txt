CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Professional Liability Insurance Coverage – Nursing Homes, Assisted Living
3 Facilities, and Nurse Midwives, and Licensed Certified Midwives – Disclosure
4 (Nyeli Rose Lewis Act of 2026)
5 FOR the purpose of requiring nursing homes, assisted living programs, and nurse
6 midwives, and licensed certified midwives to notify certain individuals at certain
7 times if the nursing home, assisted living program, or nurse midwife, or licensed
8 certified midwife either does not maintain professional liability insurance or
9 professional liability insurance coverage has lapsed and has not been renewed; and
10 generally relating to professional liability insurance disclosures.
11 BY repealing and reenacting, without amendments,
12 Article – Health – General
13 Section 19–1401(a) and (e) and 19–1801

15 (2023 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)

20 (2023 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)

19 19–1401.
20 (a) In this subtitle the following words have the meanings indicated.
21 (e) (1) “Nursing home” means a facility that offers nonacute inpatient care to
22 patients suffering from a disease, chronic illness, condition, disability of advanced age, or
23 terminal disease requiring maximal nursing care without continuous hospital services and
24 who require medical services and nursing services rendered by or under the supervision of
25 a licensed nurse together with convalescent, restorative, or rehabilitative services.
26 (2) “Nursing home” does not mean a facility offering domiciliary care or
27 personal care as those terms are defined in § 19–301 of this title.
28 19–1417.
29 (A) EACH NURSING HOME IN THE STATE SHALL NOTIFY EACH RESIDENT
30 AND PROSPECTIVE RESIDENT IN WRITING OR BY WRITTEN ELECTRONIC
31 COMMUNICATION IF:
32 (1) THE NURSING HOME DOES NOT MAINTAIN PROFESSIONAL
33 LIABILITY INSURANCE COVERAGE; OR
HOUSE BILL 442 3
1 (2) THE NURSING HOME’S PROFESSIONAL LIABILITY INSURANCE
2 COVERAGE HAS LAPSED FOR ANY PERIOD OF TIME AND HAS NOT BEEN RENEWED.
3 (B) THE NOTIFICATION UNDER SUBSECTION (A) OF THIS SECTION SHALL
4 BE:
5 (1) (I) PROVIDED TO A PROSPECTIVE RESIDENT:
6 1. AT THE FIRST VISIT BY THE PROSPECTIVE RESIDENT
7 DURING ANY PERIOD DURING WHICH THE NURSING HOME DOES NOT MAINTAIN
8 PROFESSIONAL LIABILITY COVERAGE; AND
9 2. IF THE NURSING HOME DOES NOT MAINTAIN
10 PROFESSIONAL LIABILITY INSURANCE AT THE TIME THE PROSPECTIVE RESIDENT
11 APPLIES FOR ADMISSION, AT THE TIME OF APPLICATION; AND
12 (II) IF THE PROFESSIONAL LIABILITY INSURANCE LAPSES,
13 PROVIDED TO EACH RESIDENT WITHIN 30 DAYS AFTER THE DATE THE COVERAGE
14 LAPSED;
15 (2) SIGNED BY:
16 (I) THE PROSPECTIVE RESIDENT AT THE TIME OF
17 APPLICATION FOR ADMISSION; OR
18 (II) THE RESIDENT AT THE TIME THE NOTIFICATION OF THE
19 LAPSE IN COVERAGE IS PROVIDED UNDER ITEM (1)(II) OF THIS SUBSECTION; AND
20 (3) RETAINED BY THE NURSING HOME AS PART OF THE NURSING
21 HOME’S RECORDS.
22 (C) EACH NURSING HOME OPERATING IN THE STATE THAT DOES NOT
23 MAINTAIN PROFESSIONAL LIABILITY INSURANCE COVERAGE SHALL POST IN A
24 CONSPICUOUS PLACE VISIBLE TO RESIDENTS AND GUESTS THAT THE NURSING
25 HOME DOES NOT MAINTAIN PROFESSIONAL LIABILITY COVERAGE.
