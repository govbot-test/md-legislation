A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Public Schools – School Security Personnel – Immigration Investigation and
3 Enforcement
4 FOR the purpose of prohibiting public school security personnel from engaging in certain
5 federal immigration investigation and enforcement functions; prohibiting public
6 school security personnel from producing or sharing certain student or employee
7 information for purposes of federal immigration investigation and enforcement,
8 except under certain circumstances; and generally relating to federal immigration
9 investigation and enforcement in public schools.
