1 AN ACT concerning
2 Financial Institutions – Licensing of Affiliated Insurance Producer–Mortgage
3 Loan Originators – Alteration Alterations
4 FOR the purpose of altering the licensing requirements for an affiliated insurance
5 producer–mortgage loan originator; requiring the Commissioner of Financial
6 Regulation to approve a certain financial institution that meets certain criteria or
7 certain mortgage lender lenders that meets meet certain criteria as to sponsor an
8 affiliated insurance producer–mortgage loan originator under certain circumstances;
9 and generally relating to licensing of affiliated insurance producer–mortgage loan
10 originators.
