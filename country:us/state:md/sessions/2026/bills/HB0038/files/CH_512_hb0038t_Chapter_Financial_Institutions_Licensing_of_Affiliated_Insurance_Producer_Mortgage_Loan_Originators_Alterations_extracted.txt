WES MOORE, Governor Ch. 512
Chapter 512
(House Bill 38)
AN ACT concerning
Financial Institutions – Licensing of Affiliated Insurance Producer–Mortgage
Loan Originators – Alteration Alterations
FOR the purpose of altering the licensing requirements for an affiliated insurance
producer–mortgage loan originator; requiring the Commissioner of Financial
Regulation to approve a certain financial institution that meets certain criteria or
certain mortgage lender lenders that meets meet certain criteria as to sponsor an
affiliated insurance producer–mortgage loan originator under certain circumstances;
and generally relating to licensing of affiliated insurance producer–mortgage loan
originators.
BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
Article – Financial Institutions
Section 11–603.1 and 11–619
Annotated Code of Maryland
(2020 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)

(a) Each mortgage loan originator shall be covered by a surety bond in accordance
