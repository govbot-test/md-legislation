CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Health Occupations, Public Health – Perimenopausal, Menopausal, and
3 Postmenopausal Conditions, and Insurance – Menopause – Provider Training
4 Coverage Requirements, Policy Initiatives, and Access to Care
5 FOR the purpose of altering the membership of the State Advisory Council on Health and
6 Wellness; requiring certain health occupations boards that require a licensee
7 licensees or certificate holder holders to complete certain continuing education
8 requirements as a condition of renewal to grant at least a certain amount of
9 continuing education credits for completing continuing education on
10 perimenopausal, menopausal, and postmenopausal evaluation and treatment to
11 grant a certain number of hours for every hour of continuing education for
12 menopause and menopause–associated symptoms; authorizing certain health
13 occupations boards to establish a maximum number of certain continuing education
14 credits granted; requiring the Department to identify a certain training program in
15 consultation with certain professional associations; requiring certain insurers,
16 nonprofit health service plans, and health maintenance organizations to provide
17 coverage for the evaluation and management of menopause and
18 menopause–associated conditions; requiring the Maryland Health Care
19 Commission, the Maryland Commission for Women, Women and the Maryland
20 Department of Health to take certain actions related to perimenopausal,
21 menopausal, and postmenopausal conditions; and generally relating to
22 perimenopausal, menopausal, and postmenopausal conditions provider training,
23 health insurance coverage, policy initiatives, and access to care related to
24 menopause.

28 THIS SUBSECTION, THE DEPARTMENT SHALL CONSULT WITH PROFESSIONAL
29 ASSOCIATIONS WITH EXPERTISE IN MENOPAUSE AND MENOPAUSE–ASSOCIATED
30 SYMPTOMS, INCLUDING THE MENOPAUSE SOCIETY.

32 REGULATIONS TO CARRY OUT THIS SECTION, INCLUDING REGULATIONS
33 ESTABLISHING THE MAXIMUM NUMBER OF CONTINUING EDUCATION CREDITS THAT
34 MAY BE GRANTED UNDER THIS SECTION.
