A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Tobacco Products, Other Tobacco Products, and Electronic Smoking Devices –
3 Advertising to Minors – Prohibition
4 FOR the purpose of prohibiting a person who is licensed to sell tobacco products, other
5 tobacco products, or electronic smoking devices from directly or indirectly
6 advertising or marketing tobacco products, other tobacco products, electronic
7 smoking devices, or vaping liquid to individuals under a certain age; providing that
8 certain advertisements or promotions are a violation against the prohibition on
9 advertising or marketing to certain individuals; and generally relating to tobacco
10 products, other tobacco products, and electronic smoking devices.
11 BY repealing and reenacting, without amendments,
12 Article – Business Regulation
13 Section 16–3A–01(a) and (c), 16.5–101(a) and (j), 16.7–101(a), (c), and (l), and
14 16.7–214
