2 Anne Arundel County – Board of License Commissioners – Alterations
3 FOR the purpose of increasing the membership of the Board of License Commissioners for
4 Anne Arundel County; requiring the Governor to appoint members to the Board from
5 a list of individuals recommended by the Anne Arundel County delegations to the
6 General Assembly; altering the eligibility requirements for Board membership;
7 requiring the Board to adopt and publish a certain ethics policy; and generally
8 relating to the Board of License Commissioners for Anne Arundel County.
