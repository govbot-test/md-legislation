A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Estates and Trusts – Jurisdiction Over Property of Minors or Disabled Persons –
3 Authorized Transactions
4 FOR the purpose of authorizing a circuit court to authorize or direct a transaction that
5 grants access to financial records or establishes eligibility for government assistance
6 programs for a minor or disabled person under a certain circumstance; and generally
7 relating to assuming jurisdiction over the property of a minor or disabled person.
8 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
9 Article – Estates and Trusts
10 Section 13–204
11 Annotated Code of Maryland
12 (2022 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
13 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
14 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
15 Article – Estates and Trusts
16 13–204.
17 (a) (1) If a basis exists as described in § 13–201 of this subtitle for assuming
18 jurisdiction over the property of a minor or disabled person, the circuit court, without
19 appointing a guardian, may authorize or direct a transaction with respect to the property,
20 service, or care arrangement of the minor or disabled person.
21 (2) The transactions described under paragraph (1) of this subsection
22 include:
23 (i) Payment, delivery, deposit, or retention of funds or property;
