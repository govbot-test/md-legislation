A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Financial Institutions – Mortgage Servicers – Insurance Proceeds
3 FOR the purpose of requiring a mortgage servicer that receives certain insurance proceeds
4 to deposit the proceeds into a certain interest bearing account; requiring a borrower
5 to elect the use of the interest earnings on the insurance proceeds; requiring certain
6 interest earnings to begin accruing by a certain date; authorizing a mortgage servicer
7 to charge a fee for disbursement of interest earnings subject to certain limitations;
8 requiring certain methods and procedures for disbursement of insurance proceeds
9 and interest earnings; and generally relating to mortgage servicers.

1 (B) THIS SECTION APPLIES TO A MORTGAGE SERVICER THAT RECEIVES
2 INSURANCE PROCEEDS.
3 (C) (1) THE MORTGAGE SERVICER SHALL DEPOSIT THE INSURANCE
4 PROCEEDS INTO A LOSS DRAFT ACCOUNT IN A FEDERALLY INSURED BANKING
5 INSTITUTION WITH A SIMPLE INTEREST RATE OF AT LEAST 2% PER ANNUM.
6 (2) THE BORROWER SHALL ELECT TO HAVE THE INTEREST
7 EARNINGS:
8 (I) CREDITED TO THE LOSS DRAFT ACCOUNT; OR
9 (II) PAID DIRECTLY TO THE BORROWER.
10 (3) EXCEPT AS PROVIDED UNDER PARAGRAPH (4) OF THIS
11 SUBSECTION, INTEREST EARNINGS ON INSURANCE PROCEEDS IN A LOSS DRAFT
12 ACCOUNT SHALL ACCRUE ON DEPOSIT OF THE INSURANCE PROCEEDS.
13 (4) IF INSURANCE PROCEEDS HAVE BEEN DEPOSITED INTO A LOSS
14 DRAFT ACCOUNT ON OR BEFORE JULY 1, 2026, INTEREST EARNINGS SHALL ACCRUE
15 BEGINNING JULY 1, 2026.
16 (D) (1) SUBJECT TO PARAGRAPH (2) OF THIS SUBSECTION, A MORTGAGE
17 SERVICER MAY CHARGE THE BORROWER A FEE FOR THE DISBURSEMENT OF THE
18 INTEREST EARNINGS PAID DIRECTLY TO THE BORROWER BY WIRE TRANSFER.
19 (2) A MORTGAGE SERVICER MAY NOT CHARGE THE BORROWER A FEE:
20 (I) THAT IS UNREASONABLE;
21 (II) THAT WOULD HAVE THE EFFECT OF LOWERING THE
22 INTEREST EARNINGS TO AN AMOUNT THAT WOULD EQUATE TO AN INTEREST RATE
23 THAT IS LESS THAN 2%;
24 (III) FOR THE DISBURSEMENT OF THE INTEREST EARNINGS BY
25 DIRECT DEPOSIT; OR
26 (IV) THAT EXCEEDS THE ACTUAL COSTS INCURRED BY THE
27 MORTGAGE SERVICER FOR THE DISBURSEMENT.
28 (E) (1) DISBURSEMENT METHODS OF THE INSURANCE PROCEEDS OR THE
29 INTEREST EARNINGS SHALL INCLUDE AT LEAST TWO OF THE FOLLOWING:
