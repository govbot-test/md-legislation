HOUSE BILL 1042
J2 6lr3283
By: Delegate Woorman
Introduced and read first time: February 9, 2026
Assigned to: Health
A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Health Occupations – Structural Racism Training – Funding Sources
3 FOR the purpose of clarifying the intent of the General Assembly regarding the
4 development and offering of certain structural racism training; and generally
5 relating to structural racism training funding sources.
6 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
7 Chapter 478 of the Acts of the General Assembly of 2025
8 Section 3
9 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
10 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
11 Chapter 478 of the Acts of 2025
12 SECTION 3. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That it is the intent of the General
13 Assembly that the structural racism training required under this Act be developed and
14 offered AT NO COST TO THE STATE using funding from EXTERNAL SOURCES, SUCH AS
15 the grant provided to the Maryland Nurses Association from the American Nurses
16 Association to advance antiracism efforts.
17 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect June
18 1, 2026.
