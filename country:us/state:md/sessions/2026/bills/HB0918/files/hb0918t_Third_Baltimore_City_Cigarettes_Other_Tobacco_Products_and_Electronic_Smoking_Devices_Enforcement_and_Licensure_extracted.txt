CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Baltimore City – Cigarettes, Other Tobacco Products, and Electronic Smoking
3 Devices – Local Laws Authorization Enforcement and Licensure
4 FOR the purpose of authorizing Baltimore City to enact and enforce local laws a certain
5 enforcement officer to enforce provisions regulating the sale and distribution of
6 cigarettes, other tobacco products, and electronic smoking devices, subject to certain
7 exceptions; authorizing the Executive Director of the Alcohol, Tobacco, and Cannabis
8 Commission to reprimand a licensee or suspend or revoke a license for a violation
9 identified under this Act; requiring the Comptroller to distribute a certain amount
10 of the licensure authorization request fees collected under certain provisions of this
11 Act to the Family League of Baltimore City, Inc. in a certain manner; requiring an
12 applicant for a certain initial or renewal license to request a licensure authorization
13 from the Mayor and City Council of Baltimore City and pay a certain fee; and
14 generally relating to the regulation of cigarettes, other tobacco products, and
15 electronic smoking devices in Baltimore City.

19 Annotated Code of Maryland
