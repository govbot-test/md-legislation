2 HOUSE BILL 1056
1 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
2 That Section(s) 7–417 through 7–421 of Article – General Provisions of the Annotated Code
3 of Maryland be renumbered to be Section(s) 7–418 through 7–422, respectively.
4 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That the Laws of Maryland read
5 as follows:
6 Article – General Provisions
7 7–417.
8 THE GOVERNOR ANNUALLY SHALL PROCLAIM OCTOBER 1 AS PURPLE
9 LIGHTS NIGHT TO HONOR THE INDIVIDUALS IN THE STATE WHO ARE VICTIMS OR
10 SURVIVORS OF DOMESTIC VIOLENCE AND EXPRESS SUPPORT FOR THE ELIMINATION
11 OF DOMESTIC VIOLENCE IN THE STATE.
12 SECTION 3. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect July
13 1, 2026.
