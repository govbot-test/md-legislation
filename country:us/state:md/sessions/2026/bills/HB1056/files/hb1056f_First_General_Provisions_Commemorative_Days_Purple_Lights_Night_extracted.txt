A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 General Provisions – Commemorative Days – Purple Lights Night
3 FOR the purpose of requiring the Governor to annually proclaim a certain day as Purple
4 Lights Night; and generally relating to Purple Lights Night.
5 BY renumbering
6 Article – General Provisions
7 Section 7–417 through 7–421
8 to be Section 7–418 through 7–422, respectively
9 Annotated Code of Maryland
10 (2019 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
11 BY adding to
12 Article – General Provisions
13 Section 7–417
14 Annotated Code of Maryland
15 (2019 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
16 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
17 That Section(s) 7–417 through 7–421 of Article – General Provisions of the Annotated Code
18 of Maryland be renumbered to be Section(s) 7–418 through 7–422, respectively.
19 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That the Laws of Maryland read
20 as follows:
21 Article – General Provisions
EXPLANATION: CAPITALS INDICATE MATTER ADDED TO EXISTING LAW.
[Brackets] indicate matter deleted from exist in g l aw . *hb1056*
2 HOUSE BILL 1056
1 7–417.
2 THE GOVERNOR ANNUALLY SHALL PROCLAIM OCTOBER 1 AS PURPLE
3 LIGHTS NIGHT TO HONOR THE INDIVIDUALS IN THE STATE WHO ARE VICTIMS OR
4 SURVIVORS OF DOMESTIC VIOLENCE AND EXPRESS SUPPORT FOR THE ELIMINATION
5 OF DOMESTIC VIOLENCE IN THE STATE.
6 SECTION 3. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect July
7 1, 2026.
