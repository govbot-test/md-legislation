CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Maryland Medical Assistance Program – Registered Behavior Technicians –
3 Reimbursement
4 FOR the purpose of requiring the Maryland Department of Health to provide
5 reimbursement for applied behavior analysis services provided by registered
6 behavior technicians under the Maryland Medical Assistance Program; prohibiting,
7 subject to a certain exception, the Department from requiring as a condition of
8 reimbursement that an individual receive a certain certification to provide applied
9 behavior analysis services as a registered behavior technician to Program recipients;
10 and generally relating to the Maryland Medical Assistance Program and applied
11 behavior analysis services.
12 BY adding to
13 Article – Health – General
14 Section 15–114.2

3 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
4 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
5 Article – Health – General
6 15–114.2.
7 (A) (1) IN THIS SECTION THE FOLLOWING WORDS HAVE THE MEANINGS
8 INDICATED.
9 (2) (I) “APPLIED BEHAVIOR ANALYSIS” MEANS THE DESIGN,
10 IMPLEMENTATION, AND EVALUATION OF ENVIRONMENTAL MODIFICATIONS USING
11 BEHAVIORAL STIMULI AND CONSEQUENCES TO PRODUCE SOCIALLY SIGNIFICANT
12 IMPROVEMENTS IN HUMAN BEHAVIOR.
13 (II) “APPLIED BEHAVIOR ANALYSIS” INCLUDES THE DIRECT
14 OBSERVATIONS, MEASUREMENT, AND FUNCTIONAL ANALYSIS OF THE RELATIONS
15 BETWEEN ENVIRONMENT AND BEHAVIOR.
16 (3) “AUTISM SPECTRUM DISORDER” MEANS A GROUP OF
17 DEVELOPMENTAL DISORDERS:
18 (I) CHARACTERIZED BY PERSISTENT DEFICITS IN SOCIAL
19 COMMUNICATION AND SOCIAL INTERACTION ACROSS MULTIPLE CONTEXTS;
20 (II) CHARACTERIZED BY RESTRICTED, REPETITIVE PATTERNS
21 OF BEHAVIOR, INTERESTS, OR ACTIVITIES; AND
22 (III) WHOSE SYMPTOMS CAUSE CLINICALLY SIGNIFICANT
23 IMPAIRMENT IN SOCIAL, OCCUPATIONAL, OR OTHER IMPORTANT AREAS OF
24 CURRENT FUNCTIONING.
25 (4) “BEHAVIOR TECHNICIAN” MEANS AN INDIVIDUAL WHO:
26 (I) DELIVERS APPLIED BEHAVIOR ANALYSIS SERVICES THAT
27 ARE PROVIDED TO INDIVIDUALS WITH AUTISM SPECTRUM DISORDER UNDER THE
28 SUPERVISION OF:
29 1. A LICENSED PSYCHOLOGIST;

4 (II) MEETS THE REQUIREMENTS FOR PROVIDING BEHAVIOR
5 ANALYSIS SERVICES UNDER THE PROGRAM ESTABLISHED BY THE DEPARTMENT IN
6 REGULATION.
7 (4) (5) “BOARD CERTIFIED BEHAVIOR ANALYST” MEANS A
8 LICENSED PROFESSIONAL WHO:
9 (I) RENDERS AND PROVIDES DIRECTION FOR APPLIED

11 SPECTRUM DISORDER; AND

13 CERTIFICATION.
14 (5) (6) “BOARD CERTIFIED BEHAVIOR ANALYST–DOCTORAL”
15 MEANS A LICENSED PROFESSIONAL WHO:
16 (I) RENDERS AND PROVIDES DIRECTION FOR APPLIED
17 BEHAVIOR ANALYSIS TREATMENTS THAT ARE PROVIDED TO INDIVIDUALS WITH

19 (II) HAS OBTAINED A BOARD CERTIFIED BEHAVIOR
20 ANALYST–DOCTORAL CERTIFICATION.
21 (6) “REGISTERED BEHAVIOR TECHNICIAN” MEANS A
22 PARAPROFESSIONAL WHO DELIVERS APPLIED BEHAVIOR ANALYSIS SERVICES THAT
23 ARE PROVIDED TO INDIVIDUALS WITH AUTISM SPECTRUM DISORDER UNDER THE
24 SUPERVISION OF:
25 (I) A LICENSED PSYCHOLOGIST;
26 (II) A BOARD CERTIFIED BEHAVIOR ANALYST–DOCTORAL; OR
27 (III) A BOARD CERTIFIED BEHAVIOR ANALYST.
28 (B) (1) THE DEPARTMENT SHALL PROVIDE REIMBURSEMENT FOR
29 APPLIED BEHAVIOR ANALYSIS SERVICES PROVIDED BY REGISTERED BEHAVIOR
30 TECHNICIANS TO PROGRAM RECIPIENTS WITH AUTISM SPECTRUM DISORDER.
