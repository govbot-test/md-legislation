2 SENATE BILL 339
1 (a) There is a Maryland School for the Deaf.
2 (b) The governance of the Maryland School for the Deaf is vested in the Board of
3 Trustees of the Maryland School for the Deaf.
4 (c) (1) The Board of Trustees shall consist of [19] 13 members appointed by
5 the Governor with the advice and consent of the Senate.
6 (2) Of the [19] 13 members, at least [6] 7 members shall be deaf.
7 (3) Each member of the Board shall:
8 (i) Be a resident of the State;
9 (ii) Be a member of the general public; and
10 (iii) Have demonstrated an active interest in the education of deaf
11 children.
12 (4) Each geographic region of the State shall be represented by at least one
13 member of the Board.
14 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That:
15 (a) The terms of the first 6 appointed members of the Board of Trustees of the
16 Maryland School for the Deaf whose terms expire after the effective date of this Act shall
17 terminate at the end of the last day of each respective term.
18 (b) On the termination of an appointed member in accordance with subsection (a)
19 of this section, the Governor, with the advice and consent of the Senate, shall prioritize
20 appointing members who are deaf in accordance with § 8–3A–04(c)(2) of the Education
21 Article as enacted by Section 1 of this Act.
22 SECTION 3. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect July
23 1, 2026.
