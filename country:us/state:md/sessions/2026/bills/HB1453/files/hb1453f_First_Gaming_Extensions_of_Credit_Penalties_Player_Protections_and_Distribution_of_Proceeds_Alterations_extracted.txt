A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Gaming – Extensions of Credit, Penalties, Player Protections, and Distribution
3 of Proceeds – Alterations
4 FOR the purpose of requiring the State Lottery and Gaming Control Commission to adopt
5 certain regulations governing the extension of credit to a player by a video lottery
6 operation licensee; requiring the Commission to establish a certain list of players for
7 whom the video lottery operation licensee has approved an extension of credit or
8 authorized certain privileges; altering the maximum amount of certain penalties
9 that the Commission is authorized to impose for certain violations of law governing
10 video lottery facilities and sports wagering; altering the distribution of certain
11 fantasy competition and sports wagering proceeds; prohibiting the use of credit cards
12 for certain online sports wagering–related transactions; specifying the maximum
13 wager that may be accepted by a sports wagering licensee under certain
14 circumstances; and generally relating to video lottery facilities, fantasy competitions,
15 and sports wagering.
