CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Juvenile Court – Jurisdiction, Detention, and Confinement
3 (Youth Charging Reform Act)
4 FOR the purpose of altering the jurisdiction of the juvenile court by repealing provisions
5 specifying that the juvenile court does not have jurisdiction over a child alleged to
6 have committed certain acts; requiring an intake officer to authorize detention of a
7 certain child; altering and establishing certain provisions relating to the detention,
8 confinement, and transportation of certain children; and generally relating to the
9 jurisdiction of the juvenile court.

2 Juvenile Court – Jurisdiction, Detention, and Confinement
3 (Youth Charging Reform Act)
4 FOR the purpose of altering the jurisdiction of the juvenile court by repealing provisions
5 specifying that the juvenile court does not have jurisdiction over a child alleged to
6 have committed certain acts; requiring an intake officer to authorize detention of a

9 jurisdiction of the juvenile court.
