2 SENATE BILL 414
1 Article – Alcoholic Beverages and Cannabis
2 11–102.
3 This title applies only in Anne Arundel County.
4 11–1006.3.
5 (A) THERE IS A CONSUMPTION–ONLY NONPROFIT SWIM CLUB LICENSE.
6 (B) THE BOARD MAY ISSUE THE LICENSE TO A NONPROFIT SWIM CLUB.
7 (C) (1) THE LICENSE AUTHORIZES THE LICENSE HOLDER TO ALLOW
8 MEMBERS OF THE SWIM CLUB AND THEIR GUESTS TO BRING BEER, WINE, AND
9 LIQUOR ONTO THE LICENSED PREMISES FOR ON–PREMISES CONSUMPTION.
10 (2) THE LICENSE HOLDER MAY ALLOW THE CONSUMPTION OF BEER,
11 WINE, AND LIQUOR:
12 (I) IN A DESIGNATED AREA OF THE SWIM CLUB;
13 (II) DURING A DESIGNATED EVENT; AND
14 (III) UP TO 5 DAYS PER YEAR, UNLESS ADDITIONAL DAYS ARE
15 AUTHORIZED BY THE BOARD.
16 (D) THE LICENSE HOLDER SHALL PROVIDE NOTICE TO THE BOARD AT
17 LEAST 10 DAYS PRIOR TO A DESIGNATED EVENT WHERE ALCOHOL WILL BE
18 CONSUMED.
19 (E) DURING A DESIGNATED EVENT, THE LICENSE HOLDER MAY ALLOW THE
20 CONSUMPTION OF BEER, WINE, AND LIQUOR IN THE DESIGNATED AREA FROM 2 P.M.
21 TO 10 P.M.
22 (F) (1) THE ANNUAL LICENSE FEE IS $100.
23 (2) THE FEE FOR ADDITIONAL DAYS IS $50 PER DAY, UP TO A
24 MAXIMUM OF 3 ADDITIONAL DAYS.
25 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect July
26 1, 2026.
