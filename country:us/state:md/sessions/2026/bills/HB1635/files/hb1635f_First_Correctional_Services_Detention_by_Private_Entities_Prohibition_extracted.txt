By: Delegate Alston
Introduced and read first time: February 25, 2026
Assigned to: Rules and Executive Nominations
A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Correctional Services – Detention by Private Entities – Prohibition
3 FOR the purpose of prohibiting certain governmental entities or a company acting under a
4 contract with certain governmental entities from taking certain actions relating to a
5 detention facility owned, managed, or operated by a private entity; and generally
6 relating to detention of individuals by private entities in Maryland.
7 BY adding to
8 Article – Correctional Services
9 Section 1–202
10 Annotated Code of Maryland
11 (2025 Replacement Volume)
12 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
13 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
14 Article – Correctional Services
15 1–202.
16 (A) THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY FINDS THAT THE MANAGEMENT AND
17 OPERATION OF A DETENTION FACILITY INVOLVES FUNCTIONS THAT ARE
18 INHERENTLY GOVERNMENTAL AND THAT SUCH FUNCTIONS REQUIRE THE EXERCISE
19 OF COERCIVE POLICE POWERS OVER INDIVIDUALS WHICH SHOULD NOT BE
20 DELEGATED TO THE PRIVATE SECTOR AND ARE DISTINGUISHABLE FROM
21 PRIVATIZATION IN OTHER AREAS OF GOVERNMENT.
22 (B) (1) IN THIS SECTION THE FOLLOWING WORDS HAVE THE MEANINGS
23 INDICATED.

1 (2) “DETENTION FACILITY” INCLUDES:
2 (I) A CORRECTIONAL FACILITY;
3 (II) AN IMMIGRATION DETENTION FACILITY; OR
4 (III) ANY OTHER FACILITY USED TO DETAIN INDIVIDUALS.
5 (3) (I) “ANCILLARY SERVICES” INCLUDE:
6 1. EDUCATION SERVICES;
7 2. MEDICAL SERVICES; AND
8 3. MAINTENANCE SERVICES.
9 (II) “ANCILLARY SERVICES” DO NOT INCLUDE FOOD SERVICES.
10 (C) THIS SECTION DOES NOT APPLY TO A CONTRACT TO PROVIDE
11 ANCILLARY SERVICES AT A DETENTION FACILITY.
12 (D) THE FEDERAL GOVERNMENT, THE STATE, A UNIT OF LOCAL
13 GOVERNMENT, A COUNTY SHERIFF, AN AGENCY, OFFICER, EMPLOYEE, OR AGENT OF
14 THE STATE OR A UNIT OF LOCAL GOVERNMENT, OR A COMPANY ACTING IN
15 ACCORDANCE WITH A CONTRACT WITH THE FEDERAL GOVERNMENT, THE STATE, A
16 UNIT OF LOCAL GOVERNMENT, OR A COUNTY SHERIFF MAY NOT:
17 (1) ENTER INTO AN AGREEMENT OF ANY KIND FOR THE DETENTION
18 OF INDIVIDUALS IN A DETENTION FACILITY OWNED, MANAGED, OR OPERATED, IN
19 WHOLE OR IN PART, BY A PRIVATE ENTITY;
20 (2) PAY, REIMBURSE, SUBSIDIZE, OR DEFRAY IN ANY WAY ANY COSTS
21 RELATED TO THE SALE, PURCHASE, CONSTRUCTION, DEVELOPMENT, OWNERSHIP,
22 MANAGEMENT, OR OPERATION OF A DETENTION FACILITY THAT IS OR WILL BE
23 OWNED, MANAGED, OR OPERATED, IN WHOLE OR IN PART, BY A PRIVATE ENTITY;
24 (3) RECEIVE ANY PAYMENT RELATED TO THE DETENTION OF
25 INDIVIDUALS IN A DETENTION FACILITY OWNED, MANAGED, OR OPERATED, IN
26 WHOLE OR IN PART, BY A PRIVATE ENTITY; OR
27 (4) OTHERWISE GIVE A FINANCIAL INCENTIVE OR BENEFIT TO A
28 PRIVATE ENTITY OR PERSON IN CONNECTION WITH THE SALE, PURCHASE,
29 CONSTRUCTION, DEVELOPMENT, OWNERSHIP, MANAGEMENT, OR OPERATION OF A
