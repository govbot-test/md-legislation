R2 6lr1856
By: Delegate Guyton
Introduced and read first time: February 2, 2026
Assigned to: Environment and Transportation
A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Maryland Transit Administration – Fixed–Route Transit Services –
3 Announcements
4 FOR the purpose of requiring the Maryland Transit Administration to provide certain
5 announcements on all fixed–route transit services; and generally relating to
6 announcements on fixed–route transit services.
7 BY adding to
8 Article – Transportation
9 Section 7–720
10 Annotated Code of Maryland
11 (2020 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
12 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
13 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
14 Article – Transportation
15 7–720.
16 THE ADMINISTRATION SHALL PROVIDE REAL–TIME AUDIBLE AND
17 TEXT–BASED ANNOUNCEMENTS THAT ANNOUNCE ALL STOPS AND OTHER CRITICAL
18 INFORMATION ON ALL FIXED–ROUTE TRANSIT SERVICES.
19 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
20 October 1, 2026.
