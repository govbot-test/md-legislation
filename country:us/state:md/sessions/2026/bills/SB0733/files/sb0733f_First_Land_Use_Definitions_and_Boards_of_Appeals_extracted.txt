2 Land Use – Definitions and Boards of Appeals

4 land use; altering requirements for the appointment and removal of members of a
5 board of appeals; altering and repealing certain powers of a board of appeals; altering
6 the procedures applicable to certain proceedings before a board of appeals; and
7 generally relating to land use.
