1 AN ACT concerning
2 Part–Time State Employment Pilot Program Workgroup for an Inclusive State
3 Workplace – Established
4 (Maryland Workforce Retention, Recruitment, and Reentry Act)
5 FOR the purpose of establishing the Part–Time State Employment Pilot Program in the
6 Department of Budget and Management to promote and support the inclusivity of
7 State employment by increasing the availability of part–time positions with benefits
8 in State government; requiring State agencies selected to participate in the Program
9 to evaluate full–time position vacancies to determine whether the vacant positions
10 could be converted to part–time positions with benefits; and generally relating to the
11 Part–Time State Employment Pilot Program Workgroup for an Inclusive State
12 Workplace to create a strategy and implementation work plan regarding more
13 inclusive State employment opportunities for individuals who are challenged by the
14 traditional full–time employment structure; and generally relating to the Workgroup
15 for an Inclusive State Workplace.

22 but less than 100% of the regular workweek.

24 a full–time employee may not be required to accept part–time employment as a condition
25 of continued or new State employment.

27 be a part–time employee may not be required to accept full–time employment as a condition
28 of continued or new State employment.

31 normally available to a full–time employee in a similar position with a similar grade and

13 (C) THE PURPOSE OF THE PROGRAM IS TO PROMOTE AND SUPPORT THE
14 INCLUSIVITY OF STATE EMPLOYMENT BY INCREASING THE AVAILABILITY OF
15 PART–TIME POSITIONS WITH BENEFITS IN STATE GOVERNMENT.
16 (D) THE DEPARTMENT SHALL SELECT FIVE AGENCIES IN THE EXECUTIVE
17 BRANCH OF STATE GOVERNMENT WITH A BROAD AND DIVERSE REPRESENTATION
18 OF POSITIONS AND POSITION RESPONSIBILITIES TO PARTICIPATE IN THE PROGRAM.
19 (E) (1) EACH STATE AGENCY SELECTED BY THE DEPARTMENT SHALL
20 EVALUATE FULL–TIME POSITION VACANCIES WITHIN THE AGENCY AS THE
21 POSITIONS BECOME AVAILABLE TO DETERMINE WHETHER ANY OF THE VACANT
22 FULL–TIME POSITIONS COULD BE CONVERTED TO TWO PART–TIME POSITIONS WITH
23 BENEFITS.
24 (2) IF A STATE AGENCY DETERMINES THAT THERE ARE NO VACANT
25 FULL–TIME POSITIONS WITHIN THE AGENCY THAT ARE ELIGIBLE FOR CONVERSION
26 TO TWO PART–TIME POSITIONS, THE AGENCY SHALL DOCUMENT THE REASONS FOR
27 THE DETERMINATION.
28 (F) IF A STATE AGENCY DETERMINES THAT A VACANT FULL–TIME POSITION
29 IS ELIGIBLE FOR CONVERSION TO TWO PART–TIME POSITIONS, THE AGENCY SHALL:
