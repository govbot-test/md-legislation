CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Maryland Department of Health – Forensic Review Boards and Community
3 Forensic Aftercare Program – Established
4 FOR the purpose of repealing the requirement that a forensic review board provide notice
5 to certain individuals within a certain time period after issuing a recommendation
6 to the court; authorizing an authorized agent to attend a forensic review board
7 meeting virtually; establishing the Community Forensic Aftercare Program in the
8 Maryland Department of Health to monitor committed persons on conditional
9 release and individuals with mental illness or intellectual disability who are
10 required to be monitored under certain circumstances; requiring the Program to
11 establish a community monitoring board to make certain decisions and
12 recommendations related to conditional release and out–of–state travel; and
13 generally relating to forensic review boards and the Community Forensic Aftercare
14 Program.
15 BY repealing and reenacting, without amendments,

17 Section 3–101(a), (b), and (d) and 3–121(b)
