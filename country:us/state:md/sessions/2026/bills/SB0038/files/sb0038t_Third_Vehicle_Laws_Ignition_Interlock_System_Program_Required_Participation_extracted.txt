1 Article – Transportation
2 16–404.1.
3 (a) (1) In this section the following words have the meanings indicated.
4 (2) “Approved service provider” means a person who is certified by:
5 (i) The Administration to service, install, monitor, calibrate, and
6 provide information on ignition interlock systems; and
7 (ii) A manufacturer to be qualified to service, install, monitor,
8 calibrate, and provide information on ignition interlock systems.
9 (3) “Manufacturer” means a person who manufactures ignition interlock
10 systems and who certifies that approved service providers are qualified to service, install,
11 monitor, calibrate, and provide information on ignition interlock systems.
12 (4) “Participant” means a participant in the Ignition Interlock System
13 Program.
14 (5) “Program” means the Ignition Interlock System Program.
15 (b) (1) The Administration shall establish an Ignition Interlock System
16 Program in accordance with this section.
17 (2) The Administration shall establish a protocol for the Program by
18 regulations that require certain minimum standards for all service providers who service,
19 install, monitor, calibrate, and provide information on ignition interlock systems and
20 include requirements that:
21 (i) A service provider who applies to the Administration for
22 certification as an approved service provider shall demonstrate that the service provider is
23 able to competently service, install, monitor, calibrate, and provide information to the
24 Administration at least every 30 days on individuals required to use ignition interlock
25 systems;
26 (ii) A service provider who applies to the Administration for
27 certification as an approved service provider shall be certified by a signed affidavit from
28 the manufacturer that the service provider has been trained by an authorized
29 manufacturer and that the service provider is competent to service, install, monitor,
30 calibrate, and provide information on ignition interlock systems;
31 (iii) Approved service providers be deemed to be authorized
32 representatives of a manufacturer; and

1 (iv) Any service of notice upon an approved service provider, who has
2 violated any laws or regulations or whose ignition interlock system has violated any laws
3 or regulations, be deemed as service upon the manufacturer who certified the approved
4 service provider.
5 (c) An individual may be a participant if:
6 (1) The individual’s license is suspended or revoked under § 16–205 of this
7 title for a violation of § 21–902(c) of this article or § 16–404 of this subtitle for an
8 accumulation of points under § 16–402(a)(29) of this subtitle for driving while impaired by
9 a drug, a combination of drugs, or a combination of one or more drugs and alcohol;
10 (2) The individual’s license has an alcohol restriction imposed under §
11 16–113(g)(1) of this title; or
12 (3) The Administration modifies a suspension or issues a restricted license
13 to the individual under § 16–205.1 of this title.
14 (d) (2) (i) Notwithstanding subsection (c) of this section, an individual
15 shall be a participant as a condition of modification of a suspension or revocation of a license
16 or issuance of a restricted license if the individual:
17 1. Is required to be a participant by a court order under §
18 21–902.2 of this article; [or]
19 2. Was under the age of 21 years on the date of a violation by
20 the individual of[:] § 21–902(C) OF THIS ARTICLE; OR
21 [A.] 3. [An] VIOLATED AN alcohol restriction imposed
22 under [§ 16–113(b)(1) of] this title[; or
23 B. § 21–902(c) of this article].
24 (ii) If an individual is subject to this paragraph and the individual
25 fails to participate in the Program or does not successfully complete the Program, the
26 Administration shall suspend the individual’s license for 1 year.
27 (iii) Nothing contained in this paragraph limits the authority of the
28 Administration to modify a suspension imposed under this paragraph to allow an
29 individual to be a participant in accordance with subsection (e) or (o) of this section.
30 (iv) The Administration shall issue a restricted license to an
31 individual who is required to participate in the Program under this section and who is
32 otherwise eligible.
33 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
