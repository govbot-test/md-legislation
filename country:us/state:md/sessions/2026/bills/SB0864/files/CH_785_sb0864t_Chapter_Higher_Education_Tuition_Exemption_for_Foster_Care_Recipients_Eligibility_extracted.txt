Chapter 785
AN ACT concerning
Higher Education – Tuition Exemption for Foster Care Recipients – Eligibility
FOR the purpose of altering the definition of a “foster care recipient” to alter a certain age

the age of 25 years;
(ii) The foster care recipient or homeless youth is enrolled as a
candidate for a vocational certificate, an associate’s degree, or a bachelor’s degree; and
(iii) The foster care recipient or homeless youth has filed for federal
and State financial aid.
