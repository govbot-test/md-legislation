1 AN ACT concerning
2 County Boards of Education Public High Schools – Post College and Career
3 Readiness Pathways – Payment of Costs Alterations
4 FOR the purpose of authorizing a county board of education to develop and establish income
5 eligibility guidelines and procedures for payment of costs for a certain post college
6 and career readiness pathway, except under certain circumstances; extending the
7 time period during which the State Board of Education and the Accountability and
8 Implementation Board may limit the number and types of dual enrollment as a post
9 college and career readiness pathway; altering the purpose, composition, and duties
10 of the Career and Technical Education (CTE) Committee; repealing the CTE Skills
11 Standards Advisory Committee; requiring certain State agencies and certain
12 workforce development and education programs to use a certain list of occupations
13 for certain purposes; and generally relating to post college and career readiness
14 pathways.

23 (a) In this section, “CCR standard” means the college and career readiness
24 standards established under this section.
25 (c) (1) (i) It is the goal of the State that students enrolled in public school
26 shall meet the CCR standard before the end of the 10th grade and no later than the time
27 the student graduates from high school.
28 (ii) It is the goal of the State that each student enrolled in public
29 school, regardless of the student’s race, ethnicity, gender, address, socioeconomic status, or
30 the language spoken in the student’s home, shall have equitable access to college and career
31 readiness and shall meet the CCR standard at an equal rate.
32 (2) A student shall meet the CCR standard when the student meets a
33 standard in English language arts, mathematics, and, when practicable, science that
34 enables the student to be successful in entry level credit bearing courses or postsecondary
35 education training at a State community college.

1 AN ACT concerning
2 County Boards of Education Public High Schools – Post College and Career
3 Readiness Pathways – Payment of Costs Alterations
4 FOR the purpose of authorizing a county board of education to develop and establish income
5 eligibility guidelines and procedures for payment of costs for a certain post college
6 and career readiness pathway, except under certain circumstances; extending the
7 time period during which the State Board of Education and the Accountability and
8 Implementation Board may limit the number and types of dual enrollment as a post
9 college and career readiness pathway; altering the purpose, composition, and duties
10 of the Career and Technical Education (CTE) Committee; repealing the CTE Skills
11 Standards Advisory Committee; requiring certain State agencies and certain
12 workforce development and education programs to use a certain list of occupations
13 for certain purposes; and generally relating to post college and career readiness
14 pathways.
