1 AN ACT concerning
2 Public Financing Act – Campaign Financing – Campaign Finance Reporting
3 Deadline and Fair Campaign Financing Fund – Distributions Distributions
4 FOR the purpose of altering the day that a certain campaign finance report is due to be
5 filed with the State Board of Elections; requiring that a certain campaign finance
6 report cover a certain period of time; altering the earliest date on which the State
7 Board of Elections may begin making distributions from the Fair Campaign
8 Financing Fund; and generally relating to distributions from the Fair Campaign
9 Financing Fund campaign finance.
10 BY repealing and reenacting, without amendments,
11 Article – Election Law
12 Section 13–309(a) and 15–102(a) and (f)
13 Annotated Code of Maryland
14 (2022 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
15 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
16 Article – Election Law
17 Section 13–309(c), 13–312, and 15–107(a)
18 Annotated Code of Maryland
19 (2022 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)

2 SENATE BILL 11
1 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
2 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
3 Article – Election Law
4 13–309.
5 (a) Subject to other provisions of this subtitle and except as provided in subsection
6 (d) of this section, a campaign finance entity shall file campaign finance reports as follows:
7 (1) in the gubernatorial election year only, except for a ballot issue
8 committee, on or before the third Tuesday in April, if the campaign finance entity did not
9 file the annual campaign finance report specified under subsection (b)(2) of this section on
10 the immediately preceding third Wednesday in January;
11 (2) except for a ballot issue committee, on or before the fifth Tuesday
12 immediately preceding each primary election;
13 (3) except for a ballot issue committee, on or before the second Friday
14 immediately preceding a primary election;
15 (4) on or before the last Tuesday in August immediately preceding a
16 general election;
17 (5) for a ballot issue committee only, on or before the fourth Friday
18 immediately preceding a general election;
19 (6) on or before the second Friday immediately preceding a general
20 election; and
21 (7) on or before the second Tuesday after a general election.
22 (c) In addition to the campaign finance reports required under subsection (a) of
23 this section, a continuing political committee shall file a campaign finance report on the
24 [third] FOURTH Wednesday in January of each year the committee is in existence.
25 13–312.
26 (a) Campaign finance reports filed under § 13–304 of this subtitle shall cover the
27 following reporting periods:
28 (1) the first campaign finance report shall cover the period from the date of
29 organization of the campaign finance entity through the day specified in item (3) of this
30 subsection;
SENATE BILL 11 3
1 (2) each subsequent campaign finance report shall cover the period from
2 the closing date of the previous campaign finance report through the day specified in item
3 (3) of this subsection;
4 (3) (i) except as provided in item (ii) of this item, each campaign finance
5 report shall cover the period that includes the seventh day before the day the campaign
6 finance report is due; but
7 (ii) 1. the campaign finance report that is required on or before
8 the second Friday immediately preceding an election shall cover the period through and
9 including the preceding Sunday; and
10 2. THE CAMPAIGN FINANCE REPORT THAT IS REQUIRED
11 ON OR BEFORE THE FOURTH WEDNESDAY IN JANUARY SHALL COVER THE PERIOD
12 THROUGH AND INCLUDING THE 14TH DAY BEFORE THE CAMPAIGN FINANCE REPORT
13 IS DUE; AND
14 (4) if no contribution is received and no expenditure is made during the
15 period covered by a campaign finance report, the campaign finance entity shall file a
16 campaign finance report or an affidavit under § 13–305 of this subtitle to that effect.
17 (b) A campaign finance report preceded by an affidavit filed in accordance with
18 this subtitle shall cover the period from the closing date of the previous campaign finance
19 report or date of organization of the campaign finance entity through the day specified in
20 subsection (a)(3) of this section.
21 15–102.
22 (a) In this title the following words have the meanings indicated.
23 (f) “Fund” means the Fair Campaign Financing Fund.
24 15–107.
25 (a) (1) In accordance with subsection (c) of this section and regulations
26 adopted by the State Board, the State Board shall authorize distribution of money in the
27 Fund on a continuing basis on certification that the qualifications under § 15–104 of this
28 title have been met.
29 (2) Distributions shall begin not earlier than [January] JULY 1 of the year
30 [of] BEFORE the election.
31 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect July
32 1, 2026.
