2 Family Child Care Providers – National Guard and Military Reserves Reserve
3 Component Members – Substitute Provider
4 FOR the purpose of requiring a family child care provider who is a member of the National
5 Guard or military reserves a reserve component to designate a substitute to provide
6 family child care under certain circumstances for not more than a certain amount of
7 time each year; and generally relating to family child care providers.

2 HOUSE BILL 430
1 9.5–301.
2 (a) In this subtitle the following words have the meanings indicated.
3 (b) “Child care provider” means the adult who has primary responsibility for the
4 operation of a family child care home or a large family child care home.
5 (e) “Family child care home” means a residence in which family child care is
6 provided for up to eight children.
7 (f) “Family child care provider” means an individual who cares for children in a
8 registered family child care home or a registered large family child care home.
9 9.5–305.1.
10 (A) IN THIS SECTION, “MILITARY RESERVES” MEANS THE U.S. ARMY
11 RESERVE, U.S. NAVY RESERVE, AIR FORCE RESERVE COMMAND, U.S. MARINE
12 CORPS RESERVE, OR U.S. COAST GUARD RESERVE.
13 (B) THIS SECTION APPLIES ONLY TO A FAMILY CHILD CARE PROVIDER WHO
14 IS A MEMBER OF THE NATIONAL GUARD OR MILITARY RESERVES A RESERVE
15 COMPONENT.
16 (C) (B) A FAMILY CHILD CARE PROVIDER SHALL DESIGNATE A
17 SUBSTITUTE TO PROVIDE FAMILY CHILD CARE DURING THE FAMILY CHILD CARE
18 PROVIDER’S ABSENCE FOR REQUIRED TRAINING AND ACTIVE SERVICE IN THE
19 NATIONAL GUARD OR MILITARY RESERVES A RESERVE COMPONENT.
20 (D) (C) A FAMILY CARE PROVIDER UNDER SUBSECTION (C) (B) OF THIS
21 SECTION MAY USE THE DESIGNATED SUBSTITUTE TO PROVIDE FAMILY CHILD CARE
22 DURING THE FAMILY CHILD CARE PROVIDER’S ABSENCE FOR NOT MORE THAN 45
23 WORKING DAYS IN ANY 12–MONTH PERIOD.
24 (D) THIS SECTION MAY NOT BE CONSTRUED TO OTHERWISE LIMIT THE
25 AUTHORITY OF THE DEPARTMENT TO ESTABLISH REQUIREMENTS FOR:
26 (1) A FAMILY CHILD CARE PROVIDER TO DESIGNATE A SUBSTITUTE
27 TO PROVIDE FAMILY CHILD CARE IN THE FAMILY CHILD CARE PROVIDER’S ABSENCE;
28 AND
29 (2) THE QUALIFICATIONS NECESSARY TO SERVE AS A SUBSTITUTE.
30 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect July
31 1, 2026.
