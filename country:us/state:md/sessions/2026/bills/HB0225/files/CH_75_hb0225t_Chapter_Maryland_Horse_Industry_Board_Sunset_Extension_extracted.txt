Section 1:
WES MOORE, Governor Ch. 75
Chapter 75
AN ACT concerning
Maryland Horse Industry Board – Sunset Extension
FOR the purpose of continuing the Maryland Horse Industry Board in accordance with the
provisions of the Maryland Program Evaluation Act (sunset law) by extending to a
certain date the termination provisions relating to the statutory and regulatory
authority of the Board; and generally relating to the Maryland Horse Industry
Board.
BY repealing and reenacting, without amendments,
Article – Agriculture

Section 2:
Section 2–703
Annotated Code of Maryland
(2016 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
Article – Agriculture

Section 3:
Section 2–719
Annotated Code of Maryland
(2016 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)

Section 4:
SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
Article – Agriculture
2–703.
There is a Maryland Horse Industry Board.
2–719.
Subject to the evaluation and reestablishment provisions of the Program Evaluation
Act, the provisions of this subtitle and of any rule or regulation adopted under this subtitle
shall terminate and be of no effect after July 1, [2026] 2036.

Section 5:
SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect June
1, 2026.
Approved by the Governor, April 14, 2026.
