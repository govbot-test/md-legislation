CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Commercial Law – Self–Service Storage Facilities – Alterations
3 FOR the purpose of providing for the use of an electronic rental agreement in the rental of
4 self–service storage facilities; prohibiting an occupant from using the occupant’s
5 self–service storage facility beyond the term of a rental agreement after delivery of
6 a notice of nonrenewal; requiring an operator to give an occupant a certain number
7 of days after delivery of a notice of nonrenewal to remove the occupant’s personal
8 property from the self–service storage facility; authorizing an operator to dispose of
9 an occupant’s remaining personal property after a certain amount of time has
10 elapsed; requiring an operator to give an occupant notice of intent to dispose of the
11 occupant’s personal property a certain number of days before the operator disposes
12 of that personal property; requiring an operator to include a certain statement in a
13 rental agreement concerning the removal of personal property from the self–service
14 storage facility after nonrenewal of the rental agreement; and generally relating to
15 self–service storage facilities.

8 Article – Commercial Law
9 18–501.
10 (a) In this subtitle the following words have the meanings indicated.
11 (b) “Default” means the failure to perform on time any obligation or duty set forth
12 in the rental agreement.
13 (c) “Last known address” means that address or electronic mail address provided
14 by the occupant in the rental agreement or the address or electronic mail address provided
15 by the occupant in a subsequent written notice of a change of address.
16 (d) “Leased space” means the individual storage space at the self–service facility
17 which is rented to an occupant pursuant to a rental agreement.
18 (e) “Occupant” means a person, a sublessee, successor, or assign, entitled to the
19 use of a leased space at a self–service storage facility under a rental agreement.
20 (f) (1) “Operator” means the owner, operator, lessor, or sublessor of a
21 self–service storage facility, an agent, or any other person authorized to manage the facility.
22 (2) “Operator” does not include a warehouseman, unless the operator
23 issues a warehouse receipt, bill of lading, or other document of title for the personal
24 property stored.
25 (g) (1) “Personal property” means movable property, not affixed to land.
26 (2) “Personal property” includes goods, wares, merchandise, motor
27 vehicles, watercraft, and household items and furnishings.
28 (h) “Rental agreement” means any written OR ELECTRONIC agreement that
29 establishes or modifies the terms, conditions, or rules concerning the use and occupancy of
30 a self–service storage facility.

6 18–502.
7 (a) An operator may not knowingly permit a leased space at a self–service storage
8 facility to be used for residential purposes.
9 (b) An occupant may not use a leased space for residential purposes.

8 18–503.
9 (a) The operator of a self–service storage facility has a lien on all personal
10 property stored within each leased space for rent, labor, or other charges, and for expenses
11 reasonably incurred in its sale, as provided in this subtitle.
12 (b) The rental agreement shall contain a statement, in bold type, advising the
13 occupant:
14 (1) Of the existence of the lien;
15 (2) That personal property stored in the leased space may be sold to satisfy
16 the lien if the occupant is in default;
17 (3) That personal property stored in the leased space may be towed or
18 removed from the self–service storage facility if:
19 (i) The personal property is a motor vehicle or watercraft; and
20 (ii) The occupant is in default for more than 60 days; and
21 (4) That a sale of personal property stored in the leased space to satisfy the
22 lien if the occupant is in default shall be advertised:
23 (i) In a newspaper of general circulation in the jurisdiction where
24 the sale is to be held;
25 (ii) By electronic mail; or
26 (iii) On an online website.; AND
27 (5) THAT IF THE OPERATOR DOES NOT RENEW THE RENTAL
28 AGREEMENT, THE OPERATOR SHALL, IN ACCORDANCE WITH § 18–502 OF THIS
29 SUBTITLE, DELIVER WRITTEN NOTICE ADVISING THE OCCUPANT TO REMOVE ALL
30 PERSONAL PROPERTY FROM THE SELF–SERVICE STORAGE FACILITY BY THE DATE
31 STATED IN THE NOTICE.
