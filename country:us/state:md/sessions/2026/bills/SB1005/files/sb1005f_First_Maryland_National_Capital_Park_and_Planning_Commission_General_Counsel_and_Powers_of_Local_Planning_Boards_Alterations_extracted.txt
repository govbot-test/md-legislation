A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Maryland–National Capital Park and Planning Commission General Counsel
3 and Powers of Local Planning Boards – Alterations
4 FOR the purpose of repealing the requirement that the Maryland–National Capital Park
5 and Planning Commission appoint a general counsel; requiring the Montgomery
6 County Planning Board and the Prince George’s County Planning Board to appoint
7 legal counsel for Commission operations within each respective county subject to
8 certain requirements; requiring the Montgomery County Planning Board and the
9 Prince George’s County Planning Board to exercise direct authority over certain
10 functions within each county; authorizing the Commission to use shared
11 administrative services under certain circumstances; and generally relating to the
12 Maryland–National Capital Park and Planning Commission and local planning
13 boards in Montgomery County and Prince George’s County.
14 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
15 Article – Land Use
16 Section 15–109, 15–110, 15–506, 16–102, 16–103(c), and 16–201(a)(2)
17 Annotated Code of Maryland
18 (2012 Volume and 2025 Supplement)
19 BY adding to
20 Article – Land Use
21 Section 15–110.1
22 Annotated Code of Maryland
23 (2012 Volume and 2025 Supplement)
24 BY repealing and reenacting, without amendments,
25 Article – Land Use
26 Section 16–103(a)
27 Annotated Code of Maryland
28 (2012 Volume and 2025 Supplement)
