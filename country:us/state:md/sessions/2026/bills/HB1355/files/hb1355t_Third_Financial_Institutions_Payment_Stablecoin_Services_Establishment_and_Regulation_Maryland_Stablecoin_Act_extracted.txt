1 AN ACT concerning
2 Financial Institutions – Payment Stablecoin Services – Establishment and
3 Regulation
4 (Maryland Stablecoin Act)
5 FOR the purpose of altering the examination fee for a new commercial bank charter;
6 requiring a certain nondepository trust company to include certain disclosures in its
7 articles of incorporation; exempting from certain provisions of law a certain
8 nondepository trust company authorized to provide certain services by the
9 Commissioner of Financial Regulation; authorizing a certain nondepository trust
10 company to request that the Commissioner reduce the capital stock requirements
11 under certain circumstances; establishing a regulatory and legal framework for
12 entities in the State to serve as State issuers of stablecoin–related services or as
13 payment stablecoin services institutions; and generally relating to payment
14 stablecoin services in the State.
