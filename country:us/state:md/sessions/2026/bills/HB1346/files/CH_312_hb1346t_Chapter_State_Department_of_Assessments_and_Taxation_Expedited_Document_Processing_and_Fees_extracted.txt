WES MOORE, Governor Ch. 312
Chapter 312
(House Bill 1346)
AN ACT concerning
State Department of Assessments and Taxation – Expedited Document
Processing and Fees
FOR the purpose of establishing an expedited processing fees fee for certain documents
filed with the State Department of Assessments and Taxation; requiring the
Department to process documents filed at a certain times time within a certain time
frames frame; and generally relating to document processing by the State
Department of Assessments and Taxation.

1–203.2.
(a) On payment of the APPLICABLE fee provided in § 1–203(b)(8) of this subtitle,
the Department shall process documents on an expedited basis as provided in subsection
(b) of this section.
(b) Subject to subsection (c) of this section, the Department shall:
(1) Process documents filed with the Department at least 2 hours before
the Department’s close of business within 2 hours after the documents are received; [and]
(2) PROCESS DOCUMENTS FILED WITH THE DEPARTMENT AT LEAST
1 HOUR BEFORE THE DEPARTMENT’S CLOSE OF BUSINESS WITHIN 1 HOUR AFTER
THE DOCUMENTS ARE RECEIVED; AND
(3) PROCESS DOCUMENTS FILED WITH THE DEPARTMENT AT LEAST
30 MINUTES BEFORE THE DEPARTMENT’S CLOSE OF BUSINESS WITHIN 30 MINUTES
AFTER THE DOCUMENTS ARE RECEIVED; AND
[(2)] (4) (3) To the extent practicable, process all other documents on the
same day that the documents are received.
(c) (1) The Department shall adopt regulations governing the manner of filing
and processing of documents on an expedited basis, including reasonable limitations on
filing documents of unusual volume or length.
(2) The Department may:
(i) Adopt regulations establishing:
1. Expedited document processing tiers; or
2. Additional methods of expedited document processing;
and
(ii) Charge reasonable fees for services provided in accordance with
this paragraph.
(3) DOCUMENTS FILED WITH THE DEPARTMENT UNDER SUBSECTION
(B)(2) OF THIS SECTION SHALL FIRST HAVE BEEN ACCEPTED FOR PRECLEARANCE
WITH THE DEPARTMENT UNDER § 1–201(D) OF THIS SUBTITLE.
