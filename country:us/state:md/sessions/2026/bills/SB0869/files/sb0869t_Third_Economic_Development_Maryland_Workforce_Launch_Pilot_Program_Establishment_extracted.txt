CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Economic Development – Maryland Workforce Launch Pilot Program –
3 Establishment
4 FOR the purpose of establishing the Maryland Workforce Launch Pilot Program in the
5 Department of Commerce to provide certain workforce training to qualified business
6 entities; and generally relating to the Maryland Workforce Launch Pilot Program.
7 BY adding to
8 Article – Economic Development
9 Section 3–701 through 3–706 to be under the new subtitle “Subtitle 7. Maryland
10 Workforce Launch Pilot Program”
11 Annotated Code of Maryland
12 (2024 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
13 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
14 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
15 Article – Economic Development
16 SUBTITLE 7. MARYLAND WORKFORCE LAUNCH PILOT PROGRAM.
17 3–701.
18 (A) IN THIS SUBTITLE THE FOLLOWING WORDS HAVE THE MEANINGS
19 INDICATED.
EXPLANATION: CAPITALS INDICATE MATTER ADDED TO EXISTING LAW.
[Brackets] indicate matter deleted from existing law.
Underlining indicates amendments to bill.
Strike out indicates matter stricken from the bill by amendment or deleted from the law by
amendment. *sb0869*
2 SENATE BILL 869
1 (B) “PROGRAM” MEANS THE MARYLAND WORKFORCE LAUNCH PILOT
2 PROGRAM.
3 (C) “QUALIFIED BUSINESS ENTITY” MEANS A BUSINESS ENTITY THAT:
4 (1) DOES NOT HAVE A PHYSICAL LOCATION IN THE STATE;
5 (2) IS IN ACTIVE NEGOTIATIONS WITH THE DEPARTMENT TO
6 ESTABLISH A LOCATION IN THE STATE FROM OUTSIDE THE STATE; AND
7 (3) ENTERS INTO AN AGREEMENT WITH THE DEPARTMENT TO
8 PARTICIPATE IN THE PROGRAM.
9 (D) “STRATEGIC INDUSTRY” MEANS AN INDUSTRY SECTOR IDENTIFIED BY
10 THE DEPARTMENT IN THE STATE’S ECONOMIC STRATEGIC PLAN.
11 3–702.
12 THERE IS A MARYLAND WORKFORCE LAUNCH PILOT PROGRAM IN THE
13 DEPARTMENT.
14 3–703.
15 THE PURPOSE OF THE PROGRAM IS TO PROVIDE CUSTOMIZED, NO–COST
16 WORKFORCE TRAINING AS A BUSINESS ATTRACTION TOOL TO RECRUIT STRATEGIC
17 INDUSTRIES AND QUALIFIED BUSINESS ENTITIES TO THE STATE.
18 3–704.
19 (A) THE DEPARTMENT SHALL ADMINISTER THE PROGRAM.
20 (B) THE DEPARTMENT SHALL PARTNER WITH NOT MORE THAN TWO
21 COMMUNITY COLLEGES IN THE STATE TO DELIVER TRAINING SERVICES UNDER THE
22 PROGRAM.
23 (C) THE DEPARTMENT SHALL DISTRIBUTE FUNDS TO A PARTNER
24 COMMUNITY COLLEGE TO COVER THE DIRECT COSTS OF DEVELOPING AND
25 DELIVERING CUSTOMIZED TRAINING FOR A QUALIFIED BUSINESS ENTITY.
26 (D) FOR EACH QUALIFIED BUSINESS ENTITY RECEIVING ASSISTANCE
27 UNDER THE PROGRAM, THE DEPARTMENT SHALL ESTABLISH PERFORMANCE
28 METRICS, INCLUDING:
SENATE BILL 869 3
1 (1) THE NUMBER OF FULL–TIME JOBS CREATED; AND
2 (2) THE SALARY THAT THE QUALIFIED BUSINESS ENTITY IS
3 PROVIDING TO THE INDIVIDUALS BEING TRAINED.
4 3–705.
5 (A) ANY CURRICULUM, MANUALS, OR INSTRUCTIONAL MATERIALS
6 DEVELOPED BY A COMMUNITY COLLEGE UNDER THIS SUBTITLE SHALL BE OWNED
7 BY THE COMMUNITY COLLEGE.
8 (B) THE DEPARTMENT SHALL INCLUDE IN EACH AGREEMENT WITH A
9 QUALIFIED BUSINESS ENTITY A PROVISION REQUIRING THE QUALIFIED BUSINESS
10 ENTITY TO REIMBURSE THE DEPARTMENT FOR THE COST OF THE TRAINING
11 SERVICES IF THE QUALIFIED BUSINESS ENTITY FAILS TO MEET THE PERFORMANCE
12 METRICS ESTABLISHED UNDER § 3–704 OF THIS SUBTITLE.
13 3–706.
14 ON OR BEFORE DECEMBER 31, 2028, THE DEPARTMENT SHALL SUBMIT A
15 REPORT TO THE GOVERNOR AND, IN ACCORDANCE WITH § 2–1257 OF THE STATE
16 GOVERNMENT ARTICLE, THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY ON THE EFFECTIVENESS OF THE
17 PROGRAM, INCLUDING REVENUE GENERATED BY THE QUALIFIED BUSINESS
18 ENTITIES, JOBS CREATED BY QUALIFIED BUSINESS ENTITIES, AND
19 RECOMMENDATIONS FOR STATEWIDE EXPANSION.
20 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
21 October 1, 2026. It shall remain effective for a period of 3 years and, at the end of September
22 30, 2029, this Act, with no further action required by the General Assembly, shall be
23 abrogated and of no further force and effect.
Approved:

Governor.

President of the Senate.
________________________________________________________________________________
Speaker of the House of Delegates.
