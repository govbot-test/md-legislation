CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Task Force to Modernize County and Municipal Revenue Structures
3 FOR the purpose of establishing a Task Force to Modernize County and Municipal Revenue
4 Structures to study and evaluate the revenue structure of the county and municipal
5 governments within the State; and generally relating to a Task Force to Modernize
6 County and Municipal Revenue Structures.
7 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
8 That:
9 (a) There is a Task Force to Modernize County and Municipal Revenue
10 Structures.
11 (b) The Task Force consists of the following members:
12 (1) two members of the Senate of Maryland, appointed by the President of
13 the Senate;
14 (2) two members of the House of Delegates, appointed by the Speaker of
15 the House;
16 (3) the Comptroller, or the Comptroller’s designee;
17 (4) the Secretary of Budget and Management, or the Secretary’s designee;
EXPLANATION: CAPITALS INDICATE MATTER ADDED TO EXISTING LAW.
[Brackets] indicate matter deleted from existing law.
Underlining indicates amendments to bill.
Strike out indicates matter stricken from the bill by amendment or deleted from the law by
amendment. *hb1142*
2 HOUSE BILL 1142
1 (5) the Director of Assessments and Taxation, or the Director’s designee;
2 (6) two representatives from the Maryland Municipal League, designated
3 by the Chief Executive Officer of the Maryland Municipal League;
4 (7) two representatives from the Maryland Association of Counties,
5 designated by the Executive Director of the Maryland Association of Counties; and
6 (8) one representative from Baltimore City, appointed by the Mayor.
7 (c) The President of the Senate and the Speaker of the House of Delegates jointly
8 shall designate the chair of the Task Force.
9 (d) The Department of Legislative Services shall provide staff for the Task Force.
10 (e) A member of the Task Force:
11 (1) may not receive compensation for serving as a member of the Task
12 Force; but
13 (2) is entitled to reimbursement for expenses under the Standard State
14 Travel Regulations, as provided in the State budget.
15 (f) The Task Force shall:
16 (1) study and evaluate the revenue structure of the county and municipal
17 governments within the State, including:
18 (i) current revenue sources available to each county and municipal
19 government;
20 (ii) optional revenue sources not currently utilized by county and
21 municipal governments;
22 (iii) the authority of each county and municipal government to raise
23 revenue from additional sources;
24 (iv) the revenue sources utilized by counties and municipal
25 governments in other states; and
26 (v) an analysis of the impact of potential new county or municipal
27 revenue sources on the overall affordability of goods and services and the general cost of
28 living, including an accounting of existing taxes; and
29 (2) make recommendations regarding the authority of county and
30 municipal governments to increase sources of revenue.
HOUSE BILL 1142 3
1 (g) On or before December 1, 2026, the Task Force shall submit a report of its
2 findings and recommendations to the Governor and, in accordance with § 2–1257 of the
3 State Government Article, to the Senate Budget and Taxation Committee, the House
4 Appropriations Committee, and the House Ways and Means Committee.
5 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect July
6 1, 2026. It shall remain effective for a period of 1 year and, at the end of June 30, 2027,
7 with no further action required by the General Assembly, this Act shall be abrogated and
8 of no further force and effect.
Approved:
