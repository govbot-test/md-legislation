CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Anne Arundel County Board of Education – Constituent Services
3 Liaison – Establishment
4 FOR the purpose of requiring the Anne Arundel County Board of Education to hire a
5 constituent services liaison before the beginning of a certain school year; requiring
6 the president of the county board, in consultation with counsel for the county board,
7 to negotiate a certain employment agreement; providing that the constituent
8 services liaison is subject to removal by the president of the county board under
9 certain circumstances; requiring the constituent services liaison to provide certain
10 services and to have certain experience before being hired by the county board;
11 providing for the salary of the constituent services liaison; and generally relating to
12 the constituent services liaison of the Anne Arundel County Board of Education.
13 BY adding to
14 Article – Education
15 Section 3–2A–09

2 HOUSE BILL 1463
1 3–2A–09.
2 (A) (1) SUBJECT TO PARAGRAPH (2) OF THIS SUBSECTION AND BEFORE
3 THE BEGINNING OF THE 2026–2027 SCHOOL YEAR, THE COUNTY BOARD SHALL HIRE
4 A CONSTITUENT SERVICES LIAISON.
5 (2) THE PRESIDENT OF THE COUNTY BOARD, IN CONSULTATION WITH
6 COUNSEL FOR THE COUNTY BOARD, SHALL NEGOTIATE THE TERMS OF THE
7 EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT FOR THE CONSTITUENT SERVICES LIAISON.
8 (B) THE CONSTITUENT SERVICES LIAISON SHALL BE SUBJECT TO REMOVAL
9 BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE COUNTY BOARD IN ACCORDANCE WITH THE TERMS OF
10 THE EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT REQUIRED UNDER SUBSECTION (A)(2) OF THIS
11 SECTION.
12 (C) THE CONSTITUENT SERVICES LIAISON SHALL:
13 (1) ACT AS THE LIAISON BETWEEN THE COUNTY BOARD AND
14 MEMBERS OF THE COMMUNITY;
15 (2) ASSIST COMMUNITY MEMBERS IN ACCESSING SERVICES OF ANNE
16 ARUNDEL COUNTY PUBLIC SCHOOLS AND OTHER STATE AND COUNTY
17 EDUCATION–RELATED SERVICES;
18 (3) ENGAGE COMMUNITY PARTNERS AND LOCAL ORGANIZATIONS TO
19 ADVANCE THE STRATEGIC GOALS OF ANNE ARUNDEL COUNTY PUBLIC SCHOOLS;
20 AND
21 (4) HAVE EXPERIENCE WORKING IN SPECIAL EDUCATION BEFORE
22 BEING HIRED UNDER THIS SECTION.
23 (D) THE CONSTITUENT SERVICES LIAISON SHALL RECEIVE AN ANNUAL
24 SALARY SET BY THE COUNTY BOARD.
25 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect July
26 1, 2026.
