A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Correctional Services – Reentry Readiness Program
3 FOR the purpose of establishing the Reentry Readiness Program in the Department of
4 Public Safety and Correctional Services; requiring the Department to work jointly
5 with the Motor Vehicle Administration, the Division of Vital Records in the
6 Maryland Department of Health, and the Department of Labor to staff and
7 administer the Program; and generally relating to the Reentry Readiness Program.
8 BY adding to
9 Article – Correctional Services
10 Section 9–619
11 Annotated Code of Maryland
12 (2025 Replacement Volume)
13 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
14 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
15 Article – Correctional Services
16 9–619.
17 (A) (1) IN THIS SECTION THE FOLLOWING WORDS HAVE THE MEANINGS
18 INDICATED.
19 (2) “PROGRAM” MEANS THE REENTRY READINESS PROGRAM.
20 (3) “RELEASING INSTITUTION” MEANS A STATE CORRECTIONAL
21 FACILITY.

2 SENATE BILL 944
1 (4) “RETURNING CITIZEN” MEANS AN INDIVIDUAL CONFINED IN A
2 STATE CORRECTIONAL FACILITY WHO IS EXPECTED TO BE RELEASED WITHIN 90
3 DAYS.
4 (B) (1) THERE IS A REENTRY READINESS PROGRAM IN THE
5 DEPARTMENT.
6 (2) THE DEPARTMENT SHALL WORK JOINTLY WITH THE MOTOR
7 VEHICLE ADMINISTRATION, THE DIVISION OF VITAL RECORDS IN THE MARYLAND
8 DEPARTMENT OF HEALTH, AND THE DEPARTMENT OF LABOR TO STAFF AND
9 ADMINISTER THE PROGRAM.
10 (C) (1) THE PROGRAM STAFF SHALL ASSIST EACH RETURNING CITIZEN
11 IN:
12 (I) ENROLLING IN VOCATIONAL, WORKFORCE TRAINING, AND
13 JOB PLACEMENT PROGRAMS;
14 (II) OBTAINING A CERTIFIED COPY OF THE RETURNING
15 CITIZEN’S BIRTH CERTIFICATE; AND
16 (III) SUBJECT TO PARAGRAPH (3) OF THIS SUBSECTION,
17 OBTAINING A PHOTO IDENTIFICATION OR DRIVER’S LICENSE.
18 (2) AT LEAST 90 DAYS BEFORE THE ANTICIPATED RELEASE FROM
19 CONFINEMENT OF A RETURNING CITIZEN, PROGRAM STAFF WITHIN THE RELEASING
20 INSTITUTION SHALL ASSIST A RETURNING CITIZEN IN COMPLETING AND
21 SUBMITTING ANY NECESSARY APPLICATIONS AND DOCUMENTS FOR ENROLLING IN
22 A VOCATIONAL, WORKFORCE TRAINING, OR JOB PLACEMENT PROGRAM.
23 (3) AT LEAST 60 DAYS BEFORE THE ANTICIPATED RELEASE FROM
24 CONFINEMENT OF A RETURNING CITIZEN, THE COMMISSIONER AND THE PROGRAM
25 STAFF WITHIN A RELEASING INSTITUTION SHALL:
26 (I) PROVIDE THE RETURNING CITIZEN THE APPROPRIATE
27 FORMS FOR OBTAINING A PHOTO IDENTIFICATION CARD UNDER § 12–301 OF THE
28 TRANSPORTATION ARTICLE OR A DRIVER’S LICENSE FROM THE MOTOR VEHICLE
29 ADMINISTRATION; AND
30 (II) ASSIST THE RETURNING CITIZEN IN COMPLETING THE
31 FORMS.
