HOUSE BILL 1484
E2, E1 6lr1970
By: Delegates Arentz, Buckel, Hornberger, Howard, McComas, Reilly, and Rose
Introduced and read first time: February 13, 2026
Assigned to: Judiciary
A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Child Sex Offenders – Plea Agreements and Places for Children
3 FOR the purpose of providing that a State’s Attorney may not enter into a certain plea
4 agreement; prohibiting a certain person required to register as a sex offender from
5 knowingly operating or entering a place for children; and generally relating to child
6 sex offenders.
7 BY adding to
8 Article – Criminal Law
9 Section 3–1104
10 Annotated Code of Maryland
11 (2021 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
12 BY adding to
13 Article – Criminal Procedure
14 Section 11–722.1
15 Annotated Code of Maryland
16 (2025 Replacement Volume)
17 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
18 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
19 Article – Criminal Law
20 3–1104.
21 IN A PROSECUTION BROUGHT FOR AN OFFENSE UNDER THIS SUBTITLE
22 COMMITTED AGAINST A MINOR, A STATE’S ATTORNEY MAY NOT ENTER INTO A PLEA
23 AGREEMENT THAT WOULD RESULT IN THE DISMISSAL OR NOLLE PROSEQUI OF ANY
24 CHARGE UNDER THIS SUBTITLE.

2 HOUSE BILL 1484
1 Article – Criminal Procedure
2 11–722.1.
3 (A) (1) IN THIS SECTION, “PLACE FOR CHILDREN” MEANS A LOCATION
4 SPECIFICALLY INTENDED TO BE USED OR VISITED BY MINORS.
5 (2) “PLACE FOR CHILDREN” INCLUDES:
6 (I) A PLAYGROUND;
7 (II) A THEME PARK; AND
8 (III) A CHILDREN’S MUSEUM.
9 (B) A PERSON WHO HAS BEEN CONVICTED OF AN OFFENSE IN WHICH THE
10 VICTIM OF THE OFFENSE WAS A MINOR AND THE OFFENSE REQUIRED REGISTRATION
11 UNDER THIS SUBTITLE MAY NOT KNOWINGLY OPERATE OR ENTER A PLACE FOR
12 CHILDREN.
13 (C) A PERSON WHO VIOLATES THIS SECTION IS GUILTY OF A FELONY AND
14 ON CONVICTION IS SUBJECT TO IMPRISONMENT NOT EXCEEDING 5 YEARS OR A FINE
15 NOT EXCEEDING $5,000 OR BOTH.
16 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
17 October 1, 2026.
