A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Criminal Procedure – Domestic Violence Offenders – Registration
3 FOR the purpose of requiring a certain person to register as a domestic violence offender
4 with a certain supervising authority; requiring certain information to be included in
5 a registration statement; establishing certain terms of registration; requiring the
6 Department of Public Safety and Correctional Services to make available to the
7 public and post to the Internet certain registration information; and generally
8 relating to the domestic violence offender registration.

5 FAIL TO PROVIDE ANY INFORMATION TO BE INCLUDED IN A REGISTRATION

8 (B) A PERSON WHO VIOLATES THIS SECTION:
9 (1) FOR A FIRST OFFENSE, IS SUBJECT TO A CIVIL FINE NOT
10 EXCEEDING $250;
11 (2) FOR A SECOND OFFENSE, IS SUBJECT TO A CIVIL FINE NOT
12 EXCEEDING $500; AND
13 (3) FOR A THIRD OR SUBSEQUENT OFFENSE, IS GUILTY OF A
14 MISDEMEANOR AND IS SUBJECT TO IMPRISONMENT NOT EXCEEDING 90 DAYS OR A
15 FINE NOT EXCEEDING $1,000 OR BOTH.
