CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Hospitals – Clinical Clinical Staffing Committees and Plans – Establishment
3 (Safe Staffing Act of 2026)
4 FOR the purpose of requiring certain hospitals licensed in the State to comply with certain
5 staffing standards, to establish and maintain a clinical clinical staffing committee,
6 and to implement a clinical clinical staffing plan; requiring the chief nursing
7 executive of each hospital to produce a draft clinical staffing plan and submit it to the
8 clinical staffing committee of the hospital; requiring each clinical clinical staffing
9 committee to develop finalize a clinical clinical staffing plan; and generally relating
10 to hospitals and clinical clinical staffing committees and plans.
