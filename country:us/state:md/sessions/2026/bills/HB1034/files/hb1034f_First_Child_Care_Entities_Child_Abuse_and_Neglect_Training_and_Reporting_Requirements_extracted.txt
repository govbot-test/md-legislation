A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Child Care Entities – Child Abuse and Neglect – Training and Reporting
3 Requirements
4 FOR the purpose of requiring a child care provider to complete certain training on the
5 identification, prevention, and reporting of child abuse at certain intervals; requiring
6 each child care entity to certify to the Division of Early Childhood Development in
7 the State Department of Education that each provider is in compliance with the
8 training requirements; establishing certain penalties for providers and child care
9 entities who violate certain training and reporting requirements; requiring a
10 provider to provide certain notice or make certain reports of suspected child abuse
11 or neglect; and generally relating to child abuse and neglect.
12 BY adding to
13 Article – Education
14 Section 9.5–118
15 Annotated Code of Maryland
16 (2022 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
17 BY repealing and reenacting, without amendments,
18 Article – Family Law

22 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
23 Article – Family Law

17 (1) WITHIN 6 MONTHS AFTER THE INITIAL DATE OF EMPLOYMENT

22 COMPLIANCE WITH THIS SECTION.

24 (1) A CORRECTIVE ACTION PLAN;
25 (2) SUSPENSION OR TERMINATION OF EMPLOYMENT; OR
