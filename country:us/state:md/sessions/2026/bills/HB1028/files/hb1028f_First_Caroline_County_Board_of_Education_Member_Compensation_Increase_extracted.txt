A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Caroline County Board of Education – Member Compensation – Increase
3 FOR the purpose of increasing the annual compensation of the president and voting
4 members of the Caroline County Board of Education; and generally relating to the
5 compensation of the members of the Caroline County Board of Education.
6 BY repealing and reenacting, without amendments,
7 Article – Education
8 Section 3–3A–02(a)
9 Annotated Code of Maryland
10 (2025 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
11 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
12 Article – Education
13 Section 3–3A–05(a)
14 Annotated Code of Maryland
15 (2025 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
16 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
17 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
18 Article – Education
19 3–3A–02.
20 (a) The Caroline County Board of Education consists of:
21 (1) Three elected members;
22 (2) Two appointed members; and
EXPLANATION: CAPITALS INDICATE MATTER ADDED TO EXISTING LAW.
[Brackets] indicate matter deleted from exist in g l aw . *hb1028*
2 HOUSE BILL 1028
1 (3) Two nonvoting student members.
2 3–3A–05.
3 (a) The president of the county board is entitled to receive [$4,000] $6,000
4 annually as compensation, and the other voting members are entitled to receive [$3,500]
5 $5,500 each annually as compensation.
6 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That, in accordance with Article
7 III, § 35 of the Maryland Constitution, this Act may not be construed to extend or apply to
8 the salary or compensation of the president or other voting members of the Caroline County
9 Board of Education while serving in a term of office beginning before the effective date of
10 this Act, but the provisions of this Act concerning the salary or compensation of the
11 president and other voting members of the Caroline County Board of Education shall take
12 effect at the beginning of the next following term of office. This limitation does not apply to
13 an individual appointed or elected after the effective date of this Act to fill out an unexpired
14 term.
15 SECTION 3. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect July
16 1, 2026.
