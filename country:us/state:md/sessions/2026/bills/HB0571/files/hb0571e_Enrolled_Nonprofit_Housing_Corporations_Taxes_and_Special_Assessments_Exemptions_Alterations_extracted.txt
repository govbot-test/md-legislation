CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Nonprofit Housing Corporations – Tax and Enforcement of Judgment Taxes and
3 Special Assessments Exemptions – Alterations
4 FOR the purpose of altering the definition of “nonprofit housing corporation” for purposes
5 of eligibility for an exemption from real property taxes and special assessments of
6 the State or a political subdivision; exempting certain real property owned by certain
7 subsidiary entities of a nonprofit housing corporation from taxes and special
8 assessments of the State or a political subdivision; exempting certain real property
9 of a nonprofit housing corporation from certain procedures for enforcement of a
10 judgment against the nonprofit housing corporation; and generally relating to tax
11 and enforcement of judgment taxes and special assessments exemptions for
12 nonprofit housing corporations.
13 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
14 Article – Housing and Community Development
