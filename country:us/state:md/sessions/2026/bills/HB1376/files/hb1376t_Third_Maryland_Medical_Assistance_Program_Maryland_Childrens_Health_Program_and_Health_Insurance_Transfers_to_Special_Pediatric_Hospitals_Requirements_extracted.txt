CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Maryland Medical Assistance Program, Maryland Children’s Health Program,
3 and Health Insurance – Transfers to Special Pediatric Hospitals – Requirements
4 FOR the purpose of requiring the Maryland Medical Assistance Program, and the
5 Maryland Children’s Health Program, managed care organizations, and certain
6 health insurers, nonprofit health service plans, and health maintenance
7 organizations to provide a prior authorization determination regarding a transfer on
8 request of a special pediatric hospital or referring health care provider, to provide a
9 retrospective review of a determination of medical necessity under certain
10 circumstances, and to reimburse a special pediatric hospital or a referring health
11 care provider for administrative days at the applicable rate for a certain period
12 within a certain number of business days; requiring the Maryland Department of
13 Health to adopt regulations, on or before a certain date, establishing a retrospective
14 review process for special pediatric hospitals; and generally relating to transfers to
15 special pediatric hospitals.
