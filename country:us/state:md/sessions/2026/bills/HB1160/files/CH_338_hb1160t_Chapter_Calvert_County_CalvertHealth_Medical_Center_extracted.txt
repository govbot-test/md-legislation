Section 1:
WES MOORE, Governor Ch. 338
Chapter 338
AN ACT concerning
Calvert County – CalvertHealth Medical Center
FOR the purpose of altering the statutory reference to the name Calvert Memorial Hospital
to be CalvertHealth Medical Center; and generally relating to references to the
CalvertHealth Medical Center.
BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
The Public Local Laws of Calvert County

Section 2:
Section 5–202
Article 5 – Public Local Laws of Maryland
(2002 Edition and October 2024 Supplement, as amended)

Section 3:
SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
Article 5 – Calvert County
5–202.
The County Commissioners may appropriate a sum in an amount to be determined
by them to the [Calvert Memorial Hospital] CALVERTHEALTH MEDICAL CENTER, INC.,
AND ITS SUCCESSORS.

Section 4:
SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
October 1, 2026.
Approved by the Governor, April 28, 2026.
