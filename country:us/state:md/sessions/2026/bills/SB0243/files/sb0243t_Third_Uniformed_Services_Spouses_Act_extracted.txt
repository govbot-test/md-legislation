1 AN ACT concerning
2 Uniformed Services Spouses Act
3 FOR the purpose of applying certain provisions of law regarding priority registration at
4 public institutions of higher education, resources and support at community colleges,
5 senatorial and Delegate scholarships, and preferences in hiring by the Public Service
6 Commission to the spouses of active service members and veterans; altering the
7 veterans to whom certain provisions of law regarding priority registration at public
8 institutions of higher education, resources and support at community colleges,
9 senatorial and Delegate scholarships, preferences in hiring by private employers,
10 and credits on selection tests for State personnel apply; and generally relating to
11 active service members, veterans, and the spouses of veterans and service members.
