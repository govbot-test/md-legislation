CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Elopement Response Devices – Insurance and Maryland Medical Assistance
3 Program Coverage and Provision Under State Programs
4 Virginia I. Jones Alzheimer’s Disease and Related Dementias Council and the
5 Advisory Stakeholder Group on Autism–Related Needs – Recommendations for
6 Elopement Response Devices
7 FOR the purpose of requiring that certain State programs consider approved elopement
8 response devices as a medical device, durable medical equipment, or assistive
9 technology for purposes of the State programs; requiring the Maryland Medical
10 Assistance Program, subject to certain limitations, to provide coverage for approved
11 elopement response devices and supports; establishing requirements and
12 prohibitions regarding the Program coverage for approved elopement response
13 devices; requiring certain insurers, nonprofit health service plans, and health
14 maintenance organizations to provide coverage for approved elopement response
15 devices and associated supports to certain individuals under certain circumstances
16 authorizing the Virginia I. Jones Alzheimer’s Disease and Related Dementias
17 Council and the Advisory Stakeholder Group on Autism–Related Needs to
18 recommend the use of elopement response devices as safety tools for certain
19 individuals who are at risk of elopement; and generally relating to elopement
20 response devices.
