WES MOORE, Governor Ch. 250
Chapter 250
AN ACT concerning
On–Farm Organics and Wasted Food Reduction and Diversion and Recycling
Grant Programs Program – Established
FOR the purpose of establishing the On–Farm Organics Diversion and Recycling Grant
Program in the Department of Agriculture to award grants to eligible entities to
develop and implement on–farm organics recycling and compost use, wasted food
prevention, and food rescue; establishing the Wasted Food Reduction and Diversion
Grant Program and the County Wasted Food Reduction Block Grant Program in the
Department of the Environment to award grants and fund certain projects for
infrastructure, programs, and education that reduce, rescue, and divert wasted food
in the State and certain counties; and generally relating to wasted food reduction
and diversion on–farm organics recycling and compost use, wasted food prevention,
and food rescue in the State.
