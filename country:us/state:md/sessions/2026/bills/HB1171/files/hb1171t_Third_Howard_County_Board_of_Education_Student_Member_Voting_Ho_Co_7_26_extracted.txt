2 Howard County Board of Education – Student Member – Voting

17 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,

19 Article – Education
EXPLANATION: CAPITALS INDICATE MATTER ADDED TO EXISTING LAW.
[Brackets] indicate matter deleted from existing law.
Underlining indicates amendments to bill.
Strike out indicates matter stricken from the bill by amendment or deleted from the law by

2 HOUSE BILL 1171
1 3–701.
2 (a) (1) The Howard County Board consists of:
3 (i) Seven elected members; and
4 (ii) One student member.
5 (f) (7) The student member shall vote on all matters except those relating to:
6 (i) [Geographical attendance areas under § 4–109 of this article;
7 (ii) Acquisition and disposition of real property and matters
8 pertaining to school construction under § 4–115 of this article;
9 (iii)] Employment of architects under § 4–117 of this article;
10 [(iv) Donations under § 4–118 of this article;
11 (v) Condemnation under § 4–119 of this article;
12 (vi) Consolidation of schools and transportation of students under §
13 4–120 of this article;
14 (vii)] (II) Appointment and salary of a county superintendent under
15 §§ 4–201 and 4–202 of this article;
16 [(viii)] (III) Employee discipline and other appeals under § 4–205(c) of
17 this article;
18 [(ix) Budgetary matters under Title 5 of this article;
19 (x)] (IV) Appointment and promotion of staff under § 6–201 of this
20 article;
21 [(xi)] (V) Discipline of certificated staff under § 6–202 of this article;
22 [(xii)] (VI) Collective bargaining for certificated employees under
23 Title 6, Subtitle 4 of this article; AND
24 [(xiii)] (VII) Collective bargaining for noncertificated employees under
25 Title 6, Subtitle 5 of this article[; and
26 (xiv) Student suspension and expulsion under § 7–305 of this article].
HOUSE BILL 1171 3
1 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect July
2 1, 2026.
