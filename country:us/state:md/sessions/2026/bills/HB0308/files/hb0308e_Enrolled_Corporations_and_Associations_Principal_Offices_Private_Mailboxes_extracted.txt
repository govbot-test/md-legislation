CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Corporations and Associations – Principal Offices – Private Mailboxes
3 FOR the purpose of authorizing an address in this State supplied by a commercial mail
4 receiving agency to be designated as the principal office of a Maryland corporation,
5 limited liability company, limited liability partnership, or limited partnership, under
6 certain circumstances; providing for the disclosure of certain contact information by
7 a commercial mail receiving agency; and generally relating to the principal offices of
8 corporate entities.

16 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
17 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
18 Article – Corporations and Associations
19 1–101.
20 (a) In this article, unless the context clearly requires otherwise, the following
21 words have the meanings indicated.
22 (H–1) “COMMERCIAL MAIL RECEIVING AGENCY” MEANS A PERSON THAT IS
23 AUTHORIZED BY THE U.S. POSTAL SERVICE TO ACCEPT THE DELIVERY OF MAIL ON
24 BEHALF OF ANOTHER PERSON AS A BUSINESS SERVICE.
25 (w) “Principal office” means:
26 (1) The place in this State filed or recorded with the Department as the
27 principal office of a Maryland corporation, limited liability company, limited liability
28 partnership, or limited partnership; [or]
29 (2) SUBJECT TO § 1–407 OF THIS TITLE, AN AN ADDRESS IN THIS
30 STATE SUPPLIED BY A COMMERCIAL MAIL RECEIVING AGENCY WITH WHICH A
31 MARYLAND CORPORATION, LIMITED LIABILITY COMPANY, LIMITED LIABILITY
32 PARTNERSHIP, OR LIMITED PARTNERSHIP HAS A VALID COMMERCIAL MAIL
33 RECEIVING AGREEMENT IN ACCORDANCE WITH U.S. POSTAL SERVICE
34 REGULATIONS; OR

1 (3) If there is no principal office designated UNDER ITEM (1) OF THIS
2 SUBSECTION OR ADDRESS SUPPLIED UNDER ITEM (2) OF THIS SUBSECTION, the main
3 office of the Maryland corporation, limited liability company, limited liability partnership,
4 or limited partnership in this State for the transaction of business.
5 1–407.
6 (A) IN THIS SECTION, “COMMERCIAL MAIL RECEIVING AGENCY” MEANS A
7 PERSON THAT IS AUTHORIZED BY THE U.S. POSTAL SERVICE TO ACCEPT THE
8 DELIVERY OF MAIL ON BEHALF OF ANOTHER PERSON AS A BUSINESS SERVICE.
9 (B) IF A PERSON DESIGNATES AN ADDRESS PROVIDED BY A COMMERCIAL
10 MAIL RECEIVING AGENCY AS A PRINCIPAL OFFICE FOR PURPOSES OF THIS ARTICLE,
11 THE PERSON ACKNOWLEDGES THAT THE MAIL RECEIVING AGENCY SHALL PROVIDE
12 THE CONTACT INFORMATION INCLUDED IN USPS FORM 1583 TO THE DEPARTMENT
13 OR A LAW ENFORCEMENT AGENCY ON REQUEST.
14 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
15 October 1, 2026.
