CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Sickle Cell Disease – Institutions of Higher Education – Policies, Procedures,
3 and Educational Campaigns
4 (Kaitlyn’s Law)
5 FOR the purpose of prohibiting an institution of higher education from denying access or
6 services to a student based on the student’s diagnosis of sickle cell disease; requiring
7 an institution of higher education to provide reasonable accommodations for
8 students with sickle cell disease; requiring the Maryland Department of Health to
9 develop an education and awareness campaign for institutions of higher education
10 in the State regarding students with sickle cell disease; requiring the Department,
11 in consultation with the Maryland Higher Education Commission, the Statewide
12 Steering Committee on Sickle Cell Disease, and certain community organizations to
13 develop a framework and procedures for institutions of higher education in the State
14 to follow in assessing, supporting, and treating students with sickle cell disease;
15 requiring the Department to develop or compile materials to provide a
16 comprehensive education on sickle cell disease to certain faculty and staff at
17 institutions of higher education in the State; and generally relating to sickle cell
18 disease and institutions of higher education.
19 BY adding to
20 Article – Education
21 Section 15–141
22 Annotated Code of Maryland
23 (2022 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)

6 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
7 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
8 Article – Education
9 15–141.
10 (A) AN INSTITUTION OF HIGHER EDUCATION:
11 (1) SHALL PROVIDE REASONABLE ACCOMMODATIONS TO A STUDENT
12 WITH SICKLE CELL DISEASE; AND
13 (2) MAY NOT DENY ACCESS TO FACILITIES OR SERVICES TO A
14 STUDENT BASED ON THE STUDENT’S DIAGNOSIS OF SICKLE CELL DISEASE.
15 (B) AN INSTITUTION OF HIGHER EDUCATION SHALL ESTABLISH A CLEAR
16 AND ACCESSIBLE PROCEDURE THROUGH WHICH A PERSON MAY REPORT AN
17 ALLEGED VIOLATION OF SUBSECTION (A) OF THIS SECTION.
18 (C) AN INSTITUTION OF HIGHER EDUCATION SHALL PUBLICIZE IN A
19 MANNER THAT IS CLEAR AND ACCESSIBLE TO STUDENTS:
20 (1) THE REQUIREMENTS OF SUBSECTION (A) OF THIS SECTION; AND
21 (2) THE REPORTING PROCEDURE ESTABLISHED UNDER SUBSECTION
22 (B) OF THIS SECTION.
23 Article – Health – General
24 18–510.
25 (A) THE DEPARTMENT, IN CONSULTATION WITH SICKLE CELL DISEASE
26 TREATMENT PROVIDERS AND COMMUNITY–BASED ORGANIZATIONS, SHALL
27 DEVELOP AN EDUCATION AND AWARENESS CAMPAIGN FOR INSTITUTIONS OF
28 HIGHER EDUCATION IN THE STATE REGARDING STUDENTS WITH SICKLE CELL
29 DISEASE.
HOUSE BILL 60 3
1 (B) THE CAMPAIGN DEVELOPED UNDER SUBSECTION (A) OF THIS SECTION
2 SHALL:
3 (1) DESCRIBE CHALLENGES FACED BY YOUNG ADULTS LIVING WITH
4 SICKLE CELL DISEASE, WITH PARTICULAR EMPHASIS ON CHALLENGES ARISING
5 DURING SICKLE CELL DISEASE–RELATED HEALTH CRISES AND OTHER CHRONIC
6 CONDITIONS THAT MAY COMPOUND THESE CHALLENGES; AND
7 (2) PROVIDE INFORMATION ON ACCOMMODATIONS AND SUPPORT
8 THAT INSTITUTIONS OF HIGHER EDUCATION CAN OFFER TO STUDENTS WITH SICKLE
9 CELL DISEASE TO ENABLE THE STUDENTS TO SUCCEED ACADEMICALLY WHILE
10 EFFECTIVELY MANAGING SICKLE CELL DISEASE.
11 18–511.
12 (A) THE DEPARTMENT, IN CONSULTATION WITH THE MARYLAND HIGHER
13 EDUCATION COMMISSION, THE STATEWIDE STEERING COMMITTEE ON SICKLE
14 CELL DISEASE, AND SICKLE CELL DISEASE COMMUNITY ORGANIZATIONS BASED IN
15 THE STATE, SHALL DEVELOP A FRAMEWORK AND PROCEDURES FOR INSTITUTIONS
16 OF HIGHER EDUCATION IN THE STATE TO FOLLOW IN ASSESSING, SUPPORTING, AND
17 TREATING STUDENTS WITH SICKLE CELL DISEASE.
18 (B) THE DEPARTMENT, THE MARYLAND HIGHER EDUCATION
19 COMMISSION, AND THE STATEWIDE STEERING COMMITTEE ON SICKLE CELL
20 DISEASE JOINTLY SHALL:
21 (1) BEGINNING DECEMBER 1, 2026, PROVIDE TECHNICAL
22 ASSISTANCE TO INSTITUTIONS OF HIGHER EDUCATION TO:
23 (I) IMPLEMENT THE PROCEDURES DEVELOPED UNDER THIS
24 SECTION; AND
25 (II) INSTRUCT PERSONNEL OF INSTITUTIONS OF HIGHER
26 EDUCATION REGARDING THE PROCEDURES DEVELOPED UNDER THIS SECTION; AND
27 (2) ON OR BEFORE DECEMBER 1, 2026, DEVELOP A PROCESS TO
28 MONITOR THE IMPLEMENTATION OF THE PROCEDURES BY INSTITUTIONS OF
29 HIGHER EDUCATION.
30 18–512.
31 (A) THE DEPARTMENT, IN CONSULTATION WITH THE STATEWIDE
32 STEERING COMMITTEE ON SICKLE CELL DISEASE AND SICKLE CELL DISEASE
4 HOUSE BILL 60
1 COMMUNITY ORGANIZATIONS BASED IN THE STATE, SHALL DEVELOP OR COMPILE
2 MATERIALS TO PROVIDE HEALTH PERSONNEL, FACULTY, AND ADMINISTRATIVE
3 STAFF EMPLOYED AT AN INSTITUTION OF HIGHER EDUCATION IN THE STATE WITH
4 A COMPREHENSIVE EDUCATION ON SICKLE CELL DISEASE.
5 (B) THE MATERIALS DEVELOPED OR COMPILED UNDER THIS SECTION
6 SHALL BE DESIGNED TO ENHANCE UNDERSTANDING OF SICKLE CELL DISEASE,
7 REDUCE STIGMAS, AND PROMOTE AN ALL–ENCOMPASSING AND SUPPORTIVE
8 LEARNING ENVIRONMENT FOR STUDENTS WITH SICKLE CELL DISEASE.
9 (C) EACH INSTITUTION OF HIGHER EDUCATION IS ENCOURAGED TO
10 CONDUCT PERIODIC ASSESSMENTS, SURVEILLANCE ACTIVITIES, AWARENESS
11 CAMPAIGNS, AND EDUCATIONAL SESSIONS BASED ON THE MATERIALS DEVELOPED
12 OR COMPILED UNDER THIS SECTION AS FINANCIAL RESOURCES ALLOW.
13 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
14 October 1, 2026.
