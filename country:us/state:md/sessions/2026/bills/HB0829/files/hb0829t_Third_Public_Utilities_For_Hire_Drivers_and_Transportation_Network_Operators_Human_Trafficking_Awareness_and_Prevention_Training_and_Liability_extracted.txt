CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Public Utilities – For–Hire Drivers and Transportation Network Operators –
3 Human Trafficking Awareness and Prevention Training and Liability
4 FOR the purpose of requiring an applicant for certain for–hire driver’s licenses and
5 transportation network operator’s licenses to submit to the Public Service
6 Commission documentation verifying that the applicant completed a certain human
7 trafficking awareness training program; prohibiting licensed taxicab drivers,
8 for–hire drivers, or transportation network operators and applicants for a
9 transportation network operator’s license from being held civilly or criminally liable
10 for reporting or responding to in good faith a suspected incident of human trafficking;
11 prohibiting a transportation network company from allowing certain applicants for
12 and holders of a transportation network operator’s license to provide transportation
13 network services using a digital network application if the applicant or license holder
14 has not completed a certain human trafficking awareness training program; and
15 generally relating to for–hire drivers and transportation network operators.

17 Article – Public Utilities
18 Section 10–404(g) and (h)
19 to be Section 10–404(h) and (i), respectively

8 ORGANIZATION WITH EXPERTISE IN HUMAN TRAFFICKING AWARENESS TRAINING.
9 (3) A HUMAN TRAFFICKING AWARENESS TRAINING THAT AN
10 APPLICANT OR LICENSE HOLDER COMPLETES UNDER PARAGRAPH (2) OF THIS
11 SUBSECTION MUST INCLUDE INFORMATION ON HOW TO:
12 (I) RECOGNIZE POTENTIAL VICTIMS OF HUMAN TRAFFICKING;
13 (II) RESPOND TO AN INDIVIDUAL WHO MAY BE OR IS A VICTIM OF
14 HUMAN TRAFFICKING; AND
15 (III) CONNECT AN INDIVIDUAL WHO MAY BE OR IS A VICTIM OF
16 HUMAN TRAFFICKING WITH AVAILABLE RESOURCES.
17 (4) AN APPLICANT FOR A TRANSPORTATION NETWORK OPERATOR’S
18 LICENSE OR THE HOLDER OF A TEMPORARY OR PERMANENT TRANSPORTATION
19 NETWORK OPERATOR’S LICENSE MAY NOT BE HELD CIVILLY OR CRIMINALLY LIABLE
20 FOR REPORTING OR RESPONDING IN GOOD FAITH TO A SUSPECTED INCIDENT OF
21 HUMAN TRAFFICKING.
22 SECTION 3. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
23 October 1, 2026.
