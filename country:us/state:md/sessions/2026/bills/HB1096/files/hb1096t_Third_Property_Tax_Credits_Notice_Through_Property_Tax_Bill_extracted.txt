CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Property Tax Credits – Notice Through Property Tax Bill
3 FOR the purpose of requiring the Mayor and City Council of Baltimore City or the
4 governing body of a county to give notice of certain property tax credits for which a
5 taxpayer may be eligible through the property tax bill; collector for each county to
6 include in the written materials sent with each property tax bill a website address
7 or quick–response code for a county government webpage that lists each property tax
8 credit that is available to taxpayers in the county and includes an explanation of how
9 to apply for the tax credits; and generally relating to notice of property tax credits.
10 BY renumbering
11 Article – Tax – Property
12 Section 9–101 and 9–401
13 to be Section 9–101.1 and 9–401.1, respectively
14 Annotated Code of Maryland
15 (2019 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
16 BY adding to
17 Article – Tax – Property
18 Section 9–101 and 9–401 4–201.3
19 Annotated Code of Maryland
20 (2019 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
21 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,

1 Article – Tax – Property
2 Section 9–201 and 9–301(d)

8 Annotated Code of Maryland

10 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
11 That Section(s) 9–101 and 9–401 of Article – Tax – Property of the Annotated Code of
12 Maryland be renumbered to be Section(s) 9–101.1 and 9–401.1, respectively.
13 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That the Laws of Maryland read
14 as follows:
15 Article – Tax – Property
16 9–101.
17 (A) EXCEPT AS OTHERWISE SPECIFICALLY PROVIDED, THE PROVISIONS OF
18 THIS SECTION APPLY TO PROPERTY TAX CREDITS GRANTED UNDER THIS SUBTITLE.
19 (B) EXCEPT AS PROVIDED IN SUBSECTION (C) OF THIS SECTION, WHEN A
20 PROPERTY TAX BILL IS SENT TO A TAXPAYER WHO MAY BE ELIGIBLE FOR A
21 PROPERTY TAX CREDIT UNDER THIS SUBTITLE, THE MAYOR AND CITY COUNCIL OF
22 BALTIMORE CITY OR THE GOVERNING BODY OF A COUNTY SHALL INCLUDE WITH
23 THE PROPERTY TAX BILL WRITTEN NOTICE OF EACH PROPERTY TAX CREDIT UNDER
24 THIS SUBTITLE FOR WHICH THE TAXPAYER MAY BE ELIGIBLE.
25 (C) IF A PERSON WHO IS NOT THE TAXPAYER IS THE RECIPIENT OF THE
26 PROPERTY TAX BILL, THE MAYOR AND CITY COUNCIL OF BALTIMORE CITY OR THE
27 GOVERNING BODY OF A COUNTY SHALL INCLUDE WITH THE PROPERTY TAX BILL THE
28 NOTICE REQUIRED UNDER SUBSECTION (B) OF THIS SECTION TO THAT PERSON AND
29 THAT PERSON SHALL NOTIFY THE TAXPAYER IN WRITING.
30 9–201.
31 (a) (1) In this [section] SUBSECTION, “property tax credit” means a property
32 tax credit or exemption that is granted under § 9–205 or § 9–209 of this subtitle or Subtitle
33 3 of this title.
HOUSE BILL 1096 3
1 [(b)] (2) Annually on or before October 31, the Mayor and City Council of
2 Baltimore City or each governing body that grants a property tax credit shall submit to the
3 Department on the form that the Department provides the following information for the
4 current taxable year:
5 [(1)] (I) the total value of all property tax credits granted;
6 [(2)] (II) an itemized list of all of the property tax credits granted for real
7 property; and
8 [(3)] (III) an itemized list of the property tax credits granted for personal
9 property.
10 [(c)] (3) The Mayor and City Council of Baltimore City or each governing body
11 that grants a property tax credit shall:
12 [(1)] (I) in the same manner as the assessment roll, make available for
13 public inspection bound copies of the form required by [subsection (b)] PARAGRAPH (2) of
14 this [section] SUBSECTION; and
15 [(2)] (II) identify clearly on the tax roll the properties that are granted a
16 property tax credit [under this section].
17 (B) (1) EXCEPT AS OTHERWISE SPECIFICALLY PROVIDED, THE
18 PROVISIONS OF THIS SUBSECTION APPLY TO PROPERTY TAX CREDITS GRANTED
19 UNDER THIS SUBTITLE.
20 (2) EXCEPT AS PROVIDED IN PARAGRAPH (3) OF THIS SUBSECTION,
21 WHEN A PROPERTY TAX BILL IS SENT TO A TAXPAYER WHO MAY BE ELIGIBLE FOR A
22 COUNTY PROPERTY TAX CREDIT UNDER THIS SUBTITLE, THE MAYOR AND CITY
23 COUNCIL OF BALTIMORE CITY OR THE GOVERNING BODY OF A COUNTY SHALL
24 INCLUDE WITH THE PROPERTY TAX BILL WRITTEN NOTICE OF EACH COUNTY
25 PROPERTY TAX CREDIT UNDER THIS SUBTITLE FOR WHICH THE TAXPAYER MAY BE
26 ELIGIBLE.
27 (3) IF A PERSON WHO IS NOT THE TAXPAYER IS THE RECIPIENT OF
28 THE PROPERTY TAX BILL, THE MAYOR AND CITY COUNCIL OF BALTIMORE CITY OR
29 THE GOVERNING BODY OF A COUNTY SHALL INCLUDE WITH THE PROPERTY TAX BILL
30 THE NOTICE REQUIRED UNDER PARAGRAPH (2) OF THIS SUBSECTION TO THAT
31 PERSON AND THAT PERSON SHALL NOTIFY THE TAXPAYER IN WRITING.
32 9–301.
33 (a) Except as otherwise specifically provided, the provisions of this section apply
34 to property tax credits granted under this subtitle.
