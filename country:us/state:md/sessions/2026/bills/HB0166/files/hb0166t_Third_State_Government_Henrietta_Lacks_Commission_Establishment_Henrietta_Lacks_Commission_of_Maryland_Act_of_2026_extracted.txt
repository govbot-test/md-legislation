1 AN ACT concerning
2 Public Health State Government – Henrietta Lacks Commission – Establishment
3 (Henrietta Lacks Commission of Maryland Act of 2026)
4 FOR the purpose of establishing the Henrietta Lacks Commission to take certain actions
5 related to the legacy of Henrietta Lacks and the use of HeLa cells; and generally
6 relating to the Henrietta Lacks Commission.
7 BY adding to
8 Article – Health – General
9 Section 24–2801 and 24–2802 to be under the new subtitle “Subtitle 28. Henrietta
10 Lacks Commission”

16 Lacks Commission”

18 (2021 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)

20 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:

5 COMMISSION.
6 24–2802. 9–4202.
7 (A) THERE IS A HENRIETTA LACKS COMMISSION.
8 (B) THE COMMISSION CONSISTS OF THE FOLLOWING MEMBERS:
9 (1) ONE ONE MEMBER OF THE SENATE OF MARYLAND, APPOINTED
10 BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE SENATE;
11 (2) ONE ONE MEMBER OF THE HOUSE OF DELEGATES, APPOINTED BY
12 THE SPEAKER OF THE HOUSE;
13 (3) THE THE SECRETARY OF HEALTH SOCIAL AND ECONOMIC
14 MOBILITY, OR THE SECRETARY’S DESIGNEE;
15 (4) THE THE SECRETARY OF COMMERCE, OR THE SECRETARY’S
16 DESIGNEE;
17 (5) THE THE STATE SUPERINTENDENT OF EDUCATION, OR THE
18 SUPERINTENDENT’S DESIGNEE; AND
19 (6) THE CHAIR OF THE STATE ANATOMY BOARD, OR THE CHAIR’S
20 DESIGNEE; AND
21 (7) THE THE FOLLOWING MEMBERS, APPOINTED BY THE GOVERNOR:
22 (I) ONE REPRESENTATIVE OF THE STATE ETHICS
23 COMMISSION;
24 (II) ONE REPRESENTATIVE OF THE LIFE SCIENCES ADVISORY
25 BOARD;
26 (III) ONE ONE REPRESENTATIVE OF THE MARYLAND ECONOMIC
27 DEVELOPMENT COMMISSION;
HOUSE BILL 166 3
1 (IV) (II) ONE ONE REPRESENTATIVE OF THE MARYLAND
2 BOARD OF TOURISM; AND
3 (V) (III) ONE ONE REPRESENTATIVE OF THE MARYLAND
4 STATE ARCHIVES.
5 (C) THE GOVERNOR SECRETARY OF SOCIAL AND ECONOMIC MOBILITY
6 SHALL DESIGNATE THE CHAIR OF THE COMMISSION.
7 (D) THE TERM OF A MEMBER OF THE COMMISSION IS 2 YEARS.
8 (E) THE STATE AGENCIES REPRESENTED ON THE COMMISSION JOINTLY
9 SHALL PROVIDE STAFF FOR THE COMMISSION.
10 (F) A MEMBER OF THE COMMISSION:
11 (1) MAY MAY NOT RECEIVE COMPENSATION AS A MEMBER OF THE
12 COMMISSION; BUT
13 (2) IS IS ENTITLED TO REIMBURSEMENT FOR EXPENSES UNDER THE
14 STANDARD STATE TRAVEL REGULATIONS, AS PROVIDED IN THE STATE BUDGET.
15 (G) THE COMMISSION SHALL:
16 (1) IN CONSULTATION WITH THE O’NEILL INSTITUTE OF NATIONAL
17 AND GLOBAL HEALTH LAW, CONDUCT BIOETHICAL OVERSIGHT OF LIFE SCIENCES
18 ACTIVITIES IN THE STATE THAT INVOLVE HUMAN RESEARCH AND BODY, ORGAN,
19 AND TISSUE DONATION FOR MEDICAL TRAINING;
20 (2) PROMOTE PROMOTE EDUCATION OF THE LIFE OF HENRIETTA
21 LACKS AND HELA SCIENCE AS DEVELOPED BY THE NATIONAL INSTITUTES OF
22 HEALTH;
23 (3) (2) CURATE CURATE A REGISTRY OF SIGNIFICANT TRIBUTES TO
24 HENRIETTA LACKS;
25 (4) (3) WORK WORK WITH APPROPRIATE STATE AGENCIES TO
26 INCLUDE TRIBUTES TO HENRIETTA LACKS IN STATE TOURISM CAMPAIGNS;
27 (5) (4) PROMOTE PROMOTE ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT OF THE
28 TURNER STATION COMMUNITY OF DUNDALK; AND
4 HOUSE BILL 166
1 (6) (5) MAKE MAKE RECOMMENDATIONS ON IMPLEMENTING THE
2 GOALS OF THE COMMISSION.
3 (H) ON OR BEFORE DECEMBER 1 EACH YEAR, BEGINNING IN 2027, THE
4 COMMISSION SHALL REPORT ON ITS ACTIVITIES TO THE GOVERNOR AND, IN
5 ACCORDANCE WITH § 2–1257 OF THE STATE GOVERNMENT ARTICLE THIS ARTICLE,
6 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY.
7 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
8 October 1, 2026.
