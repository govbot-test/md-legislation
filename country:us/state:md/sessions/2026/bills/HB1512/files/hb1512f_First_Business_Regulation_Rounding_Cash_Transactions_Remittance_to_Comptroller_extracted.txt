HOUSE BILL 1512
C2, K3 6lr3120
By: Delegate Conaway
Introduced and read first time: February 13, 2026
Assigned to: Economic Matters
A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Business Regulation – Rounding Cash Transactions – Remittance to
3 Comptroller
4 FOR the purpose of requiring, if a merchant rounds up the price of a cash transaction for a
5 good or service in a certain manner, the merchant to remit to the Comptroller the
6 difference between the total price the customer is required to pay after the rounding
7 occurs and the price before the rounding occurs; requiring the Comptroller to
8 distribute certain revenue to the General Fund of the State; and generally relating
9 to rounding cash transactions between merchants and customers.
10 BY adding to
11 Article – Business Regulation
12 Section 19–110
13 Annotated Code of Maryland
14 (2024 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
15 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
16 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:

18 19–110.

20 INDICATED.
21 (2) (I) “CASH” MEANS UNITED STATES COINS AND PAPER
22 CURRENCY.
23 (II) “CASH” DOES NOT INCLUDE:

6 (B) (1) THIS SUBSECTION DOES NOT APPLY TO A TRANSACTION WHERE A
7 CUSTOMER EXPRESSLY CONSENTS TO HAVING THE TRANSACTION ROUNDED UP FOR
8 THE EXPRESS PURPOSE OF PROVIDING A DONATION.
9 (2) IN A CASH TRANSACTION BETWEEN A MERCHANT AND A
10 CUSTOMER FOR A GOOD OR SERVICE, IF THE MERCHANT ROUNDS UP THE TOTAL
11 PRICE, INCLUDING ANY APPLICABLE DISCOUNT, TAX, OR FEE, TO THE NEAREST 5
12 CENTS, THE MERCHANT SHALL REMIT TO THE COMPTROLLER IN THE MANNER
13 PRESCRIBED BY THE COMPTROLLER AN AMOUNT THAT EQUALS THE DIFFERENCE
14 BETWEEN:
15 (I) THE PRICE THE CUSTOMER IS REQUIRED TO PAY AFTER THE
16 ROUNDING OCCURS; AND
17 (II) THE PRICE BEFORE THE ROUNDING OCCURS.
18 (C) THE COMPTROLLER SHALL DISTRIBUTE THE REVENUE COLLECTED
19 UNDER THIS SECTION TO THE GENERAL FUND OF THE STATE.
