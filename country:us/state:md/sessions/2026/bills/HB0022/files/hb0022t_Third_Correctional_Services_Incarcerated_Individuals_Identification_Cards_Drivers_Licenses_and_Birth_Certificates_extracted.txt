CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Correctional Services – Incarcerated Individuals – Identification Cards and,
3 Driver’s Licenses, and Birth Certificates
4 FOR the purpose of providing that certain provisions about identification documents apply
5 only to certain incarcerated individuals; requiring the Commissioner of Correction
6 to determine whether an incarcerated individual is in possession of certain
7 documentation on taking custody of the incarcerated individual; requiring the
8 Commissioner to ensure a certain individual has a valid identification card under
9 certain circumstances; providing that a certain individual is responsible for the
10 safekeeping and care of certain identification documents in the possession of an
11 incarcerated individual at the time of intake; requiring a certain correctional facility
12 to return a certain identification card or driver’s license to a certain incarcerated
13 individual at a certain time under certain circumstances; requiring the
14 Commissioner of Correction and the managing official of a local correctional facility
15 to provide certain forms to and work with a certain incarcerated individual under
16 certain circumstances to ensure a certain identification card or, driver’s license, or
17 birth certificate is available to the incarcerated individual at the time of the
18 incarcerated individual’s release from confinement; and generally relating to
19 incarcerated individuals.

24 INTAKE FROM THE TIME THE INCARCERATED INDIVIDUAL IS LAWFULLY DETAINED
