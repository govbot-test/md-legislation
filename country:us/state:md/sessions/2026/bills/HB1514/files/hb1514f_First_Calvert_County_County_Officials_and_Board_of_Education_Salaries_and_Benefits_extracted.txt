A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Calvert County – County Officials and Board of Education – Salaries and
3 Benefits
4 FOR the purpose of altering the salaries of the County Commissioners of Calvert County,
5 the president and members of the Calvert County Board of Education, and the
6 County Treasurer of Calvert County; repealing a certain provision about the salary
7 of State’s Attorneys; altering the benefits of the County Commissioners of Calvert
8 County; altering certain retirement benefits for the County Treasurer of Calvert
9 County under certain circumstances; providing that this Act does not apply to the
10 salary or compensation of the incumbent County Commissioners of Calvert County,
11 the president and members of the Calvert County Board of Education, or the County
12 Treasurer of Calvert County; and generally relating to the compensation received by
13 certain Calvert County officials and the president and members of the Calvert
14 County Board of Education.
15 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
16 The Public Local Laws of Calvert County
17 Section 2–101
18 Article 5 – Public Local Laws of Maryland

26 Article – Criminal Procedure

1 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That, in accordance with Article
2 III, § 35 of the Maryland Constitution, this Act may not be construed to extend or apply to
3 the salary or compensation of the County Commissioners of Calvert County, the president
4 and members of the Calvert County Board of Education, or the County Treasurer of Calvert
5 County while serving in a term of office beginning before the effective date of this Act, but
6 the provisions of this Act concerning the salary or compensation of the County
7 Commissioners of Calvert County, the president and members of the Calvert County Board
8 of Education, or the County Treasurer of Calvert County shall take effect at the beginning
9 of the next following term of office. This limitation does not apply to an individual appointed
10 or elected after the effective date of this Act to fill out an unexpired term.
11 SECTION 3. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
12 October 1, 2026.
