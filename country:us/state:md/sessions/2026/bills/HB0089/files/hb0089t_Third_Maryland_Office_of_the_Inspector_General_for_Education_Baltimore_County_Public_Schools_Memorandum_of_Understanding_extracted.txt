1 AN ACT concerning
2 Baltimore County – Inspector General – Authority Over Baltimore County
3 Public Schools
4 Maryland Office of the Inspector General for Education – Baltimore County
5 Public Schools – Memorandum of Understanding
6 FOR the purpose of authorizing the County Council of Baltimore County to enact a local
7 law granting the Baltimore County Office of the Inspector General certain authority
8 over Baltimore County Public Schools; and generally relating to the Baltimore
9 County Office of the Inspector General and Baltimore County Public Schools.
10 requiring the Inspector General of the Maryland Office of the Inspector General for
11 Education to pursue a memorandum of understanding with the Baltimore County
12 Inspector General on matters pertaining to the Baltimore County public school
13 system; and generally relating to the Maryland Office of the Inspector General for
14 Education and the Baltimore County public school system.
15 BY adding to
16 The Public Local Laws of Baltimore County
17 Section 3–14–113
18 Article 3 – Public Local Laws of Maryland
19 (2015 Edition and May 2024 Supplement, as amended)
20 BY adding to
21 Article – Education
22 Section 9.10–106
EXPLANATION: CAPITALS INDICATE MATTER ADDED TO EXISTING LAW.
[Brackets] indicate matter deleted from existing law.
Underlining indicates amendments to bill.
Strike out indicates matter stricken from the bill by amendment or deleted from the law by
amendment. *hb0089*
2 HOUSE BILL 89
1 Annotated Code of Maryland
2 (2022 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
3 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
4 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
5 Article 3 – Baltimore County
6 3–14–113.
7 BEGINNING IN 2027, THE COUNTY COUNCIL MAY, BY LOCAL LAW, GRANT TO
8 THE INSPECTOR GENERAL THE SAME AUTHORITY OVER BALTIMORE COUNTY
9 PUBLIC SCHOOLS THAT THE INSPECTOR GENERAL HAS OVER A DEPARTMENT OF
10 COUNTY GOVERNMENT.
11 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That it is the intent of the General
12 Assembly that if the Baltimore County Council adopts a local law to grant the Baltimore
13 County Inspector General authority over Baltimore County Public Schools under Section 1
14 of this Act, that the local law shall take effect at the beginning of the next following term
15 of office of the Inspector General.
16 Article – Education
17 9.10–106.
18 (A) THIS SECTION APPLIES ONLY TO BALTIMORE COUNTY.
19 (B) THE INSPECTOR GENERAL SHALL PURSUE A MEMORANDUM OF
20 UNDERSTANDING WITH THE BALTIMORE COUNTY INSPECTOR GENERAL ON
21 MATTERS PERTAINING TO THE BALTIMORE COUNTY PUBLIC SCHOOL SYSTEM.
22 SECTION 3. 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
23 October 1, 2026.
Approved:
________________________________________________________________________________
Governor.
________________________________________________________________________________
Speaker of the House of Delegates.
________________________________________________________________________________
President of the Senate.
