CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Professional Liability Insurance Coverage – Nursing Homes, Assisted Living
3 Facilities, and Nurse Midwives, and Licensed Certified Midwives – Disclosure
4 (Nyeli Rose Lewis Act of 2026)
5 FOR the purpose of requiring nursing homes, assisted living programs, and nurse
6 midwives, and licensed certified midwives to notify certain individuals at certain
7 times if the nursing home, assisted living program, or nurse midwife, or licensed
8 certified midwife either does not maintain professional liability insurance or
9 professional liability insurance coverage has lapsed and has not been renewed; and
10 generally relating to professional liability insurance disclosures.

17 19–1401.
18 (a) In this subtitle the following words have the meanings indicated.
19 (e) (1) “Nursing home” means a facility that offers nonacute inpatient care to
20 patients suffering from a disease, chronic illness, condition, disability of advanced age, or
21 terminal disease requiring maximal nursing care without continuous hospital services and
22 who require medical services and nursing services rendered by or under the supervision of
23 a licensed nurse together with convalescent, restorative, or rehabilitative services.
24 (2) “Nursing home” does not mean a facility offering domiciliary care or
25 personal care as those terms are defined in § 19–301 of this title.
