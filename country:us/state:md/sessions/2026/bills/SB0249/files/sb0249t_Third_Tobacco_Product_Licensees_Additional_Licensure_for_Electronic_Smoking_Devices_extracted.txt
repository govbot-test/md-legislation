CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Tobacco Product Licensees – Additional Licensure for Electronic Smoking
3 Devices
4 FOR the purpose of repealing a provision of law authorizing a person holding a certain
5 license relating to cigarettes or other tobacco products to manufacture, distribute, or
6 sell electronic smoking devices in the same capacity as a person holding a certain
7 license relating to electronic smoking devices; exempting the holder of a certain
8 license relating to cigarettes or other tobacco products who is an applicant for a
9 license to act as an electronic smoking devices retailer or vape shop vendor from the
10 requirement to pay an additional license fee for the license; and generally relating to
11 tobacco products and electronic smoking devices licensure.
12 BY repealing and reenacting, without amendments,
13 Article – Business Regulation
14 Section 16.7–101(a), (c) through (h), and (k)
15 Annotated Code of Maryland
16 (2024 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
17 (As enacted by Chapter 814 of the Acts of the General Assembly of 2017)
18 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,

20 Section 16.7–102 and 16.7–202(a)
21 Annotated Code of Maryland

3 Article – Business Regulation
4 16.7–101.
5 (a) In this title the following words have the meanings indicated.
6 (c) (1) “Electronic smoking device” means a device that can be used to deliver
7 aerosolized or vaporized nicotine to an individual inhaling from the device.
8 (2) “Electronic smoking device” includes:
9 (i) an electronic cigarette, an electronic cigar, an electronic cigarillo,
10 an electronic pipe, an electronic hookah, a vape pen, and vaping liquid; and
11 (ii) except as provided in paragraph (3) of this subsection, any
12 component, part, or accessory of such a device regardless of whether or not it is sold
13 separately, including any substance intended to be aerosolized or vaporized during use of
14 the device.
15 (3) “Electronic smoking device” does not include:
16 (i) a drug, device, or combination product authorized for sale by the
17 U.S. Food and Drug Administration under the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act; or
18 (ii) a battery or battery charger when sold separately.
19 (d) “Electronic smoking devices manufacturer” means a person that:
20 (1) manufactures, mixes, or otherwise produces electronic smoking devices
21 intended for sale in the State, including electronic smoking devices intended for sale in the
22 United States through an importer; and
23 (2) (i) sells electronic smoking devices to a consumer, if the consumer
24 purchases or orders the devices through the mail, a computer network, a telephonic
25 network, or another electronic network, a licensed electronic smoking devices wholesaler
26 distributor, or a licensed electronic smoking devices wholesaler importer in the State;
27 (ii) if the electronic smoking devices manufacturer also holds a
28 license to act as an electronic smoking devices retailer or a vape shop vendor, sells electronic
29 smoking devices in the same manner as a vape shop vendor to consumers located in the
30 State; or

1 (iii) unless otherwise prohibited or restricted under local law, this
2 article, or the Criminal Law Article, distributes sample electronic smoking devices to a

4 (e) “Electronic smoking devices retailer” means a person that:
5 (1) sells electronic smoking devices to consumers;
6 (2) holds electronic smoking devices for sale to consumers; or
7 (3) unless otherwise prohibited or restricted under local law, this article,

10 (f) “Electronic smoking devices wholesaler distributor” means a person that:
11 (1) obtains at least 70% of its electronic smoking devices from a holder of

13 located in the United States; and
14 (2) (i) holds electronic smoking devices for sale to another person for
15 resale; or
16 (ii) sells electronic smoking devices to another person for resale.
17 (g) “Electronic smoking devices wholesaler importer” means a person that:
18 (1) obtains at least 70% of its electronic smoking devices from a business
19 entity located in a foreign country; and
20 (2) (i) holds electronic smoking devices for sale to another person for
21 resale; or
22 (ii) sells electronic smoking devices to another person for resale.
23 (h) “Executive Director” means the Executive Director of the Alcohol, Tobacco,
24 and Cannabis Commission.
25 (k) “Vape shop vendor” means an electronic smoking devices business that sells
26 electronic smoking devices and related accessories to consumers on the premises of its place
27 of business.
