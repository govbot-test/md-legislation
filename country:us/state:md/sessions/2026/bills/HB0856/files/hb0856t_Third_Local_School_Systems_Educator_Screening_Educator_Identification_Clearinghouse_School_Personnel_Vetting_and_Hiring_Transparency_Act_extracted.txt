CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Local School Systems – Educator Screening – Educator Identification
3 Clearinghouse
4 (School Personnel Vetting and Hiring Transparency Act)
5 FOR the purpose of requiring the State Department of Education to register each local
6 school system in the State as an associate member of a certain national membership
7 organization; requiring each local school system in the State to utilize the Educator
8 Identification Clearinghouse to screen individuals who receive an offer of
9 employment for an educator position that requires a license and ensure that each
10 individual who receives an offer applies for and obtains the appropriate license
11 before certain occurrences; and generally relating to the hiring and screening process
12 for educators employed within local school systems in the State.
13 BY adding to
14 Article – Education
15 Section 6–103.1
16 Annotated Code of Maryland
17 (2025 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
18 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
19 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
20 Article – Education
21 6–103.1.

2 HOUSE BILL 856
1 (A) IN THIS SECTION, “NATIONAL MEMBERSHIP ORGANIZATION” MEANS A
2 NATIONAL ORGANIZATION THAT IS A NATIONAL COLLECTION POINT FOR
3 PROFESSIONAL DISCIPLINE ACTIONS TAKEN BY ALL 50 STATES, THE DISTRICT OF
4 COLUMBIA, THE U.S. DEPARTMENT OF DEFENSE EDUCATIONAL OPPORTUNITY
5 SCHOOLS, AND GUAM THAT PROVIDES MEMBERS WITH NOTIFICATION OF AN ACTION
6 TAKEN AGAINST THE CREDENTIAL OF AN EDUCATOR BY OTHER MEMBER STATES OR
7 JURISDICTIONS.
8 (B) THE DEPARTMENT SHALL:
9 (1) REGISTER EACH LOCAL SCHOOL SYSTEM IN THE STATE AS AN
10 ASSOCIATE MEMBER OF A NATIONAL MEMBERSHIP ORGANIZATION THAT PROVIDES
11 ACCESS TO THE EDUCATOR IDENTIFICATION CLEARINGHOUSE; AND
12 (2) PAY ANY APPLICABLE FEES AND DUES ASSOCIATED WITH
13 MEMBERSHIP IN THE NATIONAL MEMBERSHIP ORGANIZATION.
14 (C) EACH LOCAL SCHOOL SYSTEM IN THE STATE SHALL:
15 (1) UTILIZE THE EDUCATOR IDENTIFICATION CLEARINGHOUSE
16 THROUGH THE NATIONAL MEMBERSHIP ORGANIZATION TO SCREEN EACH
17 INDIVIDUAL WHO RECEIVES, ON OR AFTER OCTOBER AUGUST 1, 2026, AN OFFER OF
18 EMPLOYMENT FROM THE LOCAL SCHOOL SYSTEM FOR AN EDUCATOR POSITION
19 THAT REQUIRES A LICENSE; AND
20 (2) ENSURE THAT EACH INDIVIDUAL WHO RECEIVES AN OFFER
21 SPECIFIED UNDER ITEM (1) OF THIS SUBSECTION:
22 (I) APPLIES FOR THE APPROPRIATE LICENSE AFTER THE
23 OFFER OF EMPLOYMENT AND BEFORE THE START OF EMPLOYMENT; AND
24 (II) OBTAINS THE APPROPRIATE LICENSE BEFORE ANY
25 INTERACTION WITH CHILDREN.
26 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That nothing in this Act may be
27 construed to impair or effect the requirements of § 6–113 of the Education Article.
28 SECTION 3. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect July
29 1, 2026.
