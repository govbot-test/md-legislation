A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Pharmaceutical Drugs and Devices – Gifts to Health Care Professionals –
3 Prohibition
4 FOR the purpose of prohibiting sellers of certain pharmaceutical drugs and devices from
5 giving or offering to give to health care professionals gifts in a calendar year that
6 have a total combined retail value of more than a certain amount; and generally
7 relating to gifts to health care professionals.
