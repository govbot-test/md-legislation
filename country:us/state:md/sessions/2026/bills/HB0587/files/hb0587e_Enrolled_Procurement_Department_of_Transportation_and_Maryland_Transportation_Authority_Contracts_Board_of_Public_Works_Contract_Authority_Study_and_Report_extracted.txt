1 AN ACT concerning
2 Procurement – Department of Transportation and Maryland Transportation
3 Authority Contracts – Board of Public Works Contract Authority – Study and
4 Report
5 FOR the purpose of authorizing the Board of Public Works to control certain procurement
6 contracts by the Department of Transportation and the Maryland Transportation
7 Authority valued at or above a certain amount; requiring the Board to adopt
8 regulations for the review of a contract modification for certain procurement
9 contracts by the Department or the Authority; establishing a workgroup to review
10 the procurement procedures for capital expenditures in connection with State roads,
11 bridges, or highways and to issue a certain report making certain recommendations;
12 and generally relating to procurement contracts and the Department of
13 Transportation and the Maryland Transportation Authority.
