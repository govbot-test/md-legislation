A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Pesticide Applications – Subscription Contract Requirements and Registry
3 Establishment
4 FOR the purpose of establishing certain requirements for a pesticide business licensee that
5 has a subscription contract for pest control with a residential customer; requiring
6 the Department of Agriculture to establish a Residential Pollinator Protection and
7 Neighbor Notification Registry for the purpose of registering certain individuals who
8 are interested in receiving notice of certain pesticide applications; requiring the
9 Department to prepare a certain list from the Registry and distribute the list to
10 certain licensees; requiring certain licensees and pesticide applicators to take certain
11 actions before and during a pesticide application to a property contiguous or adjacent
12 to the property of a registered individual; and generally relating to pesticide
13 applications.

1 (B) THE DEPARTMENT SHALL ESTABLISH A RESIDENTIAL POLLINATOR
2 PROTECTION AND NEIGHBOR NOTIFICATION REGISTRY FOR THE PURPOSE OF
3 REGISTERING INDIVIDUALS RESIDING IN RESIDENTIAL AREAS WHO ARE
4 INTERESTED IN RECEIVING NOTICE OF PESTICIDE APPLICATIONS ON CONTIGUOUS
5 OR ADJACENT PROPERTIES.
6 (C) (1) AN INDIVIDUAL MAY APPLY FOR INCLUSION IN THE REGISTRY BY
7 COMPLETING A REGISTRATION FORM PROVIDED BY THE DEPARTMENT.
8 (2) THE REGISTRATION FORM SHALL, AT A MINIMUM:
9 (I) REQUIRE THE APPLICANT TO PROVIDE THE NAME, STREET
10 ADDRESS, AND TELEPHONE NUMBER OF THE APPLICANT AND OF EACH CONTIGUOUS
11 OR ADJACENT PROPERTY OWNER; AND
12 (II) INCLUDE CHECKBOXES TO DESIGNATE:
13 1. WHETHER ANY MEMBER OF THE APPLICANT’S
14 HOUSEHOLD HAS A PESTICIDE SENSITIVITY OR SHOULD NOT BE EXPOSED TO A
15 PESTICIDE BECAUSE OF A CURRENT DIAGNOSED CONDITION OR AILMENT;
16 2. WHETHER PETS OR DOMESTIC ANIMALS RESIDE ON
17 THE APPLICANT’S PROPERTY; AND
18 3. WHETHER THE APPLICANT’S PROPERTY INCLUDES A
19 POLLINATOR GARDEN OR OTHER WILDLIFE HABITAT.
20 (3) (I) THE DEPARTMENT SHALL REQUIRE REGISTRATIONS TO BE
21 RENEWED ANNUALLY, ON OR BEFORE A DATE DETERMINED BY THE DEPARTMENT.
22 (II) THE DEPARTMENT MAY EXTEND OR MAKE EXCEPTIONS TO
23 THE REGISTRATION DEADLINE FOR REASONABLE CAUSE.
24 (D) (1) EXCEPT AS PROVIDED IN PARAGRAPH (3) OF THIS SUBSECTION,
25 THE DEPARTMENT MAY CHARGE A REASONABLE REGISTRATION FEE TO AN
26 APPLICANT FOR INCLUSION IN THE REGISTRY.
27 (2) THE FEE SHALL BE ESTABLISHED IN REGULATION AND SHALL BE
28 DESIGNED TO COVER THE COSTS TO THE DEPARTMENT OF IMPLEMENTING THIS
29 SECTION.
