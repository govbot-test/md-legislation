Section 1:
WES MOORE, Governor Ch. 136
Chapter 136
AN ACT concerning
State Board of Environmental Health Specialists – Sunset Extension
FOR the purpose of continuing the State Board of Environmental Health Specialists, in
accordance with the provisions of the Maryland Program Evaluation Act (sunset
law), by extending to a certain date the termination provisions relating to the
statutory and regulatory authority of the Board; and generally relating to the State
Board of Environmental Health Specialists.
BY repealing and reenacting, without amendments,
Article – Health Occupations

Section 2:
Section 21–201
Annotated Code of Maryland
(2021 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
Article – Health Occupations

Section 3:
Section 21–502
Annotated Code of Maryland
(2021 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)

Section 4:
SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
Article – Health Occupations
21–201.
There is a State Board of Environmental Health Specialists in the Department.
21–502.
Subject to the evaluation and reestablishment provisions of the Program Evaluation
Act, the provisions of this title and of any rule or regulation adopted under this title shall
terminate and be of no effect after July 1, [2027] 2032.

Section 5:
SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect June
1, 2026.
Approved by the Governor, April 14, 2026.
