2 State Board of Environmental Health Specialists – Sunset Extension
3 FOR the purpose of continuing the State Board of Environmental Health Specialists, in

5 law), by extending to a certain date the termination provisions relating to the
6 statutory and regulatory authority of the Board; and generally relating to the State
7 Board of Environmental Health Specialists.

18 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
19 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:

1 Article – Health Occupations
2 21–201.

7 terminate and be of no effect after July 1, [2027] 2032.
