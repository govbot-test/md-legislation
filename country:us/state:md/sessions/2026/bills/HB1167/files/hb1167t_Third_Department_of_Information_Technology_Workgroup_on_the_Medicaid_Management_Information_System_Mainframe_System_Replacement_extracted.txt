1 AN ACT concerning
2 Maryland Department of Health – Department of Information Technology –
3 Workgroup on the Medicaid Management Information System – System
4 Mainframe System Replacement
5 FOR the purpose of requiring the Maryland Department of Health to replace the State
6 Medicaid Management Information System mainframe with a modular system
7 requiring the Department of Information Technology to convene a workgroup on the
8 Medicaid Management Information System mainframe system replacement; and
9 generally relating to the replacement of the Medicaid Management Information
10 System mainframe system.
11 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
12 That:
13 (a) The Department of Information Technology shall convene a workgroup on the
14 Medicaid Management Information System mainframe system replacement.
15 (b) The workgroup shall consist of:
16 (1) at least one representative from the Maryland Department of Health;
17 (2) at least one representative from the Department of Human Services;
