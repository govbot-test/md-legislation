Chapter 149
(House Bill 1623)
AN ACT concerning
Special Taxing Districts – Village of Drummond – Authority and Organization
MC 20–26
FOR the purpose of constituting the Village Council of Drummond as the governing body
of the special taxing district constituted as the Village of Drummond in Montgomery
County; providing for the structure, membership, duties, and obligations of the
Village Council; altering the eligible uses of funds derived from the Village’s annual
special tax levy; providing for the Village’s annual appropriations process; and
generally relating to the authority and organization of the special taxing district
constituted as the Village of Drummond in Montgomery County.
BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
The Public Local Laws of Montgomery County
