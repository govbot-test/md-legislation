A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Special Taxing Districts – Village of Drummond – Authority and Organization
3 MC 20–26
4 FOR the purpose of constituting the Village Council of Drummond as the governing body
5 of the special taxing district constituted as the Village of Drummond in Montgomery
6 County; providing for the structure, membership, duties, and obligations of the
7 Village Council; altering the eligible uses of funds derived from the Village’s annual
8 special tax levy; providing for the Village’s annual appropriations process; and
9 generally relating to the authority and organization of the special taxing district
10 constituted as the Village of Drummond in Montgomery County.
