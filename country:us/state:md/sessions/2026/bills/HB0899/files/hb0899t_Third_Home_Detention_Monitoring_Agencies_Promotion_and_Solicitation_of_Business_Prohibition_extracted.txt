CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Home Detention Monitoring Agencies – Promotion and Solicitation of Business
3 – Prohibition
4 FOR the purpose of prohibiting a private home detention monitoring agency, an agent of a
5 private home detention monitoring agency, an employee of a courthouse, or an
6 employee of a correctional facility from performing certain acts on the grounds of a
7 courthouse or correctional facility; and generally relating to the promotion of and
8 solicitation of business for private home detention monitoring agencies.
9 BY adding to
10 Article – Business Occupations and Professions Criminal Procedure
11 Section 20–702 5–210.1
12 Annotated Code of Maryland
13 (2018 2025 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
14 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
15 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
16 Article – Business Occupations and Professions Criminal Procedure
17 20–702. 5–210.1.
18 (A) IN THIS SECTION, “AGENT” MEANS A PERSON THAT ACTS OR IS
19 AUTHORIZED TO ACT AS THE REPRESENTATIVE OF A PRIVATE HOME DETENTION
20 MONITORING AGENCY “PRIVATE HOME DETENTION MONITORING AGENCY” HAS THE
EXPLANATION: CAPITALS INDICATE MATTER ADDED TO EXISTING LAW.
[Brackets] indicate matter deleted from existing law.
Underlining indicates amendments to bill.
Strike out indicates matter stricken from the bill by amendment or deleted from the law by
amendment. *hb0899*
2 HOUSE BILL 899
1 MEANING STATED IN § 20–101 OF THE BUSINESS OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS
2 ARTICLE.
3 (B) ON THE GROUNDS OF A COURTHOUSE OR CORRECTIONAL FACILITY, A
4 PRIVATE HOME DETENTION MONITORING AGENCY, AN AGENT OF A PRIVATE HOME
5 DETENTION MONITORING AGENCY, AN EMPLOYEE OF THE COURTHOUSE, OR AN
6 EMPLOYEE OF A CORRECTIONAL FACILITY MAY NOT:
7 (1) APPROACH, ENTICE, OR INVITE A PERSON TO USE THE SERVICES
8 OF A SPECIFIC PRIVATE HOME DETENTION MONITORING AGENCY;
9 (2) DISTRIBUTE, DISPLAY, OR WEAR AN ITEM THAT ADVERTISES THE
10 SERVICES OF A PRIVATE HOME DETENTION MONITORING AGENCY; OR
11 (3) OTHERWISE SOLICIT BUSINESS FOR A PRIVATE HOME DETENTION
12 MONITORING AGENCY.
13 (C) A PERSON WHO VIOLATES SUBSECTION (B) OF THIS SECTION IS GUILTY
14 OF A MISDEMEANOR AND ON CONVICTION IS SUBJECT TO:
15 (1) A FINE NOT EXCEEDING $2,500 AND, IF LICENSED IN
16 ACCORDANCE WITH THIS TITLE TITLE 20 OF THE BUSINESS OCCUPATIONS AND
17 PROFESSIONS ARTICLE, A 30–DAY LICENSE SUSPENSION FOR A FIRST OFFENSE;
18 AND
19 (2) A FINE NOT EXCEEDING $5,000 AND, IF LICENSED IN
20 ACCORDANCE WITH THIS TITLE TITLE 20 OF THE BUSINESS OCCUPATIONS AND
21 PROFESSIONS ARTICLE, A 90–DAY LICENSE SUSPENSION FOR A SUBSEQUENT
22 OFFENSE.
23 (D) A PERSON LICENSED IN ACCORDANCE WITH THIS TITLE TITLE 20 OF
24 THE BUSINESS OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS ARTICLE THAT IS CONVICTED OF
25 A VIOLATION OF THIS SECTION SHALL BE REFERRED TO THE DEPARTMENT OF
26 PUBLIC SAFETY AND CORRECTIONAL SERVICES FOR APPROPRIATE ACTION.
27 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
28 October 1, 2026.
