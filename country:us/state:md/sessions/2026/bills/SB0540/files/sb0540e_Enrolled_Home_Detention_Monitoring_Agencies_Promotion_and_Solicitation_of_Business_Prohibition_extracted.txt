CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Home Detention Monitoring Agencies – Promotion and Solicitation of Business
3 – Prohibition
4 FOR the purpose of prohibiting a private home detention monitoring agency, an agent of a
5 private home detention monitoring agency, an employee of a courthouse, or an
6 employee of a correctional facility from performing certain acts on the grounds of a
7 courthouse or correctional facility; and generally relating to the promotion of and
8 solicitation of business for private home detention monitoring agencies.
9 BY adding to
10 Article – Business Occupations and Professions Criminal Procedure
11 Section 20–702 5–210.1
12 Annotated Code of Maryland
13 (2018 2025 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)

2 SENATE BILL 540
1 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
2 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
3 Article – Business Occupations and Professions Criminal Procedure
4 20–702. 5–210.1.
5 (A) IN THIS SECTION, “AGENT” MEANS A PERSON THAT ACTS OR IS
6 AUTHORIZED TO ACT AS THE REPRESENTATIVE OF A PRIVATE HOME DETENTION
7 MONITORING AGENCY “PRIVATE HOME DETENTION MONITORING AGENCY” HAS THE
8 MEANING STATED IN § 20–101 OF THE BUSINESS OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS
9 ARTICLE.
10 (B) ON THE GROUNDS OF A COURTHOUSE OR CORRECTIONAL FACILITY, A
11 PRIVATE HOME DETENTION MONITORING AGENCY, AN AGENT OF A PRIVATE HOME
12 DETENTION MONITORING AGENCY, AN EMPLOYEE OF THE COURTHOUSE, OR AN
13 EMPLOYEE OF A CORRECTIONAL FACILITY MAY NOT:
14 (1) APPROACH, ENTICE, OR INVITE A PERSON TO USE THE SERVICES
15 OF A SPECIFIC PRIVATE HOME DETENTION MONITORING AGENCY;
16 (2) DISTRIBUTE, DISPLAY, OR WEAR AN ITEM THAT ADVERTISES THE
17 SERVICES OF A PRIVATE HOME DETENTION MONITORING AGENCY; OR
18 (3) OTHERWISE SOLICIT BUSINESS FOR A PRIVATE HOME DETENTION
19 MONITORING AGENCY.
20 (C) A PERSON WHO VIOLATES SUBSECTION (B) OF THIS SECTION IS GUILTY
21 OF A MISDEMEANOR AND ON CONVICTION IS SUBJECT TO:
22 (1) A FINE NOT EXCEEDING $2,500 AND, IF LICENSED IN
23 ACCORDANCE WITH THIS TITLE TITLE 20 OF THE BUSINESS OCCUPATIONS AND
24 PROFESSIONS ARTICLE, A 30–DAY LICENSE SUSPENSION FOR A FIRST OFFENSE;
25 AND
26 (2) A FINE NOT EXCEEDING $5,000 AND, IF LICENSED IN
27 ACCORDANCE WITH THIS TITLE TITLE 20 OF THE BUSINESS OCCUPATIONS AND
28 PROFESSIONS ARTICLE, A 90–DAY LICENSE SUSPENSION FOR A SUBSEQUENT
29 OFFENSE.
30 (D) A PERSON LICENSED IN ACCORDANCE WITH THIS TITLE TITLE 20 OF
31 THE BUSINESS OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS ARTICLE THAT IS CONVICTED OF
