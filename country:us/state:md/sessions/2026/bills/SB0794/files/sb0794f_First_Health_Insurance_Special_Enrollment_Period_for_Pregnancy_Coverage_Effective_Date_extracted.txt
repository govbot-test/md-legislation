1 AN ACT concerning
2 Health Insurance – Special Enrollment Period for Pregnancy – Coverage
3 Effective Date
4 FOR the purpose of authorizing an individual who enrolls for coverage or enrolls a
5 dependent for coverage during a certain special enrollment period for pregnancy to
6 choose a certain date option on which the coverage will become effective; requiring
7 the carrier, if the individual fails to select the effective date, to select a certain date
8 option; and generally relating to special enrollment periods for health insurance.
