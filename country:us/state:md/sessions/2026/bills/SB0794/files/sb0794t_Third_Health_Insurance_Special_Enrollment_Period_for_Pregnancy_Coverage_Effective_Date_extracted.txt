1 AN ACT concerning
2 Health Insurance – Special Enrollment Period for Pregnancy – Coverage
3 Effective Date
4 FOR the purpose of authorizing an individual who enrolls for coverage or enrolls a
5 dependent for coverage during a certain special enrollment period for pregnancy to
6 choose a certain date option on which the coverage will become effective; requiring
7 the carrier, if the individual fails to select the effective date, to select a certain date
8 option; and generally relating to special enrollment periods for health insurance.

2 SENATE BILL 794
1 15–1316.
2 (a) (1) In this section the following words have the meanings indicated.
3 (2) “Dependent” means an individual who is or who may become eligible
4 for coverage under the terms of a health benefit plan because of a relationship with another
5 individual.
6 (3) “Health care practitioner” has the meaning stated in § 1–301 of the
7 Health Occupations Article.
8 (4) “Qualifying coverage in an eligible employer–sponsored plan” has the
9 meaning stated in 45 C.F.R. § 155.300.
10 (c) A carrier participating in the Individual Exchange shall provide:
11 (1) the special enrollment periods specified in 45 C.F.R. § 155.420 for
12 individuals who purchase coverage through the Individual Exchange; and
13 (2) a special enrollment period for an individual who purchases coverage
14 through the Individual Exchange if the individual or a dependent of the individual becomes
15 pregnant, as confirmed by a health care practitioner.
16 (d) A carrier shall provide:
17 (1) the special enrollment periods specified in 45 C.F.R. § 147.104(b)(2) for
18 individuals who purchase coverage outside the Individual Exchange; and
19 (2) a special enrollment period for an individual who purchases coverage
20 outside the Individual Exchange if the individual or a dependent of the individual becomes
21 pregnant, as confirmed by a health care practitioner.
22 (e) A special enrollment period described in subsection (c)(2) or (d)(2) of this
23 section shall:
24 (1) be open for a period of 90 days; and
25 (2) begin on the date the health care practitioner confirms the pregnancy.
26 (f) (1) If an individual enrolls for coverage during one of the open enrollment
27 periods described in subsection (b) of this section or during one of the special open
28 enrollment periods described in subsections (c)(1) and (d)(1) of this section, coverage shall
29 be effective in accordance with the requirements in 45 C.F.R. § 155.420.
30 (2) (I) If an individual enrolls for coverage or enrolls a dependent for
31 coverage during a special enrollment period described in subsection (c)(2) or (d)(2) of this
