6 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That, as provided in this Act:
7 (a) The Office of the Deaf, Deafblind, and Hard of Hearing is the successor of the
8 Office of the Deaf and Hard of Hearing.
9 (b) In every law, executive order, rule, regulation, policy, or document created by
10 an official, an employee, or a unit of this State, the names and titles of those agencies and
11 officials mean the names and titles of the successor agency or official.
12 SECTION 3. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That nothing in this Act affects the
13 term of office of an appointed or elected member of any commission, office, department,
14 agency, or other unit. An individual who is a member of a unit on the effective date of this
15 Act shall remain for the balance of the term to which appointed or elected, unless the
16 member sooner dies, resigns, or is removed under provisions of law.
17 SECTION 4. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That any transaction or
18 employment status affected by or flowing from any change of nomenclature or any statute
19 amended by this Act and validly entered into or existing before the effective date of this Act
20 and every right, duty, or interest flowing from a statute amended by this Act remains valid
21 after the effective date of this Act and may be terminated, completed, consummated, or
22 enforced as required or allowed by any statute amended by this Act as though the
23 amendment had not occurred. If a change in nomenclature involves a change in name or
24 designation of any State unit, the successor unit shall be considered in all respects as
25 having the powers and obligations granted the former unit.
26 SECTION 5. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That:
27 (a) The continuity of every commission, office, department, agency, or other unit
28 is retained.
29 (b) The personnel, records, files, furniture, fixtures, and other properties and all
30 appropriations, credits, assets, liabilities, and obligations of each retained unit are
31 continued as the personnel, records, files, furniture, fixtures, properties, appropriations,
32 credits, assets, liabilities, and obligations of the unit under the laws enacted by this Act.
33 SECTION 6. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That letterhead, business cards,
34 and other documents reflecting the renaming of the Office of the Deaf and Hard of Hearing
35 to be the Office of the Deaf, Deafblind, and Hard of Hearing may not be used until all

1 letterhead, business cards, and other documents already in print and reflecting the name
2 of the Office before the effective date of this Act have been used.
