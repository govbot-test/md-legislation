A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Civil Actions – Nonprofit Organizations – Unauthorized Support of Israeli
3 Settlement Activity
4 (Not On Our Dime Act)
5 FOR the purpose of prohibiting certain nonprofit organizations from aiding or abetting
6 certain activity by the government of Israel or a citizen of Israel; authorizing the
7 Attorney General or a certain person to bring a certain action against a nonprofit
8 organization that violates this Act; requiring the Secretary of State to remove a
9 nonprofit organization from the Registry of Charitable Solicitation following receipt
10 of certain notice; and generally relating to the prohibition against unauthorized
11 support of Israeli settlement activity by a nonprofit organization.
12 BY adding to
13 Article – Courts and Judicial Proceedings
14 Section 3–2701 and 3–2702 to be under the new subtitle “Subtitle 27. Nonprofit
15 Organizations – Unauthorized Support of Israeli Settlement Activity”

2 (B) “NONPROFIT ORGANIZATION” MEANS AN ORGANIZATION EXEMPT FROM
3 TAXATION UNDER § 501(C)(3) OR (4) OF THE INTERNAL REVENUE CODE.
4 (C) “OCCUPIED TERRITORY” MEANS THE ISRAELI–OCCUPIED WEST BANK,
5 INCLUDING EAST JERUSALEM.
6 (D) (1) “PROTECTED PERSONS” MEANS INDIVIDUALS PROTECTED
7 UNDER GENEVA CONVENTION (IV) RELATIVE TO THE PROTECTION OF CIVILIAN
8 PERSONS IN TIME OF WAR, 12 AUGUST 1949, 6 U.S.T. 3516.
9 (2) “PROTECTED PERSONS” INCLUDES CIVILIANS IN OCCUPIED
10 TERRITORY IN ACCORDANCE WITH INTERNATIONAL LAW.
11 (E) “UNAUTHORIZED SUPPORT OF ISRAELI SETTLEMENT ACTIVITY” MEANS
12 AIDING AND ABETTING ACTIVITY OF THE GOVERNMENT OF ISRAEL, OR A CITIZEN OF
13 ISRAEL, THAT IS BANNED UNDER THE GENEVA CONVENTIONS SIGNED AUGUST 12,
14 1949, AS AMENDED, AND THEIR ADDITIONAL PROTOCOLS, TO WHICH THE UNITED
15 STATES IS A PARTY, INCLUDING THE FOLLOWING ACTIVITY:
16 (1) THE UNLAWFUL TRANSFER OF ISRAELI CIVILIANS INTO
17 OCCUPIED TERRITORY;
18 (2) ACTS OF VIOLENCE AGAINST PROTECTED PERSONS BY CITIZENS
19 OF ISRAEL LIVING IN OCCUPIED TERRITORIES, INCLUDING:
20 (I) ABDUCTION;
21 (II) MURDER;
22 (III) ASSAULT;
23 (IV) ARSON; AND
24 (V) THE COMMISSION OF ANY ACT DESCRIBED IN ITEMS (I)
25 THROUGH (IV) OF THIS ITEM USING A FIREARM OR EXPLOSIVE DEVICE;
26 (3) CRIMES AGAINST PROPERTY INCLUDING:
27 (I) THE KILLING OR HARMING OF ANIMALS AND LIVESTOCK;
28 (II) THE DESTRUCTION OF TREES AND CROPS;

25 (A) A NONPROFIT ORGANIZATION REGISTERED WITH THE SECRETARY OF

1 STATE TO SOLICIT CHARITABLE DONATIONS IN THE STATE MAY NOT KNOWINGLY
2 ENGAGE IN UNAUTHORIZED SUPPORT OF ISRAELI SETTLEMENT ACTIVITY.
3 (B) (1) THE ATTORNEY GENERAL MAY BRING AN ACTION FOR
4 INJUNCTIVE RELIEF AND DAMAGES ON BEHALF OF THE STATE OR ANY OF ITS
5 POLITICAL SUBDIVISIONS AGAINST A DIRECTOR, AN OFFICER, A PARTNER, A
6 TRUSTEE, A MANAGER, OR ANY OTHER AGENT OF A NONPROFIT ORGANIZATION THAT
7 VIOLATES SUBSECTION (A) OF THIS SECTION.
8 (2) IN AN ACTION FILED UNDER PARAGRAPH (1) OF THIS
9 SUBSECTION, THE ATTORNEY GENERAL MAY SEEK NOT LESS THAN $1,000,000 IN
10 DAMAGES.
11 (C) (1) A PERSON HARMED BY A VIOLATION OF SUBSECTION (A) OF THIS
12 SECTION MAY BRING AN ACTION FOR INJUNCTIVE RELIEF AND ACTUAL DAMAGES
13 AGAINST A DIRECTOR, AN OFFICER, A PARTNER, A TRUSTEE, A MANAGER, OR ANY
14 OTHER AGENT OF A NONPROFIT ORGANIZATION THAT VIOLATES SUBSECTION (A) OF
15 THIS SECTION.
16 (2) IN ADDITION TO INJUNCTIVE RELIEF AND ACTUAL DAMAGES, A
17 COURT MAY AWARD ATTORNEY’S FEES TO A PREVAILING PLAINTIFF.
18 (D) ON NOTICE FROM THE ATTORNEY GENERAL OR A PREVAILING
19 PLAINTIFF OF A JUDGMENT ENTERED AGAINST A NONPROFIT ORGANIZATION
20 UNDER THIS SECTION, THE SECRETARY OF STATE SHALL REMOVE THE NONPROFIT
21 ORGANIZATION FROM THE REGISTRY OF CHARITABLE SOLICITATION.
22 (E) AN ACTION UNDER THIS SECTION:
23 (1) MAY BE BROUGHT IN ANY COUNTY IN THE STATE; AND
24 (2) SHALL BE FILED WITHIN 10 YEARS AFTER THE DATE OF THE
25 OCCURRENCE OF THE ACT ON WHICH THE CLAIM IS BASED.
26 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
27 October 1, 2026.
