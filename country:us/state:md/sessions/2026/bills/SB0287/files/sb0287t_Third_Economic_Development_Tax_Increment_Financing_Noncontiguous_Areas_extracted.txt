2 SENATE BILL 287
1 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
2 Article – Economic Development
3 Section 12–201(i)
4 Annotated Code of Maryland
5 (2024 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
6 (As enacted by Section 1 of this Act)
7 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
8 Article – Economic Development
9 Section 12–202 and 12–203(a)(1)
10 Annotated Code of Maryland
11 (2024 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
12 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
13 That Section(s) 12–201(e) through (u) of Article – Economic Development of the Annotated
14 Code of Maryland be renumbered to be Section(s) 12–201(f) through (v), respectively.
15 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That the Laws of Maryland read
16 as follows:
17 Article – Economic Development
18 12–201.
19 (a) In this subtitle the following words have the meanings indicated.
20 (E) “BLIGHTED AREA” MEANS AN AREA IN WHICH A MAJORITY OF
21 BUILDINGS HAVE DECLINED IN PRODUCTIVITY BY REASON OF OBSOLESCENCE,
22 DEPRECIATION, OR OTHER CAUSES TO AN EXTENT THAT THEY NO LONGER JUSTIFY
23 FUNDAMENTAL REPAIRS AND ADEQUATE MAINTENANCE.
24 (i) (1) “Development district” means a contiguous OR NONCONTIGUOUS area
25 designated by a resolution.
26 (2) “Development district” includes an extraordinary development district.
27 12–202.
28 (a) (1) This subtitle is self–executing.
29 (2) A political subdivision need not amend its charter to exercise the
30 powers granted by this subtitle.
31 (b) (1) [This] EXCEPT AS PROVIDED IN PARAGRAPH (2) OF THIS
32 SUBSECTION, THIS subtitle does not apply in Baltimore City.
SENATE BILL 287 3
1 (2) THE PROVISIONS OF § 12–203(A)(1)(II) OF THIS SUBTITLE
2 PERTAINING TO NONCONTIGUOUS BLIGHTED AREAS APPLY IN BALTIMORE CITY.
3 12–203.
4 (a) Before issuing bonds, the governing body of the political subdivision shall:
5 (1) by resolution:
6 (i) designate a contiguous area within its jurisdiction as a
7 development district;
8 (ii) DESIGNATE A NONCONTIGUOUS BLIGHTED AREA WITHIN
9 ITS JURISDICTION AS A DEVELOPMENT DISTRICT;
10 (III) identify an area that has been designated a sustainable
11 community; or
12 [(iii)] (IV) identify an area that has been designated a RISE zone;
13 SECTION 3. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
14 October 1, 2026.
Approved:
