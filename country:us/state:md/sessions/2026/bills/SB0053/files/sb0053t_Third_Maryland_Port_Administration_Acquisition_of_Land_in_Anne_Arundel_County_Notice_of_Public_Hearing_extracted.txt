2 SENATE BILL 53
1 Article – Transportation
2 6–309.
3 (a) Notwithstanding any other provision of this title, this section controls as to
4 Anne Arundel County.
5 (b) (1) (I) The Administration may not acquire any interest in land or
6 improvements on land in Anne Arundel County without the prior approval of the county,
7 given after a public hearing.
8 (II) AT LEAST 2 WEEKS BEFORE THE ANNOUNCEMENT OF A
9 PUBLIC HEARING REQUIRED UNDER SUBPARAGRAPH (I) OF THIS PARAGRAPH, THE
10 ADMINISTRATION SHALL PROVIDE WRITTEN NOTICE OF THE HEARING TO THE ANNE
11 ARUNDEL COUNTY DELEGATION TO THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY.
12 (2) This subsection does not affect the right of the Administration to
13 acquire an option for later acquisition of the property or improvements once the approval
14 required by this subsection is obtained.
15 (3) (I) If the Administration makes a written request for approval of the
16 county, the approval required by this subsection is deemed to be given unless the
17 Administration is notified in writing to the contrary within 90 days after it makes the
18 request.
19 (II) THE ADMINISTRATION, IN ACCORDANCE WITH § 2–1257 OF
20 THE STATE GOVERNMENT ARTICLE, SHALL PROVIDE WRITTEN NOTICE OF A
21 REQUEST UNDER THIS PARAGRAPH TO THE ANNE ARUNDEL COUNTY DELEGATION
22 TO THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY.
23 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
24 October 1, 2026.
