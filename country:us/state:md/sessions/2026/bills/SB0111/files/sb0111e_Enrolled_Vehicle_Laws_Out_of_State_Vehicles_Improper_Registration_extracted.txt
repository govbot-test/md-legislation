CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Vehicle Laws – Out–of–State Vehicles – Improper Registration
3 FOR the purpose of authorizing the Motor Vehicle Administration and agents and
4 employees of State agencies and political subdivisions to take possession of certain
5 vehicle registration plates under certain circumstances; altering a certain policy of
6 the State; authorizing the Motor Vehicle Administrator to enter into reciprocal
7 agreements, arrangements, or declarations to provide for reciprocal enforcement of
8 certain automated enforcement violations; exempting certain vehicles from the
9 requirement to be registered in the State; exempting certain vehicles from a certain
10 tax under certain circumstances; prohibiting a title service agent from selling or
11 offering for sale a certain vehicle registration; establishing a certain process for
12 bringing vehicles owned by State residents that are improperly registered in another
13 state into compliance with the Maryland Vehicle Law; and generally relating to
14 improperly registered out–of–state vehicles.
