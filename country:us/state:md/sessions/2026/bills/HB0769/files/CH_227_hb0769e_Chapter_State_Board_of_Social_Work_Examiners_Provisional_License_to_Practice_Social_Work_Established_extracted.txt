WES MOORE, Governor Ch. 227
Chapter 227
(House Bill 769)
AN ACT concerning
State Board of Social Work Examiners – Conditional Provisional License to
Practice Social Work – Revisions Established
FOR the purpose of altering the circumstances under which the Board of Social Work
Examiners may issue a conditional license to practice social work; establishing
alternate pathways a provisional social work license through which an individual
issued a conditional provisional license may receive a full license; and generally
relating to licenses to practice social work.
BY repealing and reenacting, without amendments,
Article – Health Occupations
Section 19–101(a), (c–1), and (e) and (c–1)
Annotated Code of Maryland
(2021 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
Article – Health Occupations
Section 19–309.1 19–101(e)
Annotated Code of Maryland
(2021 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
BY adding to
Article – Health Occupations
Section 19–101(o–1) and 19–309.2
Annotated Code of Maryland
(2021 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)

19–309.2.
(A) BEGINNING NOVEMBER 1, 2026, THE BOARD SHALL ISSUE A
PROVISIONAL LICENSE TO AN APPLICANT WHO, EXCEPT FOR PASSING THE
EXAMINATION OTHERWISE REQUIRED UNDER THIS SUBTITLE, HAS MET THE
APPROPRIATE EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE REQUIREMENTS OF THIS SUBTITLE
FOR A LICENSE ISSUED UNDER § 19–302 OF THIS SUBTITLE TO PRACTICE:
(1) BACHELOR SOCIAL WORK; OR
(2) MASTER SOCIAL WORK.
(B) A PROVISIONAL LICENSE:
(1) AUTHORIZES THE HOLDER TO PRACTICE SOCIAL WORK IN THE
STATE ONLY WITHIN THE SCOPE OF THE SPECIFIC CATEGORY OF LICENSE ISSUED
BY THE BOARD;

(2) REQUIRES THE HOLDER TO PRACTICE UNDER THE SUPERVISION
OF A BOARD–APPROVED SUPERVISOR IN ACCORDANCE WITH REGULATIONS
ADOPTED UNDER THIS SECTION;
(3) MAY BE ISSUED ONLY TO AN INITIAL APPLICANT FOR LICENSURE
WHO HAS NOT PREVIOUSLY BEEN LICENSED UNDER THIS TITLE; AND
(4) DOES NOT QUALIFY THE HOLDER FOR A MULTISTATE LICENSE
UNDER THE SOCIAL WORK LICENSURE COMPACT ESTABLISHED UNDER § 19–3A–01
OF THIS TITLE.
(C) A PROVISIONAL LICENSE:
(1) EXPIRES ON A DATE SET BY THE BOARD THAT IS AT LEAST 12
MONTHS, BUT NOT MORE THAN 24 MONTHS, AFTER THE DATE OF ISSUANCE;
(2) MAY NOT BE RENEWED FOR AN ADDITIONAL TERM NOT
EXCEEDING 24 MONTHS; AND
(3) SHALL TERMINATE AUTOMATICALLY ON:
(I) THE EXPIRATION DATE; OR
(II) IF THE HOLDER APPLIES AND QUALIFIES FOR A LICENSE
UNDER SUBSECTION (E) OF THIS SECTION, THE DATE THAT THE FULL LICENSE IS
ISSUED.
(D) A HOLDER OF A PROVISIONAL LICENSE AND THE SUPERVISING SOCIAL
WORKER ARE JOINTLY RESPONSIBLE FOR ENSURING COMPLIANCE WITH THE
SUPERVISION AND EXPIRATION REQUIREMENTS OF SUBSECTIONS (B)(2) AND (C) OF
THIS SECTION.
(E) THE BOARD SHALL ISSUE A FULL LICENSE UNDER § 19–302 OF THIS
SUBTITLE TO A HOLDER OF A PROVISIONAL LICENSE WHO DEMONSTRATES TO THE
SATISFACTION OF THE BOARD THAT THE HOLDER HAS ACHIEVED A SATISFACTORY
SCORE ON AN EXAMINATION, AS REQUIRED UNDER § 19–302 OF THIS SUBTITLE.
(F) SUPERVISED HOURS COMPLETED UNDER A PROVISIONAL LICENSE:
(1) MAY BE COUNTED TOWARD THE SUPERVISED EXPERIENCE
REQUIRED FOR INDEPENDENT PRACTICE APPROVAL UNDER § 19–302(F) OF THIS
SUBTITLE; AND

(3) (2) THE NUMBER OF HOLDERS OF PROVISIONAL LICENSES WHO

(4) (3) ANY RECOMMENDATIONS FOR IMPROVING:
(I) THE IMPLEMENTATION OF THIS SECTION; AND
(II) THE IMPACT OF THIS SECTION ON THE HEALTH CARE
WORKFORCE.
