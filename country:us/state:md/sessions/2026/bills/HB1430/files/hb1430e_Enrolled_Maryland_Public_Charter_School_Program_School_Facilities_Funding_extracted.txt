CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Maryland Public Charter School Program – School Facilities – Funding
3 FOR the purpose of requiring the Interagency Commission on School Construction to
4 distribute certain funds to public charter schools for the maintenance and operation
5 of capital improvements to public charter school facilities, debt service, or rent or lease
6 payments; requiring certain public charter schools to report to the Interagency
7 Commission on School Construction on the status of certain projects; and generally
8 relating to the funding of public charter school facilities in the Maryland Public
9 Charter School Program.
10 BY adding to
11 Article – Education
12 Section 9–109(c)
13 Annotated Code of Maryland
14 (2022 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)

2 HOUSE BILL 1430
1 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
2 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
3 Article – Education
4 9–109.
5 (C) (1) (I) IN THIS SUBSECTION,, “PUBLIC THE FOLLOWING WORDS
6 HAVE THE MEANINGS INDICATED.
7 (II) “PUBLIC “PUBLIC CHARTER SCHOOL FACILITIES” MEANS
8 PREMISES THAT ARE:
9 (I) 1. (I) OWNED OR LEASED BY A PUBLIC CHARTER SCHOOL;
10 OR
11 (II) 2. (II) OCCUPIED AND UTILIZED BY A PUBLIC CHARTER
12 SCHOOL TO PROVIDE A PROGRAM OF PRIMARY OR SECONDARY EDUCATION TO ITS
13 STUDENTS.
14 (III) “TOTAL AGGREGATE STUDENT ENROLLMENT” DOES NOT
15 INCLUDE STUDENT ENROLLMENT IN LOCAL SCHOOL SYSTEMS THAT:
16 1. HAVE CLOSED OVER 50 SCHOOLS SINCE 2010; AND
17 2. MADE SURPLUS FACILITIES AVAILABLE TO CHARTER
18 SCHOOLS.
19 (2) (I) 1. THE SUBJECT TO SUBSUBPARAGRAPH 2 OF THIS
20 SUBPARAGRAPH, THE INTERAGENCY COMMISSION ON SCHOOL CONSTRUCTION
21 SHALL DISTRIBUTE FUNDS TO PUBLIC CHARTER SCHOOLS FOR THE MAINTENANCE
22 AND OPERATION OF IN ACCORDANCE WITH THIS SUBSECTION FOR CAPITAL
23 IMPROVEMENTS TO PUBLIC CHARTER SCHOOL FACILITIES, DEBT SERVICE, OR RENT
24 OR LEASE PAYMENTS.
25 2. A PUBLIC CHARTER SCHOOL MAY RECEIVE FUNDS
26 UNDER THIS SUBSECTION ONLY IF THE OPERATOR’S MOST RECENT REQUIRED
27 CERTIFIED FINANCIAL AUDIT IS UNQUALIFIED.
28 (II) FUNDS DISBURSED UNDER SUBPARAGRAPH (I) OF THIS
29 PARAGRAPH SHALL BE PAID DIRECTLY TO THE PUBLIC CHARTER SCHOOL AND MAY
30 NOT BE PAID TO A LOCAL GOVERNING BODY OR COUNTY BOARD.
HOUSE BILL 1430 3
1 (3) IF A PUBLIC CHARTER SCHOOL IS LOCATED IN A BUILDING OWNED
2 BY THE LOCAL SCHOOL SYSTEM, THE INTERAGENCY COMMISSION ON SCHOOL
3 CONSTRUCTION MAY NOT DISTRIBUTE FUNDS TO THE PUBLIC CHARTER SCHOOL
4 FOR CAPITAL IMPROVEMENTS TO THE SCHOOL FACILITY, DEBT SERVICE, OR RENT
5 OR LEASE PAYMENTS UNDER THIS SUBSECTION IN FISCAL YEARS DURING WHICH THE
6 SCHOOL FACILITY IS UNDERGOING A PROJECT THAT IS RECEIVING STATE FUNDING
7 THROUGH THE CAPITAL IMPROVEMENT PROGRAM.
8 (4) IF THE TOTAL AMOUNT OF THE CAPITAL IMPROVEMENT PROGRAM
9 IN A FISCAL YEAR IS LESS THAN THE TOTAL AMOUNT IN THE PRIOR FISCAL YEAR, THE
10 AMOUNT OF FUNDS DISTRIBUTED TO PUBLIC CHARTER SCHOOLS UNDER THIS
11 SUBSECTION FOR THE FISCAL YEAR SHALL BE PROPORTIONATELY REDUCED.
12 (5) THE INTERAGENCY COMMISSION ON SCHOOL CONSTRUCTION
13 MAY NOT USE FUNDS DISTRIBUTED TO A PUBLIC CHARTER SCHOOL UNDER THIS
14 SUBSECTION AS JUSTIFICATION TO REDUCE THE AMOUNT PROVIDED TO A LOCAL
15 SCHOOL SYSTEM THROUGH THE CAPITAL IMPROVEMENT PROGRAM.
16 (6) A PUBLIC CHARTER SCHOOL THAT RECEIVES FUNDS UNDER THIS
17 SUBSECTION DURING A FISCAL YEAR SHALL REPORT TO THE INTERAGENCY
18 COMMISSION ON SCHOOL CONSTRUCTION ON THE STATUS OF ANY PROJECT FOR
19 CAPITAL IMPROVEMENTS TO PUBLIC CHARTER SCHOOL FACILITIES, DEBT SERVICE,
20 OR RENT OR LEASE PAYMENTS BEFORE THE PUBLIC CHARTER SCHOOL MAY RECEIVE
21 FUNDS FOR THE FOLLOWING FISCAL YEAR.
22 (3) (7) FOR FISCAL YEAR 2028 AND EACH FISCAL YEAR
23 THEREAFTER, THE GOVERNOR SHALL INCLUDE IN THE ANNUAL BUDGET BILL AN
24 APPROPRIATION FOR THE MAINTENANCE AND OPERATION OF CAPITAL
25 IMPROVEMENTS TO PUBLIC CHARTER SCHOOL FACILITIES, DEBT SERVICE, OR RENT
26 OR LEASE PAYMENTS THAT IS EQUAL TO THE TOTAL AGGREGATE STUDENT
27 ENROLLMENT IN THE MARYLAND PUBLIC CHARTER SCHOOL PROGRAM FOR THE
28 PRIOR FISCAL YEAR MULTIPLIED BY $1,600 $200.
29 (4) (8) (I) THE INTERAGENCY COMMISSION ON SCHOOL
30 CONSTRUCTION SHALL ADOPT REGULATIONS TO CARRY OUT THIS SUBSECTION.
31 (II) IN THE REGULATIONS ADOPTED UNDER SUBPARAGRAPH (I)
32 OF THIS PARAGRAPH, THE INTERAGENCY COMMISSION ON SCHOOL CONSTRUCTION
33 SHALL INCLUDE REGULATIONS THAT:
34 1. ESTABLISH THE PROCEDURES TO COMPLY WITH
35 PARAGRAPH (3) OF THIS SUBSECTION IF A LOCAL SCHOOL SYSTEM SUBMITS A
36 MID–YEAR CAPITAL PROJECT FUNDING REQUEST; AND
4 HOUSE BILL 1430
1 2. ENSURE A PUBLIC CHARTER SCHOOL RECEIVES
2 FUNDS UNDER THIS SUBSECTION IF THE LOCAL SCHOOL SYSTEM RESCINDS A
3 CAPITAL PROJECT FUNDING REQUEST AND THE PUBLIC CHARTER SCHOOL IS
4 SUBSEQUENTLY ELIGIBLE FOR FUNDING UNDER THIS SUBSECTION.
5 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect July
6 1, 2026.
Approved:
________________________________________________________________________________
Governor.
________________________________________________________________________________
Speaker of the House of Delegates.

President of the Senate.
