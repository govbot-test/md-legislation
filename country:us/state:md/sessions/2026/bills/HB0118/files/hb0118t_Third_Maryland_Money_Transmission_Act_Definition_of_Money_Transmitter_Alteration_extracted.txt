1 AN ACT concerning
2 Maryland Money Transmission Act – Definition of Money Transmitter
3 – Alteration
4 FOR the purpose of altering, for a certain licensing requirement, the definition of “money
5 transmitter” under the Maryland Money Transmission Act to exclude a certain
6 person designated as an agent of a payor for the purpose of the agent’s provision of
7 payroll processing services under certain circumstances; and generally relating to
8 the Maryland Money Transmission Act.

10 Article – Financial Institutions

16 Section 12–401(q)
17 Annotated Code of Maryland
18 (2020 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
19 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
20 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:

2 HOUSE BILL 118
1 Article – Financial Institutions
2 12–401.
3 (a) In this subtitle the following words have the meanings indicated.
4 (q) (1) “Money transmitter” means a person that engages in money
5 transmission that is required to be licensed under this subtitle, whether or not the person
6 maintains a license.
7 (2) “Money transmitter” includes:
8 (i) A bill payer service; and
9 (ii) An accelerated mortgage payment service.
10 (3) “MONEY TRANSMITTER” DOES NOT INCLUDE A PERSON
11 DESIGNATED AS AN AGENT OF A PAYOR FOR THE PURPOSE OF PROVIDING PAYROLL
12 PROCESSING SERVICES FOR WHICH THE AGENT WOULD OTHERWISE NEED TO BE
13 LICENSED UNDER THIS SUBTITLE, IF:
14 (I) THERE IS WRITTEN AGREEMENT BETWEEN THE PAYOR AND
15 THE AGENT THAT DIRECTS THE AGENT TO PROVIDE PAYROLL PROCESSING
16 SERVICES ON THE PAYOR’S BEHALF;
17 (II) THE PAYOR HOLDS THE AGENT OUT TO EMPLOYEES AND
18 OTHER PAYEES AS PROVIDING PAYROLL PROCESSING SERVICES ON THE PAYOR’S
19 BEHALF; AND
20 (III) THE PAYOR’S OBLIGATION TO A PAYEE, INCLUDING AN
21 EMPLOYEE OR ANOTHER PARTY ENTITLED TO RECEIVE FUNDS VIA THE PAYROLL
22 PROCESSING SERVICES PROVIDED BY THE AGENT, IS NOT EXTINGUISHED IF THE
23 AGENT FAILS TO REMIT THE FUNDS TO THE PAYEE.
24 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
25 October 1, 2026.
