A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Police Pursuits of Fleeing Suspects – Standards
3 (Dimeka Thornton Act)
4 FOR the purpose of requiring each law enforcement agency in the State to submit the
5 agency’s current policy regarding pursuit of fleeing suspects to the Maryland Police
6 Training and Standards Commission annually on or before a certain date; requiring
7 the Commission, in consultation with the Attorney General and certain law
8 enforcement agencies, to submit a certain report to the Governor and General
9 Assembly annually on or before a certain date; requiring the Commission to develop
10 a certain model policy on or before a certain date; requiring each law enforcement
11 agency in the State to adopt a certain policy for police pursuits of fleeing suspects on
12 or before a certain date; and generally relating to police pursuits of fleeing suspects.
