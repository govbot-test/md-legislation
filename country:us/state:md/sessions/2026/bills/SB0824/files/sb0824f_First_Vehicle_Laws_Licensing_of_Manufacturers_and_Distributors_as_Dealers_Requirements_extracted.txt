A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Vehicle Laws – Licensing of Manufacturers and Distributors as Dealers –
3 Requirements
4 FOR the purpose of altering certain requirements for vehicle manufacturers or distributors
5 to be licensed as vehicle dealers; and generally relating to manufacturers and
6 dealers.
7 BY repealing and reenacting, without amendments,
8 Article – Transportation
9 Section 15–305(b) and (f)
10 Annotated Code of Maryland
11 (2020 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
12 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
13 Article – Transportation
14 Section 15–305(e)(2)
15 Annotated Code of Maryland
16 (2020 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
17 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
18 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
19 Article – Transportation
20 15–305.
21 (b) A license to deal in new vehicles may be issued only for a dealer in new vehicles
22 who holds a franchise from:
23 (1) The manufacturer of the vehicles; or
EXPLANATION: CAPITALS INDICATE MATTER ADDED TO EXISTING LAW.

2 SENATE BILL 824
1 (2) A distributor who is authorized by the manufacturer or the
2 manufacturer’s authorized importer of the vehicles.
3 (e) (2) (i) Notwithstanding subsections (b) and (f) of this section and
4 subject to [subparagraph (ii)] SUBPARAGRAPHS (II) AND (III) of this paragraph, a
5 manufacturer or distributor may be licensed as a dealer if:
6 1. The manufacturer or distributor [deals] MANUFACTURES
7 OR DISTRIBUTES only [in electric] BATTERY–ELECTRIC or nonfossil–fuel burning
8 vehicles;
9 2. No dealer in the State holds a franchise from the
10 manufacturer or distributor;
11 3. The manufacturer or distributor[, or a subsidiary, an
12 affiliate, or a controlled entity of the manufacturer or distributor, does not hold a controlling
13 interest in another manufacturer or distributor, or a subsidiary, an affiliate, or a controlled
14 entity of the other manufacturer or distributor, that is licensed as a dealer under this
15 paragraph] IS NOT OWNED BY, CONTROLLED BY, UNDER COMMON CONTROL WITH, OR
16 AFFILIATED, DIRECTLY OR INDIRECTLY, WITH ANY OTHER MANUFACTURER OR
17 DISTRIBUTOR THAT HAS FRANCHISED DEALERS; and
18 4. [No other manufacturer or distributor, or subsidiary,
19 affiliate, or controlled entity of the other manufacturer or distributor, that is licensed as a
20 dealer under this paragraph, holds a controlling interest in the manufacturer or distributor,
21 or a subsidiary, an affiliate, or a controlled entity of the manufacturer or distributor] THE
22 MANUFACTURER OR DISTRIBUTOR IS NOT A SUBSIDIARY, AN AFFILIATE, A DIVISION,
23 A BRAND, A JOINT VENTURE WITH, OR SUCCESSOR TO ANY OTHER MANUFACTURER
24 OR DISTRIBUTOR THAT HAS FRANCHISED DEALERS.
25 (ii) A MANUFACTURER OR DISTRIBUTOR SEEKING A DEALER
26 LICENSE SHALL DEMONSTRATE TO THE SATISFACTION OF THE ADMINISTRATION
27 THAT IT COMPLIES WITH THE REQUIREMENTS OF SUBPARAGRAPH (I) OF THIS
28 PARAGRAPH.
29 (III) No more than four licenses may be issued under this paragraph.
30 [(iii)] (IV) The Administration shall adopt regulations to implement
31 this paragraph.
32 (f) A manufacturer or distributor, or a person who is acting for a partnership or
33 corporation that is owned or controlled by or under common control with a manufacturer
34 or distributor, may not sell a new vehicle to a retail buyer.
SENATE BILL 824 3
1 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
2 October 1, 2026.
