CF SB 748
By: Delegates Buckel, Hinebaugh, and Baker
Introduced and read first time: February 11, 2026
Assigned to: Appropriations
A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Correctional Officers’ Retirement System – Allegany County
3 FOR the purpose of requiring membership in the Correctional Officers’ Retirement System
4 for certain local detention center officers of Allegany County in the event Allegany
5 County becomes a participating governmental unit in the Correctional Officers’
6 Retirement System; providing that certain local detention center officers who join
7 the Correctional Officers’ Retirement System and receive certain service credit shall
8 no longer be members of the Employees’ Pension System; and generally relating to
9 Allegany County’s participation in the Correctional Officers’ Retirement System.
10 BY adding to
11 Article – State Personnel and Pensions
12 Section 31–2B–09
13 Annotated Code of Maryland
14 (2024 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
15 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
16 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
17 Article – State Personnel and Pensions
18 31–2B–09.
19 (A) THIS SECTION APPLIES TO AN INDIVIDUAL WHO IS A LOCAL DETENTION
20 CENTER OFFICER OF ALLEGANY COUNTY BEFORE THE EFFECTIVE DATE AND WHO
21 REMAINS A LOCAL DETENTION CENTER OFFICER OF ALLEGANY COUNTY THROUGH
22 THE EFFECTIVE DATE.
23 (B) NOTWITHSTANDING ANY OTHER PROVISION OF LAW, IF ALLEGANY
24 COUNTY BECOMES A PARTICIPATING GOVERNMENTAL UNIT IN THE CORRECTIONAL
EXPLANATION: CAPITALS INDICATE MATTER ADDED TO EXISTING LAW.
[Brackets] indicate matter deleted from exist in g l aw . *hb1102*
2 HOUSE BILL 1102
1 OFFICERS’ RETIREMENT SYSTEM, MEMBERSHIP IN THE CORRECTIONAL OFFICERS’
2 RETIREMENT SYSTEM IS MANDATORY FOR AN INDIVIDUAL DESCRIBED UNDER
3 SUBSECTION (A) OF THIS SECTION.
4 (C) IF ALLEGANY COUNTY COMMENCES PARTICIPATION IN THE
5 CORRECTIONAL OFFICERS’ RETIREMENT SYSTEM IN ACCORDANCE WITH
6 SUBSECTION (B) OF THIS SECTION, AN INDIVIDUAL DESCRIBED UNDER SUBSECTION
7 (A) OF THIS SECTION IS ENTITLED TO ELIGIBILITY SERVICE AND CREDITABLE
8 SERVICE IN THE CORRECTIONAL OFFICERS’ RETIREMENT SYSTEM FOR
9 EMPLOYMENT WITH ALLEGANY COUNTY BEFORE THE EFFECTIVE DATE.
10 (D) ON JOINING THE CORRECTIONAL OFFICERS’ RETIREMENT SYSTEM
11 AND RECEIVING SERVICE CREDIT AS PROVIDED UNDER SUBSECTION (C) OF THIS
12 SECTION, AN INDIVIDUAL DESCRIBED UNDER SUBSECTION (A) OF THIS SECTION
13 SHALL:
14 (1) NO LONGER BE A MEMBER OF THE EMPLOYEES’ PENSION
15 SYSTEM; AND
16 (2) HAVE NO FURTHER RIGHTS TO ANY BENEFIT IN THE EMPLOYEES’
17 PENSION SYSTEM.
18 (E) THE TRANSFER OF CREDITABLE SERVICE FOR AN INDIVIDUAL
19 DESCRIBED IN SUBSECTION (A) OF THIS SECTION WHO IS A MEMBER OF THE
20 EMPLOYEES’ PENSION SYSTEM THROUGH THE EFFECTIVE DATE, AND WHO
21 TRANSFERS TO THE CORRECTIONAL OFFICERS’ RETIREMENT SYSTEM UNDER THIS
22 SECTION, IS NOT GOVERNED BY TITLE 37 OF THIS ARTICLE.
23 (F) IF ALLEGANY COUNTY BECOMES A PARTICIPATING GOVERNMENTAL
24 UNIT IN THE CORRECTIONAL OFFICERS’ RETIREMENT SYSTEM, § 31–2B–05(C) OF
25 THIS SUBTITLE SHALL GOVERN THE TRANSFER AND CREDITING OF ASSETS ON THE
26 EFFECTIVE DATE.
27 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect June
28 1, 2026.
