17 MINIMUM, READING, GRAMMAR, SOCIAL STUDIES, AND SCIENCE THAT CONFORMS
18 TO EDUCATIONAL REQUIREMENTS FOR THE ELIGIBLE STUDENT’S GRADE LEVEL AS
19 ESTABLISHED BY THE DEPARTMENT;
20 (2) TO ENROLL THE ELIGIBLE STUDENT IN AN EDUCATION PROGRAM
21 THAT MEETS REGULAR SCHOOL ATTENDANCE REQUIREMENTS;
22 (3) TO ENSURE THAT THE ELIGIBLE STUDENT WILL TAKE ALL
23 ASSESSMENTS REQUIRED UNDER STATE LAW;
24 (4) TO RELEASE THE RESIDENT SCHOOL DISTRICT FROM ALL
25 OBLIGATIONS TO EDUCATE THE ELIGIBLE STUDENT;
