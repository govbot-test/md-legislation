A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 State Procurement – Prompt Payment of Subcontractors and Suppliers –
3 Alteration
4 FOR the purpose of requiring a contractor to pay a subcontractor or supplier a certain
5 percentage of an undisputed amount to which the subcontractor or supplier is
6 entitled within a certain number of days of receiving payment from the State; and
7 generally relating to the prompt payment of suppliers under State procurement
8 contracts.

1 (2) “Supplier” means a person that has supplied labor or materials to a
2 contractor in the prosecution of work provided for in a State procurement contract.
3 (3) “Undisputed amount” means an amount owed by a contractor to a
4 subcontractor or supplier for which there is no good faith dispute, including any retainage
5 withheld.
6 (c) (1) A contractor shall pay a subcontractor or supplier AT LEAST 95% OF
7 an undisputed amount to which the subcontractor or supplier is entitled within 10 days of
8 receiving a progress or final payment from the State.
9 (2) If a contractor withholds payment from a subcontractor or supplier,
10 within the time period in which payment normally would be made, the contractor shall:
11 (i) notify the subcontractor or supplier in writing and state the
12 reason why payment is being withheld; and
13 (ii) provide a copy of the notice to the procurement officer.
14 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect July
15 1, 2026.
