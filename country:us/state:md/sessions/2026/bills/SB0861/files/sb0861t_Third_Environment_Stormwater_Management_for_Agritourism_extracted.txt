CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Environment – Stormwater Management – Agricultural Land for Agritourism
3 FOR the purpose of requiring the Department of the Environment to adopt regulations that
4 establish provisions and a model ordinance that require local stormwater
5 management programs to enforce stormwater management regulations on
6 agricultural land in a certain manner; requiring the Department, in a certain
7 stormwater design manual, to specify certain definitions and provisions related to
8 the enforcement of certain stormwater management regulations on agricultural land
9 consider certain best management practices for stormwater management for
10 agritourism; requiring Montgomery County and the Montgomery Soil Conservation
11 District, in consultation with the Department, to review certain processes for
12 reviewing and approving certain stormwater management plans; and generally
13 relating to stormwater management and agricultural land agritourism.
