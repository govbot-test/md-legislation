WES MOORE, Governor Ch. 335
Chapter 335
(Senate Bill 83)
AN ACT concerning
Health Occupations – Grounds for Discipline – Federal and Court Actions
FOR the purpose of altering the grounds for discipline by certain health occupations boards
to include being disciplined by any branch of the uniformed services or the U.S.
Department of Veterans Affairs; altering the grounds for discipline of a naturopathic
doctor by the State Board of Physicians to include being convicted or disciplined by
a court of any state or country; and generally relating to the grounds for discipline
by health occupations boards.
