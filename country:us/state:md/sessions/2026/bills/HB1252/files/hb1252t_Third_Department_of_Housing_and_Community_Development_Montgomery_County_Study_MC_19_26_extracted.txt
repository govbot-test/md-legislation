1 AN ACT concerning
2 Department of Housing and Community Development – Montgomery County
3 Study
4 MC 19–26
5 FOR the purpose of requiring the Department of Housing and Community Development to
6 conduct a study and make recommendations regarding the availability and
7 affordability of housing in Montgomery County; and generally relating to housing in
8 Montgomery County.
9 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
10 That:
11 (a) The Department of Housing and Community Development shall:
12 (1) study housing availability and affordability in Montgomery County;
13 and
14 (2) make recommendations to increase housing availability and
15 affordability in Montgomery County.
16 (b) In conducting the study and making the recommendations required under this
17 section, the Department shall:
18 (1) identify the makeup of available housing in the county by unit type;

1 (2) identify any development prospects in the county; and
2 (3) determine the impact of regulations and market forces on Montgomery
3 County’s housing supply.
4 (c) The Subject to subsection (d) of this section, the Department shall submit to
5 the Maryland–National Capital Park and Planning Commission, the Montgomery County
6 Planning Department, the Montgomery County Executive, the Montgomery County
7 Council, and, in accordance with § 2–1257 of the State Government Article, the
8 Montgomery County Delegation to the General Assembly:
9 (1) interim findings and recommendations under this section on or before:
10 (i) November 30, 2026 February 1, 2027; and
11 (ii) November 30, 2027 February 1, 2028; and
12 (2) a final report of its findings and recommendations under this section on
13 or before November 30, 2028 February 1, 2029.
14 (d) (1) The Department shall confer with the Montgomery County Department
15 of Housing and Community Affairs, Department of Permitting Services, and Planning
16 Department regarding data collection and analysis.
17 (2) Before issuing each of the reports required under subsection (c) of this
18 section, the Department shall provide each of the entities listed in paragraph (1) of this
19 subsection with an opportunity to provide a written response to its findings and
20 recommendations and include any written response as an appendix to the report.
21 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect July
22 1, 2026. It shall remain in effect for a period of 3 years and, and the end of June 30, 2029,
23 this Act, with no further action required by the General Assembly, shall be abrogated and
24 of no further force and effect.
