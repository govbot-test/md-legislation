A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 State Board of Education – Public High School Graduation Requirements
3 FOR the purpose of requiring the State Board of Education to periodically review
4 graduation requirements for public high school students; requiring implementation
5 of updated graduation requirements for a certain cohort of students following State
6 Board approval; and generally relating to public high school student graduation
7 requirements.
8 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
9 Article – Education
10 Section 7–205.1(b)
11 Annotated Code of Maryland
12 (2025 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
13 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
14 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
15 Article – Education
16 7–205.1.
17 (b) (1) The State Board shall establish high school curriculum, college and
18 career readiness standards, and graduation requirements for all public schools in
19 accordance with this section.
20 (2) The State Board shall coordinate and consult with the Maryland Higher
21 Education Commission, the Governor’s Workforce Development Board, the Maryland
22 Association of Community Colleges, and the Accountability and Implementation Board in
23 performing its duties under this subsection.
