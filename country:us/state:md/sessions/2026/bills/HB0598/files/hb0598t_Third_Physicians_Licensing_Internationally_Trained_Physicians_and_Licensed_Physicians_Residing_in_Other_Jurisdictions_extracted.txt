CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Physicians – Licensing – Internationally Trained Physicians and Licensed
3 Physicians Residing in Other Jurisdictions
4 FOR the purpose of authorizing the State Board of Physicians to issue a certain license to
5 practice medicine to an internationally trained physician under certain
6 circumstances; altering the circumstances under which a physician licensed by and
7 residing in another jurisdiction may practice medicine in the State without a license;
8 and generally relating to the licensure of internationally trained physicians.
