1 11–102.
2 This title applies only in Anne Arundel County.
3 11–1002.
4 (a) There is a barbershop and beauty salon beer [and], wine, AND LIQUOR
5 license.
6 (b) The Board may issue the license to a holder of:
7 (1) a barbershop permit issued under § 4–501 of the Business Occupations
8 and Professions Article; or
9 (2) a beauty salon permit issued under § 5–501 of the Business Occupations
10 and Professions Article.
11 (c) The license authorizes the license holder to provide not more than 12 ounces
12 of beer [or], 5 ounces of wine by the glass, OR 1.7 OUNCES OF LIQUOR for on–premises
13 consumption by a barbershop or beauty salon customer:
14 (1) when the customer is being provided:
15 (i) a barbering service under Title 4 of the Business Occupations
16 and Professions Article; or
17 (ii) a cosmetology service under Title 5 of the Business Occupations
18 and Professions Article; or
19 (2) while the customer is attending a fund–raising event at the barbershop
20 or beauty salon.
21 (d) A license holder may serve beer [and], wine, AND LIQUOR for on–premises
22 consumption during normal business hours but not later than 9 p.m.
23 (e) A license holder need not obtain a Sunday license under § 11–2004 of this title
24 to provide beer [and], wine, AND LIQUOR on Sunday.
25 (f) The annual license fee is $100.
26 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect July
27 1, 2026.
