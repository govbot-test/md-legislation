3 (A) IN THIS SECTION, “STATE OR LOCAL PUBLIC SAFETY EMPLOYEE” HAS
4 THE MEANING STATED IN § 18–601 OF THIS ARTICLE.

6 RESIDES, A FINANCIALLY DEPENDENT CHILD OF A STATE OR LOCAL PUBLIC SAFETY
7 EMPLOYEE WHO DIES AS A RESULT OF OR IN THE COURSE OF PERFORMANCE OF
8 DUTIES IS EXEMPT FROM PAYING OUT–OF–STATE OR OUT–OF–COUNTY TUITION AT
9 A PUBLIC INSTITUTION OF HIGHER EDUCATION IN THE STATE.

13 (a) (1) In this section the following words have the meanings indicated.
14 (5) “State or local public safety employee” means a person who is employed
15 in the State as:
16 (i) A career or volunteer member of a:
17 1. Fire department;
18 2. Ambulance company or squad; or
19 3. Rescue company or squad;
20 (ii) A law enforcement officer;
21 (iii) A correctional officer; or
22 (iv) A member of the Maryland National Guard who was a resident
23 of this State at the time of death.
