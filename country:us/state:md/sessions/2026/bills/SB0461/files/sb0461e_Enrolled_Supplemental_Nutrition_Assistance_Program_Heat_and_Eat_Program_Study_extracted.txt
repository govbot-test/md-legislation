2 Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program – Heat and Eat Program –
3 Eligibility Study
4 FOR the purpose of altering the eligibility requirements for the Heat and Eat Program
5 within the Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program in the Department of
6 Human Services to require a household to include an elderly or disabled individual
7 requiring the Department of Human Services to study the potential number of
8 households excluded from automatic standard utility allowance eligibility for the
9 Heat and Eat Program within the Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program due
10 to enactment of a certain federal law; and generally relating to a study on eligibility
11 for the Heat and Eat Program within the Supplemental Nutrition Assistance
12 Program and the Heat and Eat Program.

4 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
5 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
6 Article – Human Services
7 5–506.
8 (a) In this section, “standard utility allowance” means a Maryland Energy
9 Assistance Program payment to a household that is at least $21 per year.
10 (b) (1) There is a Heat and Eat Program within the Supplemental Nutrition
11 Assistance Program in the Department.
12 (2) The purpose of the Heat and Eat Program is to expand food access to
13 households that are receiving or eligible for Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program
14 benefits.
15 (c) (1) A household is eligible to participate in the Heat and Eat Program if
16 the household:
17 (I) is eligible to receive Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program
18 benefits under State and federal law; AND
19 (II) INCLUDES AN ELDERLY OR DISABLED INDIVIDUAL.
20 (2) In determining the eligibility of a household to receive Supplemental
21 Nutrition Assistance Program benefits, the Department shall apply a standard utility
22 allowance to the shelter deduction that is used for the purpose of determining countable
23 gross income for Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program eligibility.
24 (d) The Department shall adopt regulations:
25 (1) to verify household eligibility for participation in the Heat and Eat
26 Program; and
27 (2) otherwise necessary to carry out this section.
28 (a) The Department of Human Services shall study and make recommendations
29 on the potential number of households excluded from automatic standard utility allowance
30 eligibility for the Heat and Eat Program within the Supplemental Nutrition Assistance
31 Program due to enactment of the federal One Big Beautiful Bill Act of 2025.
