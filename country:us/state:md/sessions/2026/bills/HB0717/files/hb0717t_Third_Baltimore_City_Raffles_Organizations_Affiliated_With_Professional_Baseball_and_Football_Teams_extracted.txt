CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Baltimore City – Raffles – Organizations Affiliated With Professional Baseball
3 and Football Teams
4 FOR the purpose of authorizing certain organizations that are affiliated with a professional
5 National Football League team that plays its home games in Baltimore City to
6 conduct certain raffles, subject to certain limitations; repealing extending a certain
7 termination date of certain provisions of law authorizing certain organizations that
8 are affiliated with a professional Major League Baseball team that plays its home
9 games in Baltimore City to conduct certain raffles; and generally relating to raffles
10 in Baltimore City.

5 13–501.
6 (a) In this subtitle the following words have the meanings indicated.
7 (b) “Commissioner” means the Baltimore City Police Commissioner.
8 (c) “Designated city agency” means an agency designated by the Baltimore City
9 government.
10 (d) (1) “Electronic device” means a device that is capable of transmitting,
11 receiving, or recording messages, images, sounds, data, or other information by electronic
12 means or that, in appearance, purports to be a cell phone, computer, or other such device.
13 (2) “Electronic device” includes a cell phone or computer.
14 (3) “Electronic device” does not include a video lottery terminal as defined
15 under § 9–1A–01 of the State Government Article.
16 (e) “Gaming event” means a carnival, bazaar, or raffle.
17 (f) (1) “Geofence” means technology that establishes a virtual geographical
18 boundary.
19 (2) “Geofence” includes boundaries that are established or monitored
20 through the use of:
21 (i) global positioning technology;
22 (ii) cell tower connectivity;
23 (iii) cellular data;
24 (iv) radio frequency identification;
25 (v) wireless fidelity technology; or
26 (vi) any other form of location determination technology.
27 (g) “Raffle” means one or more drawings from a single series of chances sold.
