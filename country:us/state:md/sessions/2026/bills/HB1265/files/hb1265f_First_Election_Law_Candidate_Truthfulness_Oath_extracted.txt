A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Election Law – Candidate Truthfulness – Oath
3 FOR the purpose of requiring a candidate to submit a certain oath or statement with the
4 candidate’s certificate of candidacy relating to the candidate’s truthfulness regarding
5 certain information while campaigning for office; subjecting a candidate who attests
6 to the oath to certain sanctions for a violation of the oath; and generally relating to
7 oaths of candidate truthfulness.
