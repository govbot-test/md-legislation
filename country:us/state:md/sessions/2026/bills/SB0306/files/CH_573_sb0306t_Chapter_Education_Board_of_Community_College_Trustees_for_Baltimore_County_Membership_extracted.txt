Chapter 573
AN ACT concerning
Education – Board of Community College Trustees for Baltimore County –
Membership
FOR the purpose of altering the membership of the Board of Community College Trustees
for Baltimore County; and generally relating to the Board of Community College
Trustees for Baltimore County.
BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
Article – Education

Section 2:
Section 16–402
Annotated Code of Maryland
(2022 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)

Section 3:
SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
Article – Education
16–402.
(a) The Board of Community College Trustees for Baltimore County consists of
[15] 11 members appointed by the Governor, with the advice and consent of the Senate.
(b) Of the members:
(1) [1 serves] 2 SERVE at large; and
(2) [14] 9 serve from the [7] 9 councilmanic districts in Baltimore County,
[2] 1 from each district.
(c) (1) The term of a member is 5 years and begins on July 1.
(2) A member may not serve for more than 2 consecutive terms.
(d) (1) The Governor shall appoint a chairperson from among the members of
the Board as of July 1, 1997.
(2) The chairperson appointed by the Governor shall serve until June 30,
