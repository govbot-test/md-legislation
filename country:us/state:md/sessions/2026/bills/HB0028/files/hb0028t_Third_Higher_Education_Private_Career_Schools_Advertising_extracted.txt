2 Higher Education – Private Career Schools – Advertising

4 career schools to advertise certain information and collect certain contact

2 HOUSE BILL 28
1 10–101.
2 (a) In this division the following words have the meanings indicated.
3 (j) “Private career school” means a privately owned and privately operated
4 institution of postsecondary education other than an institution of higher education that
5 furnishes or offers to furnish programs, whether or not requiring a payment of tuition or
6 fee, for the purpose of training, retraining, or upgrading individuals for gainful employment
7 as skilled or semiskilled workers or technicians in recognized occupations or in new and
8 emerging occupations.
9 11–212.
10 (A) THE SECRETARY MAY APPROVE A REQUEST FROM AN APPLICANT FOR A
11 PRIVATE CAREER SCHOOL TO ADVERTISE AND COLLECT CONTACT INFORMATION
12 FROM POTENTIAL NEW STUDENTS BEFORE THE PRIVATE CAREER SCHOOL RECEIVES
13 A CERTIFICATE OF APPROVAL TO OPERATE.
14 (B) A PRIVATE CAREER SCHOOL APPROVED UNDER SUBSECTION (A) OF
15 THIS SECTION:
16 (1) SHALL:
17 (I) ADVERTISE ONLY INFORMATION RELATING TO THE
18 OPENING OF THE PRIVATE CAREER SCHOOL, INCLUDING THE ANTICIPATED DATE OF
19 OPENING, PROGRAMS OF INSTRUCTION, AND THE APPLICATION PROCESS; AND
20 (II) INCLUDE, IN ANY ADVERTISEMENT, A DISCLOSURE THAT
21 THE CERTIFICATE OF APPROVAL TO OPERATE THE PRIVATE CAREER SCHOOL IS
22 PENDING APPROVAL; AND
23 (2) MAY NOT COLLECT ANY FEES OR ENTER INTO AN ENROLLMENT
24 AGREEMENT WITH POTENTIAL STUDENTS.
25 (C) THE SECRETARY SHALL ADOPT REGULATIONS PROVIDING FOR THE
26 PROCESS TO APPROVE A PRIVATE CAREER SCHOOL TO ADVERTISE UNDER THIS
27 SECTION.
28 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect July
29 1, 2026.
