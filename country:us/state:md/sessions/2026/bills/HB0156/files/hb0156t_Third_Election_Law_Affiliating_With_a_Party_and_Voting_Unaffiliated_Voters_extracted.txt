1 AN ACT concerning
2 Election Law – Affiliating With a Party and Voting – Unaffiliated Voters
3 FOR the purpose of authorizing unaffiliated voters to request to affiliate with a political
4 party at an early voting center during before a primary election or at a precinct
5 polling place on a primary election day and vote a provisional ballot for the voter’s
6 preferred political party; and generally relating to affiliating with a political party
7 and voting in a primary election.
