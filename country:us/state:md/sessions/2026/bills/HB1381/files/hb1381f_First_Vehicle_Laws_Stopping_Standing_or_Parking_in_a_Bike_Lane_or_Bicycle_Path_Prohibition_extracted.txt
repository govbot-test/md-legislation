A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Vehicle Laws – Stopping, Standing, or Parking in a Bike Lane or Bicycle Path –
3 Prohibition
4 FOR the purpose of prohibiting a person from stopping, standing, or parking a vehicle in a
5 bike lane or bicycle path, subject to certain exceptions; and generally relating to
6 stopping, standing, or parking a vehicle in a bike lane or bicycle path.
