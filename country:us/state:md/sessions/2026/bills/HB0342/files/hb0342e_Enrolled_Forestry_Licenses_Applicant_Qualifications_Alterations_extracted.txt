1 AN ACT concerning
2 Forestry Licenses – Applicant Qualifications – Alterations
3 FOR the purpose of requiring that an institution from which an applicant for a forestry
4 license graduates on completion of a certain curriculum has certain approval and
5 accreditation; authorizing an applicant to qualify for a forestry license if the State
6 Board of Foresters determines the applicant’s education is substantially equivalent
7 to certain requirements; repealing a certain experience requirement for applicants
8 for a forestry license; and generally relating to requirements for forestry licenses.
