A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Baltimore County – Alcoholic Beverages – Manufacturer’s Limited Beer, Wine,
3 and Liquor Permit
4 FOR the purpose of establishing a manufacturer’s limited beer, wine, and liquor permit in
5 Baltimore County; authorizing the Board of License Commissioners for Baltimore
6 County to issue the permit to holders of certain manufacturer’s licenses; requiring
7 the Board to adopt certain regulations; and generally relating to alcoholic beverages
8 in Baltimore County.
9 BY repealing and reenacting, without amendments,
10 Article – Alcoholic Beverages and Cannabis
11 Section 13–102
12 Annotated Code of Maryland
13 (2024 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
14 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
15 Article – Alcoholic Beverages and Cannabis
16 Section 13–401
17 Annotated Code of Maryland
18 (2024 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
19 BY adding to
20 Article – Alcoholic Beverages and Cannabis
21 Section 13–403
22 Annotated Code of Maryland
23 (2024 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
24 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
25 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
26 Article – Alcoholic Beverages and Cannabis

1 (b) Section 2–215 (“Beer sale on credit to retail dealer prohibited”) of Division I of
2 this article does not apply in the county.
3 (C) SECTION 2–207 (“CLASS 5 BREWERY LICENSE”) OF DIVISION I OF THIS
4 ARTICLE APPLIES IN THE COUNTY, SUBJECT TO § 13–403 OF THIS SUBTITLE.
5 13–403.
6 (A) THERE IS A MANUFACTURER’S LIMITED BEER, WINE, AND LIQUOR
7 PERMIT.
8 (B) THE BOARD MAY ISSUE THE PERMIT TO A HOLDER OF A
9 MANUFACTURER’S LICENSE WITH A LOCAL ON–SITE CONSUMPTION PERMIT UNDER
10 § 2–207 OF THIS ARTICLE.
11 (C) (1) SUBJECT TO PARAGRAPH (2) OF THIS SUBSECTION, THE PERMIT
12 AUTHORIZES THE HOLDER TO ALLOW A LICENSED CATERER OR OTHER
13 CONTRACTED PARTY TO PROVIDE SEALED BEER, WINE, AND LIQUOR PRODUCTS NOT
14 MANUFACTURED BY THE PERMIT HOLDER DURING PRIVATE EVENTS FOR
15 ON–PREMISES CONSUMPTION AT THE PREMISES DESCRIBED IN THE
16 MANUFACTURER’S LICENSE.
17 (2) THE PERMIT HOLDER MAY NOT BE AN AFFILIATE OF THE
18 LICENSED CATERER OR OTHER CONTRACTED PARTY.
19 (D) THE PERMIT HOLDER MAY NOT ALLOW PATRONS WHO ARE VISITING ANY
20 PART OF THE MANUFACTURER’S LICENSED PREMISES THAT IS OPEN TO THE PUBLIC
21 TO ENTER THE PRIVATE EVENT AREA WHILE THE PRIVATE EVENT IS BEING HELD.
22 (E) THE PERMIT HOLDER SHALL REQUEST APPROVAL FROM THE BOARD
23 BEFORE EACH EVENT.
24 (F) THE BOARD SHALL ADOPT REGULATIONS TO CARRY OUT THIS SECTION,
25 INCLUDING:
26 (1) CRITERIA FOR APPROVING EVENTS; AND
27 (2) SETTING THE ANNUAL PERMIT FEE.
28 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect July
29 1, 2026.
