1 AN ACT concerning
2 County Boards of Education – Student Transportation – Sunset Repeal and
3 Study
4 FOR the purpose of repealing the termination date of certain provisions of law that
5 authorize a county board of education to provide transportation to and from school
6 for certain students using a vehicle other than a certain type of school vehicle under
7 certain circumstances; authorizing a county board to contract with a certain
8 transportation network company if the transportation network operator meets
9 certain requirements; requiring the State Department of Education, in consultation
10 with county boards and the Motor Vehicle Administration, to study the current
11 regulations for vehicles other than a certain type of school vehicle; and generally
12 relating to vehicles for student transportation and county boards of education.

26 SECTION 3. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That:
27 (a) The State Department of Education, in consultation with county boards of
28 education and the Motor Vehicle Administration, shall review the regulations adopted
29 under § 7–801(c) of the Education Article, as enacted by Chapters 197 and 198 of the Acts
30 of the General Assembly of 2021, authorizing vehicles other than Type I and Type II school
31 vehicles to transport public school students.
32 (b) In reviewing the regulations, the Department shall examine:

1 (1) the requirements for vehicles other than Type I and Type II school
2 vehicles, including whether they arbitrarily distinguish between different operators of a
3 vehicle, different types of vehicles, and the number of passengers transported;
4 (2) the requirements for the drivers of vehicles other than Type I and Type
5 II school vehicles, including whether they adequately ensure drivers are prepared for
6 transporting public school students; and
7 (3) the regulations adopted by the Public Service Commission for
8 transportation network operators and whether the regulations adopted by the Department
9 can be brought into alignment with those regulations without sacrificing student safety.
10 (c) On or before January 1, 2027, the State Department of Education shall report
11 on the results of its review to the Senate Education, Energy, and the Environment
12 Committee and the House Ways and Means Committee, in accordance with § 2–1257 of the
13 State Government Article.
14 SECTION 4. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That:
15 (a) If the State Department of Education adopts regulations regarding student
16 transportation after the review of the regulations adopted under § 7–801(c) of the Education
17 Article, as enacted by Chapters 197 and 198 of the Acts of the General Assembly of 2021,
18 authorizing vehicles other than Type I and Type II school vehicles to transport public school
19 students, Section 2 of this Act shall be abrogated and of no further force and effect.
20 (b) The State Department of Education shall notify the Department of Legislative
21 Services within 5 days after the effective date of the regulations adopted in accordance with
