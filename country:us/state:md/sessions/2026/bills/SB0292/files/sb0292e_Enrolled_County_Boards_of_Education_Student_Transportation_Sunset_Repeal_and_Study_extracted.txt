1 AN ACT concerning
2 County Boards of Education – Student Transportation – Sunset Repeal and
3 Study
4 FOR the purpose of repealing the termination date of certain provisions of law that
5 authorize a county board of education to provide transportation to and from school
6 for certain students using a vehicle other than a certain type of school vehicle under
7 certain circumstances; authorizing a county board to contract with a certain
8 transportation network company if the transportation network operator meets
9 certain requirements; requiring the State Department of Education, in consultation
10 with county boards and the Motor Vehicle Administration, to study the current
11 regulations for vehicles other than a certain type of school vehicle; and generally
12 relating to vehicles for student transportation and county boards of education.
13 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
14 Chapter 197 of the Acts of the General Assembly of 2021

1 SECTION 3. 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That:
2 (a) The State Department of Education, in consultation with county boards of
3 education and the Motor Vehicle Administration, shall review the regulations adopted
4 under § 7–801(c) of the Education Article, as enacted by Chapters 197 and 198 of the Acts
5 of the General Assembly of 2021, authorizing vehicles other than Type I and Type II school
6 vehicles to transport public school students.
7 (b) In reviewing the regulations, the Department shall examine:
8 (1) the requirements for vehicles other than Type I and Type II school
9 vehicles, including whether they arbitrarily distinguish between different operators of a
10 vehicle, different types of vehicles, and the number of passengers transported;
11 (2) the requirements for the drivers of vehicles other than Type I and Type
12 II school vehicles, including whether they adequately ensure drivers are prepared for
13 transporting public school students; and
14 (3) the regulations adopted by the Public Service Commission for
15 transportation network operators and whether the regulations adopted by the Department
16 can be brought into alignment with those regulations without sacrificing student safety.
17 (c) On or before January 1, 2027, the State Department of Education shall report
18 on the results of its review to the Senate Education, Energy, and the Environment
19 Committee and the House Ways and Means Committee, in accordance with § 2–1257 of the
20 State Government Article.
21 SECTION 4. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That:
22 (a) If the State Department of Education adopts regulations regarding student
23 transportation after the review of the regulations adopted under § 7–801(c) of the Education
24 Article, as enacted by Chapters 197 and 198 of the Acts of the General Assembly of 2021,
25 authorizing vehicles other than Type I and Type II school vehicles to transport public school
26 students, Section 2 of this Act shall be abrogated and of no further force and effect.
27 (b) The State Department of Education shall notify the Department of Legislative
28 Services within 5 days after the effective date of the regulations adopted in accordance with
29 subsection (a) of this section.
30 SECTION 2. 5. 3. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
31 June 1, 2026.
