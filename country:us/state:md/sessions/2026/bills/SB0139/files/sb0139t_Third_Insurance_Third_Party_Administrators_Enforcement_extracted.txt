17 (c) (1) In addition to any other enforcement action taken by the Commissioner
18 under this section, the Commissioner may impose a civil penalty of not more than $10,000
19 for each violation of this [subtitle] ARTICLE COMMITTED BY AN ADMINISTRATOR.
20 (2) Notwithstanding paragraph (1) of this subsection, the Commissioner
21 may impose a civil penalty of not more than $1,000 per day for each day that a person is in
