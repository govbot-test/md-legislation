A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Department of Agriculture – Regulation of Dog Breeders
3 FOR the purpose of establishing the Dog Breeder Accountability Fund as a special,
4 nonlapsing fund; requiring, on or before a certain date beginning in a certain year, a
5 certain dog breeder to register annually with the Department of Agriculture in a
6 certain manner; requiring, beginning on a certain date, a certain dog breeder to
7 submit to the Department an application for a certain puppy birth certificate in a
8 certain manner; prohibiting, beginning on a certain date, a certain dog breeder from
9 selling or otherwise transferring to the public a puppy unless certain conditions are
10 met; requiring, on or before a certain date, the Department to develop a certain
11 registry; and generally relating to the regulation of dog breeders by the Department
12 of Agriculture.
13 BY adding to
14 Article – Agriculture
15 Section 17–101 through 17–106 to be under the new title “Title 17. Regulation of Dog
16 Breeders”
17 Annotated Code of Maryland
18 (2016 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
19 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
20 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
21 Article – Agriculture
22 TITLE 17. REGULATION OF DOG BREEDERS.
23 17–101.

1 (A) IN THIS TITLE THE FOLLOWING WORDS HAVE THE MEANINGS
2 INDICATED.
3 (B) “BREEDER” MEANS A PERSON THAT BREEDS DOGS IN THE STATE TO
4 SELL, EXCHANGE, OR OTHERWISE TRANSFER TO THE PUBLIC.
5 (C) “FUND” MEANS THE DOG BREEDER ACCOUNTABILITY FUND
6 ESTABLISHED UNDER § 17–102 OF THIS TITLE.
7 (D) “MICROCHIP” MEANS A RADIO–FREQUENCY IDENTIFICATION DEVICE
8 THAT COMPLIES WITH MICROCHIP STANDARDS SET BY THE INTERNATIONAL
9 ORGANIZATION FOR STANDARDIZATION.
10 (E) “PUPPY BIRTH CERTIFICATE” MEANS A BIRTH CERTIFICATE FOR A
11 PUPPY THAT IS ISSUED BY THE DEPARTMENT TO A BREEDER UNDER § 17–103(B) OF
12 THIS TITLE.
13 17–102.
14 (A) THERE IS A DOG BREEDER ACCOUNTABILITY FUND.
15 (B) THE PURPOSE OF THE FUND IS TO:
16 (1) PROMOTE BREEDER REGISTRATION AND ACCOUNTABILITY; AND
17 (2) PROVIDE FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE TO ANIMAL SHELTERS AND
18 SPAY/NEUTER PROGRAMS TO REDUCE SHELTER INTAKE AND IMPROVE SHELTER
19 OUTCOMES.
20 (C) THE DEPARTMENT SHALL ADMINISTER THE FUND.
21 (D) (1) THE FUND IS A SPECIAL, NONLAPSING FUND THAT IS NOT
22 SUBJECT TO § 7–302 OF THE STATE FINANCE AND PROCUREMENT ARTICLE.
23 (2) THE STATE TREASURER SHALL HOLD THE FUND SEPARATELY,
24 AND THE COMPTROLLER SHALL ACCOUNT FOR THE FUND.
25 (E) THE FUND CONSISTS OF:
26 (1) REVENUE DISTRIBUTED TO THE FUND UNDER §§ 17–103 AND
27 17–106 OF THIS TITLE;
HOUSE BILL 1551 3
1 (2) MONEY APPROPRIATED IN THE STATE BUDGET TO THE FUND;
2 AND
3 (3) ANY OTHER MONEY FROM ANY OTHER SOURCE ACCEPTED FOR
4 THE BENEFIT OF THE FUND.
5 (F) THE FUND MAY BE USED ONLY AS FOLLOWS:
6 (1) 50% FOR FINANCIAL AWARDS TO ANIMAL SHELTERS AND
7 SPAY/NEUTER PROGRAMS;
8 (2) 30% FOR ADMINISTRATIVE EXPENSES; AND
9 (3) 20% FOR EDUCATION AND ENFORCEMENT ACTIVITIES.
10 (G) (1) THE STATE TREASURER SHALL INVEST THE MONEY OF THE FUND
11 IN THE SAME MANNER AS OTHER STATE MONEY MAY BE INVESTED.
12 (2) ANY INTEREST EARNINGS OF THE FUND SHALL BE CREDITED TO
13 THE GENERAL FUND OF THE STATE.
14 17–103.
15 (A) (1) ON OR BEFORE JULY 1, 2028, AND EACH YEAR THEREAFTER, A
16 BREEDER SHALL:
17 (I) REGISTER ANNUALLY WITH THE DEPARTMENT IN
18 ACCORDANCE WITH THIS SUBSECTION; AND
19 (II) AT THE TIME OF REGISTRATION, PAY TO THE DEPARTMENT
20 AN ANNUAL REGISTRATION FEE OF $150.
21 (2) AN ANNUAL REGISTRATION SHALL:
22 (I) BE SUBMITTED ON THE FORM THAT THE DEPARTMENT
23 PROVIDES; AND
24 (II) INCLUDE:
25 1. THE NAME OF THE BREEDER;
26 2. THE ADDRESS OF THE BREEDER;
4 HOUSE BILL 1551
1 3. THE NUMBER OF BREEDING FEMALES MAINTAINED
2 BY THE BREEDER FOR THE IMMEDIATELY PRECEDING CALENDAR YEAR;
3 4. THE NUMBER OF LITTERS MAINTAINED BY THE
4 BREEDER FOR THE IMMEDIATELY PRECEDING CALENDAR YEAR;
5 5. PROOF OF COMPLIANCE WITH LOCAL ZONING
6 REGULATIONS; AND
7 6. ANY OTHER INFORMATION REQUIRED BY THE
8 DEPARTMENT.
9 (B) (1) BEGINNING JULY 1, 2029, A BREEDER SHALL:
10 (I) WITHIN 30 DAYS AFTER THE BIRTH OF A LITTER, SUBMIT TO
11 THE DEPARTMENT AN APPLICATION FOR A BIRTH CERTIFICATE FOR EACH PUPPY
12 BORN IN THE LITTER ON THE FORM THAT THE DEPARTMENT PROVIDES; AND
13 (II) PAY TO THE DEPARTMENT AN APPLICATION FEE OF $50
14 FOR EACH PUPPY IDENTIFIED IN THE APPLICATION.
15 (2) EACH PUPPY BIRTH CERTIFICATE ISSUED BY THE DEPARTMENT
16 SHALL INCLUDE:
17 (I) THE REGISTRATION NUMBER ASSIGNED TO THE BREEDER;
18 (II) THE PARENTAGE OF THE PUPPY; AND
19 (III) FOR A PUPPY BORN ON OR AFTER JULY 1, 2030, THE
20 MICROCHIP IDENTIFICATION NUMBER ASSIGNED TO THE PUPPY.
21 (C) THE FEES COLLECTED BY THE DEPARTMENT UNDER THIS SECTION
22 SHALL BE DEPOSITED INTO THE FUND.
23 17–104.
24 BEGINNING JULY 1, 2028, A BREEDER MAY NOT SELL, EXCHANGE, OR
25 OTHERWISE TRANSFER TO THE PUBLIC A PUPPY UNLESS:
26 (1) THE BREEDER IS REGISTERED WITH THE DEPARTMENT UNDER §
27 17–103(A) OF THIS TITLE;

1 (2) FOR A PUPPY THAT IS BORN ON OR AFTER JULY 1, 2029, A PUPPY
2 BIRTH CERTIFICATE HAS BEEN ISSUED FOR THE PUPPY; AND
3 (3) FOR A PUPPY THAT IS BORN ON OR AFTER JULY 1, 2030, THE
4 PUPPY IS MICROCHIPPED AND THE MICROCHIP NUMBER MATCHES THE MICROCHIP
5 IDENTIFICATION NUMBER INCLUDED ON THE PUPPY’S BIRTH CERTIFICATE.
6 17–105.
7 (A) ON OR BEFORE JULY 1, 2027, THE DEPARTMENT SHALL:
8 (1) ADOPT REGULATIONS TO CARRY OUT THIS TITLE; AND
9 (2) DEVELOP A PUBLICLY ACCESSIBLE REGISTRY TO MAINTAIN
10 INFORMATION RELATED TO EACH BREEDER REGISTERED WITH THE DEPARTMENT
11 UNDER § 17–103(A) OF THIS TITLE, INCLUDING THE INFORMATION PROVIDED ON
12 EACH PUPPY BIRTH CERTIFICATE ISSUED BY THE DEPARTMENT TO THE BREEDER.
13 (B) ON OR BEFORE OCTOBER 1, 2029, AND EACH YEAR THEREAFTER, THE
14 DEPARTMENT SHALL REPORT TO THE GOVERNOR AND, IN ACCORDANCE WITH §
15 2–1257 OF THE STATE GOVERNMENT ARTICLE, THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY ON THE
16 IMPLEMENTATION OF THIS TITLE, INCLUDING:
17 (1) THE NUMBER OF BREEDERS REGISTERED WITH THE
18 DEPARTMENT;
19 (2) THE AMOUNT AND SOURCE OF MONEY DEPOSITED INTO THE
20 FUND;
21 (3) THE ACTIVITIES AND PROGRAMS THAT HAVE RECEIVED
22 FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE FROM THE FUND; AND
23 (4) THE IMPACTS ON ANIMAL SHELTER INTAKE AND SHELTER
24 OUTCOMES.
25 17–106.
26 (A) (1) A PERSON WHO VIOLATES THIS TITLE IS SUBJECT TO AN
27 ADMINISTRATIVE PENALTY OF UP TO $5,000 PER VIOLATION.
28 (2) EACH LITTER OF PUPPIES THAT IS NOT REPORTED TO THE
29 DEPARTMENT IN ACCORDANCE WITH THIS TITLE IS A SEPARATE VIOLATION.
