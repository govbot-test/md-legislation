F1 6lr3402

By: Senators Zucker, Ellis, Gile, Kramer, Lam, McCray, Rosapepe, and
Waldstreicher
Introduced and read first time: February 6, 2026
Assigned to: Budget and Taxation
A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Education – Minimum Wage for Education Support Professionals
3 FOR the purpose of establishing a certain minimum wage rate to be paid by county boards
4 of education to certain education support professionals beginning on or before a
5 certain date; and generally relating to a minimum wage for education support
6 professionals.
7 BY adding to
8 Article – Education
9 Section 6–306 to be under the amended subtitle “Subtitle 3. Salaries and Wages”
10 Annotated Code of Maryland
11 (2025 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
12 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
13 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
14 Article – Education
15 Subtitle 3. Salaries AND WAGES.
16 6–306.
17 (A) (1) IN THIS SECTION THE FOLLOWING WORDS HAVE THE MEANINGS
18 INDICATED.
19 (2) “EDUCATION SUPPORT PROFESSIONAL” MEANS A
20 NONCERTIFICATED PUBLIC SCHOOL EMPLOYEE DESIGNATED AS PART OF A
21 NONSUPERVISORY BARGAINING UNIT UNDER SUBTITLE 5 OF THIS TITLE.
EXPLANATION: CAPITALS INDICATE MATTER ADDED TO EXISTING LAW.
[Brackets] indicate matter deleted from exist in g l aw . *sb0764*
2 SENATE BILL 764
1 (3) “PUBLIC SCHOOL EMPLOYEE” HAS THE MEANING STATED IN §
2 6–501 OF THIS TITLE.
3 (B) NOTWITHSTANDING § 3–413 OF THE LABOR AND EMPLOYMENT
4 ARTICLE, BEGINNING JULY 1, 2028, EACH COUNTY BOARD SHALL PAY TO EACH
5 EDUCATION SUPPORT PROFESSIONAL A MINIMUM WAGE RATE OF AT LEAST $25.00
6 PER HOUR.
7 (C) THE STATE BOARD MAY ADOPT REGULATIONS NECESSARY TO CARRY
8 OUT THE PROVISIONS OF THIS SECTION.
9 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That, on or before December 1,
10 2026, the State Department of Education shall submit to the Governor and, in accordance
11 with § 2–1257 of the State Government Article, the General Assembly a report with an
12 estimate of the total cost of implementing a $25.00 per hour minimum wage for education
13 support professionals, with cost estimates disaggregated by local school system.
14 SECTION 3. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect July
15 1, 2026.
