1 AN ACT concerning
2 Baltimore City – Alcoholic Beverages – Related Event Promoter’s Permit
3 FOR the purpose of reestablishing a related event promoter’s permit in Baltimore City;
4 authorizing the Board of License Commissioners for Baltimore City to issue a related
5 event promoter’s permit to certain applicants under certain circumstances; requiring
6 a holder of a State caterer’s license to obtain a certain Class C per diem license from
7 the Board before the holder may act as a participating license holder at a related
8 event; and generally relating to related event promoter’s permits in Baltimore City.

4 12–1102.2.
5 (A) (1) IN THIS SECTION THE FOLLOWING WORDS HAVE THE MEANINGS
6 INDICATED.
7 (2) “CIAA BASKETBALL TOURNAMENT” MEANS THE ANNUAL
8 BASKETBALL TOURNAMENT OF THE CENTRAL INTERCOLLEGIATE ATHLETIC
9 ASSOCIATION.
10 (3) (I) “RELATED EVENT” MEANS AN EVENT IN WHICH:
11 1. A LICENSE HOLDER PARTICIPATES IN A
12 COORDINATED PROMOTION WITH A THIRD–PARTY PROMOTER TO SELL OR PROVIDE
13 ALCOHOLIC BEVERAGES DURING A SPECIFIED TIME; AND
14 2. AT LEAST 75 INDIVIDUALS ARE REASONABLY
15 ANTICIPATED TO PARTICIPATE.
16 (II) “RELATED EVENT” INCLUDES AN EVENT FOR WHICH
17 TICKETS ARE SOLD TO THE PUBLIC, EITHER IN ADVANCE OR AT THE DOOR, OR A
18 COVER CHARGE IS IMPOSED, INCLUDING A CONCERT, AN ENTERTAINMENT EVENT, A
19 HAPPY HOUR, OR A PARTY.
20 (4) “RELATED EVENT PROMOTER” MEANS AN INDIVIDUAL, A
21 FOR–PROFIT ORGANIZATION, OR A NONPROFIT ORGANIZATION THAT PROMOTES A
22 SOCIAL EVENT RELATED TO THE CIAA BASKETBALL TOURNAMENT AROUND THE
23 SAME TIME AND LOCATION AS THE CIAA BASKETBALL TOURNAMENT.
24 (B) THERE IS A RELATED EVENT PROMOTER’S PERMIT.
25 (C) (1) A RELATED EVENT PROMOTER OR PARTICIPATING LICENSE
26 HOLDER ON BEHALF OF A RELATED EVENT PROMOTER SHALL APPLY FOR A PERMIT
27 FROM THE BOARD BEFORE THE RELATED EVENT PROMOTER MAY PUBLICIZE, SELL
28 TICKETS FOR, ORGANIZE, OPERATE, PRODUCE, OR STAGE A RELATED EVENT.
29 (2) A HOLDER OF A STATE CATERER’S LICENSE SHALL OBTAIN A
30 CLASS C PER DIEM BEER, BEER AND WINE, OR BEER, WINE, AND LIQUOR LICENSE

1 FROM THE BOARD BEFORE THE HOLDER MAY ACT AS A PARTICIPATING LICENSE
2 HOLDER AT A RELATED EVENT.
3 (D) (1) EXCEPT AS PROVIDED IN PARAGRAPH (2) OF THIS SUBSECTION,
4 THE BOARD MAY GRANT THE PERMIT TO AN APPLICANT WHO SUBMITS AN
5 APPLICATION TO THE BOARD AS PROVIDED UNDER TITLE 4 OF THIS ARTICLE AT
6 LEAST 30 DAYS BEFORE THE DATE OF THE RELATED EVENT.
7 (2) BEFORE BEING GRANTED THE PERMIT, AN APPLICANT SHALL:
8 (I) OBTAIN WRITTEN CONSENT FROM A DESIGNEE OF VISIT
9 BALTIMORE;
10 (II) IF REQUIRED BASED ON THE TYPE OF PREMISES TO BE
11 USED:
12 1. OBTAIN A SPECIAL EVENT PERMIT FROM THE
13 BALTIMORE CITY DEPARTMENT OF TRANSPORTATION; AND
14 2. PROVIDE A COPY OF THE SPECIAL EVENT PERMIT TO
15 THE BOARD; AND
16 (III) PROVIDE A COMPLETED APPLICATION THAT:
17 1. IS DATED AND NOTARIZED, AND SIGNED BY EACH
18 LICENSE HOLDER THAT WILL PARTICIPATE IN THE RELATED EVENT;
19 2. LISTS EACH PREMISES FOR WHICH THE RELATED
20 EVENT WILL BE HELD; AND
21 3. IS ACCOMPANIED BY ANY OTHER DOCUMENT THAT
22 THE BOARD REQUIRES.
23 (3) AN INDIVIDUAL WHO APPLIES FOR AND IS ISSUED THE PERMIT IS
24 NOT REQUIRED TO BE A RESIDENT OF OR REGISTERED TO VOTE IN BALTIMORE
25 CITY.
26 (4) WITHIN 7 DAYS AFTER RECEIVING AN APPLICATION, THE BOARD
27 SHALL GRANT OR DENY THE PERMIT OR REQUEST MORE INFORMATION FROM THE
28 APPLICANT.
29 (5) A PERMIT MAY NOT BE ALTERED WITHIN 14 DAYS BEFORE THE
30 RELATED EVENT IS SCHEDULED TO TAKE PLACE.

24 (E) (1) FOR THE OFFENSE OF PUBLICIZING, SELLING TICKETS OR
25 IMPOSING A COVER CHARGE FOR, ORGANIZING, OPERATING, PRODUCING,
