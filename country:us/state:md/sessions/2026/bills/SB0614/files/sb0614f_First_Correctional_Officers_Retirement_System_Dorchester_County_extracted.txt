Assigned to: Budget and Taxation
A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Correctional Officers’ Retirement System – Dorchester County
3 FOR the purpose of requiring membership in the Correctional Officers’ Retirement System
4 for certain local detention center officers of Dorchester County in the event
5 Dorchester County becomes a participating governmental unit in the Correctional
6 Officers’ Retirement System; providing that certain local detention center officers
7 who join the Correctional Officers’ Retirement System and receive certain service
8 credit shall no longer be members of the Employees’ Pension System; and generally
9 relating to Dorchester County’s participation in the Correctional Officers’
10 Retirement System.
11 BY adding to
12 Article – State Personnel and Pensions
13 Section 31–2B–09
14 Annotated Code of Maryland
15 (2024 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
16 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
17 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
18 Article – State Personnel and Pensions
19 31–2B–09.
20 (A) THIS SECTION APPLIES TO AN INDIVIDUAL WHO IS A LOCAL DETENTION
21 CENTER OFFICER OF DORCHESTER COUNTY BEFORE THE EFFECTIVE DATE AND
22 WHO REMAINS A LOCAL DETENTION CENTER OFFICER OF DORCHESTER COUNTY
23 THROUGH THE EFFECTIVE DATE.
24 (B) NOTWITHSTANDING ANY OTHER PROVISION OF LAW, IF DORCHESTER
EXPLANATION: CAPITALS INDICATE MATTER ADDED TO EXISTING LAW.

2 SENATE BILL 614
1 COUNTY BECOMES A PARTICIPATING GOVERNMENTAL UNIT IN THE CORRECTIONAL
2 OFFICERS’ RETIREMENT SYSTEM, MEMBERSHIP IN THE CORRECTIONAL OFFICERS’
3 RETIREMENT SYSTEM IS MANDATORY FOR AN INDIVIDUAL DESCRIBED UNDER
4 SUBSECTION (A) OF THIS SECTION.
5 (C) IF DORCHESTER COUNTY COMMENCES PARTICIPATION IN THE
6 CORRECTIONAL OFFICERS’ RETIREMENT SYSTEM IN ACCORDANCE WITH
7 SUBSECTION (B) OF THIS SECTION, AN INDIVIDUAL DESCRIBED UNDER SUBSECTION
8 (A) OF THIS SECTION IS ENTITLED TO ELIGIBILITY SERVICE AND CREDITABLE
9 SERVICE IN THE CORRECTIONAL OFFICERS’ RETIREMENT SYSTEM FOR
10 EMPLOYMENT WITH DORCHESTER COUNTY BEFORE THE EFFECTIVE DATE.
11 (D) ON JOINING THE CORRECTIONAL OFFICERS’ RETIREMENT SYSTEM
12 AND RECEIVING SERVICE CREDIT AS PROVIDED UNDER SUBSECTION (C) OF THIS
13 SECTION, AN INDIVIDUAL DESCRIBED UNDER SUBSECTION (A) OF THIS SECTION
14 SHALL:
15 (1) NO LONGER BE A MEMBER OF THE EMPLOYEES’ PENSION
16 SYSTEM; AND
17 (2) HAVE NO FURTHER RIGHTS TO ANY BENEFIT IN THE EMPLOYEES’
18 PENSION SYSTEM.
19 (E) THE TRANSFER OF CREDITABLE SERVICE FOR AN INDIVIDUAL
20 DESCRIBED IN SUBSECTION (A) OF THIS SECTION WHO IS A MEMBER OF THE
21 EMPLOYEES’ PENSION SYSTEM THROUGH THE EFFECTIVE DATE, AND WHO
22 TRANSFERS TO THE CORRECTIONAL OFFICERS’ RETIREMENT SYSTEM UNDER THIS
23 SECTION, IS NOT GOVERNED BY TITLE 37 OF THIS ARTICLE.
24 (F) IF DORCHESTER COUNTY BECOMES A PARTICIPATING GOVERNMENTAL
25 UNIT IN THE CORRECTIONAL OFFICERS’ RETIREMENT SYSTEM, § 31–2B–05(C) OF
26 THIS SUBTITLE SHALL GOVERN THE TRANSFER AND CREDITING OF ASSETS ON THE
27 EFFECTIVE DATE.
28 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect June
29 1, 2026.
