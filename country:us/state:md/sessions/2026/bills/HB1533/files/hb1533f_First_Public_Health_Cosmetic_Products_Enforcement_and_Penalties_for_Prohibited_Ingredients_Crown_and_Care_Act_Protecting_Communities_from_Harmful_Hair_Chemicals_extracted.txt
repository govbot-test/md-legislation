A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Public Health – Cosmetic Products – Enforcement and Penalties for Prohibited
3 Ingredients
4 (Crown and Care Act – Protecting Communities from Harmful Hair Chemicals)
5 FOR the purpose of providing that a person who violates the prohibition on the
6 manufacture, sale, delivery, holding, or offering for sale a cosmetic product that
7 contains certain ingredients is liable for certain civil damages; authorizing the
8 Maryland Department of Health to investigate a complaint alleging that a person
9 included certain prohibited ingredients in cosmetic products; and generally relating
10 to prohibited ingredients in cosmetic products.

2 (D) (1) IN ADDITION TO ANY OTHER PENALTIES UNDER THIS TITLE, A
3 PERSON WHO VIOLATES THIS SECTION IS LIABLE FOR CIVIL DAMAGES SUSTAINED
4 BY THE INDIVIDUAL RESULTING FROM THE VIOLATION.
5 (2) IN ANY ACTION BROUGHT TO ENFORCE THIS SECTION, A COURT
6 MAY AWARD REASONABLE ATTORNEY’S FEES TO A PREVAILING PLAINTIFF.
7 (E) (1) THE DEPARTMENT MAY INVESTIGATE ANY COMPLAINT ALLEGING

9 (2) ON RECEIPT OF A COMPLAINT, A REPRESENTATIVE OF THE

11 THE PERSON LOCATED IN THE STATE TO DETERMINE COMPLIANCE WITH THIS
12 SECTION.
13 (3) THE PERSON MAY NOT:
14 (I) REFUSE TO GRANT ACCESS TO A REPRESENTATIVE WHO

16 PARAGRAPH (2) OF THIS SUBSECTION; OR
17 (II) INTERFERE WITH AN INSPECTION UNDER PARAGRAPH (2)
18 OF THIS SUBSECTION.
19 (4) AN INVESTIGATION OF A PREMISES OF A PERSON CONDUCTED
