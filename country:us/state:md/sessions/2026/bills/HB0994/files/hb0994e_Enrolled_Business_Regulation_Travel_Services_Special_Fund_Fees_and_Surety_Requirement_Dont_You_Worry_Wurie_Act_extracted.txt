1 AN ACT concerning
2 Business Regulation – Travel Services – Special Fund, Fees, and Surety
3 Requirement
4 (Don’t You Worry (Wurie) Act)
5 FOR the purpose of establishing the Sellers of Travel Services Registration Fund as a
6 special, nonlapsing fund in the Maryland Department of Labor; requiring that
7 certain investment earnings be credited to the General Fund of the State; requiring
8 the Secretary of Labor to annually calculate certain costs; authorizing the
9 Department to set by regulation certain fees based on certain calculations;
10 prohibiting certain fees from increasing by more than a certain amount each year;
11 requiring certain providers of travel services to file with the Department proof of
12 professional liability and errors and omissions liability in at least a certain amount;
13 and generally relating to the financial security of providers of travel services.
