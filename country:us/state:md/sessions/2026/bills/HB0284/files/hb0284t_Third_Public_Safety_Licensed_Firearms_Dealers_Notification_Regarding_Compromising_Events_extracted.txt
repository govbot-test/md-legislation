2 Public Safety – Licensed Firearms Dealers – Notification Regarding Potential
3 Compromise of Security Features Compromising Events
4 FOR the purpose of requiring a certain licensed firearms dealer to notify the Secretary of
5 State Police within a certain period of time that certain crimes have occurred or
6 certain security features may have been compromised; and generally relating to
7 licensed firearms dealers.
