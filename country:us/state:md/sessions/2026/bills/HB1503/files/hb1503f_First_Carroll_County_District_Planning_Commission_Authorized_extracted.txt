A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Carroll County – District Planning Commission – Authorized
3 FOR the purpose of authorizing the County Commissioners of Carroll County to enact a
4 local law to divide the county into certain districts, establish a process for the
5 appointment of certain district planning commissions, provide for the membership
6 and terms of a certain district planning commission, and provide for the abolishment
7 of a certain district planning commission; providing that a certain district planning
8 commission has the same powers and duties within the district as a county planning
9 commission; requiring a district planning commission to develop certain plans and
10 regulations and hold public hearings; requiring the district planning commission to
11 perform certain duties; require certain zoning regulations to be administered in the
12 same manner as other land use regulations in the county unless otherwise stated in
13 the regulation; and generally relating to district planning commissions in Carroll
14 County.

4 (A) IN ADDITION TO A PLANNING COMMISSION APPOINTED IN ACCORDANCE
5 WITH TITLE 2 OF THIS ARTICLE, THE COUNTY COMMISSIONERS OF CARROLL
6 COUNTY MAY ENACT A LOCAL LAW TO:
7 (1) DIVIDE THE COUNTY INTO DISTRICTS OF ANY NUMBER, SHAPE, OR
8 AREA IT MAY DETERMINE; AND
9 (2) ESTABLISH A PROCESS FOR THE APPOINTMENT OF ONE OR MORE
10 DISTRICT PLANNING COMMISSIONS TO PREPARE AND IMPLEMENT PLANS FOR A
11 DISTRICT;
12 (3) PROVIDE FOR THE MEMBERSHIP AND TERM OF A DISTRICT
13 PLANNING COMMISSION; AND
14 (4) PROVIDE FOR THE ABOLISHMENT OF A DISTRICT PLANNING
15 COMMISSION AT ANY TIME AND FOR ANY REASON.
16 (B) (1) THE DISTRICT PLANNING COMMISSION SHALL HAVE THE SAME
17 POWERS AND DUTIES WITH REGARD TO THE DISTRICT AS A COUNTY PLANNING
18 COMMISSION ESTABLISHED UNDER TITLE 2 OF THIS ARTICLE.
19 (2) THE DISTRICT PLANNING COMMISSION SHALL:
20 (I) DEVELOP PLANS AND REGULATIONS FOR THE ZONING,
21 SUBDIVISION, AND PROVISION OF FACILITIES AND SERVICES TO THE DISTRICT FOR
22 THE ORDERLY GROWTH OF THE DISTRICT;
23 (II) HOLD PUBLIC HEARINGS; AND
24 (III) SUBMIT THE PROPOSED PLANS AND REGULATIONS,
25 INCLUDING THE FULL TEXT OF ANY ZONING REGULATIONS AND MAPS, TO THE
26 COUNTY COMMISSIONERS OF CARROLL COUNTY FOR APPROVAL.
27 (C) ZONING REGULATIONS ADOPTED FOR A DISTRICT SHALL BE
28 ADMINISTERED WITHIN THE DISTRICT IN THE SAME MANNER AS ALL OTHER LAND
29 USE REGULATIONS OF THE COUNTY OR AS OTHERWISE PROVIDED IN THE DISTRICT
30 ZONE REGULATIONS.
