1 AN ACT concerning
2 Motor Vehicles – Intelligent Speed Assistance System Pilot Program –
3 Establishment
4 FOR the purpose of establishing the Intelligent Speed Assistance System Pilot Program;
5 requiring authorizing certain individuals whose driver’s licenses are subject to
6 certain suspension or revocation to participate in the Program; requiring the Motor
7 Vehicle Administration to issue to participants a restrictive license requiring the use
8 of an intelligent speed assistance system; prohibiting a participant from operating a
9 motor vehicle in violation of the requirements of the Program; requiring the District
10 Court to ensure that certain entries are included in a certain schedule of fines; and
11 generally relating to the Intelligent Speed Assistance System Pilot Program.
