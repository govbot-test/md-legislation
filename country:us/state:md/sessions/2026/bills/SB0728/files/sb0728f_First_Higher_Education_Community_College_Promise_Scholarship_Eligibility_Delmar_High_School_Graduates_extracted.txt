Assigned to: Education, Energy, and the Environment
A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Higher Education – Community College Promise Scholarship Eligibility –
3 Delmar High School Graduates
4 FOR the purpose of altering the eligibility for the Community College Promise Scholarship
5 to include residents of Delmar, Maryland who graduated from Delmar High School
6 in Delmar, Delaware; and generally relating to the Community College Promise
7 Scholarship.
8 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
9 Article – Education
10 Section 18–3603(b)(2)
11 Annotated Code of Maryland
12 (2022 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
13 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
14 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
15 Article – Education
16 18–3603.
17 (b) An applicant is eligible for a Maryland Community College Promise
18 Scholarship award if the applicant:
19 (2) (I) ENROLLS AT:
20 1. THE COMMUNITY COLLEGE LOCATED IN THE COUNTY
21 WHERE THE APPLICANT LIVES;
EXPLANATION: CAPITALS INDICATE MATTER ADDED TO EXISTING LAW.

2 SENATE BILL 728
1 2. THE REGIONAL COMMUNITY COLLEGE LOCATED IN
2 THE REGION WHERE THE APPLICANT LIVES; OR
3 3. A COMMUNITY COLLEGE IN THE STATE THAT HAS AN
4 ON–CAMPUS RESIDENTIAL FACILITY FOR STUDENTS; AND
5 (II) 1. Enrolls as a candidate for a vocational certificate, a
6 certificate, or an associate’s degree or participates in a registered apprenticeship after
7 graduating from a high school or successfully completing a GED in the State[:
8 (i) Except as provided in item (ii) of this item, at the community
9 college located in the county or, in the case of a regional community college, in the region,
10 where the applicant lives; or
11 (ii) At a community college in the State that has an on–campus
12 residential facility for students]; OR
13 2. A. ENROLLS AS A CANDIDATE FOR A VOCATIONAL
14 CERTIFICATE, A CERTIFICATE, OR AN ASSOCIATE’S DEGREE OR PARTICIPATES IN A
15 REGISTERED APPRENTICESHIP AFTER GRADUATING FROM DELMAR HIGH SCHOOL
16 IN DELMAR, DELAWARE; AND
17 B. IS A RESIDENT OF DELMAR, MARYLAND;
18 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect July
19 1, 2026.
