1 AN ACT concerning
2 Maryland Department of Health – Adolescent Psychiatric Inpatient Beds –
3 Reports on Capacity in Prince George’s County and Report and Outcomes
4 FOR the purpose of requiring the Maryland Department of Health to ensure the
5 establishment and ongoing operation of a minimum number of licensed adolescent
6 psychiatric inpatient beds in Prince George’s County; requiring, under certain
7 circumstances, the Department to issue a request for proposals to establish
8 adolescent psychiatric inpatient beds in the county and to administer a certain grant
9 program; requiring the Maryland Department of Health to report to the Governor
10 and the General Assembly on certain information related to adolescent psychiatric
11 inpatient bed capacity in the State; requiring the Department to report to the
12 Governor and the General Assembly on certain information related to outcomes for
13 adolescents receiving care in adolescent psychiatric inpatient beds; and generally
14 relating to adolescent psychiatric inpatient bed capacity and outcomes.

18 (2) “ADOLESCENT” MEANS AN INDIVIDUAL AT LEAST 12 YEARS OLD

20 (3) “ADOLESCENT PSYCHIATRIC INPATIENT BED” MEANS A UNIT OF
21 LICENSED INPATIENT PSYCHIATRIC CAPACITY THAT PROVIDES OVERNIGHT,
22 24–HOUR, FACILITY–BASED PSYCHIATRIC EVALUATION, STABILIZATION, AND
23 TREATMENT FOR ADOLESCENTS.
24 (B) (1) THE DEPARTMENT, IN CONSULTATION WITH THE PRINCE
25 GEORGE’S COUNTY LOCAL BEHAVIORAL HEALTH AUTHORITY, SHALL ENSURE THE

27 PSYCHIATRIC INPATIENT BEDS IN PRINCE GEORGE’S COUNTY BY:
28 (I) EXPANDING THE LICENSED INPATIENT BED CAPACITY AT
29 AN EXISTING INPATIENT FACILITY IN PRINCE GEORGE’S COUNTY;
30 (II) CONTRACTING WITH A PRIVATE OR NONPROFIT OPERATOR
31 THAT WILL ESTABLISH AND OPERATE A LICENSED ADOLESCENT PSYCHIATRIC
32 INPATIENT FACILITY; OR
