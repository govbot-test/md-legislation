WES MOORE, Governor Ch. 293
Chapter 293
AN ACT concerning
State Retirement and Pension System – Transfers Between Systems –
Workgroup – Extension
FOR the purpose of extending the reporting date and the termination date for a workgroup
convened by the State Retirement Agency to study transfers of member service
between State and local retirement and pension systems; altering the entities to
which the State Retirement Agency is required to report; and generally relating to
transfers of member service between State and local retirement and pension
systems.
BY repealing and reenacting, without amendments,
Chapter 768 of the Acts of the General Assembly of 2025
