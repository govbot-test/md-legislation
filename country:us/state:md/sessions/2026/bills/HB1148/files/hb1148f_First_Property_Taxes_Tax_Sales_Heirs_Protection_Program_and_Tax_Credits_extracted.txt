A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Property Taxes – Tax Sales, Heirs Protection Program, and Tax Credits
3 FOR the purpose of altering eligibility for certain services and programs offered by the
4 State Tax Sale Ombudsman to include certain persons acting on behalf of a deceased
5 homeowner or an heir or a legatee of a deceased homeowner; establishing an Heirs
6 Protection Program administered by the Ombudsman for the purpose of allowing
7 heirs who inherit a dwelling to become the record title holder of the dwelling,
8 preventing tax sales of dwellings inherited by heirs, and allowing heirs to remain in
9 their homes; requiring the Ombudsman to conduct certain outreach, disseminate
10 certain information, and provide certain grants to carry out the Heirs Protection
11 Program; establishing the Heirs Protection Fund financed by the State and county
12 governments to provide funding for the Heirs Protection Program; requiring interest
13 earnings to be credited to the Fund; altering eligibility for the homeowners’ and
14 homestead tax credits to include certain heirs who are not shown as the record title
15 holder of a dwelling in the land records of the county for a certain period of time if
16 certain requirements are met; and generally relating to protecting heirs from
17 property tax sales, the Heirs Protection Program, and the State property tax credit
18 programs.
