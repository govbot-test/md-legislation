CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Arbitration Reform for State Employees Act of 2026
3 FOR the purpose of altering the collective bargaining process for certain State employees,
4 including by requiring the selection of a neutral arbitrator to oversee all certain
5 aspects of collective bargaining, establishing a process of arbitration in the event of
6 impasse in negotiations between certain parties, and providing that certain decisions
7 of a neutral arbitrator are advisory; altering the matters that are required to be
8 included in collective bargaining; requiring that each budget bill submitted by the
9 Governor contain the appropriations necessary to implement all terms and
10 conditions of employment in certain memoranda of understanding for the next
11 ensuing fiscal year; and generally relating to collective bargaining for State
12 employees.
