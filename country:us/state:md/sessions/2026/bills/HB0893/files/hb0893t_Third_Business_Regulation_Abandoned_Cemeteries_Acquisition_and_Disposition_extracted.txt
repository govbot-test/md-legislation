CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Business Regulation – Abandoned Cemeteries – Acquisition and Disposition
3 FOR the purpose of authorizing a governmental unit to acquire property on which an
4 abandoned cemetery is located under certain circumstances and for a certain
5 purpose; requiring a governmental unit that acquires property under this Act to
6 make a certain effort to contact certain persons for a certain purpose; authorizing a
7 person or organization to bring an action to request the court to transfer property
8 acquired under this Act to the person or organization; and generally relating to
9 abandoned cemeteries.

5 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
6 Article – Business Regulation
7 5–805.
8 (a) (1) In this section the following words have the meanings indicated.
9 (2) “Abandoned cemetery” means a cemetery for which:
10 (i) 1. the organization established to engage in the operation of
11 the cemetery has been terminated, has been subject to administrative dissolution by the
12 State, or has otherwise ceased to exist; and
13 2. title has not been conveyed;
14 (ii) there is no property owner listed in the records of the State
15 Department of Assessments and Taxation;
16 (iii) the property has been condemned; or
17 (iv) no person who is legally responsible for the property can be found
18 and legal ownership of the property cannot be determined.
19 5–806.
20 (A) (1) IN THIS SECTION THE FOLLOWING WORDS HAVE THE MEANINGS
21 INDICATED.
22 (2) “ABANDONED CEMETERY” HAS THE MEANING STATED IN § 5–805
23 OF THIS SUBTITLE.
24 (3) “CULTURAL AFFILIATION” HAS THE MEANING STATED IN § 14–121
25 OF THE REAL PROPERTY ARTICLE.
26 (4) “DESCENDANT COMMUNITY ORGANIZATION” MEANS AN
27 ORGANIZATION WHOSE MEMBERS HAVE ANCESTORS WHO ARE INTERRED IN A
28 PARTICULAR CEMETERY OR WHO HAVE A CULTURAL AFFILIATION OR A RACIAL OR
29 ETHNIC AFFINITY WITH THE DECEASED PERSONS INTERRED IN A PARTICULAR
30 CEMETERY.

1 (B) (1) A GOVERNMENTAL UNIT MAY, BY CONDEMNATION, ACQUIRE
2 PROPERTY ON WHICH AN ABANDONED CEMETERY IS LOCATED WITH THE INTENT TO
3 TRANSFER THE PROPERTY TO:
4 (I) THE DESCENDANTS OF THE DECEASED PERSONS INTERRED
5 IN THE CEMETERY;
6 (II) A DESCENDANT COMMUNITY ORGANIZATION; OR
7 (III) A NONPROFIT ORGANIZATION; OR
8 (IV) A VETERANS’ ORGANIZATION.
9 (2) A GOVERNMENTAL UNIT USING CONDEMNATION PROCEEDINGS
10 TO ACQUIRE PROPERTY UNDER PARAGRAPH (1) OF THIS SUBSECTION SHALL
11 CONDUCT THE CONDEMNATION IN ACCORDANCE WITH MARYLAND RULE 12–204.
12 (C) (1) A GOVERNMENTAL UNIT THAT ACQUIRES PROPERTY ON WHICH
13 AN ABANDONED CEMETERY IS LOCATED UNDER SUBSECTION (B) OF THIS SECTION
14 SHALL MAKE TIMELY AND REASONABLE EFFORTS AFTER ACQUISITION TO CONTACT
15 DESCENDANTS OF THE DECEASED PERSONS INTERRED IN THE ABANDONED
16 CEMETERY IN ORDER TO IDENTIFY DESCENDANTS, A DESCENDANT COMMUNITY
17 ORGANIZATION, OR A NONPROFIT ORGANIZATION TO WHICH THE PROPERTY MAY BE
18 TRANSFERRED.
19 (2) (I) A PERSON OR ORGANIZATION LISTED UNDER SUBSECTION
20 (B)(1) OF THIS SECTION MAY BRING AN ACTION REQUESTING THE COURT TO
21 TRANSFER PROPERTY ACQUIRED BY A GOVERNMENTAL UNIT UNDER THIS SECTION
22 TO THE PERSON OR ORGANIZATION.
23 (II) WITHIN 6 MONTHS AFTER AN ACTION IS BROUGHT UNDER
24 SUBPARAGRAPH (I) OF THIS PARAGRAPH, THE COURT SHALL MAKE A
25 DETERMINATION ON THE DISPOSITION OF THE PROPERTY.
26 (D) ANY DESCENDANT, DESCENDANT COMMUNITY ORGANIZATION, OR
27 NONPROFIT ORGANIZATION THAT ACQUIRES PROPERTY UNDER SUBSECTION (B) OF
28 THIS SECTION SHALL MAINTAIN AND PRESERVE THE ABANDONED CEMETERY ON
29 THE PROPERTY.
30 (E) IF A GOVERNMENTAL UNIT THAT ACQUIRES PROPERTY UNDER
31 SUBSECTION (B) OF THIS SECTION, THE GOVERNMENTAL UNIT:
