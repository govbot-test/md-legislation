A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Commercial Driver’s Instructional Permits – School Vehicles – Knowledge Test
3 FOR the purpose of authorizing certain school vehicle driver trainers to administer the
4 commercial driver’s instructional permit knowledge test to applicants for a
5 commercial driver’s license – school bus endorsement; and generally relating to the
6 knowledge test for commercial driver’s instructional permits for school vehicles.
7 BY adding to
8 Article – Transportation
9 Section 16–816.1
10 Annotated Code of Maryland
11 (2020 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)

13 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
14 Article – Transportation
15 16–816.1.
16 (A) IN THIS SECTION, “SCHOOL VEHICLE DRIVER TRAINER” MEANS AN
17 INDIVIDUAL WHO PROVIDES FOR COMMERCIAL DRIVER’S LICENSE – SCHOOL BUS
18 ENDORSEMENT APPLICANTS:
19 (1) CLASSROOM INSTRUCTION FOR THE COMMERCIAL DRIVER’S
20 INSTRUCTIONAL PERMIT KNOWLEDGE TEST;
21 (2) HIGHWAY DRIVING INSTRUCTION; AND
22 (3) INSTRUCTION ON STATE AND FEDERAL SAFETY STANDARDS.
