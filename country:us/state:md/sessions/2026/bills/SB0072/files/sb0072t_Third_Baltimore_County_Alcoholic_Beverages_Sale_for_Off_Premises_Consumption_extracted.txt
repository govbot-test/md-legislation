CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Baltimore County – Alcoholic Beverages – Sale for Off–Premises Consumption
3 FOR the purpose of providing that certain authorization for the holders of certain licenses
4 to sell alcoholic beverages for off–premises consumption in a certain manner applies
5 only if the Board of License Commissioners for Baltimore County has adopted certain
6 regulations; authorizing the holders of certain licenses in Baltimore County that
7 authorize the sale of alcoholic beverages at a restaurant or bar to sell certain
8 alcoholic beverages for off–premises consumption under certain circumstances;
9 prohibiting the Board from charging a certain license holder an additional fee under
10 certain circumstances; authorizing a local licensing board to limit the quantity of
11 alcoholic beverages sold to an individual in a single transaction; and generally
12 relating to the sale of alcoholic beverages at restaurants and bars in Baltimore
13 County.

8 (A) (1) THIS SECTION APPLIES ONLY IF THE BOARD HAS ADOPTED
9 REGULATIONS TO AUTHORIZE THE SALE OF ALCOHOLIC BEVERAGES FOR
10 OFF–PREMISES CONSUMPTION IN ACCORDANCE WITH THIS SECTION.
11 (2) IN CONSIDERING WHETHER TO ADOPT REGULATIONS UNDER THIS
12 SECTION, THE BOARD SHALL CONSIDER THE PUBLIC HEALTH IMPACTS OF THE SALE
13 OF ALCOHOLIC BEVERAGES FOR OFF–PREMISES CONSUMPTION.
14 (B) (1) THIS SUBSECTION APPLIES ONLY TO A CLASS B OR CLASS D
15 LICENSE THAT AUTHORIZES THE SALE OF ALCOHOLIC BEVERAGES FOR
16 ON–PREMISES AND OFF–PREMISES CONSUMPTION AT A RESTAURANT OR BAR.
17 (2) A LICENSE HOLDER UNDER THIS SUBSECTION MAY OBTAIN A
18 PERMIT FROM THE BOARD THAT AUTHORIZES THE HOLDER TO SELL, IF
19 AUTHORIZED UNDER THE HOLDER’S LICENSE, MIXED DRINKS OR COCKTAILS IN
20 SEALED OR CLOSED CONTAINERS FOR OFF–PREMISES CONSUMPTION IF:
21 (I) THE MIXED DRINK OR COCKTAIL IS PURCHASED ALONG
22 WITH PREPARED FOOD OTHER THAN PREPACKAGED SNACKS;
23 (II) THE INDIVIDUAL PURCHASING THE MIXED DRINK OR
24 COCKTAIL:
25 1. IS AT LEAST 21 YEARS OLD; AND
26 2. PROVIDES VALID IDENTIFICATION AS PROOF OF AGE;
27 AND
28 (III) EACH MIXED DRINK OR COCKTAIL SOLD FOR OFF–PREMISES
29 CONSUMPTION IS:
