CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Property Tax – Agricultural Use Assessment – Community Solar Energy
3 Generating Systems
4 FOR the purpose of altering the date by which certain community solar energy generating
5 systems must be approved by the Public Service Commission in order for land that
6 is used by the community solar energy generating systems to qualify for the
7 agricultural use assessment; requiring the State Department of Assessments and
8 Taxation to issue a certain preconstruction determination under certain
9 circumstances; authorizing the Department to rescind a preconstruction
10 determination under certain circumstances; authorizing the Department to charge a
11 certain application fee; and generally relating to the agricultural use assessment.
12 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
13 Article – Tax – Property
14 Section 8–209(e)
15 Annotated Code of Maryland
16 (2019 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
17 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
18 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
19 Article – Tax – Property
20 8–209.

2 HOUSE BILL 734
1 (e) (1) (i) In this subsection the following words have the meanings
2 indicated.
3 (ii) “Agrivoltaics” has the meaning stated in § 7–237 of this article.
4 (iii) “Community solar energy generating system” has the meaning
5 stated in § 7–306.2 of the Public Utilities Article.
6 (2) Except as provided in paragraph (4) of this subsection, the Department
7 shall establish in regulations criteria to determine if land that appears to be actively used
8 for farm or agricultural purposes:
9 (i) is actually used for farm or agricultural purposes; and
10 (ii) qualifies for assessment under this section.
11 (3) The criteria shall include:
12 (i) the zoning of the land;
13 (ii) the present and past use of the land including land under the Soil
14 Bank Program of the United States;
15 (iii) the productivity of the land, including timberlands and
16 reforested lands; and
17 (iv) the gross income that is derived from the agricultural activity.
18 (4) (i) This paragraph applies through the life cycle of a community
19 solar energy generating system that:
20 1. is placed in service after June 30, 2022; and
21 2. has been approved on or before December 31, [2025] 2030,
22 as a community solar energy generating system by the Public Service Commission under §
23 7–306.2 of the Public Utilities Article.
24 (ii) 1. The Department shall assess and qualify land that is used
25 by a community solar energy generating system for agrivoltaics as land that is actively
26 used for farm or agricultural purposes.
27 2. A. ON APPLICATION, THE DEPARTMENT SHALL
28 ISSUE A PRECONSTRUCTION DETERMINATION IN ACCORDANCE WITH PARAGRAPH
29 (5) OF THIS SUBSECTION.
HOUSE BILL 734 3
1 B. A FAVORABLE PRECONSTRUCTION DETERMINATION
2 QUALIFIES THE LAND AS ACTIVELY USED FOR FARM OR AGRICULTURAL PURPOSES
3 UNDER SUBSUBPARAGRAPH 1 OF THIS SUBPARAGRAPH.
4 (5) (I) ON APPLICATION, THE DEPARTMENT SHALL ISSUE A
5 PRECONSTRUCTION DETERMINATION INDICATING WHETHER THE LAND SUBJECT TO
6 THE REQUEST WILL BE ASSESSED AS FARM OR AGRICULTURAL USE LAND UNDER
7 THIS SECTION.
8 (II) AN APPLICATION FOR A PRECONSTRUCTION
9 DETERMINATION UNDER SUBPARAGRAPH (I) OF THIS PARAGRAPH SHALL INCLUDE:
10 1. IDENTIFICATION OF THE PARCEL OR PARCELS;
11 2. THE ANTICIPATED EFFECTIVE TAX YEAR FOR WHEN
12 CONSTRUCTION WILL BE COMPLETE AND THE ANTICIPATED AGRICULTURAL
13 ACTIVITY;
14 3. A SITE PLAN SHOWING THE PROPOSED SOLAR LAYOUT
15 AND THE ACREAGE TO REMAIN IN AGRICULTURAL ACTIVITY;
16 4. AN AGRIVOLTAICS PLAN THAT INCLUDES:
17 A. THE ANTICIPATED AGRICULTURAL ACTIVITY TO BE
18 CONDUCTED DURING OPERATION OF THE COMMUNITY SOLAR ENERGY GENERATING
19 SYSTEM;
20 B. ACCESS AND FEATURES NECESSARY TO CONDUCT THE
21 ANTICIPATED AGRICULTURAL ACTIVITY; AND
22 C. HOW THE DESIGN OF THE COMMUNITY SOLAR ENERGY
23 GENERATING SYSTEM WILL ALLOW FOR THE ANTICIPATED AGRICULTURAL
24 ACTIVITY;
25 5. AN ESTIMATED CONSTRUCTION SCHEDULE AND THE
26 PROPOSED IN–SERVICE DATE;
27 6. EVIDENCE OF SITE CONTROL, INCLUDING:
28 A. AN EXECUTED LAND LEASE OR PURCHASE
29 AGREEMENT BETWEEN THE LAND OWNER AND SOLAR OPERATOR;
30 B. AN EXECUTED OPTION TO LEASE OR PURCHASE
31 BETWEEN THE LAND OWNER AND SOLAR OPERATOR; OR
4 HOUSE BILL 734
1 C. A DEED INDICATING THE SOLAR OPERATOR OWNS THE
2 LAND; AND
3 7. ANY OTHER INFORMATION REQUIRED BY THE
4 DEPARTMENT THAT IS REASONABLY NECESSARY TO DETERMINE WHETHER THE
5 LAND QUALIFIES FOR A FARM OR AGRICULTURAL USE ASSESSMENT UNDER
6 PARAGRAPH (4) OF THIS SUBSECTION.
7 (III) 1. WITHIN 90 DAYS AFTER RECEIVING A COMPLETED
8 APPLICATION FOR A PRECONSTRUCTION DETERMINATION, THE DEPARTMENT
9 SHALL REVIEW THE APPLICATION AND:
10 A. ISSUE A FAVORABLE PRECONSTRUCTION
11 DETERMINATION;
12 B. ISSUE AN UNFAVORABLE PRECONSTRUCTION
13 DETERMINATION; OR
14 C. NOTIFY THE APPLICANT THAT ADDITIONAL
15 INFORMATION IS REQUIRED TO COMPLETE REVIEW.
16 2. THE DEPARTMENT MAY RESCIND A
17 PRECONSTRUCTION DETERMINATION IF:
18 A. THE COMMUNITY SOLAR ENERGY GENERATING
19 SYSTEM, AS CONSTRUCTED OR OPERATED, MATERIALLY DIFFERS FROM THE
20 DESCRIPTION PROVIDED IN THE APPLICATION;
21 B. THE SOLAR OPERATOR HAS FAILED TO NOTIFY THE
22 DEPARTMENT OF THE COMPLETED CONSTRUCTION AND SUBMIT AN AGRICULTURAL
23 USE APPLICATION;
24 C. AGRICULTURAL ACTIVITY IS NOT BEING MAINTAINED
25 IN ACCORDANCE WITH THIS SECTION; OR
26 D. THE APPLICANT PROVIDED INACCURATE
27 INFORMATION IN THE APPLICATION.
28 (IV) A FAVORABLE PRECONSTRUCTION DETERMINATION
29 ISSUED BY THE DEPARTMENT QUALIFIES THE LAND FOR A FARM OR AGRICULTURAL
30 USE ASSESSMENT UNDER PARAGRAPH (4) OF THIS SUBSECTION FOR THE
31 IMMEDIATELY SUCCEEDING TAXABLE YEAR IF:
HOUSE BILL 734 5
1 1. THE SOLAR OPERATOR PROVIDES WRITTEN NOTICE
2 TO THE DEPARTMENT THAT THE PROJECT’S CONSTRUCTION IS COMPLETED AND
3 AGRICULTURAL USE ACTIVITY IS BEING CONDUCTED;
4 2. AN APPLICATION FOR A FARM OR AGRICULTURAL USE
5 ASSESSMENT IS SUBMITTED TO THE DEPARTMENT THAT INCLUDES THE REQUIRED
6 INFORMATION; AND
7 3. THE SOLAR OPERATOR PROVIDES ACCESS TO THE
8 SOLAR FACILITY FOR THE DEPARTMENT TO VERIFY THE AGRICULTURAL USE
9 ACTIVITY IS BEING CONDUCTED IN ACCORDANCE WITH THIS SECTION.
10 (V) THE DEPARTMENT MAY CHARGE AN APPLICATION FEE IN
11 AN AMOUNT NECESSARY TO RECOVER THE DEPARTMENT’S COSTS TO ADMINISTER
12 AND IMPLEMENT THE REQUIREMENTS OF THIS PARAGRAPH.
13 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect June
14 1, 2026, and shall be applicable to all taxable years beginning after June 30, 2026.
Approved:
