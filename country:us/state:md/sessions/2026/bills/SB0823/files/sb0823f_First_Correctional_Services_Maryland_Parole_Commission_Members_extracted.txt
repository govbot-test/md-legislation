A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Correctional Services – Maryland Parole Commission – Members
3 FOR the purpose of altering the appointing authority and method of appointment of parole
4 commissioners; and generally relating to the Maryland Parole Commission.
5 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
6 Article – Correctional Services
7 Section 7–202(a)
8 Annotated Code of Maryland
9 (2025 Replacement Volume)
10 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
11 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
12 Article – Correctional Services
13 7–202.
14 (a) (1) The Commission consists of ten members.
15 (2) (I) [With the approval of the] THE Governor [and], WITH the advice
16 and consent of the Senate, [the Secretary] shall appoint the members of the Commission
17 FROM AMONG NOMINEES CHOSEN BY A PANEL CONSISTING OF:
18 1. THE EXECUTIVE DIRECTOR OF THE MARYLAND
19 POLICE TRAINING AND STANDARDS COMMISSION;
20 2. THE SECRETARY OF PUBLIC SAFETY AND
21 CORRECTIONAL SERVICES;

6 TRAINING, OR COMMUNITY ORGANIZING, APPOINTED BY THE GOVERNOR;
7 6. THE STATE SUPERINTENDENT OF SCHOOLS;

9 SOCIAL WORKERS – MARYLAND CHAPTER, OR THE PRESIDENT’S DESIGNEE;

15 GOVERNOR.
16 (II) THE PANEL SHALL SUBMIT TO THE GOVERNOR THE NAMES
17 OF AT LEAST THREE QUALIFIED NOMINEES FOR EACH COMMISSIONER VACANCY TO
18 BE FILLED.
19 (III) THE GOVERNOR’S OFFICE OF CRIME PREVENTION AND
20 POLICY SHALL PROVIDE STAFFING FOR THE PANEL.
21 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
22 October 1, 2026.
