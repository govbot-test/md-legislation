CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Baltimore City – Alcoholic Beverages – License Extension
3 FOR the purpose of extending for a certain amount of time the expiration date of an
4 alcoholic beverages license issued for a certain area of Baltimore City for the purpose
5 purposes of completing a transfer of ownership and renewal and completing
6 renovations; and generally relating to alcoholic beverages licenses in Baltimore City.
7 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
8 That, notwithstanding § 12–2202 of the Alcoholic Beverages and Cannabis Article, a
9 racetrack license issued for a premises in the 5200 block of Park Heights Avenue shall be
10 considered unexpired until the end of July 1, 2029, 2029:
11 (1) notwithstanding § 12–1705 of the Alcoholic Beverages and Cannabis
12 Article, for the purposes of completing a transfer of ownership and renewal at the same
13 location; and
14 (2) notwithstanding § 12–2202 of the Alcoholic Beverages and Cannabis
15 Article, for the purpose of completing renovations at the same location.
16 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect July
17 1, 2026. It shall remain effective for a period of 3 years and 1 month and, at the end of July
18 31, 2029, this Act, with no further action required by the General Assembly, shall be
19 abrogated and of no further force and effect.
