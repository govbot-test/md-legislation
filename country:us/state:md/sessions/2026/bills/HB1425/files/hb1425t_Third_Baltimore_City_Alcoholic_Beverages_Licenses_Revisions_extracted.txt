CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Baltimore City – Alcoholic Beverages Licenses – Revisions
3 FOR the purpose of repealing the deadline by which a certain fee must be paid by the holder
4 of a Class A beer and light wine license or a Class A beer, wine, and liquor license to
5 exercise certain Sunday sales privileges; altering the deadline by which a license
6 holder must present to the Board certain proof that all of the license holder’s personal
7 property taxes due to Baltimore City and the State have been paid; and generally
8 relating to alcoholic beverages licenses in Baltimore City.
9 BY repealing and reenacting, without amendments,
10 Article – Alcoholic Beverages and Cannabis
11 Section 12–102
