CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Economic Development – Prince George’s County Suitland Development
3 Authority – Established
4 FOR the purpose of establishing the Suitland Development Authority in Prince George’s
5 County; requiring the Authority to support and develop a certain neighborhood
6 revitalization plan in coordination with certain residents; authorizing the Authority
7 to modify certain boundaries, subject to a certain vote; requiring the Authority to
8 take certain actions regarding the finances of the Authority; exempting the
9 Authority from certain taxation or assessments under certain circumstances; and
10 generally relating to the Suitland Development Authority.

12 Article – Economic Development
13 Section 12–1201 through 12–1214 to be under the new subtitle “Subtitle 12. Prince
14 George’s County Suitland Development Authority”

10 12–1204.
11 (A) THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY FINDS THAT:
12 (1) THERE EXISTS WITHIN VARIOUS NEIGHBORHOODS IN PRINCE
13 GEORGE’S COUNTY, PARTICULARLY THE SUITLAND ROAD AND SILVER HILL ROAD
14 INTERSECTION, A NEED FOR RESIDENTIAL AND COMMERCIAL DEVELOPMENT OR
15 REDEVELOPMENT IN FURTHERANCE OF THE PUBLIC’S INTEREST;
16 (2) THE SUITLAND ROAD AND SILVER HILL ROAD INTERSECTION
17 HAS EXPERIENCED LOW HOUSING AND ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT IN THE
18 COMMUNITY FOR DECADES;
19 (3) AREAS IN THE SUITLAND ROAD AND SILVER HILL ROAD
20 INTERSECTION INCLUDE BOTH AREAS THAT ARE CONSIDERED SLUM OR BLIGHTED
21 AND AREAS THAT ARE DETERIORATED OR SUBJECT TO FURTHER DETERIORATING
22 CONDITION, AND THOSE AREAS ARE IN NEED OF DEVELOPMENT OR
23 REDEVELOPMENT FOR THE PUBLIC BENEFIT;
24 (4) THE REVITALIZATION OF AREAS WITHIN THE SUITLAND ROAD
25 AND SILVER HILL ROAD INTERSECTION NEEDING COMMERCIAL DEVELOPMENT OR
26 REDEVELOPMENT IS AN ESSENTIAL GOVERNMENTAL FUNCTION AND IS A PUBLIC
27 USE THAT WILL CONFER A PUBLIC BENEFIT ON CITIZENS OF PRINCE GEORGE’S
28 COUNTY BY:
29 (I) RELIEVING CONDITIONS OF UNEMPLOYMENT;
30 (II) ENCOURAGING THE INCREASE OF COMMERCE AND A
31 BALANCED ECONOMY;
