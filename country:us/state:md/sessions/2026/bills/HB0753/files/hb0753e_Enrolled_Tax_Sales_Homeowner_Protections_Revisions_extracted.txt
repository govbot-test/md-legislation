CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Tax Sales – Homeowner Protections – Revisions
3 FOR the purpose of requiring the State Tax Sale Ombudsman to develop a process to allow
4 a homeowner to designate a family member or another representative of the
5 homeowner to communicate and work with the Ombudsman on the homeowner’s
6 behalf; requiring the dwelling of a homeowner to be withheld from tax sale if the
7 homeowner has a documented terminal illness or medical hardship; increasing the
8 maximum value of a dwelling that a homeowner may reside in and be eligible for the
9 Homeowner Protection Program; requiring homeowners with terminal illness or
10 medical hardship to receive priority for enrollment in the Homeowner Protection
11 Program; and generally relating to protections for homeowners in tax sale.
12 BY repealing and reenacting, without amendments,
