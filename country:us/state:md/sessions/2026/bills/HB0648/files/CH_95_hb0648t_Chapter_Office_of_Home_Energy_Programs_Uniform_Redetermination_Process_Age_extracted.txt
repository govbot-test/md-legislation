Section 1:
WES MOORE, Governor Ch. 95
Chapter 95
AN ACT concerning
Office of Home Energy Programs – Uniform Redetermination Process – Age
FOR the purpose of reducing the age at which certain eligible energy customers qualify for
a certain redetermination process for enrollment in energy assistance programs; and
generally relating to the Office of Home Energy Programs and energy assistance
programs.
BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
Article – Human Services

Section 2:
Section 5–5A–09(a)
Annotated Code of Maryland
(2019 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)

Section 3:
SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
Article – Human Services
5–5A–09.
(a) (1) The Office shall develop a uniform redetermination process to assist
eligible energy customers who are at least [65] 60 years old in enrolling in energy
assistance programs.
(2) The redetermination process developed under paragraph (1) of this
subsection shall be updated annually.

Section 4:
SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect July
1, 2026.
Approved by the Governor, April 14, 2026.
