CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Sale of Residential Real Property – Required Flood Risk Disclosure
3 FOR the purpose of requiring the Department of the Environment to develop, publish, and
4 maintain a real property flood risk disclosure statement form that includes certain
5 information about a property’s flood risk and history; requiring, beginning on a
6 certain date, a vendor of certain residential real property to provide the purchaser
7 with a completed real property flood risk disclosure statement form before entering
8 into a contract for the sale of the property; and generally relating to flood risk
9 disclosures and the sale of residential real property.

5 (A) (1) IN THIS SECTION THE FOLLOWING WORDS HAVE THE MEANINGS
6 INDICATED.
7 (2) “FEMA ELEVATION CERTIFICATE” MEANS THE CERTIFICATE
8 ISSUED BY THE FEDERAL EMERGENCY MANAGEMENT AGENCY THAT CERTIFIES
9 OFFICIAL ELEVATION AND BUILDING DATA FOR A PROPERTY LOCATED WITHIN A
10 SPECIAL FLOOD HAZARD AREA IN ORDER TO INFORM FLOOD INSURANCE PREMIUMS.
11 (3) “MODERATE–RISK FLOOD HAZARD AREA” MEANS AN AREA THAT
12 IS:
13 (I) IDENTIFIED BY THE FEDERAL EMERGENCY MANAGEMENT
14 AGENCY AS HAVING A 0.2% CHANCE OF ANNUAL FLOODING; AND
15 (II) COMMONLY KNOWN AS THE 500–YEAR FLOODPLAIN.
16 (4) “SPECIAL FLOOD HAZARD AREA” MEANS AN AREA THAT IS:
17 (I) IDENTIFIED BY THE FEDERAL EMERGENCY MANAGEMENT
18 AGENCY AS HAVING A 1% CHANCE OF ANNUAL FLOODING; AND
19 (II) COMMONLY KNOWN AS THE 100–YEAR FLOODPLAIN.
20 (B) (1) THE DEPARTMENT SHALL DEVELOP, PUBLISH, AND MAINTAIN A
21 REAL PROPERTY FLOOD RISK DISCLOSURE STATEMENT FORM FOR USE IN
22 CONNECTION WITH A SALE OF RESIDENTIAL REAL PROPERTY AS PROVIDED IN §
23 10–716 OF THE REAL PROPERTY ARTICLE.

25 MINIMUM, INCLUDE THE FOLLOWING INFORMATION:
26 (I) WHETHER THE PROPERTY IS LOCATED WHOLLY OR PARTLY
27 WITHIN A SPECIAL FLOOD HAZARD AREA;
28 (II) WHETHER THE PROPERTY IS LOCATED WHOLLY OR PARTLY
29 WITHIN A MODERATE–RISK FLOOD HAZARD AREA;
