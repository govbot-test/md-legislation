1 (B) “CORRECTIONAL OFFICER” HAS THE MEANING STATED IN § 8–201 OF
2 THE CORRECTIONAL SERVICES ARTICLE.
3 [(b)] (C) “Eligible institution” means:
4 (1) A public senior higher education institution in the State; or
5 (2) A community college in the State.
6 [(c)] (D) “Police officer” has the meaning stated in § 3–201 of the Public Safety
7 Article.
8 [(d)] (E) “Probation agent” means an individual serving as a parole and
9 probation agent, supervisor, or regional administrator for the Department of Public Safety
10 and Correctional Services.
11 [(e)] (F) “Service obligation” means to work as a police officer or probation agent
12 in the State not less than 5 years during the 8–year period after graduation.
13 18–3802.
14 (a) There is a Maryland Police Officers [and], Probation Agents, AND
15 CORRECTIONAL OFFICERS Scholarship Program.
16 (b) The purpose of the program is to provide tuition assistance for students who
17 are:
18 (1) Attending an eligible institution and enrolled in a degree OR
19 CERTIFICATE program that would further the student’s intent to become a police officer
20 [or], probation agent, OR CORRECTIONAL OFFICER after graduation; or
21 (2) Employed as a police officer [or], probation agent, OR CORRECTIONAL
22 OFFICER, attending an eligible institution, and enrolled in a degree OR CERTIFICATE
23 program that would further the police officer’s [or], probation officer’s, OR
24 CORRECTIONAL OFFICER’S career.
25 (c) The Office shall publicize the availability of the Maryland Police Officers
26 [and], Probation Agents, AND CORRECTIONAL OFFICERS Scholarship.
27 18–3803.
28 (a) Each year, the Office shall select eligible students and offer a scholarship to
29 each student selected to be used at an eligible institution of the student’s choice.

16 The recipient of a Maryland Police Officers [and], Probation Agents, AND
17 CORRECTIONAL OFFICERS Scholarship shall repay the Commission the funds received
18 as set forth in § 18–112 of this title if the recipient does not:
19 (1) Satisfy the degree OR CERTIFICATE requirements of the eligible course

21 (2) Perform the service obligation to work as a police officer [or], probation
