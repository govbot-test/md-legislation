WES MOORE, Governor Ch. 192
Chapter 192
(House Bill 42)
AN ACT concerning
Health Occupations – State Board of Massage Therapy Examiners – Revisions
FOR the purpose of authorizing an individual registered by the State Board of Massage
Therapy Examiners to practice massage therapy in the State to continue to practice
massage therapy beyond a certain date; altering the requirements an individual
must meet to qualify for a license to practice massage therapy; repealing obsolete
qualifications for registered massage practitioners; repealing the requirement that
the imposition of a certain administrative penalty be subject to certain hearing
requirements; altering certain reinstatement and reactivation requirements; and
generally relating to massage therapists.
BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
Article – Health Occupations
Section 6–301(a), 6–302, 6–305(d), 6–306, and 6–306.2
Annotated Code of Maryland
(2021 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
