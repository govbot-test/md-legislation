CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Correctional Officers’ Retirement System – Allegany County
3 FOR the purpose of requiring membership in the Correctional Officers’ Retirement System
4 for certain local detention center officers of Allegany County in the event Allegany
5 County becomes a participating governmental unit in the Correctional Officers’
6 Retirement System; providing that certain local detention center officers who join
7 the Correctional Officers’ Retirement System and receive certain service credit shall
8 no longer be members of the Employees’ Pension System; and generally relating to
9 Allegany County’s participation in the Correctional Officers’ Retirement System.

11 (A) OF THIS SECTION IS ENTITLED TO ELIGIBILITY SERVICE AND CREDITABLE
12 SERVICE IN THE CORRECTIONAL OFFICERS’ RETIREMENT SYSTEM FOR
13 EMPLOYMENT WITH ALLEGANY COUNTY BEFORE THE EFFECTIVE DATE.
14 (D) ON JOINING THE CORRECTIONAL OFFICERS’ RETIREMENT SYSTEM
15 AND RECEIVING SERVICE CREDIT AS PROVIDED UNDER SUBSECTION (C) OF THIS
16 SECTION, AN INDIVIDUAL DESCRIBED UNDER SUBSECTION (A) OF THIS SECTION
17 SHALL:
18 (1) NO LONGER BE A MEMBER OF THE EMPLOYEES’ PENSION
19 SYSTEM; AND
20 (2) HAVE NO FURTHER RIGHTS TO ANY BENEFIT IN THE EMPLOYEES’
21 PENSION SYSTEM.
22 (E) THE TRANSFER OF CREDITABLE SERVICE FOR AN INDIVIDUAL
23 DESCRIBED IN SUBSECTION (A) OF THIS SECTION WHO IS A MEMBER OF THE
24 EMPLOYEES’ PENSION SYSTEM THROUGH THE EFFECTIVE DATE, AND WHO
25 TRANSFERS TO THE CORRECTIONAL OFFICERS’ RETIREMENT SYSTEM UNDER THIS
26 SECTION, IS NOT GOVERNED BY TITLE 37 OF THIS ARTICLE.
27 (F) IF ALLEGANY COUNTY BECOMES A PARTICIPATING GOVERNMENTAL
28 UNIT IN THE CORRECTIONAL OFFICERS’ RETIREMENT SYSTEM, § 31–2B–05(C) OF
29 THIS SUBTITLE SHALL GOVERN THE TRANSFER AND CREDITING OF ASSETS ON THE
