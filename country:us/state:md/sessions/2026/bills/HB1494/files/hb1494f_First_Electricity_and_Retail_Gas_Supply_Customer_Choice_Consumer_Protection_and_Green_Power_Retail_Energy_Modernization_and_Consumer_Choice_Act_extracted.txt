A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Electricity and Retail Gas Supply – Customer Choice, Consumer Protection, and
3 Green Power
4 (Retail Energy Modernization and Consumer Choice Act)
5 FOR the purpose of authorizing the offer and use of certain time–of–use rates and products
6 relating to residential electricity supply and residential retail gas supply, including
7 offering the rates for a term greater than a certain number of months; authorizing
8 the sale and purchase of accounts receivable for certain products; authorizing certain
9 energy resource aggregators to act without the direction of an electric company;
10 requiring the Public Service Commission to adopt certain regulations regarding
11 consolidated billing, protocols for purchasing certain accounts receivable,
12 cybersecurity standards and information exchange protocols, and nondiscriminatory
13 and expedited interconnection procedures; exempting certain green power products
14 from certain provisions regarding the marketing, price, and offering of green power;
15 and generally relating to residential electricity supply, retail gas supply, and green
16 power.
17 BY repealing and reenacting, without amendments,
18 Article – Public Utilities
