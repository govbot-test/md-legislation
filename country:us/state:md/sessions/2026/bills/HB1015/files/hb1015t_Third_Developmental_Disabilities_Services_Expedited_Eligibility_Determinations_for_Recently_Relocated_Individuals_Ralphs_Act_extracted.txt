CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Developmental Disabilities Administration – Services – Expedited Eligibility
3 Determinations for Recently Relocated Individuals
4 (Ralph’s Act)
5 FOR the purpose of requiring that the Maryland Department of Health to provide an
6 expedited eligibility determination for developmental disabilities services to certain
7 individuals who were determined to be eligible to receive certain developmental
8 disabilities services in another state be eligible to continue to receive those services
9 received certain services in another state and relocated in the State; requiring the
10 Department to establish an expedited review process for certain individuals
11 relocating to the State; authorizing the Department to begin the determination
12 process before an individual establishes residency in the State; and generally
13 relating to eligibility determinations for developmental disabilities services in the
14 State.

16 Article – Health – General
17 Section 7–404.2

3 (A) AN INDIVIDUAL SHALL BE ELIGIBLE TO RECEIVE SERVICES FROM THE
4 DEPARTMENT UNDER THIS TITLE IF:
5 (A) (1) IN THIS SECTION THE FOLLOWING WORDS HAVE THE MEANINGS
6 INDICATED.
7 (2) “DEPENDENT” MEANS AN INDIVIDUAL WITH DISABILITIES WHO:
8 (I) RELIES ON SUPPORT FROM AN IMMEDIATE FAMILY
9 MEMBER WHO IS A RESIDENT OF THE STATE; OR
10 (II) HAS AN IMMEDIATE FAMILY MEMBER WHO IS A RESIDENT
11 OF THE STATE AND ACTS AS THE INDIVIDUAL’S LEGAL GUARDIAN.
12 (3) “IMMEDIATE FAMILY MEMBER” MEANS A SPOUSE, A PARENT, AN
13 ADOPTIVE PARENT, A STEPPARENT, A GRANDPARENT, A STEPGRANDPARENT, A
14 SIBLING, A STEPSIBLING, A CHILD, OR A STEPCHILD.
15 (B) THE DEPARTMENT SHALL PROVIDE AN EXPEDITED ELIGIBILITY
16 DETERMINATION FOR DEVELOPMENTAL DISABILITIES SERVICES, INCLUDING A
17 DISABILITY DETERMINATION FOR PURPOSES OF ENROLLMENT IN THE COMMUNITY
18 PATHWAYS WAIVER, TO AN INDIVIDUAL IF:
19 (1) THE INDIVIDUAL:
20 (I) IS A DEPENDENT OF AN IMMEDIATE FAMILY MEMBER;
21 (II) RECEIVED HOME– AND COMMUNITY–BASED WAIVER
22 SERVICES OR SIMILAR SERVICES UNDER A SIMILAR ASSISTANCE PROGRAM IN
23 ANOTHER STATE FOR THE IMMEDIATELY PRECEDING 10 YEARS; AND
24 (III) MOVED TO THE STATE TO LIVE WITH THE IMMEDIATE
25 FAMILY MEMBER ON THE DEATH OR INCAPACITATION OF THE INDIVIDUAL’S
26 CARETAKER IN THE OTHER STATE; AND
27 (2) THE IMMEDIATE FAMILY MEMBER ON WHOM THE INDIVIDUAL IS
28 DEPENDENT HAS RESIDED IN THE STATE FOR THE IMMEDIATELY PRECEDING 2
29 YEARS.

1 (B) (1) THE DEPARTMENT SHALL ESTABLISH AN EXPEDITED PROCESS TO
2 DETERMINE WHETHER AN INDIVIDUAL MEETS THE CRITERIA UNDER SUBSECTION
3 (A) OF THIS SECTION AND IS ELIGIBLE TO RECEIVE SERVICES UNDER THIS TITLE.
4 (2) THE PROCESS ESTABLISHED UNDER THIS SUBSECTION SHALL
5 REQUIRE THAT A DETERMINATION BE COMPLETED WITHIN 30 DAYS.
6 (C) (1) IN PROVIDING EXPEDITED ELIGIBILITY DETERMINATIONS UNDER
7 SUBSECTION (B) OF THIS SECTION, THE DEPARTMENT SHALL:
8 (I) PRIORITIZE COMPLETED APPLICATIONS FOR REVIEW ON
9 RECEIPT;
10 (II) TO THE EXTENT AUTHORIZED BY LAW, RELY ON
11 DOCUMENTATION, DIAGNOSTIC EVALUATIONS, ELIGIBILITY DETERMINATIONS
12 FROM THE INDIVIDUAL’S PREVIOUS STATE OF RESIDENCE, AND ANY OTHER
13 DOCUMENTATION THAT THE DEPARTMENT RELIES ON WHEN MAKING OTHER
14 DETERMINATIONS UNDER THIS TITLE; AND
15 (III) COMPLETE EACH EXPEDITED ELIGIBILITY DETERMINATION
16 WITHIN 60 DAYS AFTER THE RECEIPT OF A COMPLETE APPLICATION.
17 (2) IN PROVIDING AN EXPEDITED ELIGIBILITY DETERMINATION
18 UNDER SUBSECTION (B) OF THIS SECTION, THE DEPARTMENT MAY NOT WAIVE
19 SUBSTANTIVE ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS ESTABLISHED UNDER THIS TITLE OR
20 REQUIREMENTS ESTABLISHED BY REGULATION FOR THE ASSIGNMENT OF A
21 SERVICE PRIORITY CATEGORY.
22 (C) (D) IF AN INDIVIDUAL HAS BEEN DETERMINED UNDER THIS SECTION
23 TO BE ELIGIBLE TO RECEIVE SERVICES, THE INDIVIDUAL SHALL BE:
24 (1) PLACED IN THE CRISIS RESOLUTION CATEGORY; AND
25 (2) GIVEN ACCESS TO WAIVER SERVICES THROUGH THE MOST
26 APPROPRIATE RESERVE CATEGORY AS DETERMINED BASED ON THE SERVICES THE
27 INDIVIDUAL RECEIVED IN THE INDIVIDUAL’S PREVIOUS STATE OF RESIDENCE.
28 (E) IF THE DEPARTMENT DETERMINES THAT AN INDIVIDUAL WHO HAS NOT
29 YET ESTABLISHED RESIDENCY IN THE STATE WOULD QUALIFY FOR THE EXPEDITED
30 ELIGIBILITY DETERMINATION PROCESS PROVIDED UNDER SUBSECTION (B) OF THIS
31 SECTION, THE DEPARTMENT MAY DETERMINE WHETHER THE INDIVIDUAL HAS A
32 DEVELOPMENTAL DISABILITY AND THE APPROPRIATE SERVICE PRIORITY
33 CATEGORY.
