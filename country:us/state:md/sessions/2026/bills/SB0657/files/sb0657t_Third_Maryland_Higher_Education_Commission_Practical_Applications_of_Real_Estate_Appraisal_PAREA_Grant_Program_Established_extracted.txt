1 AN ACT concerning
2 Maryland Higher Education Commission – Practical Applications of Real Estate
3 Appraisal (PAREA) Grant Program – Established
4 FOR the purpose of establishing the Practical Applications of Real Estate Appraisal
5 (PAREA) Grant Program to provide grants to certain individuals with the goal of
6 becoming a real estate appraiser in certain communities; requiring the Maryland
7 Higher Education Commission to administer the Program; and generally relating to
8 the Practical Applications of Real Estate Appraisal (PAREA) Grant Program.

10 Article – Education

12 Grant Program”

2 SENATE BILL 657
1 (A) IN THIS SUBTITLE THE FOLLOWING WORDS HAVE THE MEANINGS
2 INDICATED.
3 (B) “PAREA” MEANS THE PRACTICAL APPLICATIONS OF REAL ESTATE
4 APPRAISAL.
5 (C) “PROGRAM” MEANS THE PRACTICAL APPLICATIONS OF REAL ESTATE
6 APPRAISAL GRANT PROGRAM.
7 18–4202.
8 (A) THERE IS A PRACTICAL APPLICATIONS OF REAL ESTATE APPRAISAL
9 GRANT PROGRAM IN THE COMMISSION.
10 (B) THE PURPOSE OF THE PROGRAM IS TO:
11 (1) ASSIST IN CLOSING APPRAISAL GAPS THAT OCCUR IN
12 HISTORICALLY REDLINED NEIGHBORHOODS AND UNDERREPRESENTED
13 COMMUNITIES;
14 (2) AWARD GRANTS TO MINORITIES WHO RESIDE IN HISTORICALLY
15 REDLINED NEIGHBORHOODS AND UNDERREPRESENTED COMMUNITIES WITH THE
16 GOAL OF PURSUING PAREA; AND
17 (3) DIVERSIFY THE APPRAISAL FIELD.
18 (C) THE COMMISSION SHALL ADMINISTER THE PROGRAM.
19 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect July
20 1, 2026.
