A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Motor Vehicles – Automated Traffic Enforcement Systems – Impoundment
3 FOR the purpose of authorizing a law enforcement agency to impound a motor vehicle to
4 which a certain number of certain unpaid and overdue civil citations issued through
5 the use of an automated traffic enforcement system apply; and generally relating to
6 automated traffic enforcement systems.
