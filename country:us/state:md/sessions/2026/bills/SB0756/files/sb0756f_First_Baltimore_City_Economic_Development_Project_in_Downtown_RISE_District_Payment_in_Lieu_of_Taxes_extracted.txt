A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Baltimore City – Economic Development Project in Downtown RISE District –
3 Payment in Lieu of Taxes
4 FOR the purpose of exempting an economic development project located in a certain area
5 of downtown Baltimore City from Baltimore City property taxes if the owner of the
6 economic development project and the Baltimore City Board of Estimates enter into
7 a payment in lieu of taxes agreement and certain other requirements are met; and
8 generally relating to a payment in lieu of taxes agreement for an economic
9 development project in downtown Baltimore City.
10 BY adding to
11 Article – Tax – Property
12 Section 7–504.5
13 Annotated Code of Maryland
14 (2019 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
15 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
16 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
17 Article – Tax – Property
18 7–504.5.

20 ESTATE DEVELOPMENT PROJECT THAT CONSISTS OF NEWLY CONSTRUCTED OR
21 REHABILITATED COMMERCIAL OR MULTIFAMILY RESIDENTIAL PROPERTY IF THE
22 REAL ESTATE DEVELOPMENT PROJECT:
23 (1) EXISTS WITHIN THE DOWNTOWN RISE DISTRICT AS DESCRIBED
24 IN SUBSECTION (B) OF THIS SECTION; AND

15 (C) AN ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT PROJECT IS EXEMPT OR PARTIALLY
16 EXEMPT FROM BALTIMORE CITY REAL PROPERTY TAX IF:
17 (1) THE OWNER OF THE ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT PROJECT
18 DEMONSTRATES TO THE SATISFACTION OF THE BALTIMORE CITY BOARD OF
19 ESTIMATES THAT THE CITY OF BALTIMORE OR ITS DESIGNATED AGENCY HAS
20 CONDUCTED AN ECONOMIC ANALYSIS OF THE PROJECT, INCLUDING AN
21 ASSESSMENT OF THE FINANCIAL NECESSITY FOR AN EXEMPTION AS AUTHORIZED
22 UNDER THIS SECTION; AND
23 (2) THE OWNER OF THE ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT PROJECT AND THE
24 BALTIMORE CITY BOARD OF ESTIMATES ENTER INTO A PAYMENT IN LIEU OF TAXES
25 AGREEMENT SPECIFYING THE AMOUNT THAT THE OWNER SHALL PAY TO
26 BALTIMORE CITY EACH YEAR IN LIEU OF BALTIMORE CITY PROPERTY TAXES
27 DURING THE TERM OF THE AGREEMENT.

1 (D) TO BE ELIGIBLE FOR A PAYMENT IN LIEU OF TAXES AGREEMENT UNDER
2 THIS SECTION, THE OWNER OF AN ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT PROJECT SHALL, ON
3 OR BEFORE JUNE 30, 2036:
4 (1) APPLY TO ENTER INTO A PAYMENT IN LIEU OF TAXES AGREEMENT;
5 (2) HAVE BUILDING PERMITS ISSUED; AND
6 (3) HAVE SATISFIED OR WAIVED ALL CONDITIONS FOR THE
7 FINANCING REQUIRED FOR THE CONSTRUCTION OF THE PROJECT.
8 (E) ON OR BEFORE JANUARY 1 EACH YEAR, THE CITY OF BALTIMORE OR
9 ITS DESIGNATED AGENCY SHALL REPORT TO THE PRESIDENT OF THE CITY COUNCIL
10 OF BALTIMORE AND, SUBJECT TO § 2–1257 OF THE STATE GOVERNMENT ARTICLE,
11 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY:
12 (1) A DESCRIPTION OF EACH ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT PROJECT
13 FOR WHICH THE CITY OF BALTIMORE ENTERED INTO A PAYMENT IN LIEU OF TAXES
14 AGREEMENT UNDER THIS SECTION DURING THE PRECEDING FISCAL YEAR,
15 INCLUDING A STATEMENT OF THE ANALYSIS OF THE PROJECT DESCRIBED IN
16 SUBSECTION (C)(1) OF THIS SECTION; AND
17 (2) FOR THOSE ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT PROJECTS THAT HAVE A
18 PAYMENT IN LIEU OF TAXES AGREEMENT UNDER THIS SECTION, AND FOR WHICH
19 CONSTRUCTION OR REHABILITATION HAS BEEN COMPLETED:
20 (I) THE NUMBER AND TYPES OF JOBS CREATED DURING THE
21 PRECEDING FISCAL YEAR AND ESTIMATED TO BE CREATED DURING THE FOLLOWING
22 FISCAL YEAR;
23 (II) THE TOTAL TAXES THAT THE PROJECT IS ESTIMATED TO
24 HAVE GENERATED, DIRECTLY AND INDIRECTLY, FOR THE CITY OF BALTIMORE
25 DURING THE PRECEDING FISCAL YEAR AND ESTIMATED TO BE GENERATED DURING
26 THE FOLLOWING FISCAL YEAR; AND
27 (III) ANY OTHER ECONOMIC BENEFITS OF THE PROJECT.
28 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect July
