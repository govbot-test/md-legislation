WES MOORE, Governor Ch. 472
Chapter 472
(Senate Bill 184)
AN ACT concerning
Office of the Attorney General – Environmental and Natural Resources Crimes
Unit – Reporting Requirement
FOR the purpose of altering the date by which the Office of the Attorney General is required
to report each year to the Governor and the General Assembly on certain activities
of the Office’s Environmental and Natural Resources Crimes Unit and certain
activities taken by the Department of the Environment or the Department of Natural
Resources in response to findings of the Unit; and generally relating to the
Environmental and Natural Resources Crimes Unit.
BY repealing and reenacting, without amendments,
Article – State Government
Section 6–702(a)(1)
Annotated Code of Maryland
(2021 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
Article – State Government
Section 6–702(d)
Annotated Code of Maryland
(2021 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
Article – State Government
6–702.
(a) (1) There is an Environmental and Natural Resources Crimes Unit of the
Office of the Attorney General.
(d) On or before [November 30] OCTOBER 1 each year, the Attorney General
shall report to the Governor, and, in accordance with § 2–1257 of this article, the General
Assembly on:
(1) all the activities of the Unit DURING THE PREVIOUS FISCAL YEAR;
and

(2) any actions taken by the Department of the Environment or the
Department of Natural Resources in response to findings and recommendations of the Unit.
SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect July
1, 2026.
