A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Education – Blueprint for Maryland’s Future – Oversight Requirements
3 FOR the purpose of altering the length of the term of the members of the Accountability
4 and Implementation Board; authorizing a public school or local school system to
5 appeal to the Office of Administrative Hearings after receiving a decision by the
6 Accountability and Implementation Board to withhold certain funds after a certain
7 appeals process; establishing the Blueprint for Maryland’s Future Stakeholder
8 Advisory Council; providing for the purpose, composition, terms, and duties of the
9 Council; providing that the Accountability and Implementation Board is under the
10 jurisdiction of the Inspector General for Education; establishing the Joint Oversight
11 Committee on the Blueprint for Maryland’s Future; providing for the composition,
12 terms, and duties of the Joint Oversight Committee; requiring the Office of
13 Legislative Audits to conduct a performance audit of the Accountability and
14 Implementation Board at certain intervals; providing for the termination of the
15 terms of members of the Accountability and Implementation Board; and generally
16 relating to oversight requirements for the Blueprint for Maryland’s Future.

13 Legislative Audits to conduct a performance audit of the Accountability and
