A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Commission on the House of Reformation and Instruction for Colored
3 Children – Establishment
4 FOR the purpose of establishing the Commission on the House of Reformation and
5 Instruction for Colored Children; and generally relating to the Commission on the
6 House of Reformation and Instruction for Colored Children.
7 BY adding to
8 Article – State Government
9 Section 6–1001 to be under the new subtitle “Subtitle 10. The Commission on the
10 House of Reformation and Instruction for Colored Children”
11 Annotated Code of Maryland
12 (2021 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
13 Preamble
14 WHEREAS, The State of Maryland established the House of Reformation and
15 Instruction for Colored Children (also known under various historical names) in Prince
16 George’s County for over a century as a detention and reformatory institution for youth;
17 and
18 WHEREAS, Recent reports and historical records have brought to light the existence
19 of a neglected cemetery on the grounds of the former institution containing the unmarked
20 graves of hundreds of deceased children, primarily African American boys, who died while
21 in the care and custody of the State; and
22 WHEREAS, The condition of this burial ground represents a profound and
23 long–standing failure of the State to provide dignity in death to children for whom it had
24 assumed responsibility in life, and this neglect has hidden a painful chapter of Maryland’s
25 history from public view and accountability; and

4 buried in unmarked graves, and to understand the system that allowed such a tragedy to
5 occur; now, therefore,
