18 (A) THERE IS A COMMISSION ON THE HOUSE OF REFORMATION AND
19 INSTRUCTION FOR COLORED CHILDREN.
20 (B) THE PURPOSE OF THE COMMISSION IS TO:
21 (1) RESEARCH THE HISTORY, OPERATIONS, AND RESIDENT DEATHS
22 AT THE HOUSE OF REFORMATION AND INSTRUCTION FOR COLORED CHILDREN;
23 (2) PROVIDE A COMPLETE AND PUBLIC ACCOUNTING OF THE
24 CHILDREN WHO DIED AND WERE BURIED ON THE GROUNDS OF THE HOUSE OF
25 REFORMATION AND INSTRUCTION FOR COLORED CHILDREN;
26 (3) PROMOTE HEALING AND RECONCILIATION THROUGH
27 TRUTH–TELLING, ACKNOWLEDGMENT, AND RECOMMENDATIONS FOR SYSTEMIC
28 REFORM; AND
29 (4) ENSURE DESCENDANT AND COMMUNITY VOICE THROUGHOUT
30 THE INVESTIGATION AND RECONCILIATION PROCESS.
31 (C) THE COMMISSION CONSISTS OF:
