A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Secondhand Precious Metal Object Dealers – Temporary Event–Based Dealer
3 Limited License – Establishment
4 FOR the purpose of establishing a limited license to do business as a temporary
5 event–based dealer in the State; authorizing a person that does not maintain a fixed
6 business address in the State to apply for a limited license; authorizing the Secretary
7 of Labor to issue a limited license to a person for the purpose of doing business at
8 certain locations; and generally relating to a limited license to do business as a
9 temporary event–based dealer in the State.
