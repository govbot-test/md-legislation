CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Water Pollution Control – Discharge Permits – Concentrated Animal Feeding
3 Operations
4 FOR the purpose of repealing a requirement that certain provision of law requiring a person
5 to hold a concentrated animal feeding operation (CAFO) general discharge permit
6 issued by the Department of the Environment before the person may begin
7 construction on any part of a new CAFO; authorizing the Department, under certain
8 circumstances, to authorize a person to begin construction on any part of a new
9 animal feeding operation under a certain approval process established by the
10 Department; and generally relating to the discharge of pollutants into the waters of
11 the State and concentrated animal feeding operations.
