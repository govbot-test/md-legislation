1 AN ACT concerning
2 Maryland Building Performance Standards – Energy Conservation
3 Requirements
4 Environment – Permit Applications for New Buildings – Notice
5 (Large Buildings for Tomorrow Act)
6 FOR the purpose of requiring the Maryland Department of Labor, on or before a certain
7 date and as part of the Maryland Building Performance Standards, to adopt energy
8 conservation requirements for the new construction of certain buildings that are
9 subject to the Building Energy Performance Standards adopted by the Maryland
10 Department of the Environment, beginning on a certain date, a county or
11 municipality to provide certain notice to the Department of the Environment of a
12 certain permit application for a certain new building within a certain period of time
13 after a county or municipality accepts the permit application; and generally relating
14 to the Maryland Building Performance Standards and energy conservation
15 requirements permit applications for new buildings.

16 Article – Environment
17 2–1603.
18 (A) BEGINNING JULY 1, 2026, WITHIN 10 BUSINESS DAYS AFTER A COUNTY

24 SHALL INCLUDE:

28 (4) THE PROPOSED BUILDING TYPE;
29 (5) THE ENERGY SOURCES PLANNED FOR USE IN THE PROPOSED
30 BUILDING; AND
31 (6) THE CONTACT INFORMATION FOR THE OWNER OF THE PROPOSED
32 BUILDING.
