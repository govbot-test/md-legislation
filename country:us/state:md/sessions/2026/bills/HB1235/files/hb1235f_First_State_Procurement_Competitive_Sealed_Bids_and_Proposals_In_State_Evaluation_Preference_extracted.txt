A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 State Procurement – Competitive Sealed Bids and Proposals – In–State
3 Evaluation Preference
4 FOR the purpose of requiring competitive sealed bids and proposals to be awarded based
5 on certain standardized evaluation criteria; requiring the criteria to be included in
6 an invitation for bids or a request for proposals; requiring a responsive bid or
7 proposal to include a certain State and local economic impact statement; and
8 generally relating to State procurement.
