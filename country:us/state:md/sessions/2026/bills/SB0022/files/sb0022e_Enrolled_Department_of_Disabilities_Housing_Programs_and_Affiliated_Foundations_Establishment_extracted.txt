CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Department of Disabilities – Housing Programs and Affiliated Foundations –
3 Establishment
4 FOR the purpose of requiring the Department of Disabilities to establish affordable and
5 accessible housing programs; authorizing the Department to establish affiliated
6 foundations to work with certain programs and units of the Department to solicit
7 and accept funds, partner with individuals and entities, and promote certain
8 activities that support certain projects; prohibiting an affiliated foundation from
9 influencing certain determinations made by the Department; and generally relating
10 to the establishment of housing programs and affiliated foundations by the
11 Department of Disabilities.
12 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
13 Article – Human Services

3 (B) THE PURPOSE OF AN AFFILIATED FOUNDATION IS TO:
4 (1) SOLICIT AND ACCEPT FUNDS AND PARTNER WITH INDIVIDUALS,
5 CORPORATIONS, AND OTHER ENTITIES TO SUPPORT INNOVATIVE PROJECTS
6 DESIGNED TO MAINTAIN, EXPAND, AND ENHANCE THE PROGRAMS AND UNITS
7 SPECIFIED IN SUBSECTION (A) OF THIS SECTION; AND
8 (2) PROMOTE ACTIVITIES THAT:
9 (I) ENHANCE AWARENESS OF, ACCESS TO, AND EFFECTIVE USE
10 OF ASSISTIVE TECHNOLOGY, ACCESSIBLE INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY, AND
11 ENVIRONMENTAL ADAPTATIONS THAT ALLOW INDIVIDUALS WITH DISABILITIES TO
12 PARTICIPATE IN EMPLOYMENT, EDUCATION, HOUSING, TRANSPORTATION, PUBLIC
13 SAFETY, RECREATION, HEALTH CARE, AND OTHER ASPECTS OF DAILY LIFE;
14 (II) SUPPORT TRAINING, TECHNICAL ASSISTANCE, AND
15 REASONABLE ACCOMMODATIONS THAT PROMOTE THE HIRING AND RETENTION OF
16 INDIVIDUALS WITH DISABILITIES IN PRIVATE AND PUBLIC SECTOR EMPLOYMENT;
17 AND
18 (III) CREATE OPPORTUNITIES FOR INTEGRATED, AFFORDABLE,
19 AND ACCESSIBLE HOUSING FOR INDIVIDUALS WITH DISABILITIES TO LIVE IN THE
20 COMMUNITIES OF THEIR CHOICE.

21 SECTION MAY NOT:
22 (I) BE CONSIDERED AN AGENCY OR INSTRUMENTALITY OF THE

1 (F) (1) NOTWITHSTANDING §§ 5–501 THROUGH 5–504 OF THE GENERAL
2 PROVISIONS ARTICLE, AN OFFICIAL OR EMPLOYEE OF THE DEPARTMENT MAY
3 BECOME A DIRECTOR OR AN OFFICIAL OF AN AFFILIATED FOUNDATION
4 ESTABLISHED UNDER THIS SECTION.
5 (2) AN OFFICIAL OR EMPLOYEE OF THE DEPARTMENT WHO SERVES
6 AS A DIRECTOR OR AN OFFICIAL OF AN AFFILIATED FOUNDATION ESTABLISHED
7 UNDER THIS SECTION:
8 (I) MAY NOT BE COMPENSATED, DIRECTLY OR INDIRECTLY, BY
9 THE AFFILIATED FOUNDATION; AND
10 (II) MAY BE REIMBURSED FOR BONA FIDE EXPENSES INCURRED
11 IN THE PERFORMANCE OF ACTIVITIES UNDERTAKEN ON BEHALF OF THE
12 AFFILIATED FOUNDATION AS AUTHORIZED BY THE DEPARTMENT AND THE BOARD
13 OF DIRECTORS OF THE AFFILIATED FOUNDATION.
14 (3) (I) THE DEPARTMENT SHALL NOTIFY THE STATE ETHICS
15 COMMISSION IN WRITING WHENEVER THE DEPARTMENT ALLOWS AN OFFICIAL OR
16 EMPLOYEE OF THE DEPARTMENT TO SERVE AS A DIRECTOR OR AN OFFICIAL OF AN
17 AFFILIATED FOUNDATION.
18 (II) WITHIN 30 DAYS AFTER RECEIPT OF THE NOTICE UNDER
19 SUBPARAGRAPH (I) OF THIS PARAGRAPH, THE STATE ETHICS COMMISSION SHALL
20 NOTIFY THE DEPARTMENT OF ANY OBJECTIONS OR CONCERNS PERTAINING TO THE
21 JOINT SERVICE IDENTIFIED IN THE NOTICE.
22 (III) ON RECEIPT OF A NOTICE FROM THE STATE ETHICS
23 COMMISSION UNDER SUBPARAGRAPH (II) OF THIS PARAGRAPH, THE DEPARTMENT
24 SHALL REEXAMINE THE JOINT SERVICE IDENTIFIED IN THE NOTICE.
25 (4) ON OR BEFORE SEPTEMBER 1 EACH YEAR, THE DEPARTMENT
26 SHALL REPORT TO THE GOVERNOR, THE LEGISLATIVE POLICY COMMITTEE OF THE
27 GENERAL ASSEMBLY IN ACCORDANCE WITH § 2–1257 OF THE STATE GOVERNMENT
28 ARTICLE, AND THE STATE ETHICS COMMISSION ON:
29 (I) THE NAMES OF THE OFFICIALS AND EMPLOYEES SERVING
30 AS A DIRECTOR OR AN OFFICIAL OF AN AFFILIATED FOUNDATION; AND
31 (II) HOW THE POLICIES ADOPTED UNDER SUBSECTION (C) OF
32 THIS SECTION HAVE BEEN IMPLEMENTED IN THE PRECEDING CALENDAR YEAR.
