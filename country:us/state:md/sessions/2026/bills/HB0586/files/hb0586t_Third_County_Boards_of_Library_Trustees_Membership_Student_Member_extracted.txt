18 GOOD STANDING IN THE STUDENT’S HIGH SCHOOL;
19 (II) BE SELECTED IN THE STUDENT’S SOPHOMORE OR JUNIOR

21 (III) HAVE AN INTEREST IN LIBRARY MATTERS; AND
22 (IV) SERVE A TERM OF 1 YEAR.

25 (3) The county governing body may reject the resignation if the member
26 explains his nonattendance satisfactorily.
27 (4) The resignation is effective from the date of the final review by the
28 county governing body, which shall be within 10 days after it receives the report from the
29 [chairman] CHAIR of the board of library trustees. The county governing body shall fill any
30 resulting vacancy as provided in § 23–403 of this subtitle.
