A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 School and School–Sponsored Activities – Report of Suspected Abuse or Neglect
3 – Parental Notification
4 FOR the purpose of requiring a school to provide certain notice to the parent, guardian, or
5 caretaker of a child if a mandated reporter reports certain suspected abuse or neglect
6 that occurs at a school or school–sponsored activity, except under certain
7 circumstances; and generally relating to parental notification for reports of
8 suspected child abuse or neglect at school and school–sponsored activities.
