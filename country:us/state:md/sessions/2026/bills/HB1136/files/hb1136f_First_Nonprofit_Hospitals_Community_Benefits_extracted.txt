A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Nonprofit Hospitals – Community Benefits
3 FOR the purpose of requiring certain nonprofit hospitals to provide a certain value of
4 community benefits each year; requiring the Health Services Cost Review
5 Commission to report on the compliance of nonprofit hospitals with the community
6 benefits requirement to the Attorney General and the Comptroller; authorizing the
7 Comptroller to revoke the tax–exempt status of a nonprofit hospital under certain
8 circumstances; altering the requirements of a community benefit report; and
9 generally relating to nonprofit hospitals and community benefits.
