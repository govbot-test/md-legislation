CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Health Insurance – Graduate–Level Clinical Interns – Required Reimbursement
3 FOR the purpose of establishing that insureds and any other persons covered by certain
4 health insurance policies, contracts, or certificates are entitled to reimbursement for
5 the services rendered by a graduate–level counseling, social work, or psychology
6 clinical intern under certain circumstances; and generally relating to health
7 insurance reimbursement for the services of clinical interns.
