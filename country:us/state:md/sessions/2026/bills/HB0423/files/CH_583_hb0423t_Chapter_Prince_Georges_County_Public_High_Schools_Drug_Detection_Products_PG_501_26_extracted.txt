WES MOORE, Governor Ch. 583
Chapter 583
AN ACT concerning
Prince George’s County – Public High Schools – Drug Detection Products
PG 501–26
FOR the purpose of requiring, subject to certain conditions, the Prince George’s County
Board of Education to provide certain drug detection products to public high school
students in the county in a certain manner beginning in a certain school year;
requiring each public high school in the county to submit a certain report to
Maryland’s Office of Overdose Response on or before a certain date each year; and
generally relating to drug detection products and Prince George’s County public high
schools.
BY repealing and reenacting, without amendments,
