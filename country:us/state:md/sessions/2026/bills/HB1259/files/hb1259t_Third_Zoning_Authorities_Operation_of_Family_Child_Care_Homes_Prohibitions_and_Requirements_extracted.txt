1 AN ACT concerning
2 Zoning Authorities – Operation of Family Child Care Homes – Prohibitions and
3 Requirements
4 FOR the purpose of prohibiting a local jurisdiction in the State from denying or restricting
5 the operation of certain family child care homes under certain circumstances and in
6 a certain manner; requiring a local jurisdiction to designate the operation of certain
7 family child care homes in a certain manner; and generally relating to zoning and
8 the operation of family child care homes.

9 (a) (1) In this section the following words have the meanings indicated.

14 (b) A local jurisdiction, BY LOCAL ORDINANCE, RESOLUTION, LAW, OR RULE:
15 (1) may not[, by local ordinance, resolution, law, or rule,]:
16 (I) limit the number of children for which a family child care home
17 or large family child care home provides family child care to below the number authorized
18 by the State Department of Education; OR
19 (II) DENY OR RESTRICT THE OPERATION OF A FAMILY CHILD
20 CARE HOME OR LARGE FAMILY CHILD CARE HOME THAT MEETS THE LICENSING
21 REQUIREMENTS ESTABLISHED BY THE STATE DEPARTMENT OF EDUCATION; AND
22 (2) SHALL DESIGNATE THE OPERATION OF A FAMILY CHILD CARE
23 HOME OR LARGE FAMILY CHILD CARE HOME AS:
24 (I) A RESIDENTIAL ACTIVITY; AND
25 (II) A PERMITTED USE UNDER RESIDENTIAL ZONING
