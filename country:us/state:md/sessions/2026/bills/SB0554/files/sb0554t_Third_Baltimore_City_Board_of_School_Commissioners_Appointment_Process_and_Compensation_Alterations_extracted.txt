1 AN ACT concerning
2 Baltimore City Board of School Commissioners – Appointment Process and
3 Compensation – Alterations
4 FOR the purpose of repealing provisions of law related to the Baltimore City Public School
5 Board Community Panel; altering the process for appointments to the Baltimore City
6 Board of School Commissioners; requiring the Mayor of Baltimore City to hold a
7 certain public hearing to select qualified candidates for appointment to the
8 Baltimore City Board of School Commissioners; providing that certain members of
9 the Baltimore City Board of School Commissioners are entitled to certain
10 compensation; requiring a student member of the Baltimore City Board of School
11 Commissioners to be granted a certain scholarship; and generally relating to
12 appointments for and compensation of the Baltimore City Board of School
13 Commissioners.

21 student’s junior or senior year of high school in the Baltimore City Public School System
22 who shall be elected by the high school students of the Baltimore City Public School System,
23 in accordance with procedures established by the board in collaboration with the Associated
24 Student Congress of Baltimore City.

1 (iii) Student disciplinary matters.
2 (5) The student member may not vote on matters before the board relating
3 to personnel and collective bargaining decisions.

5 special session of the board.
6 [(n)] (M) Any action by the board shall require:
7 (1) A quorum of a majority of the voting members then serving; and
8 (2) The affirmative vote of a majority of the voting members then serving.

13 shall apply to appointed members and members appointed to fill a vacancy after the
14 effective date of this Act.
