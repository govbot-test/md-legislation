1 AN ACT concerning
2 Sports Wagering – Gaming – Campaign Financing Contributions and Study on
3 Independent Evaluation of Sports Wagering
4 FOR the purpose of requiring, rather than authorizing, certain sports wagering licensees
5 and sports wagering operators that advertise in the State to contract with repealing
6 the prohibition on applicants for and holders of video lottery operation licenses and
7 persons who own an interest in video lottery facility operations in the State from
8 directly or indirectly making contributions to certain campaign finance entities;
9 requiring the State Lottery and Gaming Control Agency to study and report to the
10 Governor and the General Assembly, on or before a certain date, on certain matters
11 concerning certain independent evaluators for certain purposes sports wagering
12 content; and generally relating to the evaluation of sports wagering content gaming.

18 Government Article.
