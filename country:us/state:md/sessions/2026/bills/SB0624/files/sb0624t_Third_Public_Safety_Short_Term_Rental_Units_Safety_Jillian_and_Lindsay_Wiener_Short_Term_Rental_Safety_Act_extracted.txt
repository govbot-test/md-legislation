CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Public Safety – Short–Term Rental Units – Fire Safety
3 (Jillian and Lindsay Wiener Short–Term Rental Fire Safety Act)
4 FOR the purpose of requiring certain short–term rental units to post certain evacuation
5 diagrams and certain emergency telephone numbers in the unit and to provide fire
6 extinguishers and install certain smoke alarms and carbon monoxide alarms in the
7 unit; requiring a certain short–term rental host to replace a smoke alarm and carbon
8 monoxide alarm under certain circumstances; requiring certain booking services to
9 notify certain short–term rental hosts regarding certain fire safety requirements;
10 requiring a county certain counties or Baltimore City to require, through local law
11 or regulation, certain inspections and to report certain information to the State Fire
12 Marshal under certain circumstances; requiring the State Fire Marshal to make a
13 certain report to the General Assembly; and generally relating to fire safety
14 requirements for short–term rental units.
