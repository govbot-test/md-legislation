1 AN ACT concerning
2 Public Health – Mammograms – Arterial Calcification Notice
3 FOR the purpose of requiring certain centers where mammography testing is performed to
4 include in the screening results letter that is sent to a patient, as required by federal
5 law, a notice of the presence of arterial calcification as observed during the patient’s
6 mammogram; and generally relating to mammography testing.

18 (b) On or after July 1, 1992, a person may not perform mammography testing

1 1. Require a notice regarding breast density to be sent to a

6 (2) Subject to paragraph (3) of this subsection, a center where
7 mammography testing is performed shall include in a screening results letter that is sent
8 to a patient, as required by federal law, the following notice: “This notice contains the
9 results of your recent mammogram, including information about breast density.
10 If your mammogram shows that your breast tissue is dense, you should know that
11 dense breast tissue is a common finding and is not abnormal, with about half of women
12 having dense or highly dense breasts. However, dense breast tissue can make it harder to
13 find cancer on a mammogram and may also be associated with an increased risk of cancer.
14 IF YOUR MAMMOGRAM SHOWS BREAST ARTERIAL CALCIFICATION, YOU
15 SHOULD KNOW THAT BREAST ARTERIAL CALCIFICATION IS A COMMON FINDING.
16 HOWEVER, BREAST ARTERIAL CALCIFICATION MAY BE AN INDICATOR OF AN
17 INCREASED RISK OF CARDIOVASCULAR DISEASE. IF YOU HAVE BREAST ARTERIAL
18 CALCIFICATIONS, PLEASE DISCUSS THIS WITH YOUR PHYSICIAN TO ASSESS YOUR
19 CARDIOVASCULAR RISKS AND DETERMINE IF ADDITIONAL TESTING MAY BE
20 APPROPRIATE FOR YOU.
21 This information about the result of your mammogram is given to you to raise your
22 awareness and to inform your conversations with your physician. Together, you can decide
23 whether additional screening options are right for you based on your mammogram results,
24 individual risk factors, or physical examination. A report of your results was sent to your
25 physician.”.
26 (3) If the Department finds significant differences between the content of
27 the notice that is required to be provided under paragraph (2) of this subsection and current

29 content of the notice.
