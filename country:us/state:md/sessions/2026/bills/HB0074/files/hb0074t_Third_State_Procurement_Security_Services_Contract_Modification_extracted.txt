CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 State Procurement – Construction and Security Services – Contract
3 Modification
4 FOR the purpose of requiring a certain procurement contract for construction or security
5 services to include a clause requiring contract modification for increased costs for
6 compensation and benefits under certain circumstances; requiring a certain contract
7 that is modified to be contingent on approval by the Chief Procurement Officer chief
8 procurement officer of the appropriate primary procurement unit; and generally
9 relating to procurement contracts for construction and security services.
10 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
11 Article – State Finance and Procurement
12 Section 13–218(b)
13 Annotated Code of Maryland
14 (2021 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
15 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
16 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
17 Article – State Finance and Procurement
18 13–218.
EXPLANATION: CAPITALS INDICATE MATTER ADDED TO EXISTING LAW.
[Brackets] indicate matter deleted from existing law.
Underlining indicates amendments to bill.
Strike out indicates matter stricken from the bill by amendment or deleted from the law by
amendment. *hb0074*
2 HOUSE BILL 74
1 (b) (1) In addition to the clauses required under subsection (a) of this section,
2 a procurement contract for construction shall include:
3 [(1)] (I) a clause providing for contract modification if the condition of a
4 site differs from the condition described in the specifications; [and]
5 [(2)] (II) a clause covering the requirements for notice of contract claims,
6 submission of contract claims, and resolution of contract claims under § 15–219 of this
7 article; AND
8 (III) IF THE CONTRACT IS FOR 3 YEARS OR MORE IN DURATION
9 AND IS VALUED AT GREATER THAN $2,000,000, A CLAUSE REQUIRING CONTRACT
10 MODIFICATION FOR THE EQUITABLE ADJUSTMENT OF THE CONTRACT FOR
11 INCREASED COSTS FOR COMPENSATION AND BENEFITS AS A RESULT OF STATUTORY
12 CHANGES INCREASING REQUIRED COMPENSATION OR BENEFITS.
13 (2) IN ADDITION TO THE CLAUSES REQUIRED UNDER SUBSECTION (A)
14 OF THIS SECTION, A PROCUREMENT CONTRACT FOR SECURITY SERVICES THAT IS
15 FOR 3 YEARS OR MORE IN DURATION AND IS VALUED AT GREATER THAN $2,000,000
16 SHALL INCLUDE A CLAUSE REQUIRING CONTRACT MODIFICATION FOR THE
17 EQUITABLE ADJUSTMENT OF THE CONTRACT FOR INCREASED COSTS FOR
18 COMPENSATION AND BENEFITS AS A RESULT OF:
19 (I) STATUTORY CHANGES INCREASING REQUIRED
20 COMPENSATION OR BENEFITS; OR
21 (II) COLLECTIVE BARGAINING.
22 (3) A CONTRACT THAT IS MODIFIED IN ACCORDANCE WITH
23 PARAGRAPH (1)(III) OR (2) OF THIS SUBSECTION IS CONTINGENT ON APPROVAL BY
24 THE CHIEF PROCUREMENT OFFICER CHIEF PROCUREMENT OFFICER OF THE
25 APPROPRIATE PRIMARY PROCUREMENT UNIT.
26 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
27 October 1, 2026.
