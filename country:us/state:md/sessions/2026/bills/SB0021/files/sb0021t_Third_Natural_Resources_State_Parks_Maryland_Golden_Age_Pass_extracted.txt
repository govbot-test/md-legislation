CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Natural Resources – State Parks – Maryland Golden Age Pass
3 FOR the purpose of establishing that the holder of a Maryland Golden Age Pass is entitled
4 to free day–use entry at certain State parks and is authorized to launch a boat at
5 any State park without paying a boat launch fee; altering the days of the week on
6 which the holder of a Maryland Golden Age Pass may camp overnight in a State park
7 at a discounted fee; establishing that the free day–use entry applies to everyone in
8 the vehicle with the holder of a Maryland Golden Age Pass under certain
9 circumstances; and generally relating to the Maryland Golden Age Pass.

2 SENATE BILL 21
1 (A) A person 62 years old or older who possesses a Maryland Golden Age Pass[,]
2 issued by the Maryland Park Service[, may]:
3 (1) IS ENTITLED TO FREE DAY–USE ENTRY TO ANY STATE PARK THAT
4 CHARGES SERVICE FEES;
5 (2) MAY camp overnight in any State park at one–half of the established
6 fee [Monday] SUNDAY through Thursday, excluding holidays; AND
7 (3) MAY LAUNCH A BOAT AT ANY STATE PARK WITHOUT PAYING A
8 BOAT LAUNCH FEE.
9 (B) IF A PER–VEHICLE FEE IS IN EFFECT AT A STATE PARK, THE FREE
10 DAY–USE ENTRY UNDER SUBSECTION (A)(1) OF THIS SECTION APPLIES TO
11 EVERYONE IN THE VEHICLE WITH THE HOLDER OF A MARYLAND GOLDEN AGE PASS.
12 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
13 October 1, 2026.
