Section 1:
WES MOORE, Governor Ch. 333
Chapter 333
AN ACT concerning
Natural Resources – State Parks – Maryland Golden Age Pass
FOR the purpose of establishing that the holder of a Maryland Golden Age Pass is entitled
to free day–use entry at certain State parks and is authorized to launch a boat at
any State park without paying a boat launch fee; altering the days of the week on
which the holder of a Maryland Golden Age Pass may camp overnight in a State park
at a discounted fee; establishing that the free day–use entry applies to everyone in
the vehicle with the holder of a Maryland Golden Age Pass under certain
circumstances; and generally relating to the Maryland Golden Age Pass.
BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
Article – Natural Resources

Section 2:
Section 5–1009
Annotated Code of Maryland
(2023 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)

Section 3:
SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
Article – Natural Resources
5–1009.
(A) A person 62 years old or older who possesses a Maryland Golden Age Pass[,]
issued by the Maryland Park Service[, may]:
(1) IS ENTITLED TO FREE DAY–USE ENTRY TO ANY STATE PARK THAT
CHARGES SERVICE FEES;
(2) MAY camp overnight in any State park at one–half of the established
fee [Monday] SUNDAY through Thursday, excluding holidays; AND
(3) MAY LAUNCH A BOAT AT ANY STATE PARK WITHOUT PAYING A
BOAT LAUNCH FEE.
(B) IF A PER–VEHICLE FEE IS IN EFFECT AT A STATE PARK, THE FREE
DAY–USE ENTRY UNDER SUBSECTION (A)(1) OF THIS SECTION APPLIES TO
EVERYONE IN THE VEHICLE WITH THE HOLDER OF A MARYLAND GOLDEN AGE PASS.
– 1 –
Ch. 333 2026 LAWS OF MARYLAND

Section 4:
SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
October 1, 2026.
Approved by the Governor, April 28, 2026.
