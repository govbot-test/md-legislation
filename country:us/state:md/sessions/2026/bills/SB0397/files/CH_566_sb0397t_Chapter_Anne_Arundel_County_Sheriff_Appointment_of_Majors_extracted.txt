Section 1:
WES MOORE, Governor Ch. 566
Chapter 566
AN ACT concerning
Anne Arundel County – Sheriff – Appointment of Majors
FOR the purpose of requiring that the Sheriff of Anne Arundel County appoint a certain
number of majors at a salary determined by the County Council of Anne Arundel
County; and generally relating to the Office of the Sheriff of Anne Arundel County.
BY repealing and reenacting, without amendments,
Article – Courts and Judicial Proceedings

Section 2:
Section 2–315(a)
Annotated Code of Maryland
(2020 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
Article – Courts and Judicial Proceedings

Section 3:
Section 2–315(c)
Annotated Code of Maryland
(2020 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)

Section 4:
SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
Article – Courts and Judicial Proceedings
2–315.
(a) This section applies only in Anne Arundel County.
(c) (1) The Sheriff shall appoint deputies at a salary as provided by the County
Council of Anne Arundel County.
(2) The Sheriff may appoint a chief deputy who shall serve at the pleasure
of the Sheriff.
(3) THE SHERIFF SHALL APPOINT THREE MAJORS AT A SALARY AS
PROVIDED BY THE COUNTY COUNCIL OF ANNE ARUNDEL COUNTY.

Section 5:
SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
October 1, 2026.
Approved by the Governor, May 12, 2026.
