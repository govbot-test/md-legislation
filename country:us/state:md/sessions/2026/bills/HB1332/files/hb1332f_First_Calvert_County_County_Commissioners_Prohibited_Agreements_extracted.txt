HOUSE BILL 1332
L2 6lr3278
By: Delegate Fisher
Introduced and read first time: February 12, 2026
Assigned to: Government, Labor, and Elections
A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Calvert County – County Commissioners – Prohibited Agreements
3 FOR the purpose of prohibiting the County Commissioners of Calvert County from entering
4 into a nondisclosure agreement or a confidentiality agreement with another person;
5 and generally relating to nondisclosure agreements, confidentiality agreements, and
6 the County Commissioners of Calvert County.
7 BY adding to
8 The Public Local Laws of Calvert County
9 Section 2–202
10 Article 5 – Public Local Laws of Maryland
11 (2002 Edition and October 2024 Supplement, as amended)
12 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
13 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
14 Article 5 – Calvert County
15 2–202.
16 (A) THE COUNTY COMMISSIONERS, OR A PERSON ACTING ON BEHALF OF
17 THE COMMISSIONERS, MAY NOT ENTER INTO A NONDISCLOSURE AGREEMENT OR A
18 CONFIDENTIALITY AGREEMENT WITH ANOTHER PERSON.
19 (B) A PROVISION IN AN AGREEMENT THAT IS EXECUTED IN VIOLATION OF
20 THIS SECTION IS VOID AND UNENFORCEABLE AS CONTRARY TO PUBLIC POLICY.
21 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That a presently existing
22 agreement, obligation, or contract right may not be impaired in any way by this Act and
23 that this Act shall be construed to apply only prospectively and may not be applied or
