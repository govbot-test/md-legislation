4 FOR the purpose of altering the membership of the Oversight Committee on Quality of
5 Care in Nursing Homes and Assisted Living Facilities; and generally relating to the
6 Oversight Committee on Quality of Care in Nursing Homes and Assisted Living
7 Facilities.
8 BY repealing and reenacting, without amendments,
9 Article – Health – General
10 Section 19–1409(a)
11 Annotated Code of Maryland

15 Section 19–1409(b)

17 (2023 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)

20 Article – Health – General
21 19–1409.
22 (a) There is an Oversight Committee on Quality of Care in Nursing Homes and
23 Assisted Living Facilities.
24 (b) The Oversight Committee shall consist of the following members:

1 (iii) One representative of the Hospice Network of Maryland;

3 (v) One representative of 1199SEIU United Health Workers East;
4 (vi) One representative of the Maryland Chapter of AARP;

13 [(15)] (14) Three representatives from the assisted living industry, of which
14 one shall represent a program that cares for one to four residents, one shall represent a
15 program that cares for five to nine residents, and one shall represent a program that cares
16 for more than 10 residents.
17 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
18 October 1, 2026.
