A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Correctional Services – Maryland Correctional Enterprises – Report
3 FOR the purpose of requiring the Department of Public Safety and Correctional Services,
4 in collaboration with an institution of higher education in the State, to prepare a
5 report on correctional industry programs in certain states and recommend ways to
6 improve Maryland Correctional Enterprises to achieve successful reentry outcomes;
7 and generally relating to Maryland Correctional Enterprises.
8 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
9 That:
10 (a) (1) The Department of Public Safety and Correctional Services, in
11 collaboration with an institution of higher education in the State, shall provide a
12 comprehensive report on correctional industry programs in:
13 (i) Maine;
14 (ii) Ohio;
15 (iii) Kansas; and
16 (iv) California; and
17 (2) make recommendations regarding ways to improve Maryland
18 Correctional Enterprises to achieve successful reentry outcomes.
19 (b) (1) On or before December 1, 2026, the Department of Public Safety and
20 Correctional Services shall submit an interim report of its progress to the Governor and, in
21 accordance with § 2–1257 of the State Government Article, the General Assembly.
