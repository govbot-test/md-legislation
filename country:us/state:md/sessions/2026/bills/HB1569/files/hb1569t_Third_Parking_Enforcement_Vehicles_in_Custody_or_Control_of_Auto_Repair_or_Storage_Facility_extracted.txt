CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Parking Enforcement – Vehicles in Custody or Control of Auto Repair or
3 Storage Facility
4 FOR the purpose of establishing that if a parking citation is issued for a motor vehicle while
5 the motor vehicle is in the custody or control of an auto repair or storage facility, the
6 auto repair or storage facility, and not the registered owner, is liable for the parking
7 citation and any associated fines or late fees; prohibiting an auto repair or storage
8 facility from requiring a customer to waive the customer’s rights established under
9 this Act as a condition of service; and generally relating to parking citations issued
10 to motor vehicles in the custody or control of an auto repair or storage facility.

1 INDICATED.
2 (2) (I) “AUTO REPAIR OR STORAGE FACILITY” MEANS A PERSON
3 ENGAGED IN THE REPAIR, SERVICING, STORAGE, OR MAINTENANCE OF MOTOR
4 VEHICLES FOR COMPENSATION.
5 (II) “AUTO REPAIR OR STORAGE FACILITY” DOES NOT INCLUDE
6 A SELF–SERVICE STORAGE FACILITY, AS DEFINED IN § 18–501 OF THIS ARTICLE.
7 (3) “CUSTODY OR CONTROL” MEANS THE POSSESSION OF A MOTOR
8 VEHICLE UNDER A REPAIR ORDER, SERVICE AGREEMENT, OR OTHER
9 AUTHORIZATION FROM THE REGISTERED OWNER.
10 (B) (1) IF A PARKING CITATION IS ISSUED FOR A MOTOR VEHICLE WHILE
11 THE MOTOR VEHICLE IS IN THE CUSTODY OR CONTROL OF AN AUTO REPAIR OR
12 STORAGE FACILITY:
13 (I) THE REGISTERED OWNER OF THE MOTOR VEHICLE MAY NOT
14 BE HELD LIABLE FOR THE PARKING VIOLATION AND ANY ASSOCIATED FINE OR LATE
15 FEE ARISING FROM THE PARKING CITATION; AND
16 (II) THE AUTO REPAIR OR STORAGE FACILITY MAY BE HELD
17 LIABLE FOR THE PARKING VIOLATION.
18 (2) IT IS AN AFFIRMATIVE DEFENSE TO LIABILITY FOR A PARKING
19 CITATION IF THE REGISTERED OWNER OF THE MOTOR VEHICLE FOR WHICH THE
20 CITATION WAS ISSUED DEMONSTRATES THAT, AT THE TIME OF THE VIOLATION, THE
21 MOTOR VEHICLE WAS IN THE CUSTODY OR CONTROL OF AN AUTO REPAIR OR
22 STORAGE FACILITY.
23 (3) IF A REGISTERED OWNER IS FOUND TO BE NOT LIABLE FOR A
24 PARKING CITATION BY ASSERTING THE AFFIRMATIVE DEFENSE ESTABLISHED
25 UNDER PARAGRAPH (2) OF THIS SUBSECTION, A COURT SHALL IMMEDIATELY WAIVE
26 ALL FINES AND FEES AND DISMISS THE CITATION AGAINST THE REGISTERED
27 OWNER, REGARDLESS OF WHETHER LIABILITY IS TRANSFERABLE TO THE AUTO
28 REPAIR OR STORAGE FACILITY.
29 (C) IF SUFFICIENT EVIDENCE IS AVAILABLE TO INDICATE THAT A MOTOR
30 VEHICLE WAS IN THE CUSTODY OR CONTROL OF AN AUTO REPAIR OR STORAGE
31 FACILITY AT THE TIME THE PARKING ENFORCEMENT AGENCY ISSUED THE
32 CITATION, THE PARKING ENFORCEMENT AGENCY MAY REISSUE THE CITATION FOR
33 THE PARKING VIOLATION DIRECTLY TO THE AUTO REPAIR OR STORAGE FACILITY.

1 (D) AN AUTO REPAIR OR STORAGE FACILITY MAY NOT REQUIRE A
