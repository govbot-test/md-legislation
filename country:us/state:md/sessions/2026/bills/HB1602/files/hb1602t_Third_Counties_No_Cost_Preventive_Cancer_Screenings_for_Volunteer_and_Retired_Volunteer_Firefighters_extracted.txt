CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Counties – No–Cost Preventive Cancer Screenings for Volunteer and Retired
3 Volunteer Firefighters
4 FOR the purpose of requiring counties in which a volunteer fire company is located to offer
5 no–cost preventive cancer screenings in accordance with certain guidelines to
6 volunteer firefighters volunteering and retired volunteer firefighters who
7 volunteered with the volunteer fire company; authorizing a county to satisfy the
8 requirement by providing a certain annual examination or applying for a certain
9 grant; requiring the volunteer fire companies to maintain a certain list of volunteer
10 firefighters and retired volunteer firefighters and provide the list, annually and on
11 request, to the county in which the volunteer fire company is located; requiring the
12 counties to maintain the list provided; and generally relating to the provision of
13 no–cost preventive cancer screenings for volunteer and retired volunteer firefighters
14 by counties.
15 BY adding to
16 Article – Local Government
17 Section 9–115
18 Annotated Code of Maryland
19 (2013 Volume and 2025 Supplement)

2 HOUSE BILL 1602
1 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
2 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
3 Article – Local Government
4 9–115.
5 (A) (1) IN THIS SECTION THE FOLLOWING WORDS HAVE THE MEANINGS
6 INDICATED.
7 (2) “RETIRED VOLUNTEER FIREFIGHTER” MEANS AN INDIVIDUAL
8 WHO IS A RETIRED FIREFIGHTER WHO:
9 (I) VOLUNTEERED WITH A VOLUNTEER FIRE COMPANY IN A
10 COUNTY;
11 (II) WAS ACTIVELY INVOLVED IN FIRE SUPPRESSION WHILE
12 VOLUNTEERING WITH THE VOLUNTEER FIRE COMPANY;
13 (III) HAS COMPLETED A MINIMUM OF 3 YEARS OF VOLUNTEER
14 SERVICE; AND
15 (IV) ACCUMULATED AT LEAST 50 POINTS UNDER A LENGTH OF
16 SERVICE AWARD PROGRAM OR A POINT SYSTEM ESTABLISHED IN LIEU OF A LENGTH
17 OF SERVICE AWARD PROGRAM.
18 (2) (3) “VOLUNTEER FIRE COMPANY” INCLUDES A VOLUNTEER
19 FIRE DEPARTMENT.
20 (3) (4) “VOLUNTEER FIREFIGHTER” MEANS AN INDIVIDUAL WHO IS
21 A FIREFIGHTER ACTIVELY INVOLVED IN FIRE SUPPRESSION AND VOLUNTEERING
22 WITH A VOLUNTEER FIRE COMPANY IN A COUNTY.
23 (B) (1) EACH COUNTY IN WHICH A VOLUNTEER FIRE COMPANY IS
24 LOCATED SHALL OFFER NO–COST PREVENTIVE CANCER SCREENINGS IN
25 ACCORDANCE WITH THE LATEST SCREENING GUIDELINES ISSUED BY THE
26 INTERNATIONAL ASSOCIATION OF FIRE FIGHTERS TO EACH VOLUNTEER
27 FIREFIGHTER WHO VOLUNTEERS AND EACH RETIRED VOLUNTEER FIREFIGHTER
28 WHO VOLUNTEERED WITH THE VOLUNTEER FIRE COMPANY.
29 (2) EACH VOLUNTEER FIRE COMPANY SHALL:

HOUSE BILL 1602 3
1 (I) MAINTAIN AN UP–TO–DATE LIST OF VOLUNTEER
2 FIREFIGHTERS WHO ARE VOLUNTEERING AND RETIRED VOLUNTEER FIREFIGHTERS
3 WHO VOLUNTEERED WITH THE VOLUNTEER FIRE COMPANY; AND
4 (II) PROVIDE THE LIST TO THE COUNTY IN WHICH THE
5 VOLUNTEER FIRE COMPANY IS LOCATED:
6 1. ON OR BEFORE OCTOBER 1 EACH YEAR; AND
7 2. ON REQUEST OF THE COUNTY.
8 (3) EACH COUNTY IN WHICH A VOLUNTEER FIRE COMPANY IS
9 LOCATED SHALL MAINTAIN A LIST OF ALL VOLUNTEER FIREFIGHTERS WHO
10 VOLUNTEER AND ALL RETIRED VOLUNTEER FIREFIGHTERS WHO VOLUNTEERED IN
11 THE COUNTY AS PROVIDED BY EACH VOLUNTEER FIRE COMPANY LOCATED IN THE
12 COUNTY UNDER PARAGRAPH (2) OF THIS SUBSECTION.
13 (C) A COUNTY MAY SATISFY THE REQUIREMENT IN SUBSECTION (B)(1) OF
14 THIS SECTION BY:
15 (1) PROVIDING A NO–COST ANNUAL EXAMINATION TO VOLUNTEER
16 FIREFIGHTERS AND RETIRED VOLUNTEER FIREFIGHTERS THAT INCLUDES
17 PREVENTIVE CANCER SCREENINGS FOR FIREFIGHTERS IN ACCORDANCE WITH THE
18 LATEST SCREENING GUIDELINES ISSUED BY THE INTERNATIONAL ASSOCIATION OF
19 FIRE FIGHTERS; OR
20 (2) APPLYING FOR A GRANT UNDER TITLE 13, SUBTITLE 40 OF THE
21 HEALTH – GENERAL ARTICLE TO PAY FOR INNOVATIVE CANCER SCREENING
22 TECHNOLOGIES, INCLUDING A MULTICANCER EARLY DETECTION BLOOD TEST.
23 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect July
24 1, 2026.
