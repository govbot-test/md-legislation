CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Education – Purple Star Schools Program and Purple Star Colleges Program
3 FOR the purpose of altering the definition of “military–connected student” under the Purple
4 Star Schools Program to conform with certain terms defined under State law;
5 establishing the Purple Star Colleges Program to recognize certain colleges that
6 provide strong services and support for military–connected students; and generally
7 relating to the establishment of the Purple Star Colleges Program.

1 (C) THE COMMISSION SHALL ADMINISTER THE PROGRAM.
2 11–2003.
3 (A) SUBJECT TO SUBSECTION (B) OF THIS SECTION, THE COMMISSION
4 SHALL DESIGNATE A COLLEGE AS A PURPLE STAR COLLEGE IF THE COLLEGE
5 APPLIES AND MEETS THE CRITERIA ADOPTED BY THE COMMISSION FOR A PURPLE
6 STAR COLLEGE.
7 (B) AT A MINIMUM, THE COMMISSION’S CRITERIA FOR A PURPLE STAR
8 COLLEGE SHALL REQUIRE A COLLEGE TO:
9 (1) DESIGNATE A STAFF MEMBER AS A UNIFORMED SERVICES
10 LIAISON, WHOSE DUTIES SHALL INCLUDE:
11 (I) IDENTIFYING MILITARY–CONNECTED STUDENTS WHO ARE
12 NEWLY ENROLLED AT THE COLLEGE;
13 (II) SERVING AS THE POINT OF CONTACT BETWEEN THE
14 COLLEGE AND NEWLY ENROLLED MILITARY–CONNECTED STUDENTS;
15 (III) DETERMINING APPROPRIATE COLLEGE SERVICES
16 AVAILABLE TO NEWLY ENROLLED MILITARY–CONNECTED STUDENTS;
17 (IV) ASSISTING IN COORDINATING COLLEGE PROGRAMS FOR
18 NEWLY ENROLLED MILITARY–CONNECTED STUDENTS; AND
19 (V) GENERALLY ASSISTING IN A MILITARY–CONNECTED
20 STUDENT’S TRANSITION TO A NEW COLLEGE;
21 (2) ESTABLISH ON THE COLLEGE’S WEBSITE AN EASILY ACCESSIBLE
22 WEBPAGE THAT INCLUDES LINKS TO RESOURCES FOR MILITARY–CONNECTED
23 STUDENTS, INCLUDING LINKS TO INFORMATION REGARDING:
24 (I) RELOCATION TO, ENROLLMENT AT, REGISTRATION AT, AND
25 TRANSFERRING RECORDS TO THE COLLEGE;
26 (II) ACADEMIC PLANNING, COURSE SEQUENCES, AND
27 ADVANCED COURSES AVAILABLE AT THE COLLEGE; AND
28 (III) COUNSELING AND OTHER SUPPORT SERVICES AVAILABLE
29 FOR MILITARY–CONNECTED STUDENTS TRANSITIONING INTO THE COLLEGE;
