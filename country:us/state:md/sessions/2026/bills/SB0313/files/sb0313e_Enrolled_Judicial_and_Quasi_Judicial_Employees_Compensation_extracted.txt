1 AN ACT concerning
2 Courts – Administrative Judges – Annual Stipend
3 Judicial and Quasi–Judicial Employees – Compensation
4 FOR the purpose of authorizing the Chief Judge of the Appellate Court of Maryland and a
5 judge who serves as an administrative judge for a circuit court or the District Court
6 to receive an annual stipend in addition to the judge’s annual salary; altering the
7 compensation of the Chairman of the Workers’ Compensation Commission to be at
8 least equal to the salary of the Chief Judge of the District Court; and generally
9 relating to the compensation of administrative judges certain judicial and
10 quasi–judicial employees.
11 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
12 Article – Courts and Judicial Proceedings
13 Section 1–702
14 Annotated Code of Maryland

13 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
14 Article – Courts and Judicial Proceedings
15 1–702.
16 (a) (1) Subject to the provisions of § 1–701 of this subtitle, a justice or judge
17 shall have the salary provided in the State budget.
18 (2) THE CHIEF JUDGE OF THE APPELLATE COURT OF MARYLAND
19 AND A JUDGE WHO SERVES AS AN ADMINISTRATIVE JUDGE FOR A CIRCUIT COURT
20 OR THE DISTRICT COURT MAY RECEIVE AN ANNUAL STIPEND IN ADDITION TO THE
21 JUDGE’S ANNUAL SALARY.
22 (b) The Chief Judge of the District Court, during the period he THE INDIVIDUAL
23 serves as Chief Judge, shall have a salary equivalent to the annual salary then payable to
24 an associate judge of the Appellate Court of Maryland.
25 Article – Labor and Employment
26 9–101.
27 (a) In this title the following words have the meanings indicated.
28 (d) “Commission” means the State Workers’ Compensation Commission.
29 9–304.
30 (b) (1) Each member of the Commission is entitled to:
31 (i) the salary provided in the State budget; and
SENATE BILL 313 3
1 (ii) reimbursement for expenses under the Standard State Travel
2 Regulations, as provided in the State budget.
3 (2) The annual salary of a member of the Commission:
4 (i) shall at least equal the salary provided in the State budget for a
5 judge of the district court; and
6 (ii) for the Chairman, shall [be at least $1,500 more than the salary
7 of the other members] AT LEAST EQUAL THE SALARY OF THE CHIEF JUDGE OF THE
8 DISTRICT COURT.
9 (3) Except as provided in paragraphs (1) and (2) of this subsection, a
10 member of the Commission may not receive a fee or perquisite for the discharge of any duty
11 of office.
12 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect July
13 1, 2026.
Approved:
