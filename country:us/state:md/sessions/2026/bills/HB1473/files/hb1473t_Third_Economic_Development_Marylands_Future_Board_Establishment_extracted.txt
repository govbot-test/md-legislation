CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Economic Development – Maryland’s Future Board – Establishment
3 FOR the purpose of establishing the Maryland’s Future Board under the Department of
4 Commerce to develop, evaluate, and revise a certain visionary plan for the future of
5 Maryland by a certain date and make recommendations for projects based on the
6 plan to the Governor and the General Assembly; establishing the Maryland’s Future
7 Fund as a special, nonlapsing fund; requiring interest earnings of the Fund to be
8 credited to the Fund; and generally relating to the Maryland’s Future Board.
9 BY adding to
10 Article – Economic Development
11 Section 2.5–301 through 2.5–305 to be under the new subtitle “Subtitle 3. Maryland’s
12 Future Board”

16 Preamble
17 WHEREAS, Maryland is rich in assets, often ranking among the top in the nation
18 for its innovation, technology–based workforce, personal income levels, and research and
19 development activity; and
20 WHEREAS, Maryland, despite these significant competitive advantages, lags
21 behind the nation in economic growth, population growth, and personal income growth; and
22 WHEREAS, In 2025, Maryland’s gross domestic product growth was 3.8%, compared
23 to the national rate of 4.4%; and
24 WHEREAS, In 2025, Maryland’s personal income growth was 2.8%, compared to the
25 national rate of 3.3%; and
26 WHEREAS, In 2025, Maryland’s population growth over the precious 5 years was
27 1.4%, compared to the national rate of 2.8%; and
28 WHEREAS, Demographic changes, population shifts, and uncertainty at the federal
29 level, on which much of Maryland’s economy is dependent, makes planning for the future
30 more challenging; and
31 WHEREAS, It is incumbent on Maryland to plan for the future with intention and
32 purpose to ensure all residents have a more ideal place to work, learn, and live; and
33 WHEREAS, Priorities are often aimed at solving pressing and immediate needs,
34 however, best practices show that visionary planning is the key to producing better

7 2.5–301.
8 (A) IN THIS SUBTITLE THE FOLLOWING WORDS HAVE THE MEANINGS
9 INDICATED.
10 (B) “BOARD” MEANS THE MARYLAND’S FUTURE BOARD.
11 (C) “FUND” MEANS THE MARYLAND’S FUTURE FUND.
12 (D) “PROJECT” INCLUDES:
13 (1) AN ALLOCATION OF FUNDS IN THE OPERATING BUDGET OF THE
14 STATE;
15 (2) AN ALLOCATION OF FUNDS IN THE CAPITAL BUDGET OF THE
16 STATE; AND
17 (3) THE IMPLEMENTATION OF ANY POLICY THAT WILL ADVANCE THE
18 STATE’S SOCIOECONOMIC WELLBEING AND RESILIENCY.
19 (E) “VISIONARY PLAN” MEANS A PLAN THAT ENVISIONS A STATE THAT IS
20 EQUITABLE, PROSPEROUS, LIVABLE, AND PROVIDES OPPORTUNITIES FOR ALL
21 RESIDENTS TO THRIVE, INCLUDING:
22 (1) A FORWARD–THINKING STRATEGY THAT WOULD INCORPORATE
23 CORE VALUES AND PRINCIPLES AS ENVISIONED BY THE BOARD;
24 (2) A STRATEGY THAT COVERS BROADLY ISSUES THAT THE STATE
25 FACES;
26 (3) A STRATEGY THAT IS NOT CONSTRAINED BY EXISTING
27 TECHNOLOGY, INFRASTRUCTURE, OR FISCAL CONDITIONS OF THE STATE; AND
