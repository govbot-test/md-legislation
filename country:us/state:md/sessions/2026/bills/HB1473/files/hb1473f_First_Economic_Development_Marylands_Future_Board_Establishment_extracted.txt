A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Economic Development – Maryland’s Future Board – Establishment
3 FOR the purpose of establishing the Maryland’s Future Board under the Department of
4 Commerce to develop, evaluate, and revise a certain visionary plan for the future of
5 Maryland by a certain date and make recommendations for projects based on the
6 plan to the Governor and the General Assembly; establishing the Maryland’s Future
7 Fund as a special, nonlapsing fund; requiring interest earnings of the Fund to be
8 credited to the Fund; and generally relating to the Maryland’s Future Board.

11 Preamble
12 WHEREAS, Maryland is rich in assets, often ranking among the top in the nation
13 for its innovation, technology–based workforce, personal income levels, and research and
14 development activity; and
15 WHEREAS, Maryland, despite these significant competitive advantages, lags
16 behind the nation in economic growth, population growth, and personal income growth; and
17 WHEREAS, In 2025, Maryland’s gross domestic product growth was 3.8%, compared
18 to the national rate of 4.4%; and
19 WHEREAS, In 2025, Maryland’s personal income growth was 2.8%, compared to the
20 national rate of 3.3%; and
21 WHEREAS, In 2025, Maryland’s population growth over the precious 5 years was
22 1.4%, compared to the national rate of 2.8%; and
23 WHEREAS, Demographic changes, population shifts, and uncertainty at the federal
24 level, on which much of Maryland’s economy is dependent, makes planning for the future
25 more challenging; and
26 WHEREAS, It is incumbent on Maryland to plan for the future with intention and
27 purpose to ensure all residents have a more ideal place to work, learn, and live; and
28 WHEREAS, Priorities are often aimed at solving pressing and immediate needs,
29 however, best practices show that visionary planning is the key to producing better
30 outcomes, creating competitive advantages, and encouraging sustainable growth; now,
31 therefore,
32 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
33 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
