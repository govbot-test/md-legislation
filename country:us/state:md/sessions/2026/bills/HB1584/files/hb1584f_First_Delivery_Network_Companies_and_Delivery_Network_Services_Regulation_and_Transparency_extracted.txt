A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Delivery Network Companies and Delivery Network Services – Regulation and
3 Transparency
4 FOR the purpose of establishing a framework for the regulation of delivery network
5 companies and delivery network services in the State, including establishing
6 permitting and licensing requirements, digital network requirements, reporting
7 requirements, and payment, earnings, and receipt requirements; and generally
8 relating to delivery network companies and delivery network services.
9 BY adding to
10 Article – Public Utilities
11 Section 10A–101 through 10A–109 to be under the new title “Title 10A. Delivery
12 Network Services”
13 Annotated Code of Maryland
14 (2025 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
15 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
16 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
17 Article – Public Utilities
18 TITLE 10A. DELIVERY NETWORK SERVICES.
19 10A–101.
20 (A) IN THIS TITLE THE FOLLOWING WORDS HAVE THE MEANINGS
21 INDICATED.

2 HOUSE BILL 1584
1 (B) “DELIVERY NETWORK COMPANY” MEANS A COMPANY THAT OPERATES
2 IN THE STATE USING A DIGITAL NETWORK TO FACILITATE DELIVERY NETWORK
3 SERVICES WITHIN THE STATE ON AN ON–DEMAND BASIS.
4 (C) “DELIVERY NETWORK OPERATOR” OR “OPERATOR” MEANS AN
5 INDIVIDUAL WHO:
6 (1) HAS BEEN ISSUED A DELIVERY NETWORK OPERATOR’S LICENSE
7 OR IS OTHERWISE AUTHORIZED BY THE COMMISSION TO PROVIDE DELIVERY
8 NETWORK SERVICES;
9 (2) RECEIVES, THROUGH A DELIVERY NETWORK COMPANY’S DIGITAL
10 NETWORK APPLICATION OR PLATFORM, A CONNECTION TO A POTENTIAL CUSTOMER
11 TO FULFILL A REQUEST FOR DELIVERY NETWORK SERVICES IN EXCHANGE FOR THE
12 PAYMENT OF A FEE TO THE DELIVERY NETWORK COMPANY; AND
13 (3) USES A MOTOR VEHICLE THAT IS OWNED, LEASED, OR OTHERWISE
14 AUTHORIZED FOR USE BY THE INDIVIDUAL AND IS APPROVED FOR USE IN
15 PROVIDING DELIVERY NETWORK SERVICES BY THE COMMISSION.
16 (D) “DELIVERY NETWORK SERVICES” MEANS AN OPERATOR’S COLLECTION
17 AND DELIVERY OF ITEMS TO A CUSTOMER FOR ITEMS THAT:
18 (1) ARE REQUESTED BY A CUSTOMER THROUGH A DELIVERY
19 NETWORK COMPANY’S DIGITAL NETWORK; AND
20 (2) THE COLLECTION AND DELIVERY OF WHICH ARE FACILITATED BY
21 A DELIVERY NETWORK COMPANY.
22 (E) “ON–DEMAND BASIS” MEANS A DELIVERY NETWORK OPERATOR IS
23 PROVIDED WITH THE OPPORTUNITY TO ACCEPT OR REJECT A REQUEST FOR
24 DELIVERY NETWORK SERVICES.

27 DELIVERY NETWORK SERVICES.

4 (A) OF THIS SECTION SHALL PROVIDE FOR:
5 (1) THE ACCESSIBILITY OF WEBSITES AND MOBILE APPLICATIONS
6 USED TO PROVIDE DELIVERY NETWORK SERVICES;
7 (2) THE REASONABLE ACCOMMODATIONS NECESSARY TO SERVE
8 INDIVIDUALS WITH DISABILITIES; AND
9 (3) THE PROVISION OF INFORMATION TO AN OPERATOR BY A
10 DELIVERY NETWORK COMPANY RELATED TO THE REQUIREMENTS OF FEDERAL AND
11 STATE LAWS RELATED TO ANTIDISCRIMINATION.
12 10A–104.
13 A DELIVERY NETWORK COMPANY MAY NOT OPERATE IN THE STATE UNLESS
14 THE COMMISSION HAS ISSUED A PERMIT TO THE DELIVERY NETWORK COMPANY.
