1 AN ACT concerning
2 Nurse Licensure – Educational Requirements – Enforcement
3 FOR the purpose of requiring certain applicants for a license to practice registered nursing
4 or licensed practical nursing to produce certain evidence that the applicants’
5 educational credentials meet certain requirements; authorizing the State Board of
6 Nursing to take certain disciplinary action for the failure to complete certain
7 education requirements; and generally relating to enforcement of educational
8 requirements by the State Board of Nursing.

8 (a) Subject to the provisions of this section, the Board may issue a license by
9 endorsement and waive any appropriate examination requirement of this title for an
10 applicant who has an active unencumbered license to practice registered nursing or
11 licensed practical nursing in any other state or country.
12 (b) The Board may issue a license by endorsement under this section only if the
13 applicant:
14 (1) Submits to the Board an application on the form that the Board
15 requires;
16 (2) Submits to a criminal history records check in accordance with § 8–303
17 of this subtitle;
18 (3) Pays the application fee set by the Board under § 8–304 of this subtitle;
19 and
20 (4) (i) 1. A. Holds a valid, unencumbered multistate license
21 under the Nurse Licensure Compact; AND
22 B. IF REQUIRED BY THE BOARD, PROVIDES ADEQUATE
23 EVIDENCE, INCLUDING OFFICIAL TRANSCRIPTS, THAT THE APPLICANT’S
24 EDUCATIONAL CREDENTIALS MEET THE REQUIREMENTS ESTABLISHED UNDER §
25 8–302 OF THIS SUBTITLE; or
26 2. Except as provided in subsection (c) of this section,
27 provides adequate evidence that:
28 A. At the time the applicant graduated from a nursing
29 education program approved in the other state or country, the program was substantially
30 equivalent to the education program approved in this State; and
31 B. At the time the applicant became licensed or registered in
32 the other state or country, the applicant passed in that or any other state or country an
33 examination that was similar to the examination that then was given in this State; and
