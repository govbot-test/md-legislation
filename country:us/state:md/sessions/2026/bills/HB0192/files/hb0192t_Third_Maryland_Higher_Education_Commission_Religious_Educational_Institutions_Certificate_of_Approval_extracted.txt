1 10–101.
2 (a) In this division the following words have the meanings indicated.
3 (c) “Commission” means the Maryland Higher Education Commission.
4 11–202.1.
5 (b) (1) Subject to the requirements imposed by this section, and except as
6 prohibited in paragraph (2) of this subsection, a religious educational institution may
7 operate without a certificate of approval from the Commission and may enroll Maryland
8 students in a fully online distance education program in the State without a registration
9 from the Commission if the institution:
10 (i) Is established for religious educational purposes;
11 (ii) Provides educational programs only for religious vocations or
12 purposes;
13 (iii) Offers only sectarian instruction designed for and aimed at
14 individuals who hold or seek to learn the particular religious faiths or beliefs taught by the
15 institution; and
16 (iv) States on the certificate or diploma the religious nature of the
17 award.
18 (2) (I) THIS PARAGRAPH DOES NOT APPLY TO A RELIGIOUS
19 EDUCATIONAL INSTITUTION THAT WAS AUTHORIZED TO OPERATE IN THE STATE
20 AND HAS BEEN IN CONTINUOUS OPERATION IN THE STATE SINCE BEFORE JANUARY
21 1, 2013.
22 (II) A religious educational institution that is accredited by an
23 accrediting body recognized by the United States Department of Education may not operate
24 without a certificate of approval from the Commission.
