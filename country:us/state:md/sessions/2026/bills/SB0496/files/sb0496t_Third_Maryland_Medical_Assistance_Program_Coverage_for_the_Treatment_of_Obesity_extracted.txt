1 AN ACT concerning
2 Maryland Medical Assistance Program – Coverage for the Treatment of Obesity
3 FOR the purpose of authorizing, beginning on a certain date, the Maryland Medical
4 Assistance Program to provide comprehensive coverage for the treatment of obesity;
5 requiring the Maryland Department of Health to provide notice to Program
6 recipients if the Department elects to provide the coverage; and generally relating to
7 the Maryland Medical Assistance Program and coverage for the treatment of obesity.

2 SENATE BILL 496
1 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
2 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
3 Article – Health – General
4 15–103.
5 (a) (1) The Secretary shall administer the Maryland Medical Assistance
6 Program.
7 (2) The Program:
8 (xxvii) Beginning on January 1, 2026, if providing coverage for
9 the delivery of anesthesia, shall provide coverage for the delivery of anesthesia in
10 accordance with § 15–862 of the Insurance Article; [and]
11 (xxviii) Beginning on January 1, 2026, shall provide calcium score
12 testing in accordance with § 15–863 of the Insurance Article; AND
13 (XXIX) BEGINNING ON JANUARY 1, 2027, MAY PROVIDE
14 COMPREHENSIVE COVERAGE FOR THE TREATMENT OF OBESITY UNDER § 15–160 OF
15 THIS SUBTITLE.
16 15–160.
17 (A) IN THIS SECTION, “COMPREHENSIVE COVERAGE FOR THE TREATMENT
18 OF OBESITY” INCLUDES COVERAGE FOR INTENSIVE BEHAVIORAL THERAPY,
19 BARIATRIC SURGERY, AND ANY MEDICATION APPROVED BY THE FEDERAL FOOD
20 AND DRUG ADMINISTRATION WITH AN INDICATION FOR CHRONIC WEIGHT
21 MANAGEMENT IN PATIENTS WITH OBESITY.
22 (B) THE PROGRAM MAY PROVIDE COMPREHENSIVE COVERAGE FOR THE
23 TREATMENT OF OBESITY.
24 (C) THE PROGRAM MAY UNDERTAKE UTILIZATION MANAGEMENT TO
25 DETERMINE THE MEDICAL NECESSITY AND APPROPRIATENESS FOR TREATMENT OF
26 OBESITY UNDER THIS SECTION IF THE DETERMINATIONS ARE MADE IN THE SAME
27 MANNER AS DETERMINATIONS ARE MADE FOR THE TREATMENT OF ANY OTHER
28 ILLNESS, CONDITION, OR DISORDER COVERED BY THE PROGRAM.
29 (D) IF THE DEPARTMENT ELECTS TO PROVIDE COMPREHENSIVE COVERAGE
30 FOR THE TREATMENT OF OBESITY UNDER THIS SECTION, THE DEPARTMENT SHALL
31 PROVIDE NOTICE TO PROGRAM RECIPIENTS.

SENATE BILL 496 3
1 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That, on or before November 1,
2 2027, the Maryland Department of Health shall report to the Senate Finance Committee,
3 the Senate Budget and Taxation Committee, the House Health Committee, and the House
4 Appropriations Committee, in accordance with § 2–1257 of the State Government Article,
5 on whether the Maryland Medical Assistance Program has begun to provide comprehensive
6 coverage for the treatment of obesity as authorized under § 15–160 of the Health – General
7 Article, as enacted by Section 1 of this Act, and if not, why the Department has not begun
8 to provide coverage.
9 SECTION 3. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
10 October 1, 2026.
