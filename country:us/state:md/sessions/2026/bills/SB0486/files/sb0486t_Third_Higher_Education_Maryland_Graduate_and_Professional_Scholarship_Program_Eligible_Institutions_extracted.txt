1 AN ACT concerning
2 Higher Education – Maryland Graduate and Professional Scholarship
3 Program – Eligible Institutions
4 FOR the purpose of expanding eligibility under the Maryland Graduate and Professional
5 Scholarship Program to include certain students attending the University of
6 Maryland Eastern Shore; and generally relating to the Maryland Graduate and
7 Professional Scholarship Program.
8 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
9 Article – Education
10 Section 18–2601
11 Annotated Code of Maryland
12 (2022 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
13 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
14 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
15 Article – Education
16 18–2601.
17 (a) In cooperation with the institutions of higher education in the State, the
18 Commission shall establish and administer a Graduate and Professional Scholarship
19 Program to provide financial assistance to full–time and part–time students in the fields of
20 medicine, dentistry, law, pharmacy, nursing, social work, and veterinary medicine.

1 [(vii)] (VIII) Any institution of higher education in the State offering a
2 master’s degree in social work; or

5 (c) A graduate and professional scholarship award for a single school year may
6 not be less than $1,000 or more than $5,000.
7 (d) A recipient may not receive an award for longer than 8 semesters.
8 (e) (1) The Commission shall allocate grants for the Program to the
9 institutions of higher education specified in subsection (b)(3) of this section based on the
10 proportion of State residents enrolled in eligible programs at each institution.
11 (2) An institution of higher education that receives a grant under this
12 section shall provide the Commission with an annual audit describing the disposition of the
13 funds.
14 (f) The Commission shall:
15 (1) Establish guidelines for the awarding of graduate and professional
16 scholarships by the institutions of higher education to the students; and
17 (2) Adopt regulations necessary for the implementation of this section.
18 (g) Funds for the Maryland Graduate and Professional Scholarship Program shall
19 be as provided in the State budget.
20 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect July
