1 AN ACT concerning
2 State Police Retirement System – Mandatory Retirement Age – Alteration
3 FOR the purpose of increasing the age at which certain members of the State Police
4 Retirement System are required to retire; increasing the age at which certain
5 retirees must end participation in the Deferred Retirement Option Program;
6 authorizing certain individuals, subject to certain limitations and requirements, to
7 elect to extend their participation in the Deferred Retirement Option Program; and
8 generally relating to the mandatory retirement age for certain members of the State
9 Police Retirement System and participation in the Deferred Retirement Option
10 Program.
