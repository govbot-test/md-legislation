1 (3) THE SALARY OF EACH REGISTER SHALL BE DETERMINED BY THE
2 BOARD OF PUBLIC WORKS.
3 [(2)] (4) In determining the annual salary of the register, the Board of
4 Public Works shall be guided in the exercise of its discretion by:
5 (i) The population of the county determined by the last official
6 United States census;
7 (ii) The dollar volume of total fees and taxes collected and excess fees
8 turned over to the State for each of the preceding 5 years by the office of the register for
9 which the salary is being fixed; and
10 (iii) Other pertinent data which have relation to the reasonableness
11 of the salary in relation to the work done and volume handled by the office.
12 (c) [The minimum annual salary for the register in Baltimore City is $12,000.
13 (d)] (1) The salaries of the registers shall be paid semimonthly from the fees
14 and receipts of the office, after deducting the expenses of the office.
15 (2) Expenses include salaries of deputies and clerks, books, stationery,
16 office supplies, and other necessary and customary expenses of doing business.
17 [(e)] (D) (1) (i) If the fees and receipts of the office are insufficient in any
18 fiscal year to pay all or a part of the expenses of the office and authorized salary of a
19 register, the deficiency shall be funded from the taxes remitted to the Comptroller by the
20 register during that fiscal year.
21 (ii) Written authority for the transfer of funds shall be first obtained
22 from the Comptroller.
23 (2) In the event that tax collections for the fiscal year are insufficient, the
24 Comptroller shall make up the deficit from excess fees remitted from all other registers.
25 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That, in accordance with Article
26 III, § 35 of the Maryland Constitution, this Act may not be construed to extend or apply to
27 the salary or compensation of a clerk of a circuit court or a register of wills while serving in
28 a term of office beginning before the effective date of this Act, but the provisions of this Act
29 concerning the salary or compensation of a clerk of the circuit court or a register of wills
30 shall take effect at the beginning of the next following term of office. This limitation does
31 not apply to an individual appointed or elected after the effective date of this Act to fill out
32 an unexpired term.
