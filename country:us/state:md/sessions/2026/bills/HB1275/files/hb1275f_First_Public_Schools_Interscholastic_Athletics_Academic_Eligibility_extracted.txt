F1 6lr3431
By: Delegates Wims and Coley
Introduced and read first time: February 12, 2026
Assigned to: Ways and Means
A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Public Schools – Interscholastic Athletics – Academic Eligibility
3 FOR the purpose of requiring the standards of participation for student participation in
4 interscholastic athletics established by a county board of education to include a
5 process for a student to appeal a determination that the student is academically
6 ineligible to participate in interscholastic athletics; establishing certain
7 requirements for a certain appeal process, including a certain provisional eligibility
8 period; and generally relating to public school interscholastic athletics.
9 BY adding to
10 Article – Education
11 Section 7–137
12 Annotated Code of Maryland
13 (2025 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
14 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
15 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
16 Article – Education
17 7–137.
18 (A) (1) THIS SECTION APPLIES ONLY TO THE ACADEMIC ELIGIBILITY OF
19 A PUBLIC SCHOOL STUDENT TO PARTICIPATE IN INTERSCHOLASTIC ATHLETICS.
20 (2) NOTHING IN THIS SECTION MAY BE CONSTRUED TO PROHIBIT A
21 SCHOOL FROM:
22 (I) SUSPENDING OR EXPELLING A STUDENT; OR
EXPLANATION: CAPITALS INDICATE MATTER ADDED TO EXISTING LAW.
[Brackets] indicate matter deleted from exist in g l aw . *hb1275*
2 HOUSE BILL 1275
1 (II) PROHIBITING STUDENT PARTICIPATION IN
2 INTERSCHOLASTIC ATHLETICS DUE TO A VIOLATION OF AN ESTABLISHED
3 ATTENDANCE POLICY.
4 (B) (1) THE STANDARDS OF PARTICIPATION FOR STUDENT
5 PARTICIPATION IN INTERSCHOLASTIC ATHLETICS ESTABLISHED BY A COUNTY
6 BOARD SHALL INCLUDE A PROCESS FOR A STUDENT TO APPEAL A PUBLIC SCHOOL’S
7 DETERMINATION THAT THE STUDENT IS ACADEMICALLY INELIGIBLE TO
8 PARTICIPATE.
9 (2) AN APPEAL PROCESS REQUIRED UNDER PARAGRAPH (1) OF THIS
10 SUBSECTION SHALL INCLUDE:
11 (I) AN INTERNAL SCHOOL PROCESS TO APPEAL A
12 DETERMINATION OF ACADEMIC INELIGIBILITY, INCLUDING A HEARING;
13 (II) WRITTEN NOTICE OF:
14 1. THE DETERMINATION AND THE BASIS FOR THE
15 DETERMINATION;
16 2. THE PROCESS FOR AN INTERNAL SCHOOL APPEAL,
17 INCLUDING RELEVANT TIMELINES AND DEADLINES; AND
18 3. THE EARLIEST AVAILABLE HEARING DATE AND TIME;
19 AND
20 (III) ON RECEIPT OF NOTICE OF A STUDENT’S INTENT TO
21 APPEAL, A PROVISIONAL ELIGIBILITY PERIOD UNTIL THE EARLIER OF:
22 1. THE CONCLUSION OF THE INTERNAL SCHOOL
23 APPEAL; OR
24 2. 10 SCHOOL DAYS.
25 (C) DURING A PROVISIONAL ELIGIBILITY PERIOD OF THE APPEALS
26 PROCESS UNDER SUBSECTION (B) OF THIS SECTION, A STUDENT:
27 (1) MAY PARTICIPATE IN INTERSCHOLASTIC ATHLETICS, INCLUDING
28 PRACTICE AND COMPETITION; AND
29 (2) MAY NOT BE CONSIDERED AN INELIGIBLE STUDENT FOR
30 PURPOSES OF INTERSCHOLASTIC ATHLETICS RULES OR REGULATIONS.
HOUSE BILL 1275 3
1 (D) IF A PUBLIC SCHOOL UPHOLDS A DETERMINATION OF ACADEMIC
2 INELIGIBILITY TO PARTICIPATE IN INTERSCHOLASTIC ATHLETICS AFTER AN
3 APPEAL, THE STUDENT’S ACADEMIC INELIGIBILITY SHALL TAKE EFFECT THE NEXT
4 SCHOOL DAY.
5 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect July
6 1, 2026.
