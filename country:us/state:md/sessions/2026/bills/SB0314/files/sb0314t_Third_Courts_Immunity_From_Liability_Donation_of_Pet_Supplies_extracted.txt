1 AN ACT concerning
2 Courts – Immunity From Liability – Donation of Pet Supplies
3 FOR the purpose of establishing a certain immunity from liability for a person who donates
4 a pet item or dispenses a donated pet item; authorizing the State Department of
5 Agriculture to inspect donated commercial feed and establish procedures for
6 handling donated commercial feed; and generally relating to civil liability and the
7 donation of pet items.

2 SENATE BILL 314
1 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
2 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
3 Article – Agriculture
4 1–101.
5 (a) In this article the following words have the meanings indicated.
6 (d) “Department” means the State Department of Agriculture.
7 (g) “Person” includes the State, any county, municipal corporation or other
8 political subdivision of the State, or any of their units, or an individual, receiver, trustee,
9 guardian, executor, administrator, fiduciary, or representative of any kind, or any
10 partnership, firm, association, public or private corporation, or any other entity, unless
11 otherwise provided.
12 6–101.
13 (a) In this subtitle the following words have the meanings indicated.
14 (d) “Commercial feed” means a material or combination of materials distributed,
15 or intended for distribution, for use as feed, or for mixing in feed for any animal other than
16 man including feed prepared and distributed for consumption by dogs and cats, or any
17 domesticated animal normally maintained in a cage or tank, including gerbils, hamsters,
18 canaries, psittacine birds, mynahs, finches, tropical fish, goldfish, snakes, and turtles,
19 except:
20 (1) Unmixed whole seeds and physically altered entire unmixed seeds that
21 are not chemically altered or adulterated;
22 (2) Commodities such as hay, straw, stover, silage, cobs, husks, hulls, and
23 individual chemical compounds or substances when the commodities, compounds, or
24 substances are not intermixed with other materials or adulterated; or
25 (3) A material or combination of materials that is exempt from this
26 definition in regulations adopted by the Secretary.
27 6–106.1.
28 (A) THE DEPARTMENT MAY:
29 (1) INSPECT FOR WHOLESOMENESS ANY COMMERCIAL FEED
30 DONATED TO A NONPROFIT CORPORATION, ORGANIZATION, OR ASSOCIATION; AND

SENATE BILL 314 3
1 (2) ESTABLISH PROCEDURES FOR HANDLING COMMERCIAL FEED
2 DONATED TO ANY NONPROFIT CORPORATION, ORGANIZATION, OR ASSOCIATION.
3 (B) A PERSON SHALL HAVE THE IMMUNITY FROM LIABILITY UNDER §
4 5–634.1 OF THE COURTS ARTICLE FOR ANY ACT OR OMISSION THAT AFFECTS THE
5 NATURE, AGE, CONDITION, OR PACKAGING OF THE DONATED COMMERCIAL FEED.
6 Article – Courts and Judicial Proceedings
7 5–634.1.
8 (A) (1) IN THIS SECTION THE FOLLOWING WORDS HAVE THE MEANINGS
9 INDICATED.
10 (2) “PERSON” INCLUDES THE STATE, ANY COUNTY, MUNICIPAL
11 CORPORATION OR OTHER POLITICAL SUBDIVISION OF THE STATE, OR ANY OF THEIR
12 UNITS, OR AN INDIVIDUAL, A RECEIVER, A TRUSTEE, A GUARDIAN, AN EXECUTOR, AN
13 ADMINISTRATOR, A FIDUCIARY, OR A REPRESENTATIVE OF ANY KIND, OR ANY
14 PARTNERSHIP, FIRM, ASSOCIATION, PUBLIC OR PRIVATE CORPORATION, OR OTHER
15 ENTITY.
16 (3) (I) “PET ITEM” MEANS ANY APPARENTLY FIT TANGIBLE PET
17 SUPPLY THAT MEETS ALL QUALITY AND LABELING STANDARDS IMPOSED BY
18 FEDERAL, STATE, AND LOCAL LAWS AND REGULATIONS EVEN IF THE ITEM MAY NOT
19 BE READILY MARKETABLE DUE TO APPEARANCE, AGE, FRESHNESS, GRADE,
20 SURPLUS, OR OTHER CONDITION.
21 (II) “PET ITEM” INCLUDES APPARENTLY FIT COMMERCIAL
22 FEED AS DEFINED IN § 6–101 OF THE AGRICULTURE ARTICLE.
23 (B) UNLESS THE ACT OR OMISSION AMOUNTS TO GROSS NEGLIGENCE OR
24 WILLFUL AND WANTON MISCONDUCT, A PERSON IS NOT CIVILLY LIABLE FOR ANY
25 ACT OR OMISSION THAT AFFECTS THE NATURE, AGE, CONDITION, OR PACKAGING OF
26 A DONATED PET ITEM IF THE PERSON IN GOOD FAITH:
27 (1) DONATED THE PET ITEM FOR USE OR DISTRIBUTION BY A
28 NONPROFIT CORPORATION, ORGANIZATION, OR ASSOCIATION OR A UNIT OF STATE
29 OR LOCAL GOVERNMENT; OR
30 (2) DISPENSED THE DONATED PET ITEM ON BEHALF OF A NONPROFIT
31 CORPORATION, ORGANIZATION, OR ASSOCIATION OR A UNIT OF STATE OR LOCAL
32 GOVERNMENT.
