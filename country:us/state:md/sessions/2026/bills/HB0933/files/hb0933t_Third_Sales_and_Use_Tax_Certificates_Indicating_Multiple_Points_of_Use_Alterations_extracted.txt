CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Sales and Use Tax – Certificates Indicating Multiple Points of Use – Alterations
3 FOR the purpose of providing that authorizing, for purposes of certain laws governing the
4 sales and use tax, a buyer to issue a certain certificate indicating multiple points of
5 use of certain digital codes, digital products, or taxable services shall be deemed fully
6 completed under certain circumstances and subject to certain limitations; providing
7 that a buyer is not required to obtain prior approval from the Comptroller before
8 presenting a certificate indicating multiple points of use to a vendor; requiring the
9 Comptroller to relieve a vendor of the obligation to collect, pay, or remit certain sales
10 and use tax under certain circumstances; providing that a certificate indicating
11 multiple points of use shall remain in effect for certain future sales except under
12 certain circumstances; requiring the Comptroller to publish a certain form for a
13 certificate indicating multiple points of use in a certain manner; applying this Act
14 retroactively; providing for the application for and denial, renewal, and revocation of
15 an authorization for a buyer to issue multiple points of use certificates; providing for
16 the validity and rescission of multiple points of use certificates; requiring an
17 authorized buyer to provide and a vendor to verify a multiple points of use certificate
18 in a certain manner; and generally relating to the sales and use tax and certificates
19 indicating multiple points of use.
20 BY repealing and reenacting, without amendments,
21 Article – Tax – General
22 Section 11–403(a)
23 Annotated Code of Maryland
24 (2022 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
