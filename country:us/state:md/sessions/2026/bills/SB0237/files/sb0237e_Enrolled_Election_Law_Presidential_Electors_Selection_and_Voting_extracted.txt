1 AN ACT concerning
2 Election Law – Presidential Electors – Selection and Voting
3 FOR the purpose of altering the procedures for the selection of presidential elector
4 nominees and alternate presidential elector nominees; requiring the State
5 Administrator of Elections to preside and serve as secretary at a certain meeting of

7 electors; establishing procedures for the preparation, delivery, signing, and
