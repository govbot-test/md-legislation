Chapter 324
AN ACT concerning
Baltimore City – Natural Resources – Sunday Deer Hunting and Archery
Hunting Safety Zones
FOR the purpose of authorizing the Department of Natural Resources to allow a person to
hunt deer on each Sunday of the deer bow hunting season in Baltimore City; adding
Baltimore City to the list of jurisdictions in which the safety zone for archery hunters
extends a certain distance from certain structures; and generally relating to hunting
in Baltimore City.
BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
Article – Natural Resources
