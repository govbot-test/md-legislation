1 AN ACT concerning
2 Economic Development – Maryland Aerospace and Technology Commission –
3 Alterations
4 FOR the purpose of expanding the purpose of the Maryland Aerospace and Technology
5 Commission by promoting innovation in space science, space technology, and
6 aeronautics; altering the membership, duties, and staff responsibilities of the
7 Commission; requiring the Commission to appoint an Executive Director;
8 authorizing the Commission to provide grants to certain entities for certain
9 purposes; and generally relating to the Maryland Aerospace and Technology
10 Commission.
