CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Business Regulation – Pet Cremation and Burial Services – Requirements
3 (Pet Cremation and Burial Services Consumer Protection Act)
4 FOR the purpose of establishing certain consumer protection requirements for registered
5 cemeterians, registered cemetery operators, or and certain permit holders and
6 licensees that sell or offer for sale pet cremation services of pet remains; establishing
7 the duties of certain persons responsible for returning pet cremains to certain animal
8 owners after cremation; and generally relating to the cremation of pet remains.
