F1 6lr2219
SB 818/25 – EEE CF 6lr2151
By: Senators Watson and West
Introduced and read first time: January 27, 2026
Assigned to: Education, Energy, and the Environment
A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Education – School Building Energy Usage – Monthly Report
3 FOR the purpose of requiring each county board of education to collect data on energy usage
4 and report the data collected to the Interagency Commission on School Construction;
5 requiring the Commission to adopt regulations specifying the data to be collected
6 and the means and frequency of reporting; requiring the Commission to analyze the
7 data collected and provide recommendations for improving school building energy
8 efficiency and usage; and generally relating to school building energy usage.
9 BY adding to
10 Article – Education
11 Section 5–331
12 Annotated Code of Maryland
13 (2025 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
14 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
15 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
16 Article – Education
17 5–331.
18 (A) EACH COUNTY BOARD SHALL:
19 (1) COLLECT DATA ON SCHOOL BUILDING ENERGY USAGE; AND
20 (2) REPORT THE DATA COLLECTED TO THE INTERAGENCY
21 COMMISSION.
22 (B) THE INTERAGENCY COMMISSION SHALL ADOPT REGULATIONS THAT
EXPLANATION: CAPITALS INDICATE MATTER ADDED TO EXISTING LAW.
