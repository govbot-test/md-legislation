Section 1:
WES MOORE, Governor Ch. 543
Chapter 543
AN ACT concerning
Economic Development – Rural Readiness Program and Rural Maryland
Capacity Building Fund – Establishment
FOR the purpose of establishing the Rural Readiness Program administered by the Rural
Maryland Council to provide assistance to rural communities to enhance their
capacity for economic development; requiring priority to be given when making
certain grants to entities that have completed the Rural Readiness Program;
establishing the Rural Maryland Capacity Building Fund as a special, nonlapsing
fund to be administered by the Council to provide grants to support certain planning
and capacity building initiatives; and generally relating to rural communities.
