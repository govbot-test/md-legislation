2 SENATE BILL 783
1 (a) (1) In this section the following words have the meanings indicated.
2 (2) “Merging credit union” means a credit union that is absorbed or
3 acquired by another credit union in a merger and ceases to exist after the merger.
4 (3) “New credit union” means a credit union that is created when two or
5 more credit unions consolidate to form a newly created credit union.
6 (4) “Surviving credit union” means a credit union that absorbs or acquires
7 another credit union in a merger and continues to exist after the merger.
8 (b) (1) (i) With the approval of the Commissioner, any credit union may
9 merge or consolidate as provided in this section.
10 (ii) A merger or consolidation under this section may be with a credit
11 union organized under the laws of the United States, this State, or any other state.
12 (d) (1) A majority of the board of each credit union proposing a [merger or]
13 consolidation shall:
14 [(1)] (I) Adopt a resolution that declares that the [merger or]
15 consolidation is advisable; and
16 [(2)] (II) Set a date for a vote on the proposed [merger or] consolidation
17 by the members of each credit union party to the [merger or] consolidation at any annual
18 or special meeting of the membership or by mail ballot to be filed on or before that date.
19 (2) (I) A MAJORITY OF THE BOARD OF EACH CREDIT UNION
20 PROPOSING A MERGER SHALL ADOPT A RESOLUTION THAT DECLARES THAT THE
21 MERGER IS ADVISABLE;
22 (II) A MAJORITY OF THE BOARD OF EACH MERGING CREDIT
23 UNION PROPOSING THE MERGER SHALL SET A DATE FOR A VOTE ON THE PROPOSED
24 MERGER BY THE MEMBERS OF EACH MERGING CREDIT UNION AT ANY ANNUAL OR
25 SPECIAL MEETING OF THE MEMBERSHIP OR BY MAIL BALLOT TO BE FILED ON OR
26 BEFORE THAT DATE; AND
27 (III) A MAJORITY OF THE BOARD OF THE SURVIVING CREDIT
28 UNION PROPOSING THE MERGER MAY SET A DATE FOR A VOTE ON THE PROPOSED
29 MERGER BY THE MEMBERS OF THE SURVIVING CREDIT UNION AT ANY ANNUAL OR
30 SPECIAL MEETING OF THE MEMBERSHIP OR BY MAIL BALLOT TO BE FILED ON OR
31 BEFORE THAT DATE.
