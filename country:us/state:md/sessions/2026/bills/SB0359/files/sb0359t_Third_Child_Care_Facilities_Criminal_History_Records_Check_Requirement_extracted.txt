CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Child Care Facilities – Criminal History Records Check – Requirement
3 FOR the purpose of repealing the limitation on the number of Deputy State
4 Superintendents of Schools that the State Superintendent can appoint; requiring
5 individuals who will have direct contact with children in certain child care facilities
6 to submit to a criminal history records check; requiring the State Department of
7 Education to establish a centralized unit for the processing and management of
8 information about criminal history records checks on or before a certain date;
9 altering the applicability of probationary employment qualifications for first–time
10 child care teachers in certain child care centers; and generally relating to a criminal
11 history records check for individuals who will have direct contact with children in
12 child care facilities.
