1 AN ACT concerning
2 Task Force on Education Funding and Student Population Growth
3 FOR the purpose of establishing the Task Force on Education Funding and Student
4 Population Growth to analyze challenges related to student enrollment counts,
5 student population growth, and education funding; and generally relating to the
6 Task Force on Education Funding and Student Population Growth.
7 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
8 That:
9 (a) There is a Task Force on Education Funding and Student Population Growth.
10 (b) The Task Force consists of the following members:
11 (1) one member of the Senate of Maryland, appointed by the President of
12 the Senate;
13 (2) one member of the House of Delegates, appointed by the Speaker of the
14 House; and
15 (3) the following members, appointed by the Governor:
16 (i) one representative of the State Department of Education;
