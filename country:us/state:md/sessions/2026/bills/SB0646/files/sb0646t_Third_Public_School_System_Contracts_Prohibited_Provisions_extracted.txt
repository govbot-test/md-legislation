CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Public School System Contracts – Prohibited Provisions
3 FOR the purpose of prohibiting certain provisions from being included in certain public
4 school system contracts; specifying that certain provisions are void if included in a
5 public school system contract; specifying the applicable law and interpretation of
6 public school system contracts; and generally relating to prohibited provisions in
7 public school system contracts.
8 BY adding to
9 Article – Education
10 Section 4–148
11 Annotated Code of Maryland
12 (2025 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
13 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
14 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
15 Article – Education
16 4–148.
17 (A) IN THIS SECTION, “PUBLIC SCHOOL SYSTEM CONTRACT” MEANS ANY
18 AGREEMENT ENTERED INTO BY A PUBLIC SCHOOL SYSTEM.

2 SENATE BILL 646
1 (B) THIS SECTION DOES NOT APPLY TO COLLECTIVE BARGAINING
2 AGREEMENTS ENTERED INTO UNDER:
3 (1) TITLE 6, SUBTITLES 4 AND 5 OF THIS ARTICLE;
4 (2) TITLE 9.5, SUBTITLE 7 OF THIS ARTICLE; OR
5 (3) TITLE 22 OF THE STATE GOVERNMENT ARTICLE.
6 (B) (C) EXCEPT AS REQUIRED BY STATE OR FEDERAL LAW, A PUBLIC
7 SCHOOL SYSTEM CONTRACT MAY NOT INCLUDE A PROVISION THAT:
8 (1) REQUIRES THE PUBLIC SCHOOL SYSTEM TO INDEMNIFY, DEFEND,
9 OR HOLD HARMLESS ANOTHER PERSON WITHOUT AN APPROPRIATION OF FUNDS
10 FOR THAT PURPOSE;
11 (2) REQUIRES THE PUBLIC SCHOOL SYSTEM TO AGREE TO BINDING
12 ARBITRATION OR ANY OTHER BINDING EXTRAJUDICIAL DISPUTE RESOLUTION
13 PROCESS;
14 (3) NAMES A JURISDICTION OR VENUE FOR ANY ACTION OR DISPUTE
15 AGAINST THE PUBLIC SCHOOL SYSTEM OTHER THAN A COURT OF PROPER
16 JURISDICTION IN THE STATE;
17 (4) REQUIRES THE PUBLIC SCHOOL SYSTEM TO AGREE TO LIMIT THE
18 LIABILITY FOR ANY DIRECT LOSS TO THE PUBLIC SCHOOL SYSTEM FOR BODILY
19 INJURY, DEATH, OR DAMAGE TO PROPERTY OF THE PUBLIC SCHOOL SYSTEM
20 CAUSED BY THE NEGLIGENCE, INTENTIONAL OR WILLFUL MISCONDUCT,
21 FRAUDULENT ACT, RECKLESSNESS, OR OTHER TORTIOUS CONDUCT OF A PERSON OR
22 A PERSON’S EMPLOYEES OR AGENTS OR A PROVISION THAT WOULD OTHERWISE
23 IMPOSE AN INDEMNIFICATION OBLIGATION ON THE PUBLIC SCHOOL SYSTEM;
24 (5) REQUIRES THE PUBLIC SCHOOL SYSTEM TO BE BOUND BY A TERM
25 OR CONDITION THAT:
26 (I) IS UNKNOWN TO THE PUBLIC SCHOOL SYSTEM AT THE TIME
27 OF SIGNING A CONTRACT;
28 (II) MAY BE UNILATERALLY CHANGED BY THE OTHER PARTY;
29 OR
30 (III) IS ELECTRONICALLY ACCEPTED BY A PUBLIC SCHOOL
31 SYSTEM EMPLOYEE WITHOUT AUTHORITY;

SENATE BILL 646 3
1 (6) RESTRICTS A PUBLIC SCHOOL SYSTEM’S DISCRETION TO SELECT
2 THE APPROPRIATE LEGAL COUNSEL TO REPRESENT ITS INTERESTS;
3 (7) IS INCONSISTENT WITH THE PUBLIC SCHOOL SYSTEM’S
4 OBLIGATIONS UNDER TITLE 3 OR TITLE 4 OF THE GENERAL PROVISIONS ARTICLE;
5 (8) CREATES A DEFICIENCY, INCURS A LIABILITY, OR SPENDS MONEY
6 IN EXCESS OF THE APPROPRIATION, OR BINDS A PUBLIC SCHOOL SYSTEM TO PAY AN
7 AMOUNT UNLESS FUNDS HAVE BEEN APPROPRIATED FOR THAT PURPOSE;
8 (9) REQUIRES AUTOMATIC RENEWAL OF THE CONTRACT AND
9 OBLIGATES THE PUBLIC SCHOOL SYSTEM TO ALLOCATE FUNDING IN SUBSEQUENT
10 FISCAL YEARS; OR
11 (10) LIMITS THE PUBLIC SCHOOL SYSTEM’S ABILITY TO RECOVER A
12 DIFFERENCE IN THE COST OF A REPLACEMENT CONTRACTOR TO PERFORM THE
13 SERVICES NOT PERFORMED BY THE ORIGINAL CONTRACTOR.
14 (C) (D) IF A PUBLIC SCHOOL SYSTEM CONTRACT CONTAINS A PROVISION
15 LISTED UNDER SUBSECTION (B) (C) OF THIS SECTION, THE PROVISION IS VOID AB
16 INITIO AND THE CONTRACT CONTAINING THAT PROVISION SHALL BE ENFORCEABLE
17 AS IF IT DID NOT CONTAIN THE PROVISION.
18 (D) (E) A PUBLIC SCHOOL SYSTEM CONTRACT THAT CONTAINS A
19 PROVISION LISTED UNDER SUBSECTION (B) (C) OF THIS SECTION SHALL BE
20 GOVERNED BY AND CONSTRUED IN ACCORDANCE WITH STATE LAW,
21 NOTWITHSTANDING ANY TERM OR CONDITION TO THE CONTRARY IN THE
22 CONTRACT.
23 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall be construed to
24 apply only prospectively and may not be applied or interpreted to have any effect on or
25 application to any public school system contract executed before the effective date of this
26 Act or to the renewal or extension of a public school system contract executed before the
27 effective date of this Act.
28 SECTION 3. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect July
29 1, 2026.
