A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Criminal Law – Child Sexual Abuse Material – Artificial Intelligence Software
3 FOR the purpose of altering the term “child pornography” to be “child sexual abuse
4 material” for purposes of certain criminal offenses; defining “computer–generated
5 image” to include images created through the use of artificial intelligence software
6 as the term pertains to provisions of law related to child sexual abuse material; and
7 generally relating to child sexual abuse material.
