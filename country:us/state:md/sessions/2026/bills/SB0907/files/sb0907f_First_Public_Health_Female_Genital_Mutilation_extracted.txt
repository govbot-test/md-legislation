A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Public Health – Female Genital Mutilation
3 FOR the purpose of altering the definition of “abuse” to include female genital mutilation
4 for the purposes of a provision of law requiring certain persons to provide notice of
5 suspected abuse or neglect of a child or make a written report of suspected abuse or
6 neglect of a child; altering the actions regarding female genital mutilation in which
7 a person is prohibited from engaging; increasing the penalties for a violation of
8 certain provisions related to female genital mutilation; requiring, under certain
9 circumstances, a health occupations licensing board to revoke the license of an
10 individual who commits a violation of certain provisions of this Act; authorizing an
11 individual who is subject to female genital mutilation to bring a civil action for
12 certain relief; requiring the Maryland Department of Health, in collaboration with
13 certain government agencies and public and private organizations, to develop,
14 publish, and distribute certain educational materials regarding female genital
15 mutilation; and generally relating to female genital mutilation.
