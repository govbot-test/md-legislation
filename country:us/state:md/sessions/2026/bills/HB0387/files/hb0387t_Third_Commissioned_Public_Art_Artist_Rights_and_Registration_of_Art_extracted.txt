CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Commissioned Public Art – Artist Rights and Registration of Art
3 FOR the purpose of prohibiting an entity from including in a contract for certain State
4 public art a provision requiring artists to waive certain rights; requiring certain
5 State public art to be registered with the Maryland State Arts Council; requiring the
6 Council to establish, maintain, and update a certain database of certain art on its
7 website; and generally relating to artist rights and State public art.
8 BY adding to
9 Article – State Finance and Procurement
10 Section 13–230
11 Annotated Code of Maryland
12 (2021 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
13 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
14 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
15 Article – State Finance and Procurement
16 13–230.
17 (A) IN THIS SECTION, “STATE PUBLIC ART” MEANS PUBLIC ART
18 COMMISSIONED FOR A PROJECT THAT RECEIVES STATE FUNDING, REGARDLESS OF
19 WHETHER THE STATE FUNDING DIRECTLY PAID FOR THE PUBLIC ART.

2 HOUSE BILL 387
1 (B) (1) AN ENTITY MAY NOT INCLUDE IN A CONTRACT FOR STATE PUBLIC
2 ART A REQUIREMENT THAT AN ARTIST WAIVE COPYRIGHT PROTECTIONS OR RIGHTS
3 UNDER 17 U.S.C. § 106A.
4 (2) NOTWITHSTANDING PARAGRAPH (1) OF THIS SUBSECTION, AND
5 SUBJECT TO PARAGRAPH (3) OF THIS SUBSECTION, AN ARTIST MAY CHOOSE TO
6 WAIVE THE ARTIST’S COPYRIGHT PROTECTIONS OR RIGHTS UNDER 17 U.S.C. § 106A
7 IN A CONTRACT FOR STATE PUBLIC ART.
8 (3) A WAIVER DESCRIBED IN PARAGRAPH (2) OF THIS SUBSECTION
9 MUST BE IN WRITING.
10 (C) AN ENTITY THAT COMMISSIONS STATE PUBLIC ART SHALL REGISTER
11 THE ART WITH THE MARYLAND STATE ARTS COUNCIL, INCLUDING:
12 (1) THE NAME AND CONTACT INFORMATION OF THE ARTIST;
13 (2) THE LOCATION OF THE ART;
14 (3) THE TYPE OF ART;
15 (4) THE TITLE OF THE ART; AND
16 (5) THE DATE OF INSTALLATION OR CREATION.
17 (D) THE MARYLAND STATE ARTS COUNCIL SHALL ESTABLISH, MAINTAIN,
18 AND UPDATE A PUBLICLY AVAILABLE DATABASE OF ART REGISTERED UNDER
19 SUBSECTION (C) OF THIS SECTION ON ITS WEBSITE.
20 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall be construed to
21 apply only prospectively and may not be applied or interpreted to have any effect on or
22 application to any contract entered into before the effective date of this Act.
23 SECTION 3. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
24 October 1, 2026.
