1 AN ACT concerning
2 Public Health – Expedited Partner Therapy – Bacterial Vaginosis and Licensed
3 Certified Midwives
4 FOR the purpose of adding bacterial vaginosis to the list of diagnoses for which certain
5 health care providers practitioners may prescribe, dispense, or otherwise provide
6 antibiotic therapy to a sexual partner of a diagnosed patient without making a
7 certain assessment of the sexual partner; adding licensed certified midwives to the
8 list of health care practitioners who may prescribe, dispense, or otherwise provide
9 antibiotic therapy to a sexual partner of a diagnosed patient without making a
10 certain assessment of the sexual partner; and generally relating to expedited partner
11 therapy.

2 (a) The purpose of expedited partner therapy is to provide antibiotic therapy to
3 any partner of a patient diagnosed with a sexually transmitted infection AN INFECTION
4 TRANSMITTED THROUGH SEXUAL ACTIVITY identified in subsection (b) of this section in

6 (1) Contain and stop the further spread of the infection; and

12 partner:
13 (1) A physician licensed under Title 14 of the Health Occupations Article;

15 licensed under Title 8 of the Health Occupations Article acting in accordance with § 8–508
