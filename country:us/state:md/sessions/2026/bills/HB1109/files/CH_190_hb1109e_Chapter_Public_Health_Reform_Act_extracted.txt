WES MOORE, Governor Ch. 190
Chapter 190
AN ACT concerning
Public Health Reform Act
FOR the purpose of requiring the Maryland Department of Health to organize the
Maryland Medical Reserve Corps; requiring the Maryland Department of Health to
notify each health officer for a county of a certain procurement to offer the
opportunity for the county health department to join the procurement; authorizing
a health officer for a county to make an appointment to a position without the
approval of the Maryland Department of Health or the Department of Budget and
Management if the position is fully funded without State funding; authorizing a
health officer for a county to enter into certain contracts if the Secretary of Health
does not respond to a request for approval within a certain time period; authorizing
the Secretary of Health and, under certain circumstances, the Deputy Secretary for
Public Health to delegate the authority to approve certain contracts authorizing a
health officer for a county to enter into certain contracts if the Secretary of Health does
not respond to a request for approval within a certain time period; altering the
membership, leadership selection process, and staffing of the Commission on Public
Health on Public Health; requiring the Commission on Public Health on Public
Health to provide oversight over the implementation of recommendations made by
the Commission on Public Health; establishing the Public Health Workforce
Development Fund; requiring that interest earnings of the Public Health Workforce
Development Fund remain in the Public Health Workforce Development Fund;
authorizing the Maryland Department of Health and the Health Services Cost Review
Commission to transfer money from the Population Health Improvement Fund to the
Public Health Workforce Development Fund in a manner consistent with a certain
agreement; on Public Health; establishing the Public Health Workforce Development
Fund; requiring that interest earnings of the Public Health Workforce Development
Fund remain in the Public Health Workforce Development Fund; authorizing the
Maryland Department of Health and the Health Services Cost Review Commission
to transfer money from the Population Health Improvement Fund to the Public
Health Workforce Development Fund; requiring the Department of Legislative
Services to develop a process to assess the health equity impacts of relevant
legislation; altering the membership of the Maryland Corps Program Advisory
Board; requiring the Commission on Public Health to establish a Community
Benefits Modernization subcommittee; requiring the Maryland Department of
Health to convene a certain workgroup; and generally relating to public health.
BY repealing and reenacting, without amendments,
Article – Health – General
