A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Homeschool Students in Maryland – Right to Play
3 FOR the purpose of authorizing a public high school to allow certain homeschool students
4 to participate in athletic activities sponsored by the school under certain
5 circumstances; authorizing certain homeschool students to participate in an athletic
6 activity sponsored by certain public high schools, subject to certain requirements;
7 requiring that certain liability insurance cover certain homeschool students under
8 certain circumstances; and generally relating to homeschool students and athletic
9 activities sponsored by public high schools.

11 AN ATHLETIC ACTIVITY SPONSORED BY THE SCHOOL TO THE SAME EXTENT THAT A

13 ACTIVITY.
14 (2) A PUBLIC HIGH SCHOOL SHALL GIVE PLACEMENT PRIORITY TO A
15 STUDENT ENROLLED IN THE PUBLIC HIGH SCHOOL SPONSORING THE ATHLETIC
16 ACTIVITY OVER A STUDENT WHO IS NOT ENROLLED IN THE PUBLIC HIGH SCHOOL IF
17 THERE ARE A LIMITED NUMBER OF SPACES AVAILABLE FOR STUDENTS IN THE
18 ATHLETIC ACTIVITY.
19 (C) (1) A HOMESCHOOL STUDENT MAY PARTICIPATE IN AN ATHLETIC
20 ACTIVITY SPONSORED:
21 (I) BY THE PUBLIC HIGH SCHOOL THE HOMESCHOOL STUDENT
22 WOULD BE ASSIGNED TO ATTEND IF THE HOMESCHOOL STUDENT ATTENDED PUBLIC
23 SCHOOL; OR
24 (II) FOR A HOMESCHOOL STUDENT WHO LIVES IN AN AREA THAT
25 DOES NOT ASSIGN STUDENTS TO A SPECIFIC SCHOOL, BY A PUBLIC HIGH SCHOOL
26 THAT IS GEOGRAPHICALLY PROXIMATE WITH THE HOMESCHOOL STUDENT’S
27 RESIDENCE AS DETERMINED BY THE RELEVANT COUNTY BOARD.
28 (2) IF THE PUBLIC HIGH SCHOOL AT WHICH A HOMESCHOOL STUDENT
29 MAY PARTICIPATE IN AN ATHLETIC ACTIVITY DOES NOT OFFER THE ATHLETIC

31 HOMESCHOOL STUDENT MAY USE THE SAME METHODS THAT A STUDENT ENROLLED
32 IN THE PUBLIC HIGH SCHOOL USES TO PARTICIPATE IN ANOTHER PUBLIC HIGH

1 (3) A STUDENT ENROLLED IN A PUBLIC HIGH SCHOOL WHO
2 WITHDRAWS FROM THE PUBLIC HIGH SCHOOL TO BEGIN A HOME INSTRUCTION
3 PROGRAM IS NOT ELIGIBLE TO PARTICIPATE IN AN ATHLETIC ACTIVITY SPONSORED
4 BY ANY PUBLIC HIGH SCHOOL IF THE STUDENT, WHILE ENROLLED IN THE PUBLIC
5 HIGH SCHOOL, WAS SUBJECT TO DISCIPLINARY ACTION THAT WOULD BAR
6 PARTICIPATION IN THE ATHLETIC ACTIVITY.
7 (D) A HOMESCHOOL STUDENT WHO PARTICIPATES IN AN ATHLETIC
8 ACTIVITY SPONSORED BY A PUBLIC HIGH SCHOOL IS:
9 (1) SUBJECT TO:
10 (I) THE SAME INSURANCE, HEALTH, AND IMMUNIZATION
11 REQUIREMENTS AND EXEMPTIONS AS A STUDENT ENROLLED IN THE PUBLIC HIGH
12 SCHOOL; AND
13 (II) EXCEPT FOR ACADEMIC RULES, THE SAME CODES OF
14 CONDUCT AND SAFETY RULES AS A STUDENT ENROLLED IN THE PUBLIC HIGH
15 SCHOOL; AND
16 (2) RESPONSIBLE FOR TRANSPORTATION TO AND FROM THE PUBLIC
17 HIGH SCHOOL.
18 (E) (1) IF A PUBLIC HIGH SCHOOL HAS LIABILITY INSURANCE FOR
19 ATHLETIC ACTIVITIES, THE LIABILITY INSURANCE SHALL COVER HOMESCHOOL
20 STUDENTS PARTICIPATING IN AN ATHLETIC ACTIVITY SPONSORED BY THE PUBLIC
21 HIGH SCHOOL TO THE SAME EXTENT AS THE COVERAGE FOR STUDENTS ENROLLED
22 IN THE PUBLIC HIGH SCHOOL.
23 (2) A PUBLIC HIGH SCHOOL MAY REQUIRE A HOMESCHOOL STUDENT
24 PARTICIPATING IN AN ATHLETIC ACTIVITY SPONSORED BY THE PUBLIC HIGH
25 SCHOOL TO TAKE THE FOLLOWING ACTIONS IF THE PUBLIC HIGH SCHOOL REQUIRES
26 STUDENTS ENROLLED IN THE PUBLIC HIGH SCHOOL TO TAKE THE SAME ACTIONS:
27 (I) PAY INSURANCE PREMIUMS;
28 (II) SIGN WAIVERS; OR
29 (III) SHOW PROOF OF LIABILITY INSURANCE.
30 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect July
31 1, 2026.
