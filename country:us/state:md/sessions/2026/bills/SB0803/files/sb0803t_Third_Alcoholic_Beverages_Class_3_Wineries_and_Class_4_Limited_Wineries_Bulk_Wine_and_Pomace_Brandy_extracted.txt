1 AN ACT concerning
2 Alcoholic Beverages – Class 3 Wineries and Class 4 Limited Wineries – Bulk
3 Wine and Pomace Brandy
4 FOR the purpose of expanding the definition of “pomace brandy” authorized to be produced
5 and sold by the holder of a Class 4 limited winery license; authorizing the holder of
6 a Class 3 winery license to acquire bulk wine from the holder of a Class 4 limited
7 winery license; authorizing the holder of a Class 4 limited winery license to sell and
8 deliver bulk wine to the holder of a Class 3 winery license; clarifying that the holder
9 of a Class 4 limited winery license must report all pomace brandy distilled and
10 bottled by the holder as a distilled spirit for purposes of the alcoholic beverage tax;
11 and generally relating to Class 3 wineries and Class 4 limited wineries.
