1 AN ACT concerning
2 Baltimore City – 40th Alcoholic Beverages District – Class B Beer, Wine, and
3 Liquor Licenses Authorizations and Revisions
4 FOR the purpose of authorizing the Board of License Commissioners for Baltimore City to
5 issue Class B beer, wine, and liquor licenses for certain locations in the 40th alcoholic
6 beverages district, subject to certain limitations; prohibiting certain license holders
7 in a certain area of the 40th alcoholic beverages district from selling alcoholic
8 beverages at certain times of day; and generally relating to alcoholic beverages
9 licenses in Baltimore City.
10 BY repealing and reenacting, without amendments,
11 Article – Alcoholic Beverages and Cannabis
12 Section 12–102
13 Annotated Code of Maryland
14 (2024 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)

4 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
5 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
6 Article – Alcoholic Beverages and Cannabis
7 12–102.
8 This title applies only in Baltimore City.
9 12–1603.
10 (c) The Board may issue:
11 (13) [if the applicant executes a memorandum of understanding with the
12 Bolton Hill Community Association,] a Class C beer, wine, and liquor license in the 1200
13 block of Eutaw Place in the 40th alcoholic beverages district IF:
14 (I) THE APPLICANT EXECUTES A MEMORANDUM OF
15 UNDERSTANDING WITH THE BOLTON HILL COMMUNITY ASSOCIATION; AND
16 (II) THE BOARD WAIVES ANY REQUIREMENTS THAT SALES MAY
17 BE MADE ONLY TO MEMBERS AND GUESTS OF MEMBERS;
18 (25) a Class B beer, wine, and liquor license for a restaurant on the even
19 side of the unit block of South Carrollton Avenue in the 40th alcoholic beverages district if:
20 (i) the applicant executes a memorandum of understanding with the
21 Hollins Roundhouse Association; and
22 (ii) the Board waives the minimum seating requirement under item
23 (1)(ii)2 of this subsection; [and]
24 (26) a Class C beer, wine, and liquor license on the odd side of the 800 block
25 of North Howard Street in the 40th alcoholic beverages district if:
26 (i) the applicant does not convert the license to a different license
27 class;
28 (ii) the applicant does not transfer the license to a different location;
29 (iii) alcoholic beverages are served only to patrons indoors;
SENATE BILL 972 3
1 (iv) alcoholic beverages sales begin not earlier than 10 a.m. and end
2 not later than 9 p.m.;
3 (v) the applicant provides not more than two 12–ounce offerings of
4 beer, two 6–ounce offerings of wine by the glass, or two 1.5–ounce offerings of liquor for
5 on–premises consumption by a patron while the patron is provided a barbering service
6 regulated under Title 4, Subtitle 5 of the Business Occupations and Professions Article;
7 (vi) the Board waives any requirements that sales can be made only
8 to members and the guests of members; and
9 (vii) the applicant executes a memorandum of understanding with the
10 Mount Vernon – Belvedere Association;
11 (27) A CLASS B BEER, WINE, AND LIQUOR LICENSE FOR A RESTAURANT
12 ON THE ODD SIDE OF THE 1000 BLOCK OF WEST BALTIMORE STREET IN THE 40TH
13 ALCOHOLIC BEVERAGES DISTRICT IF:
14 (I) THE APPLICANT EXECUTES A MEMORANDUM OF
15 UNDERSTANDING WITH THE HOLLINS ROUNDHOUSE ASSOCIATION;
16 (II) THE APPLICANT DOES NOT TRANSFER THE LICENSE TO A
17 DIFFERENT LOCATION OR TO ANOTHER PERSON; AND
18 (III) THE BOARD WAIVES THE MINIMUM SEATING REQUIREMENT
19 UNDER ITEM (1)(II)2 OF THIS SUBSECTION; AND
20 (28) A CLASS B BEER, WINE, AND LIQUOR LICENSE FOR A RESTAURANT
21 ON THE EVEN SIDE OF THE 800 BLOCK OF NORTH CHARLES STREET IN THE 40TH
22 ALCOHOLIC BEVERAGES DISTRICT IF:
23 (I) THE APPLICANT EXECUTES A MEMORANDUM OF
24 UNDERSTANDING WITH THE MOUNT VERNON – BELVEDERE ASSOCIATION; AND
25 (II) THE BOARD WAIVES THE MINIMUM SEATING REQUIREMENT
26 UNDER ITEM (1)(II)2 OF THIS SUBSECTION.
27 12–2005.
28 (d) (1) This subsection does not apply to:
29 (i) a Class B beer and light wine (restaurant) license;
30 (ii) a Class B beer, wine, and liquor (restaurant) license issued under
31 § 12–1603(c)(1)(ii) of this title;

1 (iii) a Class B–BWL (H–M) license;
2 (iv) a Class C beer and wine license;
3 (v) a Class C beer, wine, and liquor license;
4 (vi) an arena license;
5 (vii) a marketplace license; or
6 (viii) a Class M–F (municipal family fun center) license.
7 (2) For a license holder in the 40th alcoholic beverages district, the hours
8 of sale for alcoholic beverages may not begin before 10 a.m. or end after 10 p.m.:
9 (i) within an area bounded:
10 1. from the intersection of Reisterstown Road and Quantico
11 Avenue, northwest on Reisterstown Road to the intersection with Wylie Avenue, northeast
12 on Wylie Avenue to the intersection with Pimlico Road, northwest on Pimlico Road to the
13 intersection with Dupont Avenue, east on Dupont Avenue to the intersection with Pall Mall

15 on Edgecombe Circle North to the intersection with Finney Avenue, south on Finney

17 intersection with Pall Mall Road, southeast on Pall Mall Road to the intersection with
18 Loyola Northway, southwest on Loyola Northway to the intersection with Pimlico Road,
19 southeast on Pimlico Road to the intersection with Quantico Avenue, and southwest on
20 Quantico Avenue to the intersection with Reisterstown Road; and
21 2. from the intersection of [Millington Avenue] BRUNSWICK
22 STREET with Wilkens Avenue, northwest on [Millington Avenue] BRUNSWICK STREET
23 to the intersection with Frederick Avenue, northeast on Frederick Avenue to the
24 intersection with [West Pratt Street] SOUTH FRANKLINTOWN ROAD, NORTHWEST ON
25 SOUTH FRANKLINTOWN ROAD TO THE INTERSECTION WITH HOLLINS STREET, east
26 on [West Pratt Street] HOLLINS STREET to the intersection with South Mount Street,
27 south on South Mount Street to the intersection with Eagle Street, southwest on Eagle
28 Street to the intersection with South Smallwood Street, northwest on South Smallwood
29 Street to the intersection with Wilkens Avenue, and southwest on THE EVEN SIDE OF
30 Wilkens Avenue to the intersection with [Millington Avenue] BRUNSWICK STREET;
31 (ii) within the area of West North Avenue between the 1000 block of
32 West North Avenue and the 3200 block of West North Avenue, inclusive;
33 (iii) for a license holder within 250 yards in any direction of the area
34 described in item (ii) of this paragraph; and
