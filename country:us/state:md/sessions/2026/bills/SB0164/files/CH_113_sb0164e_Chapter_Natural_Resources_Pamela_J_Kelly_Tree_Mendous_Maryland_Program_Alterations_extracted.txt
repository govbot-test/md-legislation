Section 1:
WES MOORE, Governor Ch. 113
Chapter 113
AN ACT concerning
Natural Resources – Pamela J. Kelly Tree–Mendous Maryland Program
– Alterations
FOR the purpose of altering the stated purpose of the Pamela J. Kelly Tree–Mendous
Maryland Program to include the advancement of forest health and resilience
through the promotion of community participation in tree planting and stewardship
and the planting and maintenance of native trees and shrubs through community
forestry activities; and generally relating to the Pamela J. Kelly Tree–Mendous
Maryland Program.
BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
Article – Natural Resources

Section 2:
Section 5–435
Annotated Code of Maryland
(2023 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)

Section 3:
SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
Article – Natural Resources
5–435.
(a) There is a Pamela J. Kelly Tree–Mendous Maryland Program in the
Department.
(b) The Program is established for the purpose of ADVANCING FOREST HEALTH
AND RESILIENCE BY PROMOTING, planting, AND MAINTAINING PROMOTING
COMMUNITY PARTICIPATION IN THE TREE PLANTING AND STEWARDSHIP AND THE
PLANTING AND MAINTAINING OF native trees and shrubs THROUGH COMMUNITY
FORESTRY ACTIVITIES on public lands, community open spaces, school grounds, and
rights–of–way.
(c) The Department shall administer the Program.
(d) The Department may adopt regulations to carry out this part.

Section 4:
SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
October 1, 2026.
– 1 –
Ch. 113 2026 LAWS OF MARYLAND
Approved by the Governor, April 14, 2026.
