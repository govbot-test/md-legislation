1 AN ACT concerning
2 Natural Resources – Pamela J. Kelly Tree–Mendous Maryland Program
3 – Alterations
4 FOR the purpose of altering the stated purpose of the Pamela J. Kelly Tree–Mendous
5 Maryland Program to include the advancement of forest health and resilience
6 through the promotion of community participation in tree planting and stewardship
7 and the planting and maintenance of native trees and shrubs through community
8 forestry activities; and generally relating to the Pamela J. Kelly Tree–Mendous
9 Maryland Program.
10 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
11 Article – Natural Resources
12 Section 5–435
13 Annotated Code of Maryland

2 SENATE BILL 164
1 (2023 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
2 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
3 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
4 Article – Natural Resources
5 5–435.
6 (a) There is a Pamela J. Kelly Tree–Mendous Maryland Program in the
7 Department.
8 (b) The Program is established for the purpose of ADVANCING FOREST HEALTH
9 AND RESILIENCE BY PROMOTING, planting, AND MAINTAINING PROMOTING
10 COMMUNITY PARTICIPATION IN THE TREE PLANTING AND STEWARDSHIP AND THE
11 PLANTING AND MAINTAINING OF native trees and shrubs THROUGH COMMUNITY
12 FORESTRY ACTIVITIES on public lands, community open spaces, school grounds, and
13 rights–of–way.
14 (c) The Department shall administer the Program.
15 (d) The Department may adopt regulations to carry out this part.
16 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
17 October 1, 2026.
Approved:
