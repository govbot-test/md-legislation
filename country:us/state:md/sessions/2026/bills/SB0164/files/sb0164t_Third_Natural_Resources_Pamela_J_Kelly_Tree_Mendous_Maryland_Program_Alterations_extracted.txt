1 AN ACT concerning
2 Natural Resources – Pamela J. Kelly Tree–Mendous Maryland Program
3 – Alterations
4 FOR the purpose of altering the stated purpose of the Pamela J. Kelly Tree–Mendous
5 Maryland Program to include the advancement of forest health and resilience
6 through the promotion of community participation in tree planting and stewardship
7 and maintenance of native trees and shrubs through community forestry activities;
8 and generally relating to the Pamela J. Kelly Tree–Mendous Maryland Program.

2 SENATE BILL 164
1 (a) There is a Pamela J. Kelly Tree–Mendous Maryland Program in the
2 Department.
3 (b) The Program is established for the purpose of ADVANCING FOREST HEALTH
4 AND RESILIENCE BY PROMOTING, planting, AND MAINTAINING PROMOTING
5 COMMUNITY PARTICIPATION IN THE PLANTING AND STEWARDSHIP OF native trees
6 and shrubs THROUGH COMMUNITY FORESTRY ACTIVITIES on public lands, community
7 open spaces, school grounds, and rights–of–way.
8 (c) The Department shall administer the Program.
9 (d) The Department may adopt regulations to carry out this part.
10 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
11 October 1, 2026.
