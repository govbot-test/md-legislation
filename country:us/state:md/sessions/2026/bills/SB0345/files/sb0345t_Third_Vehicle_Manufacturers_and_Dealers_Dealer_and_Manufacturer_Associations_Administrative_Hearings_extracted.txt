2 SENATE BILL 345
1 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
2 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:
3 Article – Transportation
4 15–201.
5 (a) In this subtitle the following words have the meanings indicated.
6 (A–1) “DEALER ASSOCIATION” MEANS A BUSINESS ENTITY THAT:
7 (1) IS PRIMARILY OWNED BY OR COMPOSED OF DEALERS THAT
8 COLLECTIVELY REPRESENT A MAJORITY OF THE FRANCHISED DEALERS IN THE
9 STATE; AND
10 (2) PRIMARILY REPRESENTS THE INTERESTS OF DEALERS.
11 (F) “MANUFACTURER ASSOCIATION” MEANS A BUSINESS ENTITY THAT:
12 (1) IS PRIMARILY OWNED BY OR COMPOSED OF MANUFACTURERS
13 THAT COLLECTIVELY REPRESENT A MAJORITY OF THE MANUFACTURERS IN THE
14 STATE; AND
15 (2) PRIMARILY REPRESENTS THE INTERESTS OF MANUFACTURERS.
16 [(f)] (G) “Second–stage manufacturer” has the meaning stated in § 13–113.2 of
17 this article.
18 15–214.
19 (A) [In] EXCEPT AS PROVIDED IN SUBSECTION (B) OF THIS SECTION, IN
20 addition to any other right to request a hearing under this subtitle and notwithstanding
21 any provisions of the franchise agreement to the contrary, a dealer, designated dealer
22 successor as provided in § 15–211.1 of this subtitle, DEALER ASSOCIATION ON BEHALF
23 OF ITSELF, A DEALER, OR A GROUP OF DEALERS, manufacturer, MANUFACTURER
24 ASSOCIATION ON BEHALF OF ITSELF, A MANUFACTURER, OR A GROUP OF
25 MANUFACTURERS, distributor, or factory branch may request a hearing under Title 12,
26 Subtitle 2 of this article to:
27 (1) Resolve a dispute under any provision of this title between a dealer [or],
28 a designated dealer successor, OR A DEALER ASSOCIATION and a manufacturer,
29 MANUFACTURER ASSOCIATION, distributor, or factory branch; or
30 (2) Seek clarification or interpretation of any provision of this [subtitle]
31 TITLE.
SENATE BILL 345 3
1 (B) SUBSECTION (A)(1) OF THIS SECTION DOES NOT APPLY TO A DISPUTE
2 INVOLVING A MANUFACTURER, DISTRIBUTOR, OR FACTORY BRANCH THAT DOES
3 NOT HAVE AT LEAST ONE FRANCHISED DEALER IN THIS STATE.
4 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
5 October 1, 2026.
Approved:
