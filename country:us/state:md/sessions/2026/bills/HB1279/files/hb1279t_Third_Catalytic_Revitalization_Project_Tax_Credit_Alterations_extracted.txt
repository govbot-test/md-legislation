CHAPTER ______
1 AN ACT concerning
2 Catalytic Revitalization Project Tax Credit – Alterations
3 FOR the purpose of altering eligibility for and the manner for claiming a certain credit
4 against the State income tax that certain persons may claim for certain construction
5 and rehabilitation costs for certain catalytic revitalization projects in the State;
6 altering certain limitations on the Secretary of Housing and Community
7 Development to accept certain applications and award certain tax credit certificates
8 adding applications promoting certain workforce housing to the types of applications
9 that certain regulations governing the application process for the catalytic
10 revitalization project tax credit must emphasize; and generally relating to the
11 catalytic revitalization project tax credit.
