13 Preamble
14 WHEREAS, Vernal pools are unique, small–scale ecosystems that contribute
15 significantly to the biodiversity of Maryland by providing habitat for species that cannot
16 reproduce in alternative habitats, including the wood frog, mole salamanders, and fairy
17 shrimp; and
18 WHEREAS, Vernal pools provide a significant amount of ecosystem services as they
19 occur frequently throughout Maryland and have the capacity to replenish groundwater and
20 filter stormwater flows; and
21 WHEREAS, Nontidal wetlands that meet the three federal wetland delineation
22 criteria of hydrology, hydric soils, and hydrophytic vegetation, including vernal pools that
23 satisfy those criteria, are regulated and protected by the State; and
24 WHEREAS, Vernal pools that do not meet all three federal wetland delineation
25 criteria are not afforded the regulatory protections applicable to nontidal wetlands in the
26 State and may suffer impairments or losses without regulatory review or approval; now,
27 therefore,
28 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
