1 INDICATED.
2 (B) “CENTER” MEANS A FINANCIAL EMPOWERMENT CENTER ESTABLISHED
3 UNDER THE PILOT PROGRAM.
4 (C) “PILOT PROGRAM” MEANS THE MARYLAND FINANCIAL
5 EMPOWERMENT CENTER NETWORK PILOT PROGRAM.
6 1–502.
7 (A) THERE IS A MARYLAND FINANCIAL EMPOWERMENT CENTER NETWORK
8 PILOT PROGRAM.
9 (B) THE PURPOSE OF THE PILOT PROGRAM IS TO ESTABLISH A STATEWIDE
10 NETWORK OF FINANCIAL EMPOWERMENT CENTERS IN DIFFERENT REGIONS OF THE
11 STATE.
12 (C) THE COMPTROLLER SHALL IMPLEMENT AND ADMINISTER THE PILOT
13 PROGRAM.
14 1–503.
15 (A) (1) EACH CENTER SHALL PROVIDE ONE–ON–ONE FINANCIAL
16 COUNSELING AND COACHING SERVICES FREE OF CHARGE, INCLUDING
17 INFORMATION ON HOW TO:
18 (I) INCREASE SAVINGS;
19 (II) PAY DOWN DEBT;
20 (III) ACCESS BANKING; AND
21 (IV) IMPROVE CREDIT SCORES.
22 (2) EACH CENTER SHALL MAKE THE INFORMATION PROVIDED UNDER
23 THIS SUBSECTION AVAILABLE IN ENGLISH, SPANISH, AND ANY OTHER LANGUAGE
24 REQUIRED BY THE OFFICE OF THE COMPTROLLER.
25 (B) EACH FINANCIAL COUNSELOR EMPLOYED BY A CENTER SHALL
26 COMPLETE TRAINING THAT MEETS OR EXCEEDS THE FINANCIAL EMPOWERMENT
27 CENTER COUNSELOR TRAINING STANDARDS DEVELOPED BY THE CITIES FOR
28 FINANCIAL EMPOWERMENT FUND.
